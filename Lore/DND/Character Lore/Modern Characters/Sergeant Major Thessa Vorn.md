# Sergeant Major Thessa Vorn

## Title
Chief Instructor of Olkgend

## Age
Late 30s

## Race
Human

## Location
Olkgend, Braewood

## Appearance
A lean, hard-muscled woman with short-cropped dark hair and intense brown eyes. Her face is angular and unsmiling, marked by a thin scar that runs from her left temple to her jaw. She moves with the controlled grace of someone who's trained in combat since childhood. Her uniform is always perfect, her boots always polished.

## Backstory
Thessa was the daughter of a Span Warden who died defending the bridge when she was twelve. She swore to honor his memory by becoming a Warden herself. She trained at Olkgend, graduated top of her class, and served on the bridge for eight years.

When Drill-Master Korrath needed a new chief instructor, he specifically requested Thessa. He'd remembered her from training—not the most naturally talented, but the most determined. She'd never given up, never made excuses, never accepted failure. That's the kind of instructor he wanted.

She's served under him for six years now, running the day-to-day training that Korrath designs but can no longer personally oversee. The trainees fear her almost as much as they fear him.

## Personality
- **Relentless:** Drives herself and her trainees to exhaustion. Rest is for after graduation.
- **Precise:** Every movement, every word, every schedule is exact. Sloppiness is unacceptable.
- **Stoic:** Shows no emotion during training. Praise is rare; criticism is common.
- **Dedicated:** Lives for the training program. Has no life outside Olkgend.

## Role in the Village
Runs all daily training operations under Drill-Master Korrath's oversight. She schedules exercises, leads instruction, evaluates progress, and recommends which trainees should wash out. The recruits see her more than they see Korrath—which means she's the face of their fear.

## Relationships
- **Drill-Master Vance Korrath:** Her commander and mentor. She's utterly loyal to him and his vision.
- **The Trainees:** They fear her, but those who graduate respect what she made them into.
- **Healer Winna Softhand:** Works together when training injuries occur. Professional respect.
- **Captain Darren Glaze:** Coordinates on graduate placement. She advocates for her best trainees.

## Notable Quote
> "Your father was a merchant? Your mother was a noble? I don't care. Here, you're nothing until you prove you're something. Now run the course again."
