# Hestia Vael

## Title
Royal Arcane Advisor

## Age
Appears mid-40s (likely older due to arcane influence)

## Race
Half-Elf

## Location
Braewood City, Braewood

## Appearance
Tall and willow-thin, with silver-white hair bound in iron rings. Her left eye glows faint violet when she channels power—a mark of her patron's influence. She speaks softly, but every syllable feels weighted, as if the words themselves carry hidden meaning. She dresses in dark purple robes that shimmer like liquid stars when they catch torchlight.

## Backstory
Hestia serves a Great Old One—an entity whose name she never speaks aloud. Her connection grants her insight into minds and the flow of events. She claims to "hear the echoes of decisions before they are made," and those who've watched her work believe it.

She came to court fifteen years ago, offering her services to the Crown. The previous arcane advisor died mysteriously the same week. No one has proven a connection, and Hestia has never addressed the rumors. What matters is that her predictions have proven accurate, her counsel invaluable, and her loyalty—to the Queen, at least—appears genuine.

## Personality
- **Eerily calm:** Nothing seems to disturb her. Her patience is unsettling.
- **Cryptic:** Speaks in layered meanings. Every conversation feels like a test.
- **Pragmatic:** Fights corruption through insight and manipulation, not direct confrontation.
- **Alien perspective:** Her patron's influence shows in how she views mortal concerns—important, but small.

## Role in the Kingdom
Advises the Queen on magical matters, investigates arcane threats, and serves as the court's primary defense against supernatural dangers. Her telepathic abilities make her an invaluable interrogator.

## Relationships
- **Queen Maera Braeden:** Serves her loyally, though their relationship is complex—mutual respect tinged with wariness.
- **Sir Kael Irondawn:** He distrusts her magic. She finds his suspicion amusing and appropriate.
- **The Court:** They fear her. She prefers it that way.
- **Her Patron:** She serves something beyond mortal understanding. What it wants, only she knows.

## Notable Quote
> "I do not predict the future. I simply listen more carefully than most."
