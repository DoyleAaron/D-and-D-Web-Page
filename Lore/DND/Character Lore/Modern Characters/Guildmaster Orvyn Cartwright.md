## Appearance
A portly man with silver hair, expensive rings, and the satisfied look of someone who's made a fortune moving other people's goods. He dresses in fine wool and silk that's slightly too ostentatious for a trade town, and his laugh is loud enough to fill any room. His hands are soft now, but the calluses of his youth haven't entirely faded.

## Backstory
Orvyn started as a carter's apprentice at fourteen. By thirty, he owned three wagons. By forty, he controlled the largest transport company in southwestern [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md). By fifty, he was Guildmaster of the Caravan Guild—the organization that manages virtually all commercial transport through [Cleamond](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Cleamond.md).

He's made himself rich and powerful through shrewd deals, strategic marriages, and a willingness to be ruthless when necessary. Some call him a monopolist; he calls himself a "facilitator of commerce."

## Personality
- **Charming when convenient:** Can make anyone feel like his best friend—until they're not useful.
- **Obsessed with control:** Hates anything moving through [Cleamond](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Cleamond.md) that he doesn't profit from.
- **Publicly generous:** Funds festivals, supports orphans, makes sure everyone knows it.
- **Privately calculating:** Every gift is an investment; every favor, a debt.

## Role in the Settlement
Controls most commercial transport through [Cleamond](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Cleamond.md). Sets prices, manages routes, negotiates with merchants from across [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md). His cooperation—or opposition—can make or break trade in the region.

## Relationships
- **Warden-Mayor Lysa Thorne:** Mutual wariness. She knows he's too powerful to ignore; he knows she's too sharp to manipulate easily.
- **Mortling Stronghold:** Major client. He ensures military supplies flow smoothly—at a premium.
- **Smaller merchants:** They depend on him and resent it. Some whisper of alternatives.

## Notable Quote
> "I don't own the roads, friend. I just make sure you can use them. For a modest consideration, of course."
