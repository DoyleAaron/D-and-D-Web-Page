## Description

Theron is reckless, impatient, and eager to prove himself as more than his mother's errand blade. He is believed to be the vampire tied to Edric Voss's turning.

## Personality

- **Impulsive:** Acts before considering fallout.
- **Proud:** Reacts badly to insult or restraint.
- **Predatory:** Sees fear as proof of dominance.

## Role in Current Arc

- Primary suspect in Dawerton's recent forced turning.
- Key target for reversing Edric's condition within the active cure window.

## Notable Quote

> "Mercy is what old predators beg for when they forget how to hunt."
