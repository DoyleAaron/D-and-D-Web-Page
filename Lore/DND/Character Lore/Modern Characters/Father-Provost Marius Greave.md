# Father-Provost Marius Greave

## Title
Father-Provost of the Cathedral of the Steady Flame, Eaveton

## Age
Early 60s

## Race
Human

## Location
Eaveton, Braewood

## Appearance
A portly man with a kind, weathered face and warm brown eyes. His hair is white and worn in a simple tonsure. He dresses in the traditional robes of the Steady Flame clergy—deep blue with golden trim—though his are often slightly rumpled from work in the charity kitchens. Callused hands from years of physical service. His voice is deep and resonant, trained for sermons but equally comfortable in quiet counsel.

## Backstory
Born to a prosperous merchant family in Eaveton, Marius rejected the family business to enter the clergy at eighteen. His calling was genuine—he'd seen the poverty hidden beneath Eaveton's prosperous facade and wanted to help.

He rose through the priesthood not through political maneuvering but through decades of devoted service. He established the Cathedral's charity kitchens, expanded its orphanage, and built a network of aid that reaches the city's poorest quarters. His appointment as Father-Provost twenty years ago was celebrated by common folk across Eaveton.

He's never sought political power but wields it nonetheless. His moral authority is such that even Lord-Regent Greythorne consults him on matters affecting the common people.

## Personality
Genuinely compassionate and humble despite his high office. Marius believes faith means service, not ceremony, and leads by example. He's often found serving in the charity kitchens or visiting the sick rather than attending formal functions.

He has a practical understanding of human nature—years of hearing confessions have left few illusions—but maintains faith in people's capacity for good. He can be surprisingly firm when moral principles are at stake.

Patient with sinners seeking redemption, implacable toward those who exploit the vulnerable.

## Role
As Father-Provost, Marius leads the largest temple in Eaveton:
- Oversees religious services and ceremonies
- Manages the Cathedral's charitable works (kitchens, orphanage, hospice)
- Provides moral counsel to city leadership
- Represents the Steady Flame's interests in civic matters

His informal influence often exceeds his official authority.

## Relationships
- **Lord-Regent Hallovar Greythorne:** Mutual respect despite very different worldviews. Greythorne is pragmatic, Marius idealistic, but they share genuine concern for Eaveton's welfare.
- **The common folk:** Beloved as a genuine champion of the poor. They come to him with problems they can't take elsewhere.
- **Other clergy:** Respected by most, envied by some who see his popularity as a threat to their advancement.
- **Father-Commander Aldous Valorian (Mortling):** Theological disagreements about the role of faith in military service, but personal respect.

## Notable Quote
*"The Steady Flame asks us to be lights in the darkness—not to admire the fire, but to share its warmth with those who have none."*
