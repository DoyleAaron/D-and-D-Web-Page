## Appearance
A frail old man with wispy white hair and watery blue eyes that still hold a spark of the ambitious trader he once was. Stooped from age but still moves with purpose through the ruins of his warehouse. Dresses in clothes that were expensive forty years ago, now faded and patched. His hands shake, but his memory is sharp as a blade.

## Backstory
Tomas [Ashwell](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Old%20Guildmaster%20Tomas%20Ashwell.md) was thirty-five when the Crown announced the Great Port Project — the plan to turn [Adaham](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Adaham.md) into a major trading hub rivaling Mortling. He invested everything he had, borrowed everything he could, and built the largest warehouse on the new docks. For five years, he dreamed of empire.

Then the project collapsed. The currents proved treacherous, Kluimontian raiders struck repeatedly, merchants refused to use the route. By 1195, the warehouses stood empty, the investors fled, and Tomas [Ashwell](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Old%20Guildmaster%20Tomas%20Ashwell.md) was ruined.

He never left. His warehouse rotted around him, but he stayed, maintaining one small section as living quarters. For forty years he has watched his dreams decay. He is the last survivor of the Great Port Project, a living monument to [Adaham](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Adaham.md)'s failure.

But he knows things. Where cargoes sank. What the original plans intended. Why the project really failed. And lately, his mind has been turning to a particular wreck — a ship that went down with a fortune in its hold.

## Personality
- **Bitter but not broken** — refuses to accept total defeat
- **Sharp-minded** — age hasn't dulled his intelligence
- **Obsessed with the past** — can't stop relitigating old decisions
- **Surprisingly resilient** — has outlived everyone who said he'd fail
- **Desperate for one last victory** — wants to salvage something before he dies

## Role in Adaham
- Living memory of the port project
- Unofficial historian of [Adaham](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Adaham.md)'s docks
- Knows the location of several wrecks
- Occasionally consulted by the Harbor-Warden
- Warns visitors about the ghost docks (whether they want to hear or not)

## Relationships
- **Harbor-Warden Colm Ravencroft:** Mutual respect; [Ravencroft](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbor-Warden%20Colm%20Ravencroft.md) checks on him regularly
- **Fleet-Captain Isolde Merrick:** She listens to his stories; he sees her as a granddaughter figure
- **Innkeeper Bess Waverly:** Brings him meals; worries about his health
- **The ghosts of his investors:** Talks to them sometimes in the empty warehouse

*"I built this warehouse with my own money, my own hands. Forty tons of stone, two hundred feet of dock frontage. The Crown said we'd be the next Mortling. They were wrong. The river was wrong. Everything was wrong. But I'm still here. The warehouse is still here, mostly. And somewhere out there, under twenty feet of water, is the Maiden's Fortune with sixty thousand gold worth of cargo. I know exactly where it is. I just need someone crazy enough to help me get it."*
