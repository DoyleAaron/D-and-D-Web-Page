# Sword-Instructor Daven Rowe

## Title
Senior Sword-Instructor of Ioxyhull

## Age
Late 30s

## Race
Human

## Location
Ioxyhull, Braewood

## Appearance
A lean, athletic man with close-cropped dark hair and watchful brown eyes. His movements are economical and precise—the mark of a trained fighter. Several scars mark his arms and one crosses his left cheek, souvenirs from the border campaigns. He dresses simply, preferring training clothes even when not actively instructing.

## Backstory
Daven earned his reputation in the border skirmishes with Kluimont raiders, where he distinguished himself as both a skilled swordsman and an effective small-unit commander. His talents caught the attention of the Crown, and he was offered a position at Ioxyhull—officially to train elite knights, unofficially to ensure the village's defenders could protect a royal evacuation.

He accepted without hesitation. The "knight training facility" cover suits him well—he genuinely loves teaching swordsmanship, and the knights who train here become some of the finest in the kingdom. That they're also prepared for a very specific emergency is knowledge he carries quietly.

## Personality
- **Patient teacher:** Believes anyone can learn to fight with proper instruction. Never mocks struggling students.
- **Combat-pragmatic:** Teaches what works, not what looks impressive. Survival matters more than style.
- **Quietly intense:** Doesn't raise his voice often, but when he speaks about combat, people listen.
- **Watchful:** Old habits from the border. Always knows the exits, always notes who's carrying what.

## Role in the Village
Oversees all combat training for knights rotating through the facility. His curriculum emphasizes personal protection, escort duties, and defensive fighting—skills that would be essential during an evacuation. He also maintains a small cadre of permanent guards trained to his exacting standards.

## Relationships
- **Knight-Commander Cedric Hallam:** His superior and the man who recruited him. Deep mutual respect.
- **Harbor-Master Gwen Ashbury:** Coordinates on evacuation protocols. If the call comes, his fighters protect the path to her ship.
- **Training Knights:** Views each one as both student and potential lifesaver. Takes their development personally.
- **His Border Comrades:** Still exchanges letters with soldiers he served with. The frontier shaped who he is.

## Notable Quote
> "I'm not teaching you to win duels. I'm teaching you to keep someone alive when everything goes wrong. Different skill entirely."
