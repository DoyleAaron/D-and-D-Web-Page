# Captain Hilda Stonebreaker

## Title
Captain of the Strane City Guard

## Age
50s

## Race
Dwarf

## Location
Strane (Medium City), Northern Braewood Peninsula


## Appearance
Broad-shouldered and formidable, with the kind of presence that makes crowds part without a word. Iron-grey hair worn short and practical. A face that looks carved from weathered stone — not unkind, but not soft either. Pale grey eyes that have seen too much and forgotten none of it.

Wears her guard captain's insignia with obvious pride. Her armor is well-maintained but shows the dents and repairs of decades of use. Carries a warhammer that's older than most of her guards — a family heirloom.

Walks with absolute confidence. Speaks the same way.

## Backstory
Born to one of the northern dwarven clans, Hilda left home young to join the Braewood military. She served twenty years in various posts — border patrols, city watches, even a stint in the capital — before being offered the Strane captaincy.

She took it because Strane needed someone who wouldn't be corrupted by merchant bribes or scared of smugglers. That was twenty-three years ago. Three Lord-Wardens have come and gone; Hilda remains.

She's buried twelve guards in that time. She remembers every name.

## Personality
- **Incorruptible** — has been offered fortunes; never even considered them
- **Blunt** — doesn't waste words or tolerate people who do
- **Protective** — her guards are her responsibility; she takes it seriously
- **Pragmatic** — knows the law isn't always justice; acts accordingly
- **Stubborn** — once she decides something is right, she's immovable

## Role in Strane
- Commands the city guard (approximately 120 soldiers)
- Responsible for law enforcement and defense
- Answers to Lord-Warden Velthorpe (but has significant autonomy)
- Coordinates with Elder Copperkettle on dwarven matters
- De facto peacekeeper between factions

## The Guard Under Her Command
- 120 guards total, rotating shifts
- Mix of humans, dwarves, and a few half-elves
- Well-trained by regional standards
- Underpaid and underappreciated
- Fiercely loyal to Hilda personally

## Relationships
- **Lord-Warden Velthorpe:** Respects his competence; frustrated by his politics
- **Elder Bram Copperkettle:** Old friend from the dwarven quarter; trusts completely
- **Magistra Liriel:** Suspicious of magic-users in general; tolerates her usefulness
- **The Masked Lanterns:** Knows they exist; maintains an unspoken truce
- **Her Guards:** Family; she'd die for them and they know it

---

*"I've been called incorruptible like it's some kind of virtue. It isn't. It's just practicality. Take a bribe once, you're owned forever. Stay clean, you can look anyone in the eye and tell them exactly what you think. That's worth more than gold."*

*"...Besides, my hammer's worth more than most bribes anyway."*
