# High Archon Lysanthe Kilmora

## Title
High Archon of Kluimont

## Age
Appears early 50s (rumored much older due to arcane lifespan extension)

## Race
Human (Kluimont)

## Location
Kluimont City, Kluimont

## Appearance
A severe woman with iron-gray hair pulled back from a face that seems carved from determination. Her eyes hold the faint glow of someone who has channeled too much arcane energy for too long. She dresses in formal robes of deep purple and silver, and always wears the Archon's Chain—a necklace of interlocked magical sigils that supposedly connects her to Kluimont's ley line network.

## Backstory
Lysanthe rose to power through the Arcane Synod, the council of mages that truly rules Kluimont behind its noble facade. She has spent decades consolidating arcane knowledge, intensifying research programs, and—most importantly—maintaining the secrecy around Kluimont's greatest asset: the subdued brass dragon that powers their most potent rituals.

The Arcane Storm of 1220 C.C. nearly exposed everything. Lysanthe contained the damage, silenced witnesses, and emerged stronger than ever. Now she rules absolutely, and the question of what Kluimont is truly building haunts her rivals across the continent.

## Personality
- **Brilliant and ruthless:** Did not reach her position through kindness.
- **Obsessed with control:** Of magic, of secrets, of everything.
- **Paranoid:** Too many people want what Kluimont has. She trusts no one completely.
- **Visionary:** Sees a future where arcane power reshapes the world. She intends to guide that reshaping.

## Role in the Kingdom
Absolute ruler of Kluimont. Head of the Arcane Synod. Keeper of the kingdom's darkest secrets. Her word shapes policy, her will shapes magic, and her ambition shapes the future.

## Relationships
- **The Arcane Synod:** She controls them. They know better than to challenge her.
- **The Brass Dragon:** Whatever relationship exists between them is Kluimont's most guarded secret.
- **Braewood:** The enemy. Their military strength threatens everything she's built.
- **Lavalto:** Useful trading partners. She doesn't trust vampires, but she respects their discretion.

## Notable Quote
> "Power is not taken. Power is understood. Those who grasp this truth rule. Those who don't serve those who do."
