## Appearance
A weathered woman with sun-browned skin and grey hair pulled back in a practical bun. Her face is deeply lined from decades of outdoor work, and her hands are rough and capable. She dresses simply in homespun wool—the product of her own village—and carries herself with the quiet dignity of someone who has earned her authority through a lifetime of service.

## Backstory
Maeven was born in [Trares](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Trares.md) and never left. She learned shepherding from her parents, took over their flock when they passed, and built it into one of the largest in the village. When disputes arose, neighbors came to her for fair judgment. When hard times hit, she organized relief. When the previous elder died, no one questioned who would replace him.

She has led [Trares](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Trares.md) for twenty years, through good seasons and bad. Under her guidance, the village has prospered—wool prices are up, the flock numbers are healthy, and the old traditions remain strong. She knows every family, every field, every sheep by name.

## Personality
- **Wise:** Has seen enough seasons to know that most problems solve themselves with patience.
- **Fair:** Her judgments are respected because she never favors one family over another.
- **Humble:** Lives in a modest cottage despite her position. Ostentation is not the [Trares](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Trares.md) way.
- **Protective:** Fiercely defends her village and its traditions. Outsiders who threaten either find her implacable.

## Role in the Village
Governs [Trares](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Trares.md) as elder, mediating disputes, coordinating with [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md), and maintaining the traditions that bind the community together. Her word is final in village matters. She also trains her granddaughter, Young Kira, as her likely successor.

## Relationships
- **High Steward Merylla Davross:** Represents [Trares](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Trares.md) in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md). They meet regularly at festival councils.
- **Young Kira Shawl:** Her granddaughter and apprentice. Maeven is preparing her for eventual leadership.
- **Corbin Fleecewarden:** The master shepherd who manages the grazing lands. Essential working relationship.
- **The Other Village Elders:** Knows her counterparts in [Dalo](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Dalo.md), [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md), and [Rens](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Rens.md). They're siblings in the [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) network.

## Notable Quote
> "My grandmother survived the famine because [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) shared its grain. I've spent my whole life making sure we deserve that gift. Every day, we prove worthy of what they gave us."
