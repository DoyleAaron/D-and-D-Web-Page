## Appearance
A small, neat halfling woman with honey-brown hair pulled back in a practical bun and gentle green eyes that have seen every kind of training injury. Her hands are indeed soft—she uses special creams to keep them supple for delicate medical work. She wears a healer's apron with many pockets for supplies and moves with quiet efficiency.

## Backstory
Winna trained as a healer in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md), learning the arts of medicine under the herbalists there. She came to [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md) seeking a different kind of challenge—military medicine, treating wounds from combat training rather than common illnesses.

She found her calling. The training at [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md) is brutal, and injuries are constant. Broken bones, deep cuts, exhaustion, and worse pass through her healing house daily. She patches them up and sends them back to training—or, in severe cases, makes the call that a trainee is too injured to continue.

The trainees call her "[Softhand](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Healer%20Winna%20Softhand.md)" with genuine affection. She's the only gentleness in their grueling experience, the one person who treats them with kindness. She knows this and uses it—a calm word from her can motivate trainees more than all the Sergeant Major's shouting.

## Personality
- **Gentle:** Speaks softly, touches carefully. Healing requires trust.
- **Competent:** Don't mistake gentleness for weakness. She's seen everything and can handle anything.
- **Maternal:** The trainees are mostly young, far from home. She provides what comfort she can within the strict environment.
- **Firm:** Won't clear injured trainees for duty until they're ready. Not even [Korrath](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Drill-Master%20Vance%20Korrath.md) overrules her on medical matters.

## Role in the Village
Runs the healing house, treating all training injuries. She evaluates medical fitness, teaches basic first aid to trainees, and makes the difficult calls about who can continue training and who must stop. She also maintains supplies of healing herbs and medicines.

## Relationships
- **Drill-Master Vance Korrath:** Respects his purpose but pushes back when his demands damage trainees beyond recovery.
- **Sergeant Major Thessa Vorn:** Cool professional relationship. They have different priorities.
- **The Trainees:** They trust her. She's the one kind face in [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md).
- **Root-Mother Yseult Greenbough:** Trained in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md), maintains contacts with healers there. Gets rare medicines from [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md).

## Notable Quote
> "They come to me broken, and I put them back together. Then they go back out and break again. But each time, they're a little stronger. That's how warriors are made—and how healers earn their calluses."
