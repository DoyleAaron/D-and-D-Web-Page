# Lord-Regent Hallovar Greythorne

## Appearance
A solidly built man of slightly below average height, with iron-grey hair worn short in military fashion. His features are broader than typical for [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) nobility—the source of persistent rumors about dwarven ancestry. Dark, calculating eyes sit beneath heavy brows. He dresses in practical but expensive clothing: dark wool and leather rather than silks, always with the [Greythorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Regent%20Hallovar%20Greythorne.md) family sigil (a grey thorn tree) prominent. His hands are calloused despite his noble station, evidence of his insistence on personally inspecting the garrison's training.

## Backstory
The [Greythorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Regent%20Hallovar%20Greythorne.md) family has governed [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md) for three generations, granted the regency by King Tarvin's grandfather as reward for loyal service during border conflicts with [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md). Hallovar inherited the position from his father and has ruled for nearly thirty years.

The rumors of dwarven blood—never confirmed, never denied—stem from his great-grandmother's marriage to a [Greythorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Regent%20Hallovar%20Greythorne.md) lord. Whatever the truth, Hallovar has used the whispers strategically: they make him more acceptable to the dwarven craftsmen who work [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md)'s forges, while giving him a reputation for practicality over prejudice.

Under his governance, [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md) has become the kingdom's most efficient military training center and its wealthiest city outside the capital. The merchant guilds have flourished, and the Grain Exchange sets prices that affect farmers throughout [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md). His success has earned him both the Crown's trust and the quiet resentment of nobles who believe a "half-dwarf" should not hold such power.

## Personality
Pragmatic above all else. Hallovar cares little for ideological disputes or racial tensions—only for results. He judges people by their usefulness and loyalty, not their bloodline. This makes him effective but also somewhat cold; he has few genuine friends, only allies and subordinates.

He speaks bluntly, with little patience for courtly flattery. Military precision informs his governance: clear chains of command, regular reports, swift decisions. He expects competence and punishes failure quickly but fairly.

Despite his pragmatism, he harbors genuine pride in [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md)'s prosperity. The city's success is his legacy, and he works tirelessly to maintain it.

## Role
As Lord-Regent, Hallovar holds complete administrative authority over [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md) and its surrounding territories. He answers directly to King Tarvin but operates with considerable autonomy given the distance from the capital. His responsibilities include:
- Overseeing the Grain Exchange and Merchant Courts
- Commanding the [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md) garrison and training facilities
- Collecting Crown taxes (from which he takes a substantial percentage)
- Maintaining the River Road and trade infrastructure
- Supplying trained soldiers to [Mortling Stronghold](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Mortling%20Stronghold.md) and [Kadena](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md)

His influence extends beyond [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md): the satellite towns of [Gitstin](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md) and [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) fall within his administrative sphere, and their local barons answer to him.

## Relationships
- **King Tarvin Braeden IV:** His liege lord. Hallovar's loyalty to the Crown is genuine and pragmatic—the monarchy's stability ensures his own power.
- **Marshal Corin Ashfeld:** His military commander and closest thing to a friend. They've worked together for two decades.
- **Guildmistress Petra Thornwood:** The most powerful guild leader in [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md). They maintain a careful alliance—she supports his policies, he protects guild privileges.
- **Lord-Marshal Caelric Ironward (Mortling):** Professional respect between military men. Hallovar supplies the soldiers Caelric trains for border duty.
- **Lord-Governor Callum Westmarch (Phine):** Distant but cordial relations. Both serve the Crown's interests in major cities.
