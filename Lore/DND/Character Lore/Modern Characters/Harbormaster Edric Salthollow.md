## Appearance
A weathered man with deep-set grey eyes that have spent decades watching the horizon. Salt-and-pepper hair cropped short, a beard kept trimmed but never quite neat. Hands calloused from rope and net. Walks with a slight limp from an old fishing accident. Always smells faintly of [brine](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Widow%20Selna%20Brine.md) and pipe smoke.

## Backstory
Born in [Vorton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Vorton.md) to a fishing family that has worked these waters for six generations. Inherited the title of Harbormaster from his father, though "harbormaster" of a village of 1,200 is more elder than official. 

Lost his wife Mira to the sea fifteen years ago — a sudden storm took her boat and three others. Never remarried. Threw himself into the village instead, becoming its de facto leader through sheer stubborn dedication.

Edric remembers when [Vorton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Vorton.md) was slightly larger, when more ships would stop, when the old Tharnic lighthouse meant something. He's watched the slow decline and feels personally responsible for stopping it, even though he knows one man can't hold back the tide.

## Personality
- **Practical to a fault** — deals in fish counts, tide tables, and repair schedules
- **Deeply superstitious** — maintains the old rituals, leaves offerings at the Drowner's Shrine
- **Protective** — treats all 1,200 villagers as extended family
- **Quietly stubborn** — won't abandon [Vorton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Vorton.md) even as it fades
- **Carries grief** — the sea took his wife; he respects and fears it equally

## Role in Vorton
- Manages the docks and fishing schedules
- Settles disputes (usually over bowls of chowder in [Salthollow](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Edric%20Salthollow.md) Hall)
- Organizes the annual Tide Festival
- Coordinates with [Strane](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Strane.md) on tithes (resentfully)
- Commands the volunteer Shore Watch (in theory)

## Relationships
- **Maren Tidecaller:** Respects her wisdom
- **Jorik Ironbolt:** Old friend, shares drinks and complaints about the walls
- **Kessa Vane:** Sees his younger self in her restlessness; fears she'll leave
- **Lord-Warden Velthorpe (Strane):** Never met personally; resents his tithe demands
