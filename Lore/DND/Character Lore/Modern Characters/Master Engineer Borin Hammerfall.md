# Master Engineer Borin Hammerfall

## Title
Head of the Stoneguard Engineers Guild

## Age
~180 (middle-aged for a dwarf)

## Race
Dwarf

## Location
Mortling Stronghold, Braewood

## Appearance
A stout dwarf with arms like tree trunks and hands permanently stained with morite dust—the powdered stone used in runic fortifications. His beard is braided into three thick ropes, each weighted with a different mason's tool. He wears leather work clothes rather than armor, and his eyes have the distant look of someone who sees stress fractures before they form.

## Backstory
Borin comes from a long line of dwarven engineers who served Braewood's fortresses for generations. His grandfather helped build the second wall of Mortling; his father reinforced it after the Siege of 892 C.C. Borin himself completed the third wall's capstone—the great runic keystone that supposedly makes the inner wall impervious to siege magic.

He knows every flaw, every weakness, every secret passage in Mortling's defenses. This knowledge makes him indispensable—and a target. Kluimont would pay any price for what's in his head.

## Personality
- **Obsessively detail-oriented:** Will delay a project for weeks over a misaligned stone.
- **Gruff but not unkind:** Yells at apprentices constantly but also feeds them when they forget to eat.
- **Paranoid about security:** Keeps the most sensitive construction secrets in his memory alone—no plans on paper.
- **Deeply proud:** His work will outlast kingdoms. He knows it.

## Role in the Settlement
Commands all fortification construction, repair, and improvement in Mortling. The Stoneguard Engineers answer to him alone. He advises Lord-Marshal Ironward on structural matters and maintains the runed defenses that make Mortling nearly impregnable.

## Relationships
- **Lord-Marshal Ironward:** Fifteen years of partnership. They don't always like each other, but they trust each other completely.
- **Stoneguard Engineers:** His guild, his family. He's trained most of them personally.
- **Dwarven Enclaves:** Maintains connections with dwarven communities throughout Braewood. Mortling treats dwarves better partly because of his influence.

## Notable Quote
> "Walls don't fail. Engineers fail. I don't intend to fail."
