## Appearance
An elegant vampire with silver-streaked dark hair and an almost theatrical presence. He dresses impeccably in the latest fashion from multiple kingdoms—mixing Braewood military styling with Islefield embroidery and Kluimont arcane motifs. His smile is knowing, his wit is sharp, and his eyes hold centuries of political maneuvering.

## Backstory
Varis was once a diplomat in a kingdom that no longer exists, serving courts that crumbled to dust centuries ago. He came to Dawerton seeking stability and found it in the city's unique vampiric society. His experience with foreign powers makes him invaluable when negotiating with Braewood, Islefield, or Kluimont—he understands how mortals think, and more importantly, how to manipulate them.

## Personality
- **Wryly amused:** Finds mortal politics endlessly entertaining after centuries of watching empires rise and fall.
- **Politically insightful:** Offers observations that cut to the heart of any negotiation.
- **Charmingly cynical:** Believes everyone has a price; his job is to find it.
- **Secretly idealistic:** Beneath the cynicism, he genuinely wants Dawerton to thrive.

## Role in the Council
Varis manages foreign relations—treaties, trade agreements, and diplomatic incidents with other kingdoms. He is often the public face of Dawerton when dealing with non-vampire powers. His ability to blend in with mortal society (and his willingness to walk in daylight through magical means) makes him uniquely valuable.
