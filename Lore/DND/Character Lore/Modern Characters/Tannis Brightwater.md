# Tannis Brightwater

## Title
Innkeeper of the Pilgrim's Rest

## Age
Mid-50s

## Race
Halfling

## Location
Osegas, Braewood

## Appearance
A plump, cheerful halfling with curly grey-streaked brown hair and warm hazel eyes that crinkle when she smiles—which is often. Her hands are always busy, her apron always spotless, and her kitchen always smells of fresh bread. She moves through crowded rooms with practiced grace, remembering every face and every name.

## Backstory
Tannis grew up in the Pilgrim's Rest—her grandmother founded it two generations ago specifically to embody Osegas's tradition of hospitality. The inn has always operated on an unusual principle: every guest receives a hot meal regardless of their ability to pay. Those with coin pay; those without receive the same food for free. The difference is covered by donation and by the inn's modest profit on lodging.

When Tannis inherited the inn, she expanded this principle. The Pilgrim's Rest now feeds the hungry year-round, not just travelers. During the lean winter months, families who've fallen on hard times know they can always find a bowl of soup and a piece of bread.

## Personality
- **Generous:** Truly believes that no one should go hungry. Acts on this belief daily.
- **Practical:** Generosity requires organization. She tracks donations, manages supplies, and stretches every coin.
- **Warm:** Makes everyone feel welcome, from high priests to homeless wanderers.
- **Stubborn:** Don't try to tell her that feeding the poor is bad for business. She's heard it before.

## Role in the Town
Runs the Pilgrim's Rest, Osegas's largest inn and its informal social center. Beyond hospitality, she oversees the town's informal charity network, coordinating with other halfling families to ensure no one goes hungry. She also organizes the food for major festivals.

## Relationships
- **High Steward Merylla Davross:** Shares her values completely. They coordinate on relief efforts for the surrounding villages.
- **The Halfling Families:** Leader of Osegas's halfling community by respect rather than title.
- **Brother Aldric Sunhallow:** He directs pilgrims to her inn. She feeds them in the name of all gods.
- **Kira Stoneheart:** The dwarf who coordinates with the four villages often works with her on supply logistics.

## Notable Quote
> "Empty bellies make angry hearts, and angry hearts make broken communities. Feed people, and half your problems solve themselves. That's not charity—that's common sense."
