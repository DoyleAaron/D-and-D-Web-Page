## Appearance
A small woman with kind eyes and work-roughened hands, dressed in the simple gray robes of her order. Her hair, once brown, is now mostly silver and usually covered by a practical headscarf. She moves with the quick efficiency of someone who always has too much to do, and her smile is genuine even when she's exhausted—which is often.

## Backstory
Evalyn came to [Cleamond](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Cleamond.md) thirty years ago as a young woman fleeing a bad marriage in [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) City. The previous Mother of Mercy House took her in, trained her, and eventually passed the mantle to her. For twenty years, Evalyn has run the almshouse, feeding the hungry, sheltering the homeless, and tending to those the town would rather forget.

She's seen everything—every secret, every shame, every quiet act of kindness and cruelty. People confess to her because they trust she won't judge. They're right.

## Personality
- **Genuinely compassionate:** Believes everyone deserves help, no matter what they've done.
- **Surprisingly tough:** You don't run an almshouse for twenty years without developing steel.
- **Keeper of secrets:** Knows more about [Cleamond](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Cleamond.md)'s citizens than anyone. Never tells—unless telling saves lives.
- **Quietly devout:** Her faith is personal and practical; she prays with her hands, not just her words.

## Role in the Settlement
Runs Mercy House, the town's primary almshouse. Provides food, shelter, and basic medical care to the poor and desperate. Also serves as an unofficial confessor and counselor to those who have nowhere else to turn.

## Relationships
- **The poor of Cleamond:** Her children, all of them. She knows their names, their stories, their hopes.
- **Warden-Mayor Thorne:** Mutual respect. [Thorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Warden-Mayor%20Lysa%20Thorne.md) funds Mercy House when she can; Evalyn votes for [Thorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Warden-Mayor%20Lysa%20Thorne.md) when it matters.
- **Guildmaster Cartwright:** Accepts his donations; doesn't accept his attempts to influence her. He's learned not to try.

## Notable Quote
> "Everyone who walks through my door has a story. Most of them are sad. My job isn't to judge the story—it's to help them write a better next chapter."
