## Appearance
A young woman with honey-brown hair usually tied back in a practical braid and lively hazel eyes. Her face shows the beginnings of the weathering that will come from a life outdoors, and her hands are already calloused from farm work. She dresses in practical wool clothes—village-made, of course—and carries herself with the self-conscious dignity of someone learning to lead.

## Backstory
Kira grew up in her grandmother's shadow, learning the stories of the village, the traditions of the Gratitude Stone, and the responsibilities of leadership. She knew from childhood that she was being prepared for something, though she didn't fully understand what until she was a teenager.

Now she serves as her grandmother's apprentice, attending council meetings, learning to mediate disputes, and representing [Trares](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Trares.md) at festivals in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md). She's intelligent and capable, but she feels the weight of expectation heavily. Everyone assumes she'll be the next elder—including, sometimes unfortunately, herself.

## Personality
- **Earnest:** Takes her responsibilities seriously, perhaps too seriously. Needs to learn to relax.
- **Quick-witted:** Grasps concepts quickly and remembers what she's taught.
- **Uncertain:** Privately doubts whether she can live up to her grandmother's legacy.
- **Compassionate:** Genuinely cares about the village and its people. That's her greatest strength.

## Role in the Village
Serves as Elder Maeven's apprentice and likely successor. She attends meetings, handles minor administrative tasks, and represents the village at some external events. She's learning, growing, and being evaluated by everyone—whether she likes it or not.

## Relationships
- **Elder Maeven Shawl:** Her grandmother, mentor, and the standard she's measured against. Loves and respects her, but also feels the pressure of those expectations.
- **Kira Stoneheart:** The dwarf coordinator in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) shares her name. They've met at festivals and have an easy rapport.
- **Other Young People:** Few remain in the village; most leave for cities. She understands why but has chosen to stay.
- **The Four Villages:** Knows her counterparts in [Dalo](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Dalo.md), [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md), and [Rens](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Rens.md). The next generation of the [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) network.

## Notable Quote
> "Grandmother makes it look easy—listening to everyone, finding fair solutions, carrying the village's problems. It's not easy. But someone has to learn. So I learn."
