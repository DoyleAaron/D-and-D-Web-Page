## Appearance
A solidly built man with a soldier's bearing and a guardsman's patience. His brown hair is going gray at the temples, and his face carries the weathered look of someone who's spent too many nights on patrol. He wears practical chain mail over leather, keeps his sword maintained but not flashy, and carries a small notebook where he records everything—names, faces, suspicious behavior.

## Backstory
Marcus served in the Crown army for fifteen years before a leg wound slowed him down enough to consider other options. The Warden-Mayor position at [Cleamond](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Cleamond.md) had just opened, and [Lysa Thorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Warden-Mayor%20Lysa%20Thorne.md) was looking for someone who understood both military discipline and the realities of small-town policing. Marcus fit. He's been her right hand for eleven years.

He's never quite healed from the wound that ended his army career, and wet weather makes him limp. But his mind is as sharp as ever, and he knows [Cleamond](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Cleamond.md)'s streets better than anyone.

## Personality
- **Steady and reliable:** The opposite of flashy. Does his job, does it well, goes home.
- **Frustrated by limitations:** Knows his guard is too small for the threats they face.
- **Loyal to Thorne:** She gave him a second career; he won't forget it.
- **Quietly observant:** That notebook contains years of patterns. He's noticed things.

## Role in the Settlement
Commands the town guard. Manages patrols, investigates crimes, coordinates with Crown soldiers passing through, and serves as Warden-Mayor [Thorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Warden-Mayor%20Lysa%20Thorne.md)'s military advisor.

## Relationships
- **Warden-Mayor Lysa Thorne:** Complete loyalty. They understand each other without words.
- **Crown soldiers:** Complicated. He was one of them; now he's a small-town guard captain. Some respect him; others don't.
- **The southern roads:** Something's wrong out there. His patrols keep finding signs—but never answers.

## Notable Quote
> "I don't have enough men, enough coin, or enough time. I've got what I've got, and I make it work. That's the job."
