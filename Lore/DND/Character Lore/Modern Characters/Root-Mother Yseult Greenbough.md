## Appearance
A stooped elderly woman with silver-white hair worn long and loose, and eyes that remain startlingly sharp and green despite her age. Her face is deeply lined, her hands gnarled but still nimble enough for delicate herb work. She dresses in simple robes of forest green, always with an apron covered in plant stains and pockets filled with leaves, seeds, and dried herbs.

## Backstory
Yseult came to [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md) as a young woman, drawn by its reputation for healing arts. She apprenticed under the previous Root-Mother for twenty years, learning every plant in the marshes, every remedy in the tradition, every secret passed down through generations.

When her mentor died, Yseult took the title—not by inheritance, but by demonstrated mastery. For thirty years since, she has led [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md), trained apprentices, and maintained the village's reputation as the finest source of herbal medicine in southern [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md). Her remedies are sought by healers across the kingdom.

## Personality
- **Wise:** Seventy years of life and thirty years of healing have given her deep understanding.
- **Patient:** Teaching takes time. Healing takes time. She has learned not to rush.
- **Direct:** Doesn't soften her words. If you're wrong, she tells you.
- **Devoted:** The healing arts are her entire life. She lives for the work.

## Role in the Village
Leads [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md) as Root-Mother, a title that combines spiritual and practical authority. She oversees all healing work, trains apprentices, maintains the sacred groves, and represents the village to the outside world. Her word on medical matters is considered final throughout southern [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md).

## Relationships
- **Young Tam Greenbough:** Her grandson and current apprentice. She hopes he'll be worthy of the title someday.
- **Aelindra Mistwalker:** The elven healer who brings ancient knowledge. They work as partners.
- **Healer Winna Softhand:** The medic at [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md) trained in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) and maintains contact. They share knowledge.
- **High Steward Merylla Davross:** Represents [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md) in the [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) network. Mutual respect.

## Notable Quote
> "I've spent fifty years learning what the marshes teach. Every plant has a purpose; every illness has a remedy. The knowledge is endless. I'm still just a student."
