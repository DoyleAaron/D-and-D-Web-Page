## Description

Isolde Valdris presents herself as a refined noble widow managing an old country estate. In truth, she leads a predatory vampire household tasked with destabilizing roads and settlements near Dawerton.

She sees herself as a protector of her "family," but her protection has become tyranny over both kin and servants.

## Methods

- Uses collared human servants as disposable labor and early warning screens.
- Hides brutality behind etiquette, hospitality, and "proper" household structure.
- Treats roadside murders as logistics, not cruelty.

## Role in Current Arc

- Commands the vampire family cell linked to the recent Dawerton turning cases.
- Shelters Theron, whose actions directly connect to Edric Voss's condition.
- Acts as a field extension of Maltrius's pressure campaign before the summit.

## Notable Quote

> "Civilization is a table setting. Remove the candles and silver, and everyone remembers what they are."
