# Bridge-Warden Elara Thornguard

## Title
Bridge-Warden of Kadena

## Age
Late 40s

## Race
Human

## Location
Kadena, Braewood

## Appearance
A stern woman with iron-grey hair cut short and practical, and cold blue eyes that assess everyone she meets. Her face is angular, her jaw firm, and her posture military-perfect. She wears a distinctive uniform that incorporates elements of both Braewood and Islefield military dress—a visual reminder of her unique authority. The twin badges on her collar show the arms of both kingdoms.

## Backstory
Elara rose through the Braewood military as an exemplary officer—disciplined, incorruptible, and utterly dedicated to duty. When the previous Bridge-Warden retired, her name topped every list. The Islefield Crown, given joint approval over the position, agreed without objection.

The role suits her perfectly. She answers to two monarchs and is controlled by neither. Her authority over the Grand Span is absolute, her judgments final. She works daily with her counterpart in Rye, Shield-Captain Celia Verdant, maintaining the complex security apparatus that keeps the bridge open while preventing its misuse.

She has held the post for eight years. In that time, no major incident has disrupted bridge traffic. She intends to keep it that way.

## Personality
- **Incorruptible:** Has refused bribes that would make most people wealthy. Her integrity is absolute.
- **Demanding:** Expects excellence from everyone under her command. Mediocrity is not tolerated.
- **Fair:** Applies the same rules to Braewood nobles and Islefield merchants. No exceptions.
- **Reserved:** Keeps professional distance from everyone. The job requires impartiality.

## Role in the City
Commands both the city garrison and the Span Wardens who patrol the Grand Span. She oversees all security operations, from inspecting wagons to investigating smuggling attempts. She reports to both crowns but takes orders from neither—her mandate is the bridge itself.

## Relationships
- **Shield-Captain Celia Verdant:** Her counterpart in Rye. They meet weekly at the Central Neutral Zone. Professional respect borders on friendship.
- **Captain Drem Holt:** Her Span Warden commander. He executes her policies on the bridge.
- **Both Royal Courts:** Communicates with Braewood City and Islefield City regularly. Navigates their interests carefully.
- **Guildmaster Loren Fairweather:** The merchant consortium head. Their relationship is professional but wary.

## Notable Quote
> "I serve the bridge. Not Braewood, not Islefield—the bridge. It was here before both kingdoms, and if we do our jobs properly, it will outlast us all."
