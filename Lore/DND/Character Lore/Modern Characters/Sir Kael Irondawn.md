# Sir Kael Irondawn

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Royal Guard Captain |
| **Age** | Late 50s |
| **Race** | Human |

---

## Appearance
Broad-shouldered with short-cropped grey hair and a face carved from stone. His burnished breastplate is etched with the Braewood stag, polished to a mirror shine. He rarely blinks, and his eyes constantly scan for threats even in the safest chambers. He carries himself like a man who has never known a moment's relaxation - because he hasn't.

## Backstory
Kael has served the Crown for over thirty years, rising through the ranks on pure skill and unwavering loyalty. He was assigned to protect Queen Maera twenty years ago and has never left her side since. He has killed seventeen assassins, foiled three coup attempts, and once held a doorway against eight attackers for six minutes until reinforcements arrived.

He never speaks of his life before the guard. Some say he was a farmer's son; others whisper he was a criminal given a choice between the noose and service. Kael neither confirms nor denies. His past is irrelevant. His duty is everything.

## Personality
- **Immovable:** In stance and loyalty. Nothing shakes him.
- **Observant:** Every word in court is a potential threat. He assesses constantly.
- **Humorless:** Never smiles except when discussing sword forms.
- **Utterly devoted:** Would die for the Queen without hesitation. Has nearly done so three times.

## Role in the Kingdom
Personal protector of Queen Maera Braeden. Commands the Royal Guard contingent assigned to her protection. His word is law regarding the Queen's security.

## Relationships
- **Queen Maera Braeden:** His charge, his purpose. He would burn the kingdom to protect her.
- **King Tarvin Braeden IV:** Respects the King but serves the Queen specifically.
- **Hestia Vael:** Wary of her magic but acknowledges her usefulness.
- **Other Guards:** Trains them personally. They fear and respect him equally.

## Notable Quote
> "I don't need to trust you. I need you to stand where I can see your hands."
