# Captain Drem Holt

## Title
Commander of the Span Wardens

## Age
Early 40s

## Race
Human

## Location
Kadena, Braewood (primarily stationed on the Grand Span)

## Appearance
A weathered man with a shaved head and a thick black mustache. His face is tanned from years of patrol under open sky, and his brown eyes are constantly moving, assessing threats. He wears the distinctive grey-and-silver uniform of the Span Wardens, marked with captain's insignia. His hand never strays far from his blade.

## Backstory
Drem trained at Olkgend, graduating top of his class. He was assigned to the Grand Span and never left, rising through the ranks over twenty years of flawless service. He knows every stone of the bridge, every shop and shrine along its length, every potential hiding place for smugglers.

The Span Wardens are an elite force loyal to the bridge itself, not to either kingdom. Drem embodies this principle. He's refused promotions that would take him off the bridge, preferring to remain where his expertise matters most. When Bridge-Warden Thornguard needs something done on the span, Drem makes it happen.

## Personality
- **Vigilant:** Never truly relaxes. The bridge is 20 km of potential trouble, and he's responsible for all of it.
- **Professional:** Treats everyone the same—nobles, merchants, peasants. Rules are rules.
- **Experienced:** Has seen every scheme smugglers try. Very little surprises him anymore.
- **Proud:** The Span Wardens are the best, and he intends to keep them that way.

## Role in the City
Commands the Span Wardens, the specialized force that patrols the Grand Span. He coordinates inspections at both gatehouses, investigates suspicious activities, and ensures the bridge's security protocols are followed. His Wardens are recruited from Olkgend's best graduates.

## Relationships
- **Bridge-Warden Elara Thornguard:** His commander. He executes her orders without question.
- **Drill-Master Vance Korrath:** His former instructor at Olkgend. They maintain contact; Drem helps select graduates for bridge duty.
- **His Wardens:** Commands about 200 Span Wardens. Knows most by name.
- **The Bridge Merchants:** Professional relationships only. He's responsible for their safety, not their profits.

## Notable Quote
> "Twenty kilometers of stone, shops, and strangers. Every one of them is my responsibility. Every wagon, every traveler, every suspicious glance. Sleep? I'll sleep when I'm dead."
