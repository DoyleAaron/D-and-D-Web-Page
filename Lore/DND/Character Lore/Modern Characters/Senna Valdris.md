## Description

Senna is the family's shield and executioner. Unlike Theron, she is disciplined and tactical, prioritizing Isolde's survival over personal glory.

## Role in Current Arc

- Serves as Isolde's defensive enforcer during assaults on the manor.
- May be held back as reserve unless the household is clearly losing.

## Notable Quote

> "If you came here for justice, bring a shovel."
