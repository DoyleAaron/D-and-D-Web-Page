## Appearance
In his court days, [Ralston](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Prince%20Ralston.md) was handsome in a sharp-featured way—dark hair, calculating eyes, and a smile that never reached them. He dressed ostentatiously, favoring reds and golds that emphasized his royal blood. Now, if the rumors are true, he travels under assumed names, his appearance altered, his finery traded for practical travel clothes.

## Backstory
[Ralston](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Prince%20Ralston.md) was cousin to [King Tarvin Braeden IV](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/King%20Tarvin%20Braeden%20IV.md)—close enough to the throne to covet it, too far to reach it legitimately. Around 1172 C.C., his conspiracy was exposed: he had been meeting secretly with dwarven iron lords, promising them concessions and autonomy in exchange for their support in a coup.

The plot failed. [Ralston](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Prince%20Ralston.md)'s co-conspirators were executed. [Ralston](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Prince%20Ralston.md) himself was exiled—some say because the King couldn't bring himself to execute family, others say because dead martyrs are more dangerous than living embarrassments.

He disappeared into the wider world, taking a band of loyal followers with him. Occasionally, rumors surface: he was seen in [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md), he's building an army in the southern islands, he's made contact with [Lavalto](../../Kingdoms/Lavalto/Lavalto.md)'s vampires. None confirmed. None forgotten.

## Personality
- **Ambitious:** Believed he would be a better king. Still might.
- **Calculating:** The conspiracy was years in the making. He's patient.
- **Bitter:** Exile has curdled whatever charm he once possessed.
- **Dangerous:** A man with nothing to lose and everything to prove.

## Role in History
Symbol of royal betrayal. Reminder that even blood cannot guarantee loyalty. A threat that might return—or might already be working from the shadows.

## Relationships
- **King Tarvin Braeden IV:** His cousin and nemesis. One of them will destroy the other eventually.
- **The Dwarven Iron Lords:** Some may still honor old promises. Most pretend they never knew him.
- **His Followers:** Those who chose exile with him. Their loyalty is absolute—they have nothing else.
- **Braewood:** His home, his obsession, his target.

## Notable Quote
> "The throne belongs to those strong enough to take it. Tarvin took it by birth. I'll take it by right." — Attributed, unconfirmed
