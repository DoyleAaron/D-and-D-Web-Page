## Appearance
A tall, angular man with sharp features and spectacles that catch the light at unfortunate angles. He favors a hex-mesh cloak that shimmers faintly with force-ward patterns and carries an Arc-Cog Staff etched with circuit-like glyphs. His most distinctive companion is [Scrit](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Scrit.md), a hovering brass-and-glass eye familiar that whirs and clicks at his shoulder, iris always recording.

## Backstory
Chris grew up on the administrative fringes of the [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) academies—brilliant, petty, and obsessed with being right. As a junior researcher he advanced by ratting out colleagues: "plagiarized glyphs," "unsafe summoning clearances," "improper reagent custody"—anything to curry favor with inspectors and the High Archon's office.

When the Arcane Synod tightened security after the **Arcane Storm of 1220 C.C.**—those three nights of tearing sky above the western forest near Qrey—Chris "helpfully" compiled incident dossiers on rival mages and delivered them straight to [High Archon Lysanthe Kilmora](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/High%20Archon%20Lysanthe%20Kilmora.md). He framed it as civic duty; everyone else saw the snake. The payoff: a Council seat and access to restricted labs.

### Necromancy → Techmancy
Chris began as a classic necromancer, but Kluimont's rumored brass-dragon research and the Synod's device workshops pushed him from graves to gearboxes. He discovered he could imprint a dying soul's "pattern" into etched sapphire lattices, making "ghost-code" that powers constructs. It's morally gray, fabulously efficient, and exactly the kind of arcana that the Synod quietly monetizes.

He calls himself "the bees' knees." Everyone else calls him "the audit."

## Personality
- **Condescending:** Will correct your sigils mid-cast and then report you for "methodological sloppiness."
- **Bureaucratic snake:** Leaks colleagues' mistakes to the Synod, then offers to "mentor" their apprentices.
- **Irritatingly competent:** He'll fix your ward while lecturing you like you're lucky to hear him talk.
- **Self-serving:** Lawful neutral in the worst way—follows rules that benefit him.

## Role in the Kingdom
Third seat on the **Council of Ten**, [Kluimont's](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) secretive ruling circle of mages. He oversees arcane compliance, containment protocols, and "field audits" of sensitive projects—including the binding wards around [Bagi](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Bagi%20the%20Brass%20Tempest.md).

## Relationships
- **[High Archon Lysanthe Kilmora](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/High%20Archon%20Lysanthe%20Kilmora.md):** His patron. She values his efficiency and ignores his personality.
- **[Scrit](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Scrit.md):** His familiar construct. Obedient, fussy, always recording.
- **[Bagi the Brass Tempest](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Bagi%20the%20Brass%20Tempest.md):** A research subject. Chris studies the binding and dreams of extracting more.
- **Fellow Synod Mages:** They tolerate him. None trust him.
