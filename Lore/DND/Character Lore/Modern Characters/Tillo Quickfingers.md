# Tillo Quickfingers

## Title
Net-Master of Dalo

## Age
Late 30s

## Race
Halfling

## Location
Dalo, Braewood

## Appearance
A wiry halfling man with nimble hands that never stop moving and bright, clever brown eyes. His curly dark hair is perpetually disheveled, and he usually has rope fibers or net threads caught in it. He wears practical seafarer's clothes sized for his small frame, always with multiple pockets for his tools.

## Backstory
Tillo's family has been making nets in Dalo for generations. His grandmother came from Osegas during the rebuilding after the Tharnic collapse, bringing skills that the fishing village desperately needed. Since then, the Quickfingers have been essential to Dalo's survival.

Tillo learned the craft from his father and proved to be the most talented net-maker in family memory. His fingers move with supernatural speed and precision—hence the surname—and his nets are both stronger and lighter than anyone else's. Fishing crews compete to use his work.

## Personality
- **Crafty:** Both in the sense of skilled and clever. Always thinking, always improving his methods.
- **Chatty:** Unlike most craftsmen, he loves to talk while he works. The Net Loft is always filled with conversation.
- **Generous:** Teaches his skills freely. A good net saves lives; keeping secrets would be wrong.
- **Restless:** Can't sit still unless his hands are busy. Even at meals, he's usually mending something.

## Role in the Village
Runs the Net Loft, where fishing nets are made and repaired. He trains apprentices, maintains the village's net inventory, and creates custom nets for specific fishing needs. The Net Loft is also the village's social center during winter months when fishing is slow.

## Relationships
- **Headman Cotter Marsh:** Provides essential equipment for the fishing fleet. Important working relationship.
- **Fishing Crews:** Knows every captain and what they need. His nets are their lifeline.
- **Old Barnacle Gorm:** The elder fisherman often visits the Net Loft. They trade stories and techniques.
- **Halfling Families:** Connected to the halfling community in Osegas through extended family.

## Notable Quote
> "A net isn't just rope tied in knots. It's a trap, a safety line, and a promise. The fish don't get away; the crew doesn't go hungry. My fingers make that promise every day."
