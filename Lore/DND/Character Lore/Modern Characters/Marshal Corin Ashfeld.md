# Marshal Corin Ashfeld

## Title
Marshal of Eaveton, Commander of the Training Grounds

## Age
Early 50s

## Race
Human

## Location
Eaveton, Braewood

## Appearance
A tall, lean man with the weathered look of a career soldier. His face bears several small scars from decades of training accidents and minor skirmishes. Sandy brown hair, now heavily streaked with grey, is kept short. Pale blue eyes are sharp and constantly assessing. He wears practical military attire—leather and chainmail when on the training grounds, simple wool and the Eaveton garrison badge for administrative duties. Moves with the economy of motion common to experienced fighters.

## Backstory
Born to a minor noble family in Eaveton, Corin showed aptitude for military service early. He rose through the garrison ranks on merit, gaining recognition during border patrols and anti-bandit operations along the River Road.

Lord-Regent Greythorne promoted him to Marshal twenty years ago, recognizing both his tactical ability and his absolute reliability. Since then, Corin has transformed the Training Grounds into the kingdom's finest military academy outside the capital. Soldiers trained under his methods serve in garrisons across Braewood.

He has never married, considering the military his family. The soldiers under his command respect him as a demanding but fair commander who never asks more than he gives himself.

## Personality
Disciplined, methodical, and quietly intense. Corin speaks only when necessary and expects his orders to be followed without question. He leads by example, still participating in training exercises despite his age and rank.

Beneath the stoic exterior lies genuine care for his soldiers' welfare. He remembers names, notices when someone struggles, and has been known to personally mentor promising recruits from poor backgrounds.

He has little patience for politics or court intrigue, preferring the clarity of military hierarchy.

## Role
Commands the Eaveton garrison and oversees the Training Grounds. Responsible for:
- Training soldiers for deployment to Mortling Stronghold, Kadena, and border posts
- Defending Eaveton and the surrounding territory
- Coordinating with Lord-Regent Greythorne on military matters
- Maintaining discipline and readiness among the garrison

## Relationships
- **Lord-Regent Hallovar Greythorne:** His commander and closest friend. Decades of trust between them.
- **Commandant Yara Steele (Mortling):** Professional rivalry—they compare training methods and soldier quality.
- **High Chamberlain Aldous Fenn:** Tolerates the administrator's bureaucracy as necessary evil.
- **The recruits:** Treats them firmly but fairly. Many former students serve in high positions across Braewood.

## Notable Quote
*"War doesn't care about your bloodline, your feelings, or your excuses. Only your training and your courage. We can provide the first—the second is on you."*
