## Appearance
A broad, imposing dwarf with copper-red hair and a beard braided with iron rings. Eyes like molten metal, always seeming to burn with intensity. Dresses in expensive dwarven craftsmanship — leather and metal in the old styles. Moves with the deliberate authority of someone who expects to be obeyed. A ceremonial hammer hangs at his belt, more symbol than weapon (though it could serve as both).

## Backstory
[Brogar Flamevein](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Guildmaster%20Brogar%20Flamevein.md) comes from a family that suffered under the Tharnics. His great-grandfather died in the slave mines. His grandfather fought in the War of the Sundered Chains. His father built the family trading business in [Ardismouth](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Ardismouth.md), always believing that success would prove dwarven worth.

It didn't. Humans still treated dwarves as second-class citizens. Crown policies still favored human businesses. The old wounds never healed.

Brogar inherited the family business and the family grievance. He consolidated the scattered dwarven traders into the Consortium, turning economic power into political leverage. He is now the de facto leader of [Ardismouth](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Ardismouth.md)'s dwarven community — and he uses that power to push for autonomy.

He is not a radical. He doesn't want violence. But he believes, deeply and truly, that dwarves will never be treated fairly until they have the power to demand it.

## Personality
- **Charismatic and commanding** — natural leader
- **Politically shrewd** — knows how to apply pressure
- **Genuinely aggrieved** — the anger is real, not performed
- **Pragmatic about methods** — prefers negotiation but will escalate
- **Secretly uncertain** — wonders if he's pushing too hard or not hard enough

## Role in Ardismouth
- Leader of the dwarven merchant community
- Chief spokesman for dwarven interests
- Controls significant trade through the Consortium
- Organizes dwarven workers and traders
- Maintains connections to [Yotherdon](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Yotherdon.md) nationalists

## Relationships
- **Portreeve Garrett Dunholm:** Views him as weak but not hostile; pushes him constantly
- **Captain Mira Blackshore:** Mutual distrust bordering on hatred
- **Elder Mordin Greymantle:** Former ally turned moderate — now conveniently missing
- **Yotherdon dwarves:** Strong connections; coordinates with nationalist movement
- **His followers:** Inspire and are inspired by him; loyalty runs deep

---

*"My great-grandfather's bones lie somewhere in a Tharnic mine. Unmarked. Unknown. The Empire fell, but the humans who inherited it learned nothing. They still treat us as labor, as tools, as problems to be managed. That ends. [Ardismouth](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Ardismouth.md)'s dwarves built half this town. We run a third of its trade. It's time we had a voice in how it's governed. If the Crown won't listen to requests, perhaps they'll listen to demands."*
