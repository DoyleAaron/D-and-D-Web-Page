## Description

**Maera of Silverhill** serves as the linguist and analyst for Braewood's diplomatic mission to Dawerton. Her expertise extends beyond mere translation—she reads people, situations, and hidden meanings with the same precision she applies to foreign languages.

Cold and critical by nature, Maera maintains professional distance from those she works with, reserving particular disdain for those she considers untrustworthy or morally questionable.

## Role

### Linguist
- Translates foreign communications and documents
- Interprets diplomatic nuances and cultural differences
- Ensures accurate understanding between parties

### Analyst
- Assesses political situations and hidden agendas
- Identifies threats and opportunities
- Provides intelligence insights to the delegation

## Personality

Maera is notably reserved and judgmental. She shows:
- **Clear disdain** toward Amdir, remaining cold and critical throughout their interactions
- Professional competence in her duties
- Little patience for those she considers beneath her standards

Her cold demeanor suggests either personal history with dark magic practitioners, or simply a firm moral compass that rejects those who dabble in forbidden arts.

## The Dawerton Mission

As part of the Braewood escort detail, Maera provided:
- Translation services for diplomatic meetings
- Analysis of the Dawerton Council's intentions
- Assessment of political dynamics between Dawerton, Kluimont, and Islefield

## Relationships

- **Envoy Pasan:** Supports his diplomatic work with analysis and translation
- **Amdir Aeristel:** Clear personal animosity; remains cold and critical toward him
- **Sergeant Elira Thorn:** Professional working relationship
- **The Party:** Maintains professional distance from most members

## Notable Quote

> "Words carry weight. Every phrase, every pause, every choice of term reveals intention. My task is to ensure nothing is lost—or hidden—in translation."
