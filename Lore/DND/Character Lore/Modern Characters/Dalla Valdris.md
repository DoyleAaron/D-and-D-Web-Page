## Description

Dalla was turned into the household and never fully embraced it. She follows Isolde's rules in public, but privately carries resentment toward both the family and Maltrius's wider network.

## Role in Current Arc

- Potential informant if given a credible way out.
- Knows movement patterns, hidden routes, and which servants are closest to collapse.

## Notable Quote

> "People call this immortality. Mostly it's bookkeeping and guilt."
