# Elder Mira Fell

## Title
Elder of Ineross (Formerly)

## Age
Mid-60s

## Race
Human

## Location
Ineross, Braewood

## Appearance
**Before:** A weathered but sturdy woman with iron-gray hair and the practical hands of someone who's worked land all her life. Her face carried deep laugh lines, and her voice was strong enough to settle village disputes from across the square.

**Now:** Those who claim to have seen her describe someone... different. The same face, but wrong. Skin too smooth. Movements too fluid. Eyes that don't quite track. Her voice, when she speaks, comes from slightly too deep in her throat.

## Backstory
Mira led Ineross for twenty years. She was beloved—fair, practical, and genuinely invested in her village's wellbeing. When her husband died of fever ten years ago, she threw herself into leadership. When her only son left for Sheyton, she focused on the village that remained.

She was the first to notice the standing stones changing. The first to investigate. The first to stop coming to council meetings.

No one has seen the real Mira Fell in six weeks.

## Personality
**Before:** Warm, practical, fiercely protective of her village.

**Now:** Unknown. Those who interact with "Mira" describe a hollow pleasantness, like a play performance where the actor has forgotten why the lines matter.

## Role in the Settlement
Officially, she still leads the village council. In practice, whatever wears her face serves a different purpose now.

## Relationships
- **The villagers:** They trusted her. Now they obey something wearing her shape.
- **Her son in Sheyton:** Hasn't heard from her in months. Assumes she's just busy.
- **Whatever claimed her:** Unknown.

## Notable Quote
**Before:** "We look after our own in Ineross. Always have, always will."

**Now:** "Everything is fine. Everyone is fine. Stay. Rest. You look tired."
