# Magistra Liriel Dawnweaver

## Title
Magistra (Senior Instructor) of the Arcanist's Lodge

## Age
90s (young adult by elven reckoning)

## Race
Elf (Islefield-born, Kluimont-trained)

## Location
Strane (Medium City), Northern Braewood Peninsula


## Appearance
Tall and slender with the otherworldly grace common to elves. Silver-blonde hair worn in elaborate braids interwoven with thin copper wire — both aesthetic and functional for certain spells. Eyes that shift between pale blue and violet depending on the light (or her mood). Features that are beautiful in an unsettling way, too perfect, too still.

Wears the deep blue robes of Kluimont's arcane tradition, though she's adapted them for the northern cold. Always carries a staff of pale wood with a crystalline focus that pulses faintly with contained energy.

Moves like someone who finds physical space slightly inconvenient.

## Backstory
Born in Islefield to a minor noble family, Liriel showed magical aptitude early. Her family sent her to Kluimont for training — unusual, as elven magic and Kluimont's arcane traditions differ significantly. She thrived in the secretive academies, mastering divination and abjuration.

But Kluimont's rigid hierarchy frustrated her. After fifty years (barely past apprenticeship by their standards), she requested a remote posting. Strane's Arcanist's Lodge — barely a proper institution by Kluimont standards — was her exile and her freedom.

Fifteen years later, she's built the Lodge into something respectable. Not powerful, but competent. And far from Kluimont's politics.

## Personality
- **Brilliant** — genuinely exceptional at magical theory and practice
- **Arrogant** — aware of her brilliance and insufficiently modest about it
- **Isolated** — elven lifespan means most people feel temporary
- **Genuinely curious** — the northern mysteries intrigue her intellectually
- **More loyal than she admits** — Strane has become home despite herself

## Role in Strane
- Leads the Arcanist's Lodge (12 mages of varying ability)
- Provides magical services (identification, wards, consultations)
- Trains promising locals in basic arcane theory
- Advises the Lord-Warden on magical matters
- Maintains the wards on the sealed Tharnic cellars (her most secret duty)

## The Arcanist's Lodge
- Small stone building in the Warden's Quarter
- 12 members: Liriel plus 11 lesser mages
- Focuses on practical magic: wards, identification, minor enchantments
- Has a small library of arcane texts (valuable but not exceptional)
- Officially independent; practically answers to Kluimont and the Lord-Warden

## Relationships
- **Lord-Warden Velthorpe:** Uses each other; neither fully trusts
- **Captain Stonebreaker:** Mutual wariness; respects her incorruptibility
- **Elder Copperkettle:** He knows stories that match her findings
- **Kluimont:** Complicated — they trained her, own her loyalty, frustrate her
- **Her Students:** Fond of them in an abstract way; too temporary to attach to
