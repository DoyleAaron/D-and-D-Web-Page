## Appearance
A weathered man with a soldier's bearing and suspicious eyes. His gray-streaked hair is cropped short in military fashion, and old scars mark his hands and forearms. He dresses simply but always carries a ceremonial short sword at his hip—a reminder that his authority comes backed with steel.

## Backstory
Fenric served as a captain in Lavalto's harbor guard before retiring to politics. His experience with piracy, smuggling, and the occasional supernatural threat made him the obvious choice for the council's security seat. He is one of the few humans in Dawerton who knows the full extent of vampire activity in the city—and has made peace with it, so long as order is maintained.

## Personality
- **Observant:** Watches everyone, especially guards and security personnel. Notices weapons, formations, and threats.
- **Protocol-focused:** Believes in proper procedures and chains of command.
- **Quietly suspicious:** Trusts actions over words; new arrivals must earn his respect.
- **Pragmatic protector:** Will work with anyone—vampire, human, or otherwise—who keeps Dawerton safe.

## Role in the Council
Fenric manages the city watch, harbor defenses, and coordinates with the vampires' own security forces. He ensures that the delicate balance between mortal law and vampiric authority doesn't tip into chaos. When visitors bring "extra muscle," he is the first to notice and assess the threat.

## Relationships

## Notable Quote
> "I don't care what you are. I care what you do. Cause trouble in my city, and you'll learn what I can do."
