# Kira Stoneheart

## Title
Village Delegate Coordinator

## Age
90s (appears middle-aged for a dwarf)

## Race
Dwarf

## Location
Osegas, Braewood

## Appearance
A sturdy dwarf woman with iron-grey hair worn in practical braids and sharp dark eyes that miss nothing. Her face is weathered but kindly, with deep laugh lines around her eyes. She dresses practically in well-made but unadorned clothing, always carrying a leather satchel filled with documents, maps, and correspondence.

## Backstory
Kira was born in Dalo, one of the four villages saved by Osegas during the Tharnic collapse. Her grandmother survived that famine and made sure Kira knew exactly who to thank for her family's existence. That debt shaped her life.

She moved to Osegas as a young adult, determined to serve the town that had saved her people. Her organizational talents quickly became apparent, and she rose to become the official coordinator between Osegas and the four allied villages. For forty years, she's managed the complex web of tribute, trade, delegates, and festivals that binds these communities together.

## Personality
- **Organized:** Keeps meticulous records. Can tell you exactly what Trares sent to last year's festival and what Rens owes for the road repairs.
- **Fair:** Treats all four villages equally, even when their interests conflict.
- **Patient:** Negotiating between five communities requires endless patience. She has it.
- **Loyal:** The debt her family owes Osegas is personal. She will never betray that trust.

## Role in the Town
Manages all communications and coordination between Osegas and the four allied villages (Trares, Dalo, Frore, and Rens). She receives delegates, tracks tribute and trade agreements, organizes joint festivals, and ensures that the Osegas network runs smoothly. She also advises the High Steward on village affairs.

## Relationships
- **High Steward Merylla Davross:** Works closely with her on village matters. Merylla relies on her expertise completely.
- **Village Elders:** Knows all of them personally—Elder Maeven Shawl, Headman Cotter Marsh, Root-Mother Yseult, and Harbor-Elder Brennan Cole.
- **Her Dalo Family:** Still has relatives in her home village. Visits when she can.
- **Tannis Brightwater:** Coordinates food supplies for festivals. They're old friends.

## Notable Quote
> "Five villages, one family. That's what we are. My grandmother was starving until Osegas shared its grain. I will spend my life making sure we never forget what that means."
