# Orna Blackhoof

## Title
Master Farrier of Gitstin

## Age
120s (appears middle-aged for a dwarf)

## Race
Dwarf

## Location
Gitstin, Braewood

## Appearance
A stocky dwarf woman with powerful arms and shoulders developed from decades of smithing work. Her dark hair is braided tightly and pinned close to her head to keep it away from the forge. Her brown eyes are sharp and assessing, and her hands are permanently darkened by soot despite regular washing. She wears a leather apron over practical clothes.

## Backstory
Orna came to Gitstin from Yotherdon fifty years ago, seeking work away from the political tensions of the dwarven republican settlements. She'd trained as a farrier under her mother and grandmother, and Gitstin—with its endless stream of caravan horses—offered more work than she could handle.

She established her shop on Wheelers' Row and built a reputation as the finest horse-shoer in southern Braewood. Merchants specifically request her work, and some delay their journeys to wait for her availability. She's become an institution—"Orna's approval" means a horse is fit for the road.

## Personality
- **Perfectionist:** Accepts nothing less than flawless work from herself. A badly shod horse can go lame, and that's unacceptable.
- **Blunt:** Says what she means without diplomatic softening. Some find it refreshing; others find it rude.
- **Businesslike:** Doesn't gossip, doesn't chat. Talks about horses, shoes, and payment.
- **Quietly kind:** Despite her gruff exterior, she's known to shoe horses for free when families are struggling.

## Role in the Town
Runs the busiest farrier shop in Gitstin. Shoes horses for caravans, examines horses for sale at the weekly market, and trains apprentices in the trade. Her assessment of a horse's fitness is considered definitive—merchants trust her judgment completely.

## Relationships
- **Reeve Arton Fellmark:** Respects him as a former caravan man who understands horses. He consults her on matters affecting livestock trade.
- **The Smiths of Wheelers' Row:** Respected colleague. They send overflow work to each other.
- **Caravan Masters:** Many long-term professional relationships. They book her services weeks in advance.
- **Her Apprentices:** Currently training two—a human and a young dwarf. Demands excellence but teaches fairly.

## Notable Quote
> "A horse is only as good as its feet. Cheat the shoeing, you cheat the horse, you cheat the rider, you cheat everyone down the road who depended on that delivery. I don't cheat."
