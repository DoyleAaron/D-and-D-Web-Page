## Appearance
Still powerfully built despite his age, with a soldier's bearing that never relaxes. His hair has gone completely white, cropped in military fashion, and a scar runs from his left temple to his jaw—a memento from the border campaigns. He dresses in practical training clothes most days, saving his formal armor for inspections and ceremonies.

## Backstory
Cedric earned his knighthood through decades of service on [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)'s borders, fighting [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) raiders and monster incursions alike. His valor was never in question, but it was his discretion that earned him the [Ioxyhull](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Ioxyhull.md) posting.

When the Crown needed someone to oversee the "Capital Training Facility," they chose Cedric—a man who understood that some duties require silence rather than glory. He knows exactly what [Ioxyhull](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Ioxyhull.md) really is: the Queen's escape route if the kingdom ever falls. He trains knights to the highest standard while maintaining a ship always ready to carry the royal family to safety.

He has held this post for fifteen years. He hopes he never has to fulfill its true purpose.

## Personality
- **Disciplined:** Runs [Ioxyhull](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Ioxyhull.md) with military precision. Everything has a schedule, everything has a purpose.
- **Loyal beyond question:** Would die—or kill—without hesitation if the Crown required it.
- **Secretive:** Speaks carefully, reveals nothing of the village's true purpose even under pressure.
- **Patient mentor:** Despite his stern exterior, genuinely cares about developing the knights under his command.

## Role in the Village
Complete authority over [Ioxyhull](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Ioxyhull.md). Oversees knight training, maintains the escape vessel's readiness, and ensures the village's true purpose remains secret.

## Relationships
- **The Crown:** Reports directly to the highest levels. Has met the Queen personally.
- **Harbor-Master Gwen Ashbury:** Manages the ship. They coordinate to ensure constant readiness.
- **Sword-Instructor Daven Rowe:** His senior trainer, handles day-to-day combat instruction.
- **Knights in Training:** He knows most by name, watches their development closely.
- **Foreign Contacts:** Knows the destinations where the Queen could flee—information he will take to his grave.

## Notable Quote
> "Every knight who trains here learns to serve without question. Most never learn why that lesson matters more here than anywhere else."
