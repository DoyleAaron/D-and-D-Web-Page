# Brother Aldric Sunhallow

## Title
Elder Priest of the Temple Mount

## Age
Late 70s

## Race
Human

## Location
Osegas, Braewood

## Appearance
A venerable man with a long white beard and surprisingly sharp blue eyes. His face is deeply lined, his hands gnarled with age, but he moves with purpose and speaks with clarity. He wears the simple brown robes of a sun priest, though the golden sun emblem at his throat is worn smooth from decades of unconscious touching.

## Backstory
Aldric came to Osegas as a young priest fifty years ago, assigned to the sun god's shrine on Temple Mount. He expected a brief posting before returning to grander temples in Braewood City. Instead, he fell in love with Osegas's unique spirit.

The Temple Mount's tradition of interfaith harmony fascinated him. He learned from priests of other faiths, attended their ceremonies, and came to believe that the divine manifests in many forms. This wasn't heresy—it was wisdom. Over the decades, he became the eldest voice on the Council of Faiths, respected by priests of all traditions.

## Personality
- **Devout:** His faith in the divine is absolute, even as his understanding of it has expanded.
- **Ecumenical:** Believes all sincere worship honors the gods. Welcomes every tradition to the Mount.
- **Grandfatherly:** Offers counsel with warmth, not judgment. Has a gift for making people feel heard.
- **Serene:** Has found peace with age, mortality, and the mysteries he'll never solve.

## Role in the Town
Serves as the eldest member of the Council of Faiths, which oversees the Temple Mount and advises the High Steward on spiritual matters. He performs marriages, funerals, and festivals. Young priests of all faiths seek his counsel. His blessing carries weight throughout southern Braewood.

## Relationships
- **High Steward Merylla Davross:** Works closely with her on matters of faith and governance. Deep mutual respect.
- **The Council of Faiths:** Leads without dominating. Other priests see him as first among equals.
- **Father Aldwin Sarre:** The priest in Gitstin. They correspond about interfaith matters.
- **Pilgrims:** Thousands have received his blessing over the decades. Some return year after year.

## Notable Quote
> "I've spent fifty years on this hill, praying beside priests of a dozen different gods. You know what I've learned? The prayers go up to the same sky. What happens after that is above my understanding."
