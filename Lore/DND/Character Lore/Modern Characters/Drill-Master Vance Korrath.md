## Appearance
A grizzled, weathered man with close-cropped iron-grey hair and hard grey eyes that have seen decades of combat and training. His face is scarred in several places, and his left hand is missing—severed at the wrist by a Kluimontian raider's blade thirty years ago. The stump is capped with a leather covering. Despite his age and injury, he moves with the economy of a trained fighter.

## Backstory
[Vance](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md) served on [the Grand Span](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/POI/The%20Grand%20Span.md) for twenty-five years, rising to become one of the most respected Span Wardens of his generation. He lost his hand defending the bridge during a raid, killing the man who took it before collapsing from blood loss.

When he could no longer serve on active duty, he was offered retirement with honors. Instead, he requested reassignment to [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md). If he couldn't guard the bridge himself anymore, he would train those who could. For fifteen years, he has transformed [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md)'s training program into the finest military instruction in the kingdom. Every Span Warden who guards the bridge learned their trade under his watchful eye.

## Personality
- **Demanding:** Accepts nothing less than excellence. Weakness gets people killed.
- **Fair:** His standards are impossibly high, but they're the same for everyone.
- **Respected:** Even those who fail his training respect him. He's earned everything he asks of others.
- **Haunted:** The hand he lost still aches. The comrades he's buried still visit his dreams.

## Role in the Village
Commands [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md)'s entire training operation. He sets standards, designs curriculum, and personally evaluates every graduate. Roughly 40% of trainees wash out—those who remain become the elite Span Wardens. He also serves as the village's de facto leader, though he cares little for administration.

## Relationships
- **Sergeant Major Thessa Vorn:** His second-in-command. She handles the day-to-day instruction he no longer has patience for.
- **Captain Drem Holt:** His former student, now commanding the Span Wardens. Their correspondence is regular.
- **Bridge-Warden Elara Thornguard:** Respects her completely. She's the kind of officer he'd have followed anywhere.
- **Master Smith Grondar Anvilborn:** The dwarf smith who maintains [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md)'s equipment. Essential partnership.

## Notable Quote
> "I lost this hand defending the bridge. Thirty years later, I still feel it. You think you're tired? You think this is hard? Let me tell you about hard."
