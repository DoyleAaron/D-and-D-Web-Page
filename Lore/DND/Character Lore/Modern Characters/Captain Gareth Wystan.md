# Captain Gareth Wystan

## Title
Captain of the Town Watch, Gitstin

## Age
Mid-40s

## Race
Human

## Location
Gitstin, Braewood

## Appearance
A tall, broad-shouldered man with close-cropped grey hair and calm grey eyes. His face is weathered but not unkind, marked by a faded scar across his chin from his soldiering days. He carries himself with military bearing but without stiffness—relaxed but always alert. His uniform is well-maintained but clearly well-used.

## Backstory
Gareth served fifteen years in the Braewood army before mustering out as a sergeant. He'd fought in border skirmishes, trained recruits, and learned that the best way to keep peace is to make trouble more costly than cooperation.

When Gitstin's previous watch captain died suddenly, Gareth applied for the position. His military background impressed the Reeve, but what sealed his appointment was his approach to the job: he told Arton Fellmark that he wanted to prevent fights, not win them. A town that runs on trade needs security that doesn't scare away customers.

## Personality
- **Calm under pressure:** Has seen enough real violence that tavern brawls don't rattle him.
- **Practical:** Prefers solving problems quietly to making dramatic arrests.
- **Fair but firm:** Treats everyone the same under the law—merchants, farmers, and nobles alike.
- **Veteran's wisdom:** Knows when to show force and when to show patience.

## Role in the Town
Commands Gitstin's watch of about 80 guards. Maintains order, investigates crimes, and coordinates security for the busy merchant traffic. His guards patrol the roads entering town and work closely with caravan guards to ensure safe passage.

## Relationships
- **Reeve Arton Fellmark:** His commander. They share a practical, working-class background and mutual respect.
- **Dorinda Kettlebrook:** Coordinates on inn security. She tips him off about troublemakers.
- **Kadena Span Wardens:** Maintains contact with the bridge security. Shared information on criminals and smugglers.
- **His Guards:** Knows every one by name. Trains them to de-escalate before drawing steel.

## Notable Quote
> "My job isn't to catch thieves—though I do that too. My job is to make this town safe enough that honest folk don't worry. Different thing entirely."
