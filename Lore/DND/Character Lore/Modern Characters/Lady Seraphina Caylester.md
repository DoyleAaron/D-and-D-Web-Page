# Lady Seraphina Caylester

## Title
Head of the Pureblood Council

## Age
Unknown (Immortal Vampire; active since 1061 C.C.)

## Race
Vampire (Human origin)

## Location
Darewaeton, Lavalto Isle

## Appearance
A woman of terrible beauty—pale as marble, with dark hair that flows like shadow and eyes that hold centuries of hunger. She dresses in blacks and deep crimsons, favoring styles from eras long past. Her presence commands attention; her gaze commands obedience. She moves with the predatory grace of something that has hunted for longer than most kingdoms have existed.

## Backstory
Seraphina was already ancient when Braewood sent an expedition to purge Lavalto's vampires in 1061 C.C. She led the defense personally, supposedly slaying Knight-Captain Stravus in single combat. The Darewaeton Purge failed, and Seraphina emerged as the island's most feared defender.

Now she leads the Pureblood Council—the inner circle of vampiric nobility that truly rules Lavalto behind the facade of mortal governance. She maintains the island's secrecy, practices rituals that other vampires find disturbing, and ensures that the outside world never learns the full truth of what Lavalto has become.

## Personality
- **Ancient and alien:** Has lived so long that mortal concerns seem... quaint.
- **Ruthless:** Did not survive centuries by showing mercy.
- **Protective of Lavalto:** The island is her domain. She will destroy any threat to it.
- **Mysterious:** Even other vampires don't know her full history or capabilities.

## Role in Lavalto
Head of the Pureblood Council. Ultimate authority on vampiric matters. Her word is law among the undead, and her influence shapes everything on the island.

## Relationships
- **Lord Adrian Silwynn:** Her diplomat. Useful, if too concerned with mortal opinions.
- **Serath Valen:** Her enforcer. Blunt but effective.
- **Father Maltrius Kael:** The infernal priest. She tolerates his presence. Barely.
- **Braewood:** They tried to purge her once. She remembers. She always remembers.

## Notable Quote
> "I was killing before your kingdom had a name. Do not presume to threaten me."
