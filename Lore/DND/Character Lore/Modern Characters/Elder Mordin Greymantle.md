## Appearance
*Last seen:* An elderly dwarf with a long grey beard from which his name derives, braided simply without ornament. Kind brown eyes set in a weathered face. Dressed modestly compared to other dwarven leaders — simple wool and leather. Walked with a slight stoop but moved steadily. Carried a walking staff carved with peaceful symbols.

## Backstory
[Mordin Greymantle](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Elder%20Mordin%20Greymantle.md) has lived in [Ardismouth](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Ardismouth.md) for over a century, long before the current tensions erupted. He remembers when humans and dwarves worked side by side without counting grievances. He remembers when [Ardismouth](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Ardismouth.md) was just a fishing town, before the trade routes made it important and the importance made it contested.

He became an elder through wisdom, not wealth. While other dwarven leaders built fortunes and nursed grudges, Mordin built bridges. He spoke at human festivals. He mediated disputes before they became feuds. He believed, genuinely and perhaps naively, that cooperation was possible.

As [Brogar Flamevein](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Guildmaster%20Brogar%20Flamevein.md)'s nationalist faction grew stronger, Mordin found himself increasingly marginalized. His calls for patience were dismissed as weakness. His attempts at mediation were sabotaged by hardliners on both sides. Two weeks ago, he went for his evening walk and never returned.

## Personality
- **Genuinely peaceful** — believes in reconciliation, not victory
- **Politically naive** — doesn't understand the depth of hatred
- **Stubbornly hopeful** — refuses to abandon his ideals
- **Dangerously moderate** — makes enemies on both sides
- **Lonely** — fewer and fewer listen to him

## Role in Ardismouth (Before Disappearance)
- Voice of moderation in dwarven community
- Informal mediator between factions
- Counselor to Portreeve [Dunholm](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Portreeve%20Garrett%20Dunholm.md)
- Last hope for peaceful resolution

## Disappearance
Mordin vanished ten days ago. The official investigation has found nothing. Theories abound:
- **Flamevein's faction silenced him** — the most common human theory
- **Crown agents kidnapped him** — the most common dwarven theory
- **He fled** — the theory no one wants to consider
- **The road north** — he was last seen walking toward the coastal road

No body has been found. No ransom has been demanded. [Mordin Greymantle](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Elder%20Mordin%20Greymantle.md) simply vanished, and with him, the last chance for peaceful resolution in [Ardismouth](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Ardismouth.md).
