## Appearance
A weather-worn woman with salt-streaked brown hair that she keeps in a severe bun. Her hands are rough from years of rope work, and she walks with the rolling gait of someone who's spent more time on water than land. Her eyes are a striking pale blue—sailor's eyes, they call them. She wears practical seafaring clothes regardless of the occasion.

## Backstory
Gwen served in the Crown Navy for fifteen years before her "retirement" to [Ioxyhull](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Ioxyhull.md). In truth, she was handpicked for the Harbor-Master position because of her unquestionable loyalty, her seamanship, and her ability to keep secrets.

She knows the true purpose of [Ioxyhull](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Ioxyhull.md)—the emergency escape route for the royal family. She has never spoken of this to anyone outside the handful of people cleared to know. Her entire life is dedicated to ensuring that the Crown vessel is always ready, always crewed, and always capable of vanishing over the horizon at a moment's notice.

## Personality
- **Unshakeable:** Nothing fazes her. She's seen storms, battles, and worse. A crisis is just another problem to solve.
- **Secretive:** Keeps the village's true purpose locked in her heart. Even her crew only knows what they need to know.
- **Demanding:** Accepts nothing less than perfection from herself and everyone under her command.
- **Loyal beyond question:** Would die before betraying the Crown or compromising the escape route.

## Role in the Village
Manages Crown Harbor and ensures the escape vessel is always ready for immediate departure. She maintains a permanent crew on rotating standby, handles all ship maintenance and provisioning, and coordinates with Knight-Commander [Hallam](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Knight-Commander%20Cedric%20Hallam.md) on security matters. Every supply shipment is inspected personally.

## Relationships
- **Knight-Commander Cedric Hallam:** Her counterpart on land. They work together seamlessly, each understanding their role in the village's true mission.
- **Sword-Instructor Daven Rowe:** Respects his training work, but their paths rarely cross. She focuses on sea; he focuses on sword.
- **Her Crew:** Handpicked sailors who've sworn absolute loyalty. She's trained each one personally.
- **The Crown:** Has met members of the royal family during inspections. The weight of their lives rests on her readiness.

## Notable Quote
> "When the call comes—and pray it never does—there won't be time for second chances. We will be ready. That's not hope. That's certainty."
