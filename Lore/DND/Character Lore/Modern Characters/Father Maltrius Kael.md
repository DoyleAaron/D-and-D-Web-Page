# Father Maltrius Kael

## Title
Vampiric High Priest of Asmodeus

## Age
Unknown (Immortal Vampire)

## Race
Vampire (Human origin)

## Location
Lavalto Isle (Volcanic Manor)

## Appearance
A tall, imposing figure in dark ecclesiastical robes trimmed with infernal red and gold. His pale skin has an almost marble quality, and his eyes burn with unholy fire. He carries himself with the absolute certainty of one who speaks for a god—even if that god is the Lord of the Nine Hells. His fangs are visible when he smiles, which he does often, especially before violence.

## Backstory
Maltrius was once a mortal priest who sought power beyond what his faith could offer. He found it in Asmodeus, trading his soul and his mortality for infernal gifts. His vampirism came later—a blessing from the Blood Lords of Lavalto who recognized his usefulness.

Now he serves as a bridge between the vampiric nobility and infernal powers. His manor sits atop volcanic vents that he claims connect to the lower planes. Whether this is true or merely theatrical, the rituals he conducts there are undeniably potent—and undeniably evil.

## Personality
- **Absolutely certain:** His faith in Asmodeus is complete and terrifying.
- **Sadistically patient:** Takes pleasure in breaking wills, not just bodies.
- **Theatrically evil:** Every ritual, every sermon, every murder is a performance.
- **Genuinely dangerous:** Behind the pageantry is power that can devastate.

## Role in Lavalto
Serves as spiritual authority for those vampires who follow infernal paths. Conducts rituals, brokers deals with devils, and maintains the volcanic manor that serves as temple and fortress.

## Relationships
- **Lord Adrian Silwynn:** Tolerated as useful. They are not allies, merely co-existing powers.
- **Lady Seraphina Caylester:** Mutual disdain hidden behind politeness.
- **Asmodeus:** His true master. Everything else is secondary.
- **His Followers:** Tools to be used and discarded.

## Notable Quote
> "Hell does not ask for faith. Hell offers contracts. I find the clarity... refreshing."
