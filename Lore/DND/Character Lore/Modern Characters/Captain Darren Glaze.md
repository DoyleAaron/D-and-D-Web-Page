## Appearance
A clean-cut young officer with neatly trimmed brown hair and earnest blue eyes. His uniform is always crisp, his boots polished, his bearing military but not harsh. He looks more like a diplomat than a warrior—which is precisely why he was chosen for his current role. His smile comes easily, and he moves with the confidence of someone who knows his worth.

## Backstory
Darren graduated from [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md) eight years ago and served on [the Grand Span](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/POI/The%20Grand%20Span.md) as a Span Warden. He was competent at the work, but his true talents lay elsewhere—he was the one other Wardens came to when they had problems with assignments, pay, or personality conflicts. He could smooth things over, find compromises, and make people feel heard.

When Bridge-Warden [Thornguard](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Bridge-Warden%20Elara%20Thornguard.md) needed a permanent liaison with the training village, she requested someone with diplomatic skills. Darren was transferred to [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md) with a new mission: coordinate graduate placement, communicate the bridge's needs, and ensure the training program produces what the Span Wardens actually require.

## Personality
- **Diplomatic:** Can frame difficult news in ways that people accept. Rarely creates enemies.
- **Organized:** Tracks every graduate, every assignment, every request. His records are impeccable.
- **Ambitious:** Sees this posting as a stepping stone to higher command. Works hard to distinguish himself.
- **Genuine:** Despite his ambitions, he actually cares about the trainees and their futures.

## Role in the Village
Coordinates between [Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md) and [Kadena](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md). He communicates the Span Wardens' needs to the training staff, advocates for top graduates' assignments, handles administrative matters, and ensures smooth transitions when new Wardens join the bridge force.

## Relationships
- **Drill-Master Vance Korrath:** Respects him enormously but isn't intimidated. That's earned [Korrath](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Drill-Master%20Vance%20Korrath.md)'s approval.
- **Bridge-Warden Elara Thornguard:** His ultimate superior. He reports to her on training matters.
- **Captain Drem Holt:** Works closely with him on Warden assignments. Professional allies.
- **The Graduates:** Advocates for their placement. Many owe their positions to his recommendations.

## Notable Quote
> "[Olkgend](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Olkgend.md) produces the finest warriors in the kingdom. My job is making sure the right warrior ends up in the right position. Get that wrong, and everyone suffers—the bridge, the Wardens, and the graduate."
