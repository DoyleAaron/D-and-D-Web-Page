## Description

**Aric "Ash" Vell** serves as the rogue skirmisher and intelligence asset for the Braewood escort detail. Operating in the shadows between formal military structure and covert operations, Aric handles the tasks that require a lighter touch—or a sharper blade.

His quick wit and irreverent attitude mask genuine skill in infiltration, information gathering, and combat. Those who dismiss him as merely a joker often discover their mistake too late.

## Role

### Rogue Skirmisher
- Light combat support
- Flanking and ambush tactics
- Target elimination when necessary

### Intelligence Asset
- Covert information gathering
- Infiltration of hostile locations
- Assessment of threats and opportunities
- Shadow operations

## Personality

Aric presents himself as casual and humorous, bonding quickly with those who share his attitudes. He particularly connected with:
- **Amdir Aeristel:** The two bonded early over shared attitudes and humor, forming a rapport that persisted throughout the mission

Despite his light demeanor, Aric is a capable professional who takes his actual duties seriously—he simply sees no reason to be grim about it.

## The Dawerton Mission

As part of the escort detail, Aric provided:
- Intelligence support for the diplomatic mission
- Skirmish capabilities during potential conflicts
- Information gathering in Dawerton's shadier districts

### Session 8 (1225 C.C.)
During the sea voyage to Dawerton, Aric participated in a friendly hand-to-hand spar with Morjor. Though he lost the bout, the exchange earned Morjor quiet respect from the rogue—professionals recognize skill when they see it.

### Session 14 (1225 C.C.)
Following the completion of the mission, Aric departed with Elira, Bram, and Selene, their assignment concluded.

## Relationships

- **Amdir Aeristel:** Fast friends; bonded over shared humor and attitudes
- **Morjor the Enlightened:** Respects his combat skill after their spar
- **Sergeant Elira Thorn:** Follows her command while operating semi-independently
- **Corporal Bram Stone:** Fellow scout with complementary skills
