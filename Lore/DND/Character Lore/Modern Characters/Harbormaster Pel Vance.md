## Appearance
A round-faced man with thinning grey hair and an easy smile that never quite reaches his eyes. Dresses like a prosperous merchant — good cloth, practical cut. Hands soft for a dockworker, ink-stained from ledgers. Walks with a deliberate pace, always seeming to have time for conversation. A heavy signet ring on his right hand, unmarked but clearly valuable.

## Backstory
No one knows where [Pel Vance](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md) came from. He appeared in [Phine](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Phine.md) thirty years ago with enough money to buy a small warehouse and enough connections to fill it. Within a decade he controlled a third of the dock traffic. When the previous harbormaster died (natural causes, probably), [Vance](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md) was the obvious choice for replacement.

The truth, known to very few: [Vance](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md) was originally an agent for the Masked Lanterns of [Barge](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md). He was sent to establish smuggling networks in [Phine](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Phine.md) and did so brilliantly. Over time, his loyalty shifted from the Lanterns to himself. He still maintains connections, but [Pel Vance](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md) serves [Pel Vance](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md).

He knows every smuggler, every corrupt inspector, every hidden cargo route through the docks. He allows what benefits him and stops what threatens him. The Crown suspects. They can't prove anything.

## Personality
- **Affable and disarming** — everyone likes [Pel Vance](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md)
- **Utterly pragmatic** — morality is a luxury he abandoned long ago
- **Carefully generous** — buys loyalty with well-placed favors
- **Survival-focused** — will sacrifice anyone to protect himself
- **Genuinely loves Phine** — in his own corrupt way

## Role in Phine
- Manages both the river and ocean docks
- Coordinates customs inspections (and their failures)
- Maintains smuggling networks (discreetly)
- Provides intelligence to whoever pays (carefully)
- Serves as unofficial banker for illicit funds

## Relationships
- **Lord-Governor Callum Westmarch:** Mutual understanding; [Westmarch](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Governor%20Callum%20Westmarch.md) allows limited smuggling in exchange for stability
- **Bridge-Commander Serena Coldwell:** His nemesis; she suspects everything and can prove nothing
- **Merchant-Prince Yoren Salk:** Business partner in legitimate trade; rival in illegitimate trade
- **The Masked Lanterns (Barge):** Former employers; current contacts; complicated
- **Kluimontian agents:** Occasional customers; carefully managed
