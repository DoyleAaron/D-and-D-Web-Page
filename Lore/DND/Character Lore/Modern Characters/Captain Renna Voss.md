# Captain Renna Voss

## Title
Captain, Commander of the Nightwatch

## Age
Early 30s

## Race
Human

## Location
Mortling Stronghold, Braewood

## Appearance
Athletic and sharp-featured, with close-cropped auburn hair and a faint constellation of freckles across her nose that she hates being mentioned. She wears the dark leather armor of the Nightwatch rather than standard garrison plate—built for silence, not protection. A pair of daggers hang at her belt alongside her short sword. Her eyes are always moving, always watching.

## Backstory
Renna grew up in Mortling, the daughter of a garrison sergeant who died in a border skirmish when she was twelve. She enlisted at sixteen, determined to serve where her father had served. Her talent for stealth and unconventional thinking caught Commandant Steele's attention; her first mission beyond the walls—a solo reconnaissance that identified a Kluimont staging point—caught Lord-Marshal Ironward's.

At twenty-eight, she was given command of the Nightwatch, Mortling's elite scout and infiltration unit. They operate beyond the walls, gathering intelligence, disrupting enemy movements, and occasionally making problems disappear quietly.

## Personality
- **Intensely focused:** When she has a mission, everything else fades away.
- **Ambitious:** Wants to prove herself worthy of higher command—and knows she's not there yet.
- **Loyal to her unit:** The Nightwatch are her family. She'd die for any of them.
- **Secretly uncertain:** Worries she was promoted too fast; compensates with relentless preparation.

## Role in the Settlement
Commands Mortling's premier reconnaissance and special operations unit. Reports directly to Lord-Marshal Ironward on matters of intelligence and border security.

## Relationships
- **Lord-Marshal Ironward:** Her mentor and commander. She desperately wants his approval but fears disappointing him.
- **Commandant Steele:** First noticed her potential; still provides occasional guidance.
- **The Nightwatch:** Twelve hand-picked soldiers who trust her implicitly.
- **Kluimont:** She's crossed the border six times. They may not know her name yet, but they know someone is watching them.

## Notable Quote
> "The best victories are the ones they never see coming. By the time they know we're there, we're already gone."
