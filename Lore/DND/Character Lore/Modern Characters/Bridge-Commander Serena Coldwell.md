## Appearance
A compact, muscular woman with close-cropped brown hair and sharp green eyes. Carries herself with military precision. A long scar runs down her right forearm — a souvenir from a border skirmish. Wears her armor even at formal occasions, polished to a mirror shine. Never seen without her sword.

## Backstory
Born to a military family in [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) City. Her father and grandfather both served in the Crown's armies; she never considered another path. Rose through the ranks quickly, demonstrating both tactical skill and the ability to command respect from soldiers twice her age.

The Westbridge posting is both honor and exile. Honor because the bridge is one of the most strategically important points in the kingdom. Exile because Serena made enemies in the capital — she exposed a supply officer's corruption and embarrassed several nobles in the process. They couldn't punish her openly, so they sent her somewhere her talents would be useful but her presence would be distant.

She's been at [Phine](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Phine.md) for eight years. She no longer cares about returning to the capital. The bridge is hers.

## Personality
- **Fiercely professional** — the garrison runs like clockwork
- **Distrustful of politicians** — including Lord-Governor [Westmarch](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Governor%20Callum%20Westmarch.md)
- **Protective of her soldiers** — will fight any bureaucrat who threatens them
- **Secretly frustrated** — suspects smuggling she can't prove
- **Loyal to Braewood, not the Crown specifically** — subtle but important distinction

## Role in Phine
- Commands the 2,000-soldier Westbridge garrison
- Responsible for bridge security and border defense
- Leads river patrols against smugglers
- Can seal the bridge independently in emergencies
- Reports to Crown military command, not the Lord-Governor

## Relationships
- **Lord-Governor Callum Westmarch:** Respects his competence; resents his tolerance of smuggling
- **Harbormaster Pel Vance:** Suspects him deeply; can't prove anything
- **Consul Alistair Bryne:** Professional cooperation on border incidents
- **Her soldiers:** Earned their loyalty through shared hardship
