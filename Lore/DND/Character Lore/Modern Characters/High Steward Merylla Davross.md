## Appearance
A graceful half-elf woman with silver-streaked auburn hair that she wears loose to her shoulders. Her eyes are a striking amber color, warm and perceptive. Fine lines around her eyes and mouth suggest wisdom rather than age. She dresses in flowing robes of earth tones, often adorned with symbols from multiple faiths—a deliberate statement of her inclusive leadership.

## Backstory
Merylla was born in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) to an elven trader and a human farmer's daughter. Growing up as a half-elf in [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) was not easy, but [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md)'s unique culture of acceptance made it bearable. She learned early that her perspective—caught between two worlds—could be a strength rather than a weakness.

She rose through local politics not through ambition but through service. When floods damaged the southern farmsteads, she organized relief. When religious disputes threatened the peace, she mediated. When the previous High Steward died without a clear successor, the Council of Faiths and the town's elders unanimously asked her to serve.

That was thirty years ago. Under her leadership, [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) has become even more of what it always was—a beacon of tolerance and mutual aid in a kingdom not known for either.

## Personality
- **Wise:** Sees multiple sides of every issue. Rarely makes hasty judgments.
- **Diplomatic:** Can find common ground between parties who thought they had none.
- **Compassionate:** Genuinely cares about every resident, from the wealthiest merchant to the poorest farmer.
- **Patient:** Half-elven lifespan has taught her that most problems look different with time.

## Role in the Town
Leads [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) as both secular ruler and chair of the Council of Faiths. She represents the town to the Crown, mediates between the allied villages, and maintains the traditions of mutual aid that define [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md)'s identity. Her word settles disputes that have divided families for generations.

## Relationships
- **Brother Aldric Sunhallow:** The eldest priest on the Council of Faiths. They've worked together for decades.
- **The Four Villages:** Speaks for [Trares](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Trares.md), [Dalo](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Dalo.md), [Frore](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Frore.md), and [Rens](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Village/Rens.md) in dealings with the Crown. They trust her completely.
- **Crown Officials:** Navigates the Crown's occasional discomfort with [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md)'s independence with diplomatic skill.
- **Tannis Brightwater:** The halfling innkeeper who feeds the hungry. Merylla values her practical compassion.

## Notable Quote
> "[Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) saved four villages by sharing what we had when we could have kept it for ourselves. That choice made us who we are. Every day, we choose to be that people again."
