## Appearance
A lean, weathered man with salt-and-pepper hair and observant brown eyes. His face is unremarkable—the kind you'd forget in a crowd—which suits him perfectly. He dresses in plain clothes rather than uniform, preferring to blend in with the townspeople he protects. Only the sword at his hip marks him as more than an ordinary citizen.

## Backstory
Horace came to [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) twenty years ago as a young soldier assigned to "observe" the town's unusual governance. The Crown had concerns about [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md)'s independence, and Horace was supposed to report on potential sedition.

Instead, he fell in love with the place. The openness, the genuine community, the way people helped each other without being asked—it was nothing like the suspicious, competitive cities he'd known. When his assignment ended, he requested permanent posting. Then he resigned his Crown commission entirely and joined the town guard.

He rose to captain not through ambition but through competence. He knows every resident by name, every street and alley, every family's troubles and triumphs. He's a peacekeeper, not a soldier—which is exactly what [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md) needs.

## Personality
- **Observant:** Notices everything. Can spot tension before it becomes conflict.
- **Quiet:** Speaks rarely, listens always. When he does speak, people pay attention.
- **Just:** Applies the same standards to everyone. Has arrested merchants' sons and beggars alike.
- **Protective:** Genuinely loves [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md). Would die to protect it, though he'd prefer not to.

## Role in the Town
Commands [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md)'s small guard force of about forty peacekeepers. They maintain order, help travelers, and assist with festivals and events. Serious crime is rare—most of his work involves mediating disputes and preventing small problems from becoming large ones.

## Relationships
- **High Steward Merylla Davross:** Reports to her. She trusts his judgment; he trusts her wisdom.
- **The Town Guard:** Knows each one personally. They're peacekeepers, not soldiers, and he trains them accordingly.
- **Brother Aldric Sunhallow:** Consults with him on matters involving pilgrims or religious disputes.
- **The Visiting Delegates:** Keeps an eye on visitors from the four villages and the Crown, ensuring all goes smoothly.

## Notable Quote
> "I've been in towns where guards are feared. Here, children wave at me. Which kind of place do you think has less crime? I rest my case."
