## Description

**Corporal Bram Stone** serves as the ranger scout for the Braewood escort detail. His skills in tracking, reconnaissance, and survival make him invaluable for navigating unfamiliar territory and identifying threats before they become problems.

Practical and reliable, Bram represents the capable professional soldiers that form the backbone of Braewood's military strength.

## Role

### Ranger Scout
- Advance reconnaissance of routes and locations
- Tracking and navigation
- Threat identification
- Survival expertise

### Street-Level Operations
In urban environments like Dawerton, Bram adapts his skills to:
- Local scouting
- Street-level credibility
- Information gathering
- Additional protection

## The Dawerton Mission

Bram served under Sergeant Elira Thorn as part of the escort detail. During the mission, he provided:
- Scouting during the overland journey from Braewood to Mortling
- Navigation support during the sea voyage to Dawerton
- Local reconnaissance in Dawerton

### Session 12 (1225 C.C.)
When the party began investigating disturbances in Dawerton, they recruited Bram to assist. He provided:
- Local scouting capabilities
- Street-level credibility with Dawerton citizens
- Additional protection as threats escalated

### Session 14 (1225 C.C.)
Following the completion of the mission, Bram departed with the rest of the escort team, his duties fulfilled.
