# Isolde Merrick

## Title
Fleet-Captain of the Adaham Elite Sailors

## Age
Early 30s

## Race
Human

## Location
Adaham (Village), River Patrol Command


## Appearance
A young woman with sun-darkened skin and hair the color of wet sand, usually pulled back in a practical braid. Sharp grey eyes that miss nothing on the water. Athletic build hardened by years of river work. Dresses in naval uniform when on duty, simple practical clothing otherwise. A compass tattoo on her left wrist — a sailor's tradition.

## Backstory
Isolde grew up on the rivers, daughter of a barge captain who ran goods between Mortling and the interior. She could navigate by the age of twelve, command a crew by sixteen. The Crown recruited her at nineteen for her exceptional skills.

She rose quickly through the river navy, earning a reputation for speed and initiative. When the elite sailor program in Adaham needed a field commander, she was the obvious choice. At thirty-two, she commands the fastest crews in Braewood.

Isolde is hungry. Not for wealth or power, but for recognition. She believes the Adaham sailors are wasted in this backwater and lobbies constantly for more active assignments. Harbor-Warden Ravencroft restrains her more aggressive impulses, but she chafes at the limitation.

## Personality
- **Ambitious and driven** — wants to prove herself and her sailors
- **Tactically brilliant** — can outmaneuver any ship on the river
- **Impatient with bureaucracy** — action over paperwork
- **Fiercely loyal to her crews** — will fight anyone who disrespects them
- **Slightly reckless** — sometimes moves faster than wisdom allows

## Role in Adaham
- Commands the elite sailor crews directly
- Plans and executes rapid response operations
- Trains new recruits to elite standards
- Maintains the response vessels in peak condition
- Advocates for the garrison's interests with naval command

## Relationships
- **Harbor-Warden Colm Ravencroft:** Mentor and restraint; respects him deeply even when she disagrees
- **Her crews:** Would die for them; they would die for her
- **Crown Naval Command:** Frustrated by their neglect of Adaham's potential
- **Old Guildmaster Tomas Ashwell:** Likes the old man; listens to his stories about Adaham's potential

---

*"Give me two hours' warning and I can have boats at any point on the river network. Mortling, Phine, the border — anywhere. That's not a boast, that's a fact. The Adaham sailors are the fastest in Braewood. We're wasted here watching empty docks rot. Put us where we're needed and we'll show you what we can do."*
