# Lady Vera Silke

## Title
Merchant (Official), Masked Lanterns Representative (Unofficial)

## Age
Early 40s

## Race
Human

## Location
Barge (Medium City), Central-Northern Braewood


## Appearance
Elegant in a carefully calculated way. Auburn hair worn in elaborate styles that are fashionable but never impractical. Green eyes that sparkle with warmth that doesn't quite reach their depths. A face that's beautiful in the way that makes you trust her — which should make you suspicious.

Dresses in expensive silks and fine jewelry, the picture of a successful merchant. Her hands are soft, uncalloused — the hands of someone who's never done physical labor. Moves with the grace of a dancer.

Her smile is her most dangerous weapon.

## Backstory
No one knows where Vera Silke came from. She appeared in Barge fifteen years ago with capital, connections, and an import business that was immediately profitable. Within five years, she was one of the city's most respected merchants.

Within ten, everyone knew she spoke for the Masked Lanterns.

How she rose to represent the thieves guild is unclear. Some say she founded it. Some say she killed her way up. Some say the previous leadership simply recognized talent. She doesn't clarify.

What matters is that when the Lanterns need to negotiate with the legitimate world, they send her.

## Personality
- **Charming** — disarmingly personable; everyone likes her
- **Ruthless** — the charm is a tool; she'll use whatever works
- **Intelligent** — sees patterns others miss; plans accordingly
- **Controlled** — never loses her temper, never shows weakness
- **Curious** — genuinely interested in information, not just profit

## Official Role
- Runs a successful import/export business
- Member of the Merchant Consortium
- Attends civic functions and charity events
- Maintains a beautiful townhouse in the Market Quarter
- Known philanthropist (genuinely; confused the authorities)

## Unofficial Role
- Voice of the Masked Lanterns in legitimate circles
- Negotiator with Magistrate Hallas
- Manager of the guild's "clean" investments
- Intelligence gatherer (everyone talks to a charming merchant)
- The person you see when you want to hire the Lanterns

## Relationships
- **Magistrate Hallas:** They have an understanding; mutual convenience
- **Guildmaster Ironhand:** Complicated business arrangement
- **Commander Ashworth:** Actively avoids her; won't cross her
- **Her Kluimontian Contacts:** Valuable; mysterious
- **The Lanterns:** She speaks for them; does she control them?
