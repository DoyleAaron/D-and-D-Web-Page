## Appearance
A thin, precise man with thinning grey hair and spectacles perched on a sharp nose. His clothing is always immaculate but never ostentatious—dark wool robes with the chamberlain's chain of office his only adornment. Ink-stained fingers betray his constant work with documents. His posture is slightly stooped from decades hunched over ledgers. Moves quietly and efficiently, as if noise and wasted motion were personal failings.

## Backstory
Born to a family of minor clerks in [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md), Aldous showed exceptional aptitude for numbers and administration from a young age. He entered city service at sixteen and spent four decades climbing the bureaucratic ladder through sheer competence and reliability.

Lord-Regent [Greythorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Regent%20Hallovar%20Greythorne.md) elevated him to High Chamberlain fifteen years ago, recognizing that the city's growing complexity required an exceptional administrator. Aldous has never disappointed. Under his management, tax collection runs smoothly, infrastructure is maintained efficiently, and the vast machinery of city governance operates without friction.

He has never married, considering his work his life's purpose. Few know that he maintains a small garden behind his quarters—his only hobby and the one place he allows himself to be inefficient.

## Personality
Meticulous, patient, and utterly devoted to proper procedure. Aldous believes that good administration is the foundation of civilization. He takes genuine satisfaction in balanced ledgers, organized records, and smoothly functioning systems.

He's not cold—merely focused. He cares deeply about the city's welfare but expresses that care through competent management rather than emotional displays. He has quiet contempt for corruption and inefficiency.

Surprisingly dry-humored in private, though few see that side of him.

## Role
Manages [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md)'s vast bureaucracy:
- Treasury and tax collection
- Infrastructure maintenance (roads, bridges, walls, sewers)
- City records and archives
- Coordination between various departments and offices
- Reports directly to Lord-Regent [Greythorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Regent%20Hallovar%20Greythorne.md)

He's the invisible machinery that keeps [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md) functioning.

## Relationships
- **Lord-Regent Hallovar Greythorne:** Loyal subordinate. [Greythorne](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Regent%20Hallovar%20Greythorne.md) trusts his administration completely.
- **Guildmistress Petra Thornwood:** Professional cooperation on tax and trade matters. Mutual respect for competence.
- **Marshal Corin Ashfeld:** Handles garrison funding and logistics. The Marshal finds paperwork tedious but appreciates Aldous's efficiency.
- **The clerks and bureaucrats:** A quiet mentor to those who show dedication. Intolerant of laziness or dishonesty.

## Notable Quote
*"The lords make policy, the soldiers keep order, the merchants move wealth—but without administration, none of it would function for a week. I keep this city running, and I take pride in that."*
