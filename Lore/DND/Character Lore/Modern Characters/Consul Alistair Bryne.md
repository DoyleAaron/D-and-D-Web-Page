# Alistair Bryne

## Overview

---

## Appearance
A slender man with grey-streaked auburn hair and pale blue eyes. His face is carefully neutral, trained by decades of diplomacy to reveal nothing. Dresses in formal Crown attire when on duty, simpler clothes when off. Speaks multiple languages fluently, including Kluimontian dialects. Moves quietly, a habit from years of court intrigue.

## Backstory
Born to a minor noble family with a tradition of Crown service. Showed early aptitude for languages and was pushed toward the diplomatic corps. Spent twenty years in various postings — [Islefield](../../Kingdoms/Islefield/Islefield.md), [Lavalto](../../Kingdoms/Lavalto/Lavalto.md), even a brief stint in [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) City itself — before being assigned to [Phine](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Phine.md) as permanent consul.

The consul's role is exhausting: constant negotiation over trade disputes, border incidents, smuggling accusations, and the occasional murder. Alistair has done it for fifteen years. He knows his Kluimontian counterparts by name, knows their children's names, knows their secrets. They know his.

The recent murder of a Kluimontian diplomat on [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)'s side of the bridge has tested all his skills. War seems unlikely but not impossible. Alistair sleeps poorly these days.

## Personality
- **Endlessly diplomatic** — can find common ground with anyone
- **Secretly exhausted** — the job has worn him down
- **Genuinely curious about Kluimont** — understands them better than most Braewoodians
- **Loyal to peace** — believes war would destroy everything
- **Quietly devout** — prays more than he admits

## Role in Phine
- Primary negotiator with [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) officials
- Manages cross-bridge incidents and disputes
- Gathers intelligence through diplomatic channels
- Advises Lord-Governor on Kluimontian affairs
- Maintains back-channel communications during crises

## Relationships
- **Lord-Governor Callum Westmarch:** His superior; trusts [Westmarch](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Governor%20Callum%20Westmarch.md)'s judgment
- **Bridge-Commander Serena Coldwell:** Coordination on border incidents; she finds diplomacy frustrating
- **Kluimontian Consul (across the bridge):** Complex relationship of rivalry and respect
- **His wife Elara:** Married thirty years; she manages their household while he manages kingdoms

---

*"The bridge is sixty meters of stone. On one side, [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md). On the other, [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md). My job is to keep people walking across it instead of marching across it. Most days, that means paperwork and patience. Some days, it means talking two angry men down from a sword fight. I prefer the paperwork days."*
