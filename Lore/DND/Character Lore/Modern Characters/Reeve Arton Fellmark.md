## Appearance
A solidly built man with a weathered face, calloused hands, and the permanent tan of someone who spent decades on the trade roads. His brown hair is streaked with grey, and deep lines crinkle around his warm brown eyes when he smiles—which is often. He dresses practically, favoring sturdy boots and plain tunics even for official business.

## Backstory
Arton began his working life at fourteen as a caravan guard, protecting merchant wagons on the routes between [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md) and [Kadena](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md). Over thirty years, he rose through the ranks—guard, wagon master, caravan captain—learning every road, every inn, and every danger in southern [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md).

When he finally retired from the roads at forty-five, he settled in [Gitstin](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md)—the town he'd passed through more times than he could count. The townspeople, who'd known him for decades as an honest and capable man, elected him Reeve within two years. He accepted because someone had to do it, and he figured he might as well do it properly.

## Personality
- **Pragmatic:** Sees problems as things to be solved, not complained about. Always asks "what can we actually do?"
- **Fair-minded:** Treats merchants, farmers, and travelers with equal respect. Has no patience for those who put on airs.
- **Approachable:** Genuinely likes people and remembers their names, their problems, their children.
- **Road-wise:** Thirty years of travel taught him to read people, anticipate trouble, and stay calm under pressure.

## Role in the Town
Governs [Gitstin](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md) from [Fellmark](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Reeve%20Arton%20Fellmark.md)'s Hall, mediating disputes, coordinating with the Crown's tax collectors, and ensuring the roads stay safe for trade. He holds weekly open courts where anyone can bring grievances. His decisions are respected because everyone knows he's been in their boots.

## Relationships
- **Dorinda Kettlebrook:** The halfling innkeeper who coordinates [Gitstin](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md)'s social life. They consult regularly.
- **Captain Gareth Wystan:** His head of the town watch. Arton trusts him to handle security while he handles politics.
- **Father Aldwin Sarre:** The priest at Crossroads Shrine. Arton values his counsel on matters of conscience.
- **Orna Blackhoof:** The dwarf master farrier. Arton respects craftsmen who know their trade.
- **Lord-Regent Hallovar Greythorne:** The governor of [Eaveton](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Large%20Cities/Eaveton.md). Arton reports to him but maintains local independence.

## Notable Quote
> "I've walked every road from here to [Kadena](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md), and I've learned one thing: treat people fair, keep your word, and don't borrow trouble. Works for caravans, works for towns."
