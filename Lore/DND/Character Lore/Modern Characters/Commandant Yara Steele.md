# Commandant Yara Steele

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Commandant of the War College of Mortling |
| **Age** | Mid-40s |
| **Race** | Human |
| **Location** | Mortling Stronghold, Braewood |

---

## Appearance
Tall and lean, with the build of someone who still trains alongside her students. Her dark hair is pulled back in a severe bun, and a thin scar runs from her left temple to her jaw—a [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) blade, they say. She wears the same cadet uniform as her students, deliberately stripped of rank insignia to emphasize that at the War College, results matter more than titles.

## Backstory
Yara rose through Mortling's ranks as a field officer, distinguishing herself in skirmishes along the [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) border. Her most famous moment—or infamous, depending on who tells it—came when she killed a [Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) spy in Lord-Marshal [Ironward](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Marshal%20Caelric%20Ironward.md)'s own command tent. With her bare hands, before he could reach the map table. [Ironward](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Marshal%20Caelric%20Ironward.md) promoted her to Commandant the next day.

She has run the War College for eight years, transforming it from a finishing school for nobles into a forge for officers who will actually survive their first battle.

## Personality
- **Iron discipline:** Expects perfection; accepts improvement.
- **Brutally honest:** Will tell a noble's son he's useless to his face—and then show him how to be useful.
- **Obsessed with readiness:** Every lesson connects to survival; no theory without practice.
- **Hidden kindness:** Keeps a small fund to support orphaned cadets from common families.

## Role in the Settlement
Runs [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)'s premier military academy. All officers who graduate from Mortling bear her stamp—her methods, her standards, her philosophy. She shapes the kingdom's military future.

## Relationships
- **Lord-Marshal Ironward:** Deep mutual respect. He gave her purpose after her field career ended; she gives him officers who won't fail.
- **Cadets:** Feared, admired, and ultimately thanked. Many graduates credit her with saving their lives.
- **Kluimont operatives:** She's killed three spies personally. They know her name across the border.

## Notable Quote
> "Every training scar you earn here is a death you won't suffer out there. Now get up and do it again."
