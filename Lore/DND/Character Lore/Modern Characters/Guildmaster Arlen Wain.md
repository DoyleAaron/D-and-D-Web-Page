# Guildmaster Arlen Wain

## Title
Guildmaster of the Carterhouse

## Age
Early 60s

## Race
Human

## Location
Sheyton, Braewood

## Appearance
A heavyset man with a merchant's shrewd eyes and a laborer's hands. His hair is white now, his face lined and weathered, but there's still strength in his grip and sharpness in his gaze. He dresses in practical but expensive clothing—quality fabric, practical cut. A thick gold chain around his neck marks his guild rank.

## Backstory
Arlen built his fortune the hard way, starting as a carter's assistant and working every job in the transport trade until he knew it inside and out. He's been Guildmaster of the Carterhouse for fifteen years, controlling most commercial transport in southwestern Braewood.

He's watched the Red Scarves grow with mounting alarm. They're organized, they're growing, and they're targeting his workers. Three of his best carters have joined them. Two more have been robbed. He doesn't believe in coincidences.

## Personality
- **Blunt and direct:** No patience for politics or pleasantries.
- **Protective of his guild:** The Carterhouse is his life's work. He'll fight for it.
- **Genuinely worried:** The Red Scarves aren't just competition—they're something worse.
- **Old-fashioned honor:** Keeps his word, pays his debts, expects the same from others.

## Role in the Settlement
Controls the Carterhouse, which manages most commercial transport through Sheyton. His cooperation—or opposition—can make or break trade in the region.

## Relationships
- **Lord-Mayor Vellan Harcrow:** Mutual distrust. Arlen thinks Harcrow is a bureaucrat who doesn't understand real work.
- **The Red Scarves:** Open opposition. He's been the most vocal critic of Mother Selyse and her movement.
- **Guildmaster Orvyn Cartwright (Cleamond):** Rivals but respectful. They control different sections of the same trade network.

## Notable Quote
> "I've been moving goods for forty years. I know every road, every trick, every danger. And I'm telling you—those red scarves aren't what they seem."
