# Greta Borugh

## Title
Administrator of Borugham

## Age
150s

## Race
Dwarf

## Location
Borugham (Town), Northern Braewood


## Appearance
Shares her brother's broad build and solid presence. Iron-grey hair worn in intricate braids — the traditional style of Borugh women. Sharp blue eyes — the Borugh eyes — warmer than Aldric's. Her face shows the same hard dwarven angles, softened by laugh lines and centuries of pragmatic patience.

Dresses well but practically, always in the dark Borugh colors. Wears her sigil ring on her left hand and a chain of office that signifies her administrative role. Moves efficiently, like someone who has too much to do and not enough time.

Looks like a dwarf who carries the weight of history without being crushed by it.

## Backstory
Greta was born thirty years after Aldric, which meant everything was already decided: he would rule, she would administer. In a different family, this might have bred resentment. Instead, Greta found purpose in it.

She handles Borugham's logistics — trade negotiations with Barge, tax calculations, supply management, the thousand tedious details that keep a settlement functioning. This gives her contact with the outside world that Aldric prefers to avoid.

She's traveled to Barge. She's met Crown officials. She's seen how other dwarven communities — and human ones — handle the balance between autonomy and authority. She believes Borugham's moderate path is correct, but she also knows they must adapt carefully to survive.

## Personality
- **Pragmatic** — focused on what works, not what feels righteous
- **Diplomatic** — has learned to navigate relationships with humans and Crown officials
- **Thoughtful** — considers the long view, measured in dwarven lifetimes
- **Loyal** — loves her brother and supports his leadership
- **Quietly observant** — sees more than she says; knows the cracks in every position

## Role in Borugham
- Chief administrator and logistics manager
- Primary negotiator with Barge merchants
- Tax collector and recordkeeper
- The person who actually makes the town function
- Unofficial ambassador to the human world

## Relationships
- **Aldric Borugh:** Loves her brother; they work as a team
- **Hagen Wheelock:** Genuinely friendly; appreciates his role bridging the community
- **Barge Merchants:** Professional relationships; her connections to the outside
- **Yotherdon Contacts:** Maintains quiet communication; knows Borugham may need allies
- **The Family Legacy:** Carries it differently than Aldric — less weight, more responsibility

---

*"My brother speaks of our ancestors' blood, and he's right — we earned this place. But I've read the histories too, and I see something he doesn't want to see: we survived the Tharnic Empire because we adapted. We worked their mines and waited for our moment. We didn't charge into death; we endured until we could win. That's what I'm doing now. The Crown isn't the Empire. But if it ever becomes the Empire... we'll be ready. In the meantime, I negotiate their taxes, smile at their officials, and make sure Borugham has everything it needs to survive another five centuries."*
