## Appearance
Shares her brother's broad build and solid presence. Iron-grey hair worn in intricate braids — the traditional style of [Borugh](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Thane%20Aldric%20Borugh.md) women. Sharp blue eyes — the [Borugh](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Thane%20Aldric%20Borugh.md) eyes — warmer than Aldric's. Her face shows the same hard dwarven angles, softened by laugh lines and centuries of pragmatic patience.

Dresses well but practically, always in the dark [Borugh](../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Thane%20Aldric%20Borugh.md) colors. Wears her sigil ring on her left hand and a chain of office that signifies her administrative role. Moves efficiently, like someone who has too much to do and not enough time.

Looks like a dwarf who carries the weight of history without being crushed by it.

## Backstory
Greta was born thirty years after Aldric, which meant everything was already decided: he would rule, she would administer. In a different family, this might have bred resentment. Instead, Greta found purpose in it.

She handles [Borugham](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Borugham.md)'s logistics — trade negotiations with [Barge](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md), tax calculations, supply management, the thousand tedious details that keep a settlement functioning. This gives her contact with the outside world that Aldric prefers to avoid.

She's traveled to [Barge](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md). She's met Crown officials. She's seen how other dwarven communities — and human ones — handle the balance between autonomy and authority. She believes [Borugham](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Borugham.md)'s moderate path is correct, but she also knows they must adapt carefully to survive.

## Personality
- **Pragmatic** — focused on what works, not what feels righteous
- **Diplomatic** — has learned to navigate relationships with humans and Crown officials
- **Thoughtful** — considers the long view, measured in dwarven lifetimes
- **Loyal** — loves her brother and supports his leadership
- **Quietly observant** — sees more than she says; knows the cracks in every position

## Role in Borugham
- Chief administrator and logistics manager
- Primary negotiator with [Barge](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md) merchants
- Tax collector and recordkeeper
- The person who actually makes the town function
- Unofficial ambassador to the human world

## Relationships
- **Aldric Borugh:** Loves her brother; they work as a team
- **Hagen Wheelock:** Genuinely friendly; appreciates his role bridging the community
- **Barge Merchants:** Professional relationships; her connections to the outside
- **Yotherdon Contacts:** Maintains quiet communication; knows [Borugham](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Borugham.md) may need allies
- **The Family Legacy:** Carries it differently than Aldric — less weight, more responsibility

*"My brother speaks of our ancestors' blood, and he's right — we earned this place. But I've read the histories too, and I see something he doesn't want to see: we survived the Tharnic Empire because we adapted. We worked their mines and waited for our moment. We didn't charge into death; we endured until we could win. That's what I'm doing now. The Crown isn't the Empire. But if it ever becomes the Empire... we'll be ready. In the meantime, I negotiate their taxes, smile at their officials, and make sure [Borugham](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Borugham.md) has everything it needs to survive another five centuries."*
