# Father Aldwin Sarre

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Priest of the Crossroads Shrine |
| **Age** | Early 60s |

| **Location** | Gitstin, Braewood |

---

## Appearance
A gentle-faced man with a neatly trimmed white beard and kind blue eyes behind spectacles. His hair is thin and white, and he walks with a slight stoop that comes from decades of bending over scripture. He wears simple robes in earth tones and always carries a small prayer book in his pocket.

## Backstory
Aldwin trained as a priest of the Harvest Lord in [Braewood](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) City, but he was never comfortable in the grand temples. He requested assignment to a smaller posting and was sent to [Gitstin](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md) thirty years ago, expecting a brief assignment. He never left.

The Crossroads Shrine suited him perfectly. Rather than serving one faith exclusively, it welcomes travelers of all beliefs. Aldwin has spent three decades learning about other traditions, inviting visiting priests to share their wisdom, and creating a space where differences are bridges rather than barriers.

## Personality
- **Ecumenical:** Believes all sincere faiths honor the divine in their own way. Welcomes all to his shrine.
- **Scholarly:** Loves learning about different religious traditions. His personal library is extensive.
- **Gentle:** Speaks softly and listens more than he talks. Never judges those who come to him.
- **Wise:** Decades of hearing confessions and counseling travelers have given him deep insight into human nature.

## Role in the Town
Maintains the Crossroads Shrine, a multi-faith temple where travelers can offer prayers regardless of their tradition. He performs services, counsels the troubled, and helps mediate disputes when religious matters are involved. He also oversees the festivals of multiple faiths throughout the year.

## Relationships
- **Reeve Arton Fellmark:** The Reeve sometimes seeks his counsel on difficult decisions. Aldwin provides perspective without telling him what to do.
- **Dorinda Kettlebrook:** Coordinates on festival planning. The inn consortium and the shrine often work together on celebrations.
- **Visiting Priests:** Welcomes clerics of all faiths who pass through. Many return specifically to visit him.
- **Brother Aldric Sunhallow:** His counterpart in [Osegas](../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Osegas.md). They correspond regularly about interfaith matters.

## Notable Quote
> "The gods, if they notice us at all, care more about how we treat each other than which words we use to praise them. At least, that's what I've come to believe."
