## Description

**Envoy Pasan** serves as the diplomatic lead for Braewood's mission to Dawerton. A non-combatant by nature and profession, Pasan represents the crown's interests through negotiation, protocol, and careful diplomacy rather than military force.

As part of the escort team assembled for the Dawerton mission, Pasan works alongside military personnel while maintaining focus on the political objectives of the delegation.

## Role

### Diplomatic Lead
- Represents Braewood's interests in trade negotiations
- Handles formal protocols and introductions
- Manages relationships with foreign powers
- Ensures the delegation maintains proper diplomatic conduct

### Non-Combatant
Unlike the military escort, Pasan is not trained for combat. Protection falls to Sergeant Elira Thorn and her team, allowing Pasan to focus entirely on political matters.

## The Dawerton Mission

Pasan was assigned to lead the diplomatic component of the Braewood escort detail sent to Dawerton in 1225 C.C. The mission involved:
- Trade discussions
- Port and shipping route negotiations
- Assessing Dawerton's political landscape
- Managing relationships with the Dawerton Council

## Relationships

- **Sergeant Elira Thorn:** Works alongside her military command for protection
- **Maera of Silverhill:** Relies on her linguistic and analytical skills
- **The Party:** Accompanied by their additional capabilities
- **The Dawerton Council:** Primary diplomatic contacts

## Notable Quote
