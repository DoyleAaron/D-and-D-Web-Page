## Appearance
A lean man of middling height with an easy posture that reads as relaxed until he raises a ward—at which point the air tightens around him like a drawn bowstring. He favors plain travel coats over robes and keeps his dark hair short. He carries no visible staff, but a brass clasp on his collar is, on close inspection, covered in tiny abjuration formulae. His expression is almost always mild, bordering on amused.

## Backstory
Vaelor grew up inside Kluimont's academic system, the son of a ward-keeper who spent her career maintaining the city's outer barrier network. He absorbed abjuration the way most children absorb language—naturally and early—and earned a Council seat in his early thirties, which is unusually fast by Synod standards.

What he did not earn was permission to leave. Kluimont's regulations on Synod members restrict travel: any seat-holder who departs the island weakens the city's collective ward-lattice, and the Synod classifies such gaps as security liabilities. Vaelor has spent years requesting exceptions. Every one was denied.

In **1225 C.C.**, he was named Kluimont's delegate to the **Kadena-Rye Summit**—the first time he has been permitted to leave the island in his adult life.

## Personality
- **Calm and casual:** Speaks like a colleague, not a superior; comfortable with silence and comfortable with noise.
- **Genuinely confident:** Doesn't need to posture. Being laughed at doesn't bother him. Being wrong doesn't either—he just adjusts.
- **Dry humour:** Makes jokes at mildly inconvenient moments. Never rude, rarely serious for long.
- **Quietly curious:** He is seeing the world for the first time. He notices things other seasoned travellers stopped noticing years ago.

## Role in the Kingdom
Eighth seat on the **Council of Ten**, [Kluimont's](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) secretive ruling circle of mages. His portfolio covers defensive ward architecture, summit security, and anti-assassination countermeasures. He was assigned to the Kadena-Rye Summit as Kluimont's representative—a role that required him to leave the island for the first time.

## Relationships
- **[High Archon Lysanthe Kilmora](./High%20Archon%20Lysanthe%20Kilmora.md):** Respects her authority. She chose him for this mission because she trusts his wards more than his politics.
- **[Chris Meudec](./Chris%20Meudec.md):** Same institution, completely different temperament. They are cordial at a distance.
- **The Party:** If treated with respect rather than caution, he becomes curious about them rather than guarded. He admires people who travel freely.

## Magical Discipline
Vaelor specialises in **abjuration** — protective barriers, counterspell architecture, and ward-field manipulation. His techniques are efficient and unheroic-looking; he does not announce himself with flashing effects. The result just holds.

He is known within the Synod for **Tinn's Interlocking Aegis**, a barrier configuration of his own design that allows positioning several linked panels simultaneously — useful for both structural protection and crowd geometry.
