# Corin Valdris

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Youngest of the Valdris household |
| **Race** | Vampire |
| **Location** | Blackthorn House attic and upper halls |
| **Affiliation** | Valdris Household |

---

## Description

Corin is the youngest member of the brood and the least committed to its violence. He is often used as lookout but is visibly fearful during direct conflict.

## Role in Current Arc

- Can become a moral decision point during the manor assault.
- May flee or surrender if Isolde falls.

## Notable Quote

> "I don't remember what hunger felt like before this. I only remember being afraid."
