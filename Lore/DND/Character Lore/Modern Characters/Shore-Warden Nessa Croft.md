# Shore-Warden Nessa Croft

## Title
Shore-Warden of Eimgend

## Age
Early 30s

## Race
Halfling

## Location
Eimgend, Braewood

## Appearance
A small, sturdy halfling woman with sun-darkened skin and sharp hazel eyes that miss nothing on the water. Her curly auburn hair is always tied back in a practical braid. She wears a worn leather vest over practical river-folk clothing, and her hands are callused from years of rowing and rope work.

## Backstory
Nessa comes from a long line of river-folk who know the waters around Lavalto Lake better than anyone. Her family has lived along these shores for generations, learning every current, every sandbar, and every mood of the great lake.

She was recruited to the Watch at nineteen after she spotted and reported an unusual vessel crossing from Lavalto territory that no one else had noticed. Her keen eyes and intimate knowledge of the waters made her invaluable, and Watch-Keeper Dunwald quickly recognized her worth. She was promoted to Shore-Warden within six years—the fastest advancement in the post's history.

## Personality
- **Keen-eyed:** Her eyesight is legendary. She can spot ripples from a submerged object at distances that strain human vision.
- **River-born:** The water is her element. She's more comfortable in a boat than on land.
- **Quiet confidence:** Doesn't boast, but knows exactly how good she is at her job.
- **Superstitious:** Respects the old river-folk beliefs about the lake and what lives beneath it.

## Role in the Village
Manages the Shore Post, the observation point 5km west at the lake's edge. She coordinates the small team of watchers stationed there and reports all observations to Watch-Keeper Dunwald. She also maintains the small fleet of patrol boats and personally leads reconnaissance missions when something unusual is spotted.

## Relationships
- **Watch-Keeper Jareth Dunwald:** Her superior and mentor. He trusts her judgment completely, which is the highest compliment he gives.
- **The Shore Post Team:** Commands four other watchers at the lakeside post. Runs a tight operation.
- **Her Family:** Still has relatives among the river-folk communities. They occasionally share information that outsiders would never tell the Watch.
- **Signal-Master Roderick Pale:** Coordinating signals between Shore Post and the village tower is essential work.

## Notable Quote
> "The lake talks, if you know how to listen. Most folks just hear water. I hear warnings."
