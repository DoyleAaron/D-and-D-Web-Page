# Guildmistress Petra Thornwood

## Title
Guildmistress of the Merchant Courts, Lady of the Grain Exchange

## Age
Mid-40s

## Race
Human

## Location
Eaveton, Braewood

## Appearance
A sharp-featured woman with auburn hair pinned in elaborate braids—the style favored by Eaveton's merchant class. Green eyes that miss nothing, particularly when money is involved. She dresses in expensive but practical clothing: fine wool and leather in muted colors, always with visible guild insignia. Her hands are soft—she handles ledgers, not goods—but her grip when shaking hands is deliberately firm. Carries herself with the confidence of someone who knows exactly how much power she wields.

## Backstory
The Thornwood family built their fortune in the grain trade three generations ago. Petra inherited the family business at twenty-five when her father died unexpectedly, and immediately faced attempts by rival merchants to absorb the Thornwood holdings.

She not only survived but dominated. Within a decade, she controlled the largest share of grain moving through the Exchange and had been elected head of the Merchant Courts—a position that gives her authority to settle trade disputes and set market regulations.

Her alliance with Lord-Regent Greythorne is pragmatic: she supports his policies and delivers tax revenue smoothly; he protects guild privileges and keeps the Crown's interference minimal. Together, they've made Eaveton the kingdom's wealthiest city per capita.

## Personality
Calculating, ambitious, and ruthlessly practical. Petra views every interaction as a negotiation and enters none without knowing what she wants. She can be charming when it serves her purposes, cold when it doesn't.

She has genuine respect for competence and despises inherited privilege divorced from ability. Self-made merchants earn her admiration; lazy nobles earn her contempt.

Despite her hard edge, she's known for fair dealing. Cheating is bad for long-term business, and Petra always thinks long-term.

## Role
As head of the Merchant Courts, Petra wields significant power:
- Settles trade disputes among Eaveton's merchants
- Sets regulations for the Grain Exchange
- Represents guild interests to Lord-Regent Greythorne
- Controls much of the grain trade flowing to Braewood City
- Influences prices that affect farmers across the kingdom

Her personal wealth and political connections make her one of the most powerful commoners in Braewood.

## Relationships
- **Lord-Regent Hallovar Greythorne:** Political alliance based on mutual benefit. Genuine respect, if not friendship.
- **High Chamberlain Aldous Fenn:** Works with him on tax matters. Finds him competent but unimaginative.
- **Rival guild leaders:** Maintains dominance through a combination of savvy dealing and ruthless competition.
- **Yoren Salk (Phine):** Another powerful merchant-prince. They've clashed over trade routes and occasionally cooperated on matters affecting guild power generally.

## Notable Quote
*"The Crown rules by swords, but gold moves the kingdom. Never forget which one pays for the other."*
