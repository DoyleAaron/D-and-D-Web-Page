# Watch-Keeper Jareth Dunwald

## Title
Watch-Keeper of Eimgend

## Age
Late 40s

## Race
Human

## Location
Eimgend, Braewood

## Appearance
A weathered man with grey eyes that seem to look through people rather than at them. His face is lined from years of squinting across the lake in all conditions. He keeps his dark hair cropped short and practical, and always wears the Watch-Keeper's badge—a silver eye over crossed oars.

## Backstory
Jareth began his service as a river scout at sixteen, spending years learning every current and depth of the Lavalto Lake's eastern shores. His patience, keen observation, and absolute discretion caught the attention of the Crown, and he was appointed Watch-Keeper when his father retired from the same position.

The Dunwalds have served as Watch-Keepers for three generations. Jareth takes this responsibility with deadly seriousness. He has never seen anything emerge from Lavalto that required urgent action, but he has seen enough strange lights and unexplained movements on the water to know that vigilance is not paranoia.

## Personality
- **Patient:** Can watch the same stretch of water for hours without losing focus.
- **Discreet:** Knows things about the lake he will never discuss with anyone except the Queen's representatives.
- **Duty-bound:** His family's legacy and his own oath define his entire existence.
- **Observant:** Notices details others miss—a habit that extends beyond the lake to everything around him.

## Role in the Village
Absolute authority in Eimgend. He coordinates the watch schedules, receives reports from the Shore Post, maintains the signal system, and sends reports directly to the Crown—bypassing the usual chains of command.

## Relationships
- **The Crown:** His reports go directly to Queen Maera's office. He has met Crown representatives but never the royals themselves.
- **Shore-Warden Nessa Croft:** His most trusted subordinate, manages the lake observation post.
- **Signal-Master Roderick Pale:** Maintains the beacon system that connects Eimgend to Sheyton.
- **Sheyton Officials:** Knows Lord-Mayor Harcrow formally, but Eimgend's business bypasses normal channels.

## Notable Quote
> "The lake remembers things we've forgotten. My job is to watch until it decides to remind us."
