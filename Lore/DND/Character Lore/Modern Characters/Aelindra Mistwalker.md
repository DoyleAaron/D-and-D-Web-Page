# Aelindra Mistwalker

## Title
Elven Healer of Frore

## Age
~200 (has lived in Frore for 80+ years)

## Race
Elf

## Location
Frore, Braewood

## Appearance
A slender elf woman with silver-grey hair that reaches her waist and pale violet eyes that seem to look beyond the physical world. Her face is ageless and serene, showing neither youth nor age. She dresses in flowing robes of grey and green, and moves with the ethereal grace common to her people.

## Backstory
Aelindra came to Frore eighty years ago, following rumors of a village that practiced the old healing arts—the kind of work her people had done before retreating to Islefield's forests. She expected to visit briefly, learn what she could, and move on.

Instead, she stayed. Frore's commitment to healing, its respect for the land, and its acceptance of outsiders spoke to something deep within her. She shared elven plant knowledge that had been forgotten by humans and learned human techniques that elves had never developed. The exchange enriched both traditions.

Now she's an integral part of Frore, neither fully assimilated nor entirely separate. She bridges the gap between human urgency and elven patience, between practical medicine and spiritual healing.

## Personality
- **Serene:** Nothing rushes her. She moves at her own pace, and that pace is correct.
- **Mysterious:** Keeps her own counsel about her past and her people. Doesn't explain herself.
- **Compassionate:** Came to Frore because healing calls to her. Stayed because people need her.
- **Ancient:** Eighty years is a short visit for an elf. She thinks in longer timescales than humans can grasp.

## Role in the Village
Serves as a senior healer and keeper of elven plant knowledge. She treats the sick, trains those few apprentices suited to elven methods, and maintains connections with her people in Islefield. Her perspective tempers the human tendency toward hasty solutions.

## Relationships
- **Root-Mother Yseult Greenbough:** Partners in healing. They've worked together for thirty years and complement each other perfectly.
- **Young Tam Greenbough:** Has offered to teach him elven techniques if he proves patient enough.
- **Lira Dewfoot:** Sometimes joins gathering expeditions. They share a love of the wild places.
- **Islefield Elves:** Maintains distant contact with her people. Visits occasionally but always returns.

## Notable Quote
> "Humans measure healing in days and weeks. My people measure in seasons and years. The truth, as always, lies between. Some wounds need urgent care; some need only time."
