## Appearance
A powerfully built dwarf with a beard of copper-red, streaked with gray by war's end. Broldir wore armor forged from reclaimed Imperial steel—a deliberate insult to his former masters. His hammer, *Chainbreaker*, was said to have been forged from the melted shackles of freed slaves.

## Backstory
Broldir was born in chains. His earliest memories were of forge-heat and overseers' whips in a Tharnic foundry. He watched his parents work themselves to death. He watched friends executed for minor infractions. He learned to hate with a patience that dwarves are uniquely suited for.

When he finally escaped, Broldir didn't flee to safety—he started organizing. For years he moved between scattered dwarven communities, building networks, stockpiling weapons, and waiting for the Empire to show weakness.

That moment came in **710 C.C.** The Empire, overextended and rotting from within, couldn't respond quickly enough when Broldir launched his uprising. His first target was **Treadfast Hold**, where [Legate Sienna Valtoris](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Legate%20Sienna%20Valtoris.md) had ruled through terror. Broldir killed her personally, and the rebellion had its first great victory.

Over the next two decades, Broldir united the scattered clans, forged alliances with elven houses (including [Princess Araleth Oakheath](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Princess%20Araleth%20Oakheath.md)), and systematically dismantled Imperial control over the northern mountains. The War of the Sundered Chains ended with dwarven autonomy secured—and Broldir's name immortalized.

## Personality
- **Patient hatred:** Waited decades for the right moment to strike.
- **Inspiring leader:** Could turn slaves into soldiers with a single speech.
- **Ruthless when necessary:** Showed no mercy to slavers or collaborators.
- **Visionary:** Fought not just for freedom but for a future where his people would never be chained again.

## Role in History
Broldir Flameforged is revered as the **Liberator** who spearheaded the War of the Sundered Chains and laid the foundation for dwarven autonomy in the north. His name is invoked in dwarven oaths, his deeds taught to every dwarven child, and his hammer—if it still exists—would be the most sacred relic of his people.

## Relationships
- **[Legate Sienna Valtoris](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Legate%20Sienna%20Valtoris.md):** His first great enemy. He killed her at Treadfast Hold.
- **[Princess Araleth Oakheath](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Princess%20Araleth%20Oakheath.md):** His elven ally. Together they coordinated the coalition that broke the Empire.
- **[Imperial Warmaster Dravarian](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Imperial%20Warmaster%20Dravarian.md):** The Empire's last hope. Broldir's coalition destroyed him at Hammerfall.
- **The Dwarven Clans:** His people. He gave them freedom.
