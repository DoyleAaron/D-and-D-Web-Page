## Appearance
A weathered woman with sun-darkened skin and salt-crusted hair, Aris was most recognizable by her distinctive steel eye-patch—not a patch at all, but a polished metal prosthetic that covered her ruined left eye socket. Rumor claimed she could see through fog with it, that it was enchanted to spot ships beyond the horizon. The truth was simpler: she'd lost the eye in her first mutiny and replaced it with something that scared people.

## Backstory
Aris began as a common sailor on a merchant vessel, but common was never in her nature. She organized her first mutiny at nineteen, took her first prize at twenty, and by thirty commanded a fleet of raiders that controlled the treacherous western reefs.

Her genius was organization. Where other pirates fought each other as often as their prey, Aris forged a **Reef Confederacy**—a loose alliance of captains who answered to her and split their takes according to her rules. Ships paid tolls to pass through "her" waters. Those who refused were never seen again.

For decades, the Confederacy dominated the western seas, raiding coastal settlements and choking trade routes. But Aris made an enemy she couldn't intimidate: [Sea Lord Helmar Lynth](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Sea%20Lord%20Helmar%20Lynth.md), a naval commander who dreamed of unifying the scattered maritime fiefdoms into something greater.

Their rivalry came to a head at the **Naval Battle of Sunken Crags**. Aris's flagship was caught between Lynth's fleet and the reefs she knew so well—but Lynth had learned those reefs too. When her ship went down, so did the Confederacy.

## Personality
- **Ruthlessly pragmatic:** Built an organization out of chaos through sheer force of will.
- **Charismatic:** Could make murderers and thieves follow rules—her rules.
- **Proud:** Refused to flee when Lynth cornered her. She'd rather die a queen than live a fugitive.

## Role in History
Aris's defeat allowed [Sea Lord Helmar Lynth](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Sea%20Lord%20Helmar%20Lynth.md) to unify the western maritime region under a single banner, paving the way for [Kluimont's](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) formation. Ironically, the kingdom that rose from her defeat would later shelter pirates of its own—though none as ambitious as the Steel-Eye.

## Relationships
- **[Sea Lord Helmar Lynth](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Sea%20Lord%20Helmar%20Lynth.md):** Her nemesis. He ended her reign at Sunken Crags.
- **The Reef Confederacy:** Her creation. It died with her.
- **[Kluimont](../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md):** The kingdom built on her grave.

## Notable Quote
> "The sea doesn't care about crowns or flags. It cares about strength. And I am the strongest thing in these waters."
