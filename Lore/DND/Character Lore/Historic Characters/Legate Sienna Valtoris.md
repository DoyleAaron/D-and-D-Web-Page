## Appearance
A cold-eyed woman who favored severe Imperial dress and kept her dark hair pulled back in military fashion. Valtoris was not physically imposing, but her presence carried the weight of absolute authority. She was never seen without guards, and never without a thin smile that promised suffering.

## Backstory
Sienna Valtoris was appointed governor of Treadfast Hold, one of the Empire's most productive dwarven mining operations. Her mandate was simple: keep the ore flowing. Her methods were entirely her own.

Valtoris ruled through calculated terror. Public executions for minor infractions. Families punished for individuals' crimes. Quotas set impossibly high, with brutal consequences for failure. She kept detailed records of every punishment, every death, every "example made"—she considered it good governance.

What Valtoris didn't understand was that terror has limits. Push hard enough, and the fear becomes rage. By the time [High Thane Broldir Flameforged](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/High%20Thane%20Broldir%20Flameforged.md) arrived at Treadfast Hold with his growing army, the slaves inside were ready to rise.

The battle was short. Valtoris's guards were overwhelmed by the very workers they'd brutalized for years. Broldir found her hiding in her quarters, surrounded by her meticulous records. He killed her with *Chainbreaker*, the hammer forged from slaves' shackles.

## Personality
- **Methodical cruelty:** Treated oppression as administration.
- **Arrogant:** Never believed her victims would—or could—fight back.
- **Record-keeper:** Documented everything, as if atrocities were merely data points.

## Role in History
Valtoris's defeat at Treadfast Hold was a **major turning point** in the dwarven uprising. It proved the Empire could be toppled from within, that its governors could be killed by the very people they enslaved. Her death became a rallying cry, and her records—preserved by the victors—became evidence of Imperial crimes.

## Relationships
- **The Tharnic Empire:** Her employers. She served their interests efficiently.
- **[High Thane Broldir Flameforged](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/High%20Thane%20Broldir%20Flameforged.md):** Her killer. He came for her personally.
- **The Dwarven Slaves:** Her victims. They helped tear down her gates.

## Notable Quote
> "Productivity requires discipline. Discipline requires examples. The mathematics are simple."
