## Appearance
A towering figure in ornate golden armor, Dravarian looked like the Empire's propaganda made flesh. His presence on a battlefield was meant to inspire terror and awe in equal measure. He wore a plumed helm that made him visible for miles—a deliberate choice that would prove fatal.

## Backstory
Dravarian was the Empire's answer to the growing rebellion. When [High Thane Broldir Flameforged](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/High%20Thane%20Broldir%20Flameforged.md) began uniting the dwarven clans and [Legate Sienna Valtoris](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Legate%20Sienna%20Valtoris.md) fell at Treadfast Hold, the Emperor summoned his greatest general and gave him command of the largest legion ever assembled.

Dravarian was not incompetent—he had won dozens of campaigns against human enemies. But he had never faced a coalition of dwarven infantry and elven archers fighting on terrain they knew intimately. He underestimated dwarven stubbornness. He underestimated elven archery. Most fatally, he underestimated how much the oppressed peoples of the Empire wanted him dead.

At the **Hammerfall Uprising**, Dravarian's legion marched into a carefully prepared killing ground. Dwarven shields locked while elven arrows darkened the sky. By the time Dravarian realized the trap, retreat was impossible. He died somewhere in the carnage—his golden armor made him an easy target.

## Personality
- **Arrogant:** Genuinely believed Imperial forces were invincible against "lesser races."
- **Conventional:** Fought the kind of war he understood, against enemies who refused to cooperate.
- **Loyal:** Served the Empire faithfully, if not wisely.

## Role in History
Dravarian's defeat at Hammerfall marked the **beginning of the Empire's collapse**. His death proved the Empire could be beaten, that its legions could be destroyed, that its generals could die like anyone else. The moral and strategic victory energized the rebellion and demoralized Imperial loyalists across the continent.

## Relationships
- **The Tharnic Empire:** His masters. He failed them.
- **[High Thane Broldir Flameforged](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/High%20Thane%20Broldir%20Flameforged.md):** His enemy. Broldir's coalition destroyed him.
- **[Princess Araleth Oakheath](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/Princess%20Araleth%20Oakheath.md):** Her archers turned Hammerfall into a slaughter.
