**Age**: Deceased (died around 720 C.C.)  
**Race**: Human (Tharnic Imperial)  
**Backstory**:
- Tasked with crushing the allied forces during the War of the Sundered Chains; commanded the Empire’s largest legion.
- Underestimated the dwarven-elven coalition and was killed at the **Hammerfall Uprising**.

**Importance**:
- His defeat marked the **beginning of the Empire’s collapse**, giving rebels a moral and strategic victory.
