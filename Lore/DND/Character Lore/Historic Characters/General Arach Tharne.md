## Appearance
Records describe a broad, brutal man in blood-red Imperial armor, his face scarred from decades of mountain warfare. He wore his hair cropped short in military fashion and carried a mace said to have cracked dwarven shields like eggshells.

## Backstory
Arach Tharne rose through the Tharnic military during the Empire's expansion into the northern mountains. He specialized in one thing: breaking dwarven resistance. Where other commanders negotiated or besieged, Tharne simply crushed.

His most infamous campaign was the **Battle of Iron Break**, where his legions shattered a coalition of dwarven holds and enslaved thousands to fuel the Empire's foundries. The dwarves who survived were marched in chains to forges across the Empire, their skills exploited until death.

Tharne served the Empire faithfully until his death, never questioning whether his methods were justified. To him, the dwarves were resources to be extracted, nothing more.

## Personality
- **Brutal efficiency:** Saw warfare as a problem to be solved with overwhelming force.
- **Loyal to the Empire:** Never questioned orders, never showed mercy unless commanded.
- **Contemptuous:** Viewed non-humans as tools at best, obstacles at worst.

## Role in History
Tharne's name became a curse among dwarven clans. Stories of his atrocities were passed down through generations, fueling the hatred that eventually exploded in the War of the Sundered Chains. When dwarven rebels rose against the Empire decades later, they invoked Tharne's victims as justification for every Imperial death.

## Relationships
- **The Tharnic Empire:** His masters. He served without question.
- **Dwarven Clans:** His victims. They remember him with hatred that burns centuries later.
- **[High Thane Broldir Flameforged](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/High%20Thane%20Broldir%20Flameforged.md):** Though they never met, Broldir's rebellion was built on rage against men like Tharne.

## Notable Quote
