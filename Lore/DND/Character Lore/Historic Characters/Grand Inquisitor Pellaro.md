## Appearance
A gaunt figure in white robes stained with the work of his profession. Pellaro's face was unremarkable—the kind of face you'd forget—but his eyes were said to see lies the way others saw colors. He carried no weapons; his instruments of persuasion were kept by assistants.

## Backstory
Pellaro rose through the Tharnic religious hierarchy during the Empire's declining years, when paranoia gripped the ruling class. Rebels, heretics, and "foreign sympathizers" lurked everywhere—or so the Empire believed. Pellaro made it his sacred duty to find them.

His methods were legendary in their cruelty. Witch hunts swept through provincial towns. Torture chambers filled with anyone who spoke against Imperial rule. "Re-education" camps turned dissidents into hollow-eyed loyalists—or corpses. Pellaro justified it all as divine mandate: the Empire was chosen by the gods, and resistance was blasphemy.

When the War of the Sundered Chains turned against the Empire, Pellaro fled deeper into Imperial territory, but the rebellion caught up with him at **Barash Gorge**. A dwarven tribunal tried him for crimes against free peoples. The trial lasted three days. The execution took considerably longer.

## Personality
- **Fanatically devoted:** Believed absolutely in the Empire's divine right to rule.
- **Methodical cruelty:** Saw torture not as punishment but as purification.
- **Self-righteous:** Never doubted, never questioned, never showed mercy.

## Role in History
Pellaro became a symbol of everything wrong with the Tharnic Empire—its religious zealotry, its casual brutality, its contempt for non-human life. His public execution at Barash Gorge became a moral victory for the rebellion, proof that even the Empire's most feared agents could be brought to justice.

## Relationships
- **The Tharnic Empire:** His faith. He served it unto death.
- **The Rebellion:** His enemies. They ensured his death was not quick.
- **[High Thane Broldir Flameforged](../../Character%20Lore/Historic%20Characters/High%20Thane%20Broldir%20Flameforged.md):** It was Broldir's forces that captured him.
