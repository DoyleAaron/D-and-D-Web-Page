**Name:** Leontius of Osegas

**Age:** 26 (mid-twenties)

**Class:** Cleric (Peace Domain)

**Level:** 1

**Origin/Home:** Osegas, a humble town south of Braewood near Gisten and Eaveton

**Appearance:**

- **Height:** 6'5" (195 cm)
    
- **Weight:** 245 lbs (111 kg) Leontius is a towering figure, large in stature with a muscular build sculpted from years of wielding a heavy executioner's axe:
    
- **Sacred Blindfold:** Marked with the holy symbol of Serenia—an open hand draped with cloth—covering his eyes yet granting him divine sight.
    
- **Clerical Robes:** Simple and modest, adorned subtly with symbols of forgiveness and peace.
    
- **Scarred Back:** Numerous whip marks, painful reminders of his brutal past under the crown's harsh discipline.
    
- **Gentle Demeanor:** Contrasts his imposing physical presence, reflecting the peace he now seeks to embody.
    

**Backstory:** From a young age, Leontius was forced into the grim role of executioner, a legacy imposed by generations of his family serving the crown. Recognized early for his remarkable skill, he was transferred at fifteen from Osegas to serve directly under King Tarvin Braeden IV in the royal capital. Life there was brutal; seen merely as a weapon, Leontius suffered constant surveillance and harsh punishments. Haunted by the faces of those he executed, he inwardly despised his role.

His salvation came from Serenia, The Veiled Heart, goddess of forgiveness, who appeared to him offering a chance at redemption. Accepting her offer, he renounced violence, taking up a sacred blindfold symbolizing his commitment and receiving the gift of divine sight. His gift is conditional—should he ever intentionally harm another, it will vanish forever.

Now a Cleric of Peace, Leontius travels offering guidance and redemption, helping those burdened by guilt and sorrow.

**Personality:** Leontius is gentle yet solemn, deeply introspective, and committed fully to Serenia’s teachings. He continually battles his violent past, striving daily to uphold his vow of peace.

**Traits:**

- **Divine Sight:** Sees clearly through darkness due to Serenia’s blessing, though contingent on his vow of non-violence.
    
- **Gentle Giant:** His intimidating physical presence contrasts his peaceful intent and compassionate heart.
    
- **Bearer of Burdens:** Deep empathy drives him to alleviate the suffering and guilt of others.
    
- **Constant Vigilance:** Always wary of his capacity for violence, his past actions fuel his determination to maintain peace at all costs.
