**Backstory:**  
Raised in the volcanic port city of Darewaeton, Tearitus learned the art of leverage before he could read. With a dockworker mother and an absent smuggler father, he found family in the Thieves’ Guild, running messages and scoping marks before most children finished school.  

Everything changed when he broke into a noble estate and stumbled into a vampiric ritual. They never caught him, but their eyes haunted him from that day forward. Since then, he’s carried a bone-deep fear of vampires, one that drives his paranoia and shapes his every move in the dark.  

Despite his fear, Tearitus climbed the guild’s ladder by selling secrets and playing both sides of the city’s politics. He now lives in relative comfort, but comfort has dulled his thrills. He seeks something greater—infamy, a name that endures.  

Unusual among rogues, Tearitus also keeps a **bicycle**, a curious contraption he brings everywhere. It’s maintained by a dedicated fixer named **Benjamin Netanyahu**, whose skills keep it rolling smoothly through Lavalto’s uneven streets. While some mock it as eccentric, the bicycle has become a symbol of Tearitus’s independence—his way of moving faster than both his enemies and his past.
