**Backstory:**  
Once a gilded heir to the storied Aeristel bloodline of Islefield, Amdir’s future was etched in silver and song—until he turned from the light. His obsession with forbidden power, shadow-bound tomes, and the art of necromancy led to his banishment by none other than his own aunt, Queen Atheliana Aeristel. Stripped of title and home, Amdir became a cautionary tale whispered through the ivy halls of Shercam.

But exile was not the end—it was ignition.

Furious at his dismissal and intrigued by the arcane undercurrents emanating from Kluimont, Amdir sought an audience with their hidden magical elite, hoping to uncover the truth behind the rumored subjugation of dragons and delve deeper into his craft. Denied access, and unwelcome in courtly circles, he now resides in the quiet town of Ansdale—a seemingly unremarkable settlement, yet close enough to the heart of Kluimont’s secrets to serve his ambition.

In Ansdale’s forgotten corners, Amdir conducts his research with unwavering focus. His knowledge of dark magic is formidable, and whispers speak of strange lights in the woods and bones that shift beneath the earth. He is no longer the golden heir—but a shadow reborn.

Amdir Aeristel walks a razor’s edge between arcane mastery and damnation. And he wouldn’t have it any other way.

**Personality:**  
Amdir is sly and calculating, always several steps ahead in both conversation and ambition. Power is not a temptation to him—it is a necessity, a truth he embraces with cold, logical clarity. He speaks with calm precision, rarely revealing more than he intends, and views emotional attachments as distractions from his pursuit of mastery. Every move he makes is deliberate, every ally a tool or a test. But he has one personality flaw, his aching to belong after his family issues.

**Traits:**

- **Cold Precision:** Every word, gesture, and spell is calculated—Amdir rarely acts without intent, and his unemotional demeanor unsettles even seasoned mages.    
    
- **Relentless Seeker:** His hunger for arcane power knows no bounds. Forbidden libraries, cursed artifacts, and ancient tombs are not obstacles—they’re invitations.
    
- **Exiled Elegance:**  Though stripped of title, Amdir still carries himself with noble poise. His robes are immaculate, his posture regal, and his disdain finely sharpened.
    
- **Shadow-Touched Mind:** Long exposure to necromantic forces and eldritch tomes has left subtle marks—he sometimes speaks to things others cannot see, and the dead seem oddly drawn to him.
