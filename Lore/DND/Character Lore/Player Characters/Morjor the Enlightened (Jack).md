**Backstory:**  
Born to House Aedrent’s comfort and expectations, Mordor left at eighteen, seeking silence over status. He spent fifteen years in Shercam Woods, living simply and refining a contemplative martial art that neutralises rather than harms. A moment of clarity ended his self-exile; now he walks the world to understand it, offering stillness where he can.

**Personality:**  
Patient, gentle, sparing with words. Prefers questions to judgments and balance to bravado. Firm when peace is threatened.

**Traits:**
- **Forest-Taught Discipline:** Movement as meditation; defence over domination.  
- **Quiet Strength:** Resolves conflict with restraint and positioning.  
- **Compassionate Perspective:** Sees ambition’s traps; responds with empathy.  
- **Enlightened Wanderer:** Forsook wealth for wisdom; keeps no excess.
