## Appearance

A battle-scarred orc with the disciplined bearing of a trained soldier. His frame is powerful, built for both brute force and strategic combat. He wears the remnants of Kluimont military gear—battered but maintained with the care of a professional. Burn marks and salt-stained equipment tell the story of his near-death experience in the sea.

## Backstory

Panyr Wulkor was born in one of the military-bred enclaves of Kluimont's mainland frontier, where the kingdom allowed orcs and goblinoids to live semi-autonomously. Raised among harsh drills, arcane experiments, and whispered rumors of dragons in the western forest, Panyr found early purpose in the Kluimont Army. There, he trained in both brute force and strategic discipline—an effort by the kingdom to elevate "monstrous races" into an efficient auxiliary force.

Panyr's unit, known as the **Ashwrought**, was often dispatched into the Rimdiff Isles and along the Kluimont-Braewood border to secure trade routes and suppress piracy. But during a patrol near the mainland coast, a skirmish with Braewood mercenaries turned into an ambush. His squad was overrun, and Panyr was believed dead after a cliffside explosion hurled him into the sea.

He awoke battered on the shores near Sheyton, deep in Braewood territory—behind enemy lines, but far from the war front.

## Current Situation

Panyr finds himself in a precarious position:
- **Stranded** in enemy territory with no way to contact his unit
- **Presumed dead** by Kluimont command
- **An orc** in human-dominated Braewood, where his kind are viewed with suspicion at best
- **A soldier without a war**, uncertain of his purpose

He must decide whether to attempt the dangerous journey back to Kluimont, find a new purpose in Braewood, or seek out mercenary work to survive.

## Personality

- **Disciplined:** Military training runs deep; he approaches problems with tactical thinking
- **Pragmatic:** Survival comes first; ideology can wait
- **Proud:** Despite his situation, he carries himself as a soldier, not a refugee
- **Conflicted:** Raised to serve Kluimont, now questioning what that service truly meant
- **Watchful:** Always assessing threats, exits, and opportunities

## Skills

- **Combat Training:** Both brute force and strategic discipline from Kluimont's auxiliary program
- **Survival:** Can endure harsh conditions and live off the land
- **Military Knowledge:** Understands tactics, formations, and chain of command
- **Border Experience:** Familiar with Kluimont-Braewood tensions and trade routes

## Relationships

- **The Ashwrought:** His former unit, who believe him dead
- **Kluimont Command:** The military that shaped him, now beyond his reach
- **Braewood Forces:** Technically his enemies, though the war seems far away now
- **Local Population:** Views him with suspicion or fear—orcs are not welcome in most Braewood settlements

## Notable Quote

> "I was trained to fight and die for Kluimont. The sea took half of that bargain. What remains is mine to decide."
