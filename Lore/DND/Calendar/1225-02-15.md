# February 15, 1225

## Investigation & Return to Braewood

### Investigating the Book
- The party investigated **how to open Balthazar's Book** in Sheyton
- Visited the local library to research Balthazar, star charts, ancient vaults, and magical codices
- **Amdir charmed the receptionist**, gaining unrestricted access to the private stacks
- Information was fragmented - Balthazar's work predates most recorded archives

### The Wizard's Tower Discovery
- Visited Sheyton's wizard tower for expert insight
- Learned a crucial secret: **The Book can only be opened when submerged in water that has touched a magical artefact gifted by Balthazar**
- The water must carry the "signature" of one of his relics
- This links to the fountain water from the Quad Vault

### Encounter with Slave Traders
- On the return journey to Braewood, the party encountered **slave traders**
- They intervened and **freed the slaves** from captivity

### Return to Braewood
- Armed with knowledge about the Book, the party returned to Braewood
- Needed to report their findings and seek access to controlled magical environments to safely open the Codex
