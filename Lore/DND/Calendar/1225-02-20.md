# February 20, 1225

## Diplomacy, Discovery & Dinner

### The Dawerton Council Meeting
- The party attended a **formal diplomatic meeting** at Dawerton's council chamber
- Trade, ports, and shipping routes discussed openly
- **Liora**, a prominent council figure, quietly studied the party's presence
- The meeting ended positively with no conflict
- Liora extended an invitation for a **private dinner**

### Library Visit & Meeting Adrian
- Before dinner, the party visited the **Dawerton library** to research:
  - Local history, volcanic trade, ancient magic and artefacts
- They encountered **Adrian**, the **vampiric mayor of Dawerton**
- The exchange was cordial and civil with polite conversation
- The party sensed that Adrian is concealing something beneath his calm demeanor

### Dinner with Liora
- Formally escorted to **Liora's residence** for an elegant dinner
- Liora asked careful questions about the party's travels and role
- Subtly probed their intentions without accusation
- Mentions of old families, missing shipments, and volcanic interests surfaced indirectly
- **Liora proposed a potential job**, hinting at deeper involvement
- The party requested time to discuss privately

### Nightfall Chaos
- Returning to lodging, **Morjor was heavily intoxicated** and fell down the stairs
- **Varin healed him**, preventing serious injury
- **Amdir manipulated Morjor** into entering Sister Calene's room
- Calene reacted violently, attempting to burn Morjor
- Morjor retaliated by trying to throw a table at Amdir
- **Sergeant Elira** intervened and subdued Morjor
