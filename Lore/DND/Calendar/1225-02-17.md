# February 17, 1225

## The Escort Assembles & Travel Begins

### Meeting the Escort Team
The party met the full Braewood escort detail assigned to the Dawerton mission:
- **Envoy Pasan** – Diplomatic lead, non-combatant
- **Maera of Silverhill** – Linguist and analyst (translator)
- **Sergeant Elira Thorn** – Frontline commander
- **Corporal Bram Stone** – Ranger scout
- **Sister Calene Dusk** – War cleric and spiritual support
- **Aric "Ash" Vell** – Rogue skirmisher and intelligence asset

### Early Dynamics
- **Aric and Amdir** bonded early over shared attitudes and humour
- **Maera** showed clear disdain toward Amdir, remaining cold and critical
- **Sister Calene** displayed noticeable interest in Amdir and Varin, often using Morjor as an excuse to remain close

### Travel to Cleamond
- The combined group departed Braewood
- Overland travel proceeded without incident
- Arrived in **Cleamond** for a brief rest stop before continuing the journey
