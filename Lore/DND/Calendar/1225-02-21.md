# February 21, 1225

## Accepting the Job & Beginning the Investigation

### Informing Elira
- The party told **Sergeant Elira** about Liora's proposal
- After discussion, decided to proceed with the job

### Accepting Liora's Offer
- Returned to Liora and formally **accepted her proposed job**
- Payment agreed: **Three questions** to be answered truthfully by Liora and her network:
  1. Information on **Balthazar** - his history, works, and known movements
  2. Relations between **Dawerton and Kluimont** - political tensions, trade, hidden conflicts
  3. Relations between **Dawerton and Islefield** - alliances, grievances, and trust

### The Investigation Begins
- Early information gathering revealed a troubling pattern:
  - Multiple citizens reported **shared nightmares** with consistent descriptions
  - **Missing loved ones seen walking around at night** as apparitions
  - The phenomenon affected everyone within **2km of the missing people**

### The Grieving Father
- Met with a **grieving father and his daughter**
- The girl reported seeing her deceased grandfather walking the streets at night
- **Varin made the father blind** then "healed" him to gain trust and information

### Amdir's Discovery
- **Amdir went off alone** and made a discovery
- Reunited at the tavern and told the party about a **note he found**
- The note was from **Adrian to Liora** referencing a **Balthazar gifted item**
- Confirms Dawerton's leadership knows more about Balthazar than they admit

### Recruiting Bram & The Missing Priest
- The party recruited **Corporal Bram Stone** for the investigation
- Through questioning, learned of a **priest who recently vanished**
- The priest was last seen acting strangely before disappearing

### The Haunted House
- Located the priest's residence to investigate
- Inside, they were **attacked by multiple hostile spirits**
- The fight was brutal - several party members nearly died
- Resources heavily depleted, but the party **survived and prevailed**
