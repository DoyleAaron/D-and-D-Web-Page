# February 13, 1225

**Summary:** The party awoke in Braewood City and began their journey toward Barge. Along the road they encountered a solitary dwarf pilgrim / traveler who, after sharing pipe smoke and hard bread, unspooled the long, meandering tale of his life to Morjor. Little of it seemed immediately useful—but a few names and places may matter later.

## Travel & Encounters
- Departed: Braewood City (morning)
- Destination: Barge (en route)
- Road Conditions: Cold, clear skies, light wagon traffic.
- Significant NPC: An unnamed older dwarf (scar over left brow, brass-ring beard ties). Claimed to have mined “under three banners” and outlived them all.

## Notable Moments
- Dwarf’s Story: Themes of lost holds, betrayal by a human foreman, a treasure that was “more curse than coin,” and a final vow to wander until he “hears the Deep Ring again.”
- Morjor listened politely; others kept marching pace and only half‑listened.

## Hooks / Leads
- “Deep Ring” could be a dwarven cultural or religious artifact/signal.
- Mentioned a collapsed shaft near “Redfen Rise” (unfamiliar to party) where “echoes still sing wrong.”
