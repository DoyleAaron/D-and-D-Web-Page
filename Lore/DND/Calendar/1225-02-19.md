# February 19, 1225

## Arrival in Dawerton & Diplomatic Tensions

### Early Morning Arrival
- The ship docked in **Dawerton** early in the morning
- Dawerton is a busy port city steeped in trade, secrecy, and intrigue

### Unexpected Escort Assignment
- The party was assigned to travel alongside a second group:
  - **Tarth** – A black dragonborn acting as a Kluimont diplomat
  - **Four Kluimont knights** from the city of Eigmend
- The joint escort was tense due to political friction between Braewood and Kluimont

### Rising Hostility
- **Amdir deliberately tripped Tarth**, worsening relations
- Insults and posturing continued between both escort groups

### Tavern Confrontation
- At a tavern for the night, conversation turned openly hostile
- Arguments nearly escalated into a **full brawl** - narrowly avoided
- The night ended with tension unresolved, both sides retreating to rest under uneasy terms

### The Mysterious Disappearance
- When the party awakened the following morning:
  - **Tarth was gone**
  - More disturbingly, **none of the party could remember Tarth at all**
  - Only indirect signs remained: extra beds disturbed, confused knights, gaps in memory
