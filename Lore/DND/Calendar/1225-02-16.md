# February 16, 1225

## Shopping, Royal Audience & New Mission

### Shopping in Braewood
- The party spent time shopping and resupplying in Braewood
- **Amdir** got into a heated argument with a vendor, causing tension
- **Morjor** was verbally harassed by a shady local
- The shopping trip highlighted the less-friendly side of Braewood's streets

### Meeting with the Clerk
- Met with the **clerk** to receive promised compensation and paperwork
- Each member redeemed **Quartermaster chits**
- Selected gear upgrades, utility packs, consumables, and **one Minor Curio** from the Crown Tray
- Subtle irregularities in the records hinted at clerical manipulation

### Audience with the Queen
- Granted a short audience with the **Queen of Braewood**
- She formally acknowledged their success
- Presented a **new proposal**: The Lavalto Assignment
  - Escort and protect a diplomatic group to Lavalto
  - Quietly gather information on nighttime port activity, volcanic reagents, and "nocturnal shipments"
- **Rewards offered**: 100gp base pay + 25gp expenses per character

### Amdir's Incident
- **Amdir got high** during the day's events
- The party discussed and **accepted the Queen's offer**
