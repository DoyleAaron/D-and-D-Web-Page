# February 12, 1225

**Summary:** Began in Cleamond; traveled to Braewood City. Day dominated by shopping misadventures, misdirection, and minor divine embarrassment.

## Morning – Departure from Cleamond
- Broke camp / lodgings and set out for Braewood City.
- Weather: Overcast but mild; easy road.

## Arrival & Urban Intentions
- Goal: Acquire supplies—alchemy, armor, general adventuring kit.
- Party split loosely inside the city.

## Key Events
1. **Morjor’s “Apothecary” Detour**  
   - Amdir deceived Morjor, steering him not to an apothecary but to a pleasure house named **“Kiss and Forget.”**  
   - Morjor realized the ruse only after entry; departed with wounded dignity but intact coin.
2. **Actual Apothecary Attempt #1**  
   - Genuine apothecary located; standard healing potion price exceeded Morjor’s current funds.
3. **Amdir’s Armor Ambition**  
   - Entered an upscale armorer—display pieces well beyond budget.  
   - Settled instead for chain (chain shirt / chain links) after abandoning the idea of a fine breastplate.
4. **Morjor’s Back‑Alley “Discount”**  
   - Sought cheaper alchemical goods; lured by a suspicious vendor (“back alley bum”).  
   - Vendor turned aggressive—swiftly neutralized by Morjor. No serious injury to Morjor reported.
5. **Varin’s Devotional Failure**  
   - Visited a shrine / temple of Bahamut. Prayer attempt faltered (poor omen / lack of focus).  
   - Possible future need for guidance or atonement.

## Outcomes & Notes
- No major purchases besides more modest armor upgrade for Amdir.
- Party cohesion tested by prank; no lasting fracture.
- Minor urban altercation leaves question of whether guards took notice (unclear).

## Hooks / Follow‑Ups
- Morjor may still require affordable alchemical source.  
- Varin may seek renewed divine favor.  
- House “Kiss and Forget” could resurface as a social / information hub.
