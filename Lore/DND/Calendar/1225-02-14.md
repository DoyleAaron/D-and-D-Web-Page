# February 14, 1225

## The Dungeon of Balthazar

### Journey North
- The party travelled north toward the foothills near Plalisle to recover **Balthazar's Star-Charted Codex**
- They haggled with a stablemaster, trading garments and coin for a horse and carriage rental
- On the road, they were **attacked by wolves** - Adolf Smitler nearly died but was stabilised
- Smitler was left with the horse while the party entered the dungeon on foot

### The Dungeon Challenges
- **Bat Room**: Stepped on trapped tiles, triggering a swarm of bats including a boss bat. Killing the boss opened the door
- **Counterweight Puzzle**: Complete disaster - threw random objects, poured oil and set it on fire. Amdir eventually solved it by throwing dead bats into the bowl until the weight requirement was satisfied
- **Peacebinding Hall**: Left weapons and foci as instructed, passing through without triggering the stone guardians

### Audience with Balthazar's Fountain
- Stood before the Quad Fountain, each asking one personal question
- **The Sacrifices**:
  - Morjor offered nothing
  - Amdir sacrificed his pendant
  - Varin sacrificed his **entire arm** (highest cost)
  - Tearius sacrificed his beloved bike (strong sentimental value)
- **Rewards received**:
  - The Chart of the Threefold Way (Balthazar's Book)
  - Compass of Safe Passage (Compass of Lines Unseen)
- The vault's exit rune **teleported the group back to Sheyton**
