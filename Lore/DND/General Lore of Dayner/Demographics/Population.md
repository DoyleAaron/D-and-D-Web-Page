
# Overall Population: ~2.25 Million

## Braewood (Approx. 1.05 Million)

> **Largest kingdom by land area and total population.**  
> **Northern Braewood** specifically has the highest concentration of dwarves in the entire continent.

- **Humans (55%)**: ~580k
    - Humans are the clear majority, especially in central and southern Braewood.
- **Dwarves (20%)**: ~210k
    - Concentrated heavily in the **north**, near mountains and mining enclaves.
- **Elves (5%)**: ~50k
    - Found mostly in urban trade hubs or as visiting merchants.
- **Halflings & Gnomes (5%)**: ~50k
    - Halfling farming communities dot the southern plains; gnomes cluster around craft centers.
- **Half-Orcs, Orcs & Goblins (5%)**: ~50k
    - Often live on the edges of Braewood society or in smaller enclaves.
- **Other Races (10%)**: ~110k
    - Tieflings, dragonborn, tabaxi, genasi, etc. Often found in port cities and trading routes.

### Notable Points

- Large **standing army**, but each city tends to be self-sufficient.
- **Racial tensions**—especially up north—mean non-humans and half-humans can face prejudice.

---

## Islefield (Approx. 750k)

> **Elven-ruled** kingdom with the richest farmland and plentiful natural resources.

- **Elves (40%)**: ~300k
    - Heavily concentrated in the **southern** portion (including the Shercam Woods) and areas near the Prairie Kingfisher Grove.
- **Humans (40%)**: ~300k
    - More common in the mixed northern/central regions and cities near farmland.
- **Dwarves (5%)**: ~35k
    - Typically in scattered enclaves or trade outposts, fewer than in Braewood.
- **Halflings & Gnomes (5%)**: ~35k
    - Small villages dotted around farmland, known for skilled craft and horticulture.
- **Half-Orcs, Orcs & Goblins (3%)**: ~25k
    - Mostly integrated in frontier towns or as migrant workers; acceptance varies by local leadership.
- **Other Races (7%)**: ~55k
    - A diverse remainder, often living in larger port cities or traveling caravans.

### Notable Points

- Powerful **elven aristocracy** or monarchy; well-developed trade routes.
- Known for **diplomacy** and resource export (grain, wood, fish).

---

## Kluimont (Approx. 350k)

> **Island-based** kingdom in the northwest, spread across the main isle, the Rimdiff Isles, and a mainland strip.

- **Humans (40%)**: ~140k
    - Found in the main city (Kluimont) and trade hubs, often the “public face” of the kingdom.
- **Elves (5%)**: ~18k
    - Typically concentrated in scholarly or arcane enclaves; some families intermix with local nobility.
- **Dwarves (5%)**: ~18k
    - Fewer dwarves here than in Braewood, but still present as engineers, miners, or crafters.
- **Halflings & Gnomes (5%)**: ~18k
    - Found in smaller farming or fishing villages on the isles.
- **Half-Orcs, Orcs & Goblins (25%)**: ~88k
    - Notably large presence compared to other kingdoms, especially in **Rimdiff Isles** and on the **mainland strip**; Kluimont’s relatively tolerant policies attract them.
- **Other Races (20%)**: ~68k
    - Includes tieflings, genasi, dragonborn, and more “uncommon” peoples drawn by Kluimont’s **arcane reputation**.

### Notable Points

- **Arcane advancements** revolve around rumored subjugation or study of an ancient brass dragon in the western forest.
- The **weakest military** on paper but quite formidable in hidden magical might.
- Difficult to **enter** the main city (Kluimont proper) without an official invitation or approval.

## Lavalto Island (Approx. 100k)

> **Independent** volcanic isle in the center, with no allegiance to any kingdom.

- **Humans (60%)**: ~60k
    - Still the bulk of the working populace and merchants.
- **Elves (5%)**: ~5k
    - Some elven clans maintain small trade posts; others serve the ruling family in Darewaeton.
- **Dwarves (5%)**: ~5k
    - Typically involved in volcanic mining or metalwork near geothermal vents.
- **Halflings & Gnomes (5%)**: ~5k
    - Tinkerers, traders, or agriculturalists taking advantage of fertile volcanic soil.
- **Half-Orcs, Orcs & Goblins (10%)**: ~10k
    - Not uncommon as mercenaries or dock workers; the island’s “freeport” vibe draws all sorts.
- **Other Races (15%)**: ~15k
    - Includes tieflings, shapeshifters, or even more exotic races, plus a small fraction of **secret vampires** among the island’s ruling elite.

### Notable Points

- Dominated by the **vampiric noble family** controlling Darewaeton and two other settlements.
- Gains wealth from unique volcanic resources, exotic flora, and **ritual tourism** (for those in the know).
- Each major kingdom typically **avoids open conflict** with Lavalto, wary of rumored dark powers beneath the volcano.
