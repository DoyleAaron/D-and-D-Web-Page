> *Distances and spatial relationships for consistent worldbuilding*

## Kingdom Shape
Braewood is shaped like an **upside-down boot**:
- **Toe (Northeast):** Vorton peninsula
- **Heel (Northwest):** Slada area
- **Center:** Braewood City

## Trade Route Notes

### Why Vorton/Strane Are Bypassed
- Most trade with **Rimduff Isles** and **Kluimont** uses rivers
- Ships land at **Mortling** or **Phine** instead
- Land journey from Strane to capital is very long (~245 km via Barge)
- Peninsula location makes it a dead-end for through-traffic

### River Border
- Western border of Braewood with Kluimont is a **river**, not ocean
- This river is a key strategic/trade boundary
- Barge sits 61 km from this river border (to the west)

## Racial Distribution Notes (Northern Braewood)

### Dwarven Influence Pattern
- **More west = More republican/rebellious dwarves**
- **Barge:** High dwarven presence, tension with Crown
- **Strane/Vorton:** Isolated from dwarven politics, more diverse
- Peninsula isolation means less dwarven demographic impact

## Deep South Braewood (The Osegas Region)

### The Grand Span (Major POI)
*Also known as: Pontem inter Mundos (official), Tharnic Bridge (common)*
- **Names:** Originally the **Tharnic Bridge**; renamed **"Pontem inter Mundos"** ("Bridge between Worlds") by Queen Atheliana Aeristel of Islefield. Common folk still call it the Tharnic Bridge.
- **Length:** 20 km bridge connecting Braewood (Kadena) and Islefield (Rye)
- **Width:** 40+ meters (shops, taverns, inns, shrines along its length)
- **Significance:** Tharnic Empire's greatest engineering achievement
- **Security:** Most sophisticated in the country — weapons surrendered, wagons inspected
- **Control:** Jointly controlled by Braewood and Islefield with neutral zone at center
