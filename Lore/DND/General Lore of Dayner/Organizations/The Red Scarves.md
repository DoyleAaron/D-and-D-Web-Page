## Description

The **Red Scarves** are a charitable religious organization based in Sheyton, known for their work feeding the hungry and helping the downtrodden. To most citizens, they appear as a benevolent group doing genuine good in impoverished areas.

However, beneath this charitable facade lies something darker. The organization employs magical compulsion to influence followers and has connections to mysterious powers that even members don't fully understand.

## Leadership

### Mother Selyse
The spiritual leader of the Red Scarves, Mother Selyse is a woman of indeterminate age with an unsettling presence. She:
- Gives passionate sermons about charity and community
- Wears flowing red robes and speaks with hypnotic cadence
- Claims to channel divine power (the true source is unclear)
- Commands fierce loyalty from her followers

### Cleric Veyner Drosk
A mid-ranking priest within the organization who serves as one of Mother Selyse's trusted agents. Notable for:
- Possessing a **magical pendant** that creates confusion and compulsion in targets
- Operating in the field to recruit and "convince" potential members
- Carrying out missions that require more... persuasive methods

## Activities

### Public Face
- **Soup kitchens** for Sheyton's poor
- **Clothing distribution** (red scarves prominently featured)
- **Shelter** for the homeless
- **Community organizing** in impoverished districts

### Hidden Activities
- Mind control through magical items and possibly ritual magic
- Recruitment of vulnerable individuals
- Unknown connections to darker powers
- Possible ties to blood magic or vampirism (unconfirmed)

## History

### Origins
The Red Scarves emerged in Sheyton sometime in recent decades. Their origins are murky—whether Mother Selyse founded the group or inherited it is unknown.

### Session 2 Encounter (1225 C.C.)
The party encountered the Red Scarves while investigating trouble in Sheyton. Cleric Veyner Drosk attempted to use his confusion pendant on party members. The encounter revealed the organization's true nature—beneath the charity work lay magical coercion.

## Beliefs

The Red Scarves preach:
- **Community over self:** Share with those in need
- **The Red Thread:** All members are connected by fate
- **Submission to guidance:** Trust in Mother Selyse's wisdom
- **Sacrifice for the greater good:** Give of yourself freely

Whether these beliefs mask worship of a specific deity or power remains unclear. Some scholars note similarities to Shar's doctrine (hidden truths, secrets) while others see echoes of older, stranger faiths.

## Membership

Members are identified by their **red scarves**, worn visibly at all times. Deeper members may have additional markings or jewelry.

| Rank | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Petitioners** | Recipients of charity, potential recruits |
| **Brothers/Sisters** | Initiated members who serve in daily work |
| **Deacons** | Trusted members with leadership roles |
| **Clerics** | Inner circle with access to magical tools |
| **Mother/Father** | Title reserved for supreme leadership |

## Relationships

- **Sheyton authorities:** Tolerated due to genuine charitable work
- **Islefield Crown:** Unaware of darker aspects
- **General public:** Viewed as helpful, if eccentric, do-gooders
- **The party:** Hostile after Session 2 confrontation
- **Unknown patron:** The true source of their power remains hidden

## Locations

### Sheyton Chapter House
Their primary base of operations, a converted warehouse in Sheyton's poorer districts. Features:
- Large hall for sermons and community meals
- Private chambers for leadership
- Basement areas (restricted access)
