## Description

The **Wayfarers' Accord** is the largest and most respected guild of mercenaries, investigators, monster hunters, and problem-solvers on the continent. With licensed chapters in most cities and towns, the Accord provides a framework for adventurers to find work and for clients to find reliable help.

Guild halls are recognizable by their **golden compass sigil**—a reminder that members travel where the work takes them.

## History

The Accord was founded in **Adaham** over two centuries ago, when a group of veteran adventurers grew tired of being cheated by clients and blamed for problems they didn't cause. They established a system of contracts, rankings, and mutual protection that has since spread across the continent.

Today, the Accord operates chapters in every major city and most towns. While individual chapters maintain some independence, all follow the same core rules and honor each other's credentials.

## Motto

> *"For coin, for cause, for completion."*

## Membership

### Benefits
- **Travel Papers:** Guild credentials grant easier passage between kingdoms
- **Guild Lodging:** Discounted rates at affiliated inns across the continent
- **Information Network:** Access to job boards in any chapter
- **Legal Protection:** Guild contracts are legally binding; payment is guaranteed
- **Insurance:** Partial death benefits for registered next-of-kin
- **Equipment Access:** Discounts at guild-affiliated smiths and suppliers

### Requirements
- **Registration Fee:** 10 gold pieces (one-time)
- **Annual Dues:** 5 gold pieces (waived if 3+ jobs completed per year)
- **Code of Conduct:** Members must honor contracts, treat clients fairly, and not bring dishonor to the guild

## The Tier System

The Accord ranks both individual members and registered parties using a tier system that determines what jobs they can accept.

### Tier Rankings

| Tier | Name | Risk Level | Typical Jobs |
|------|------|------------|--------------|
| **E** | Errands | Minimal | Deliveries, pet-sitting, basic labor |
| **D** | Difficulties | Low | Petty crime investigation, pest control, safe escort |
| **C** | Challenges | Moderate | Murder investigations, dangerous escorts, creature tracking |
| **B** | Battles | High | Monster hunts, criminal organizations, war-adjacent work |
| **A** | Annihilations | Extreme | Major monster exterminations, dungeon clearing, kingdom threats |
| **S** | Sagas | Legendary | World-altering tasks, multi-year quests, planar threats |

### Ranking Up

**Individual Ranking:**
- Complete 10 solo jobs at your current tier to advance to the next

**Party Ranking:**
- Registered parties start at D-tier
- Complete 1 job from the tier above to advance (e.g., complete a C-tier job to become C-tier)

**S-Tier Access:**
- Requires completion of at least 3 A-tier jobs
- Must present yourself at a Capital Board and be formally accepted
- S-tier jobs may take years or even decades to complete

### Job Access Rules

Members can accept jobs at their current tier and **one tier above** (as a challenge/advancement opportunity). Higher tier jobs are locked until you prove yourself.

## The Job Board System

Every guild chapter maintains a job board where contracts are posted. Jobs are categorized by tier and include:
- Client name (or "Anonymous" for sensitive work)
- Task description
- Duration estimate
- Reward amount
- Any special requirements or warnings

### Posting Fees

Clients pay fees to post jobs, which determines how widely the job is advertised:

| Tier | Posting Fee | Visibility |
|------|-------------|------------|
| E | 5 gp | Local chapter only |
| D | 25 gp | Local + nearby towns |
| C | 100 gp | Regional (within kingdom) |
| B | 500 gp | Kingdom-wide |
| A | 2,000 gp | Multi-kingdom (major cities) |
| S | 10,000 gp | All capital boards |

## Notable Chapters

### Wavecrest Hall (Dawerton)
A converted warehouse on the docks, three stories tall with the guild's golden compass above the entrance. Known for maritime contracts and monster hunting jobs related to Lavalto Lake.

### The Iron Compass (Braewood City)
The largest chapter on the continent, located near the military district. Handles many Crown-contracted jobs and military support work.

### The Gilded Quill (Islefield City)
An elegant chapter house that specializes in investigation, diplomatic escort, and artifact recovery. Known for its extensive archives.

### The Hidden Hand (Kluimont City)
The most secretive chapter, befitting its home. Specializes in arcane recovery, planar investigation, and jobs that require... discretion.

## Famous S-Tier Postings

While specific S-tier jobs are confidential, some have become legendary:

- **The Trinity of Balthazar:** A quest to locate three legendary artifacts. Posted for 47 years.
- **The Sealed God:** An ongoing mission to prevent the return of a dark deity. Posted for 112 years.
- **The Dragon Concordat:** A diplomatic mission to the dragon flights of the Northern Reaches. Multiple failures recorded.

## Relationships

- **The Crown of Braewood:** Strong working relationship; many military contracts
- **Islefield Throne:** Respectful cooperation, particularly for trade protection
- **Kluimont Synod:** Cautious partnership; some jobs are "off the books"
- **Lavalto Rulers:** Professional distance; the guild operates there but asks few questions

## Notable Quote

> — Guildmaster Tobren Ashford, Wavecrest Hall (Dawerton)
