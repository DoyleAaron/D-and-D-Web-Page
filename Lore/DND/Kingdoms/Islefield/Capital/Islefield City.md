>_All star ratings (Wealth, Military, Happiness) are in **perspective to other settlements of the same size category**._

### **Name:**

Islefield City

### **Kingdom:**

Islefield

### **Leader/Queen:**

Queen Atheliana Aeristel

### **Population:**

**200,000** (second-largest city on the continent, sprawling port city and trade hub)

### **Wealth:**

★★★★★ (5 stars)

- The wealthiest city on the continent.
    
- Ancient elven houses own vast trade fleets, fertile farmland, and export operations for timber, grains, and exotic goods.
    
- Merchant guilds thrive under stable governance; the port sees constant trade from across the kingdoms.
    

### **Military:**

★★★ (3 stars)

- Strong enough to defend the city and its trade routes, but less focused on large armies and more on navy strength and elite royal guards.
    
- The elven navy patrols surrounding waters, but land-based forces are moderate and largely ceremonial.
    

### **Happiness:**

★★★★½ (4.5 stars)

- High prosperity, cultural richness, and political stability ensure widespread satisfaction.
    
- Generous social programs for citizens, thriving festivals celebrating harvests and sea voyages, and opportunities for artisans and merchants to rise in status.
    
- Some mild class tension between ancient elven houses and human merchants, but largely peaceful.
    

### **History:**

- Founded by elven nobility in the aftermath of the War of the Sundered Chains, Islefield City became the crown jewel of trade and diplomacy.
    
- Expanded rapidly after the Great Resource Dispute, solidifying control over southern trade routes and becoming the nexus of commerce between kingdoms.
    
- Known for its grand marble structures, vine-wrapped towers, and wide markets where exotic goods from distant lands can be found.
