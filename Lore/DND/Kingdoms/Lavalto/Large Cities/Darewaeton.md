>_All star ratings (Wealth, Military, Happiness) are in **perspective to other settlements of the same size category**._

### **Name:**

Darewaeton

### **Kingdom:**

None — **Independent Isle (Lavalto)**

### **Leader:**

Lord Adrian Silwynn (Vampiric Patriarch)

### **Population:**

**85,000** (the largest and most infamous city on the isle, acting as a trade hub and the seat of vampiric power)

### **Wealth:**

★★★★ (4 stars)

- The vampiric nobility control vast wealth from rare volcanic minerals, exotic flora, and “exclusive” trade routes.
    
- Merchant classes are prosperous, though never truly free of the ruling family’s shadow.
    
- The common populace pays heavy “protection” taxes but benefits from steady trade and enforced safety.
    

### **Military:**

★★★½ (3.5 stars)

- Small standing army, but bolstered by dark magic, vampiric thralls, and enchanted guardians.
    
- Coastal defenses and cunning sabotage capabilities make invasion nearly impossible.
    
- Secret networks of assassins and spies ensure threats are neutralized from within.
    

### **Happiness:**

★★ (2 stars)

- The upper classes and merchants are content, protected by their loyalty.
    
- Rural citizens live in quiet fear of vampiric rituals and blood taxes, though they benefit from the city’s relative safety and commerce.
    
- Frequent festivals and mysterious masquerades distract from the underlying tension.
    

### **History:**

- Founded on the fertile volcanic slopes as a neutral trading port between kingdoms.
    
- The ruling family, secretly vampiric, consolidated control over two centuries and transformed Darewaeton into a bastion of secrecy and dark luxury.
    
- Known for towering basalt spires, smoky streets lit by enchanted lanterns, and whispers of catacombs beneath the city that lead to ancient lava-choked chambers.
