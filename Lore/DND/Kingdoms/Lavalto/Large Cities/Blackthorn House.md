## Description

Blackthorn House is a well-kept two-story manor with an attic and full basement, surrounded by dead rose hedges and wrought-iron fencing. By appearance it is a wealthy private estate. In reality it serves as a hunting nest and logistics point for the Valdris brood.

The household uses controlled human servants wearing rune-etched metal collars. These servants move with minimal expression and are often positioned at chokepoints to slow intruders before the family engages.

## Internal Layout

- **Ground Floor:** Foyer, dining hall, study, kitchen, servant passages
- **Top Floor:** Family rooms, gallery hall, locked reliquary room
- **Attic:** Sealed storage, occult traces, hidden trunks
- **Basement:** Cells, ritual spaces, blood channels, secure vault

## Role in Current Arc

- Base of operations for vampire raids linked to recent Dawerton incidents.
- Source location for intelligence on Maltrius-linked pressure operations.
- Possible recovery site for enchanted items and control-collar evidence.

## Notable Detail

Visitors often report hearing polite housekeeping sounds from within even when the house should be empty.
