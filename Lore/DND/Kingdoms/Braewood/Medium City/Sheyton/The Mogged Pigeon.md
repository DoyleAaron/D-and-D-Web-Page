## Description

The **Mogged Pigeon** is a cramped, smoky tavern with low ceilings and crooked beams. The floor is sticky with spilled ale, and the faint smell of roast boar mixes with damp wood. It serves as a gathering place for dockworkers, market laborers, and those who prefer their drinking establishments without pretense.

This was the starting location of the party's adventures—the place where fate first brought them together.

## Atmosphere

The tavern embodies Sheyton's working-class character:
- **Cramped interior** with crooked beams and low ceilings
- **Sticky floor** from decades of spilled ale
- **Mixed scents** of roast boar and damp wood
- **Low-burning hearth** throwing long shadows
- **Constant noise** of dice cracking and mugs sloshing

Patrons are mostly locals from the docks and market—tired workers drinking off the day. A few shady figures typically occupy the back tables, speaking in hushed tones. A one-eyed bard plucks a lute in the corner—badly.

## Key NPCs

### Tilda Greybrow (Owner/Barmaid)
- **Personality:** Gruff, practical, knows everyone's business but keeps it close to her chest
- **Motivation:** Run her tavern without trouble; suspicious of newcomers
- **Voice:** Dry, sharp tongue
- **Notable Lines:**
  - *"Coin first, chatter later."*
  - *"Don't bring trouble through my door. The Guard hardly pays me to clean up corpses."*

### Old Brann (Regular Patron)
- **Personality:** Loud, friendly drunk who latches onto strangers
- **Motivation:** Wants company for drinking; will spill rumors if encouraged
- **Knowledge:** 
  - Guards have been more aggressive in Sheyton lately
  - Thieves are being pressured by "folk above their station"
  - Talk of Kluimont stirring things up

## Campaign Significance

The Mogged Pigeon served as the launching point for the party's adventures. It was here that they first met and received their initial call to action when a young girl named **Lysa** burst through the doors, tearfully begging for help to recover her mother's stolen locket.

### Session 1 Events
- The party gathered in the tavern
- Lysa entered, pleading for help with thieves who stole her mother's locket
- This led to the party's first adventure, pursuing the thieves through Sheyton

## Location

The Mogged Pigeon is located in Sheyton's working-class district, convenient to both the docks and the market. Its position makes it a natural crossroads for rumors, travelers, and those who prefer anonymity.
