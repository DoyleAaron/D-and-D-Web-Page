### **Name:**

Phine
### **Kingdom:**

Braewood

### **Population:**

**90,000** (4th largest large city in the kingdom; narrowly behind Eigend in Kluimont)

### **Wealth:**

★★★ (3 stars)

- Modest trade and wealth; isolation limits its economic reach.
    
- Relies on local production, small trade caravans, and self-sufficiency.
    

### **Military:**

★★★½ (3.5 stars)

- Maintains a capable defense force but lacks the overwhelming focus seen in Mortling.
    
- Distance from the capital reduces military urgency.
    

### **Happiness:**

★★★★½ (4.5 stars)

- The city’s independence and self-reliance foster high morale and civic pride.
    
- Community bonds are strong, and citizens enjoy freedom from external interference.
