[Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)

Large City

### **Leader:**
**Lord-Governor Callum Westmarch** — A Crown-appointed administrator in his 60s, responsible for managing the delicate border relationship with [Kluimont](../../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md). Known for patient diplomacy and iron-willed negotiations. Despite his Crown title, he governs with a light touch, understanding that Phine's people value their independence.

**~90,000** (4th largest city in the kingdom)

### **Racial Demographics:**
- **Humans (58%):** [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) loyalists, Crown officials, merchants
- **Dwarves (22%):** Significant population, primarily smiths and craftsmen
- **Half-Elves (8%):** Border proximity brings Kluimontian mixing
- **Halflings (6%):** River traders and dock workers

### **Geography & Climate:**

**Position:**
Phine occupies a narrow wedge of land squeezed between water on three sides:
- **North:** The northern ocean (immediate coast)
- **South:** The Vael River ([Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)-[Kluimont](../../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) border)
- **West:** 23km to the western coast; the border curves west then south here
- **East:** 90km to [Barge](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md), 160km to [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) City, 190km to the east coast

**The Westbridge:**
One of only two bridges connecting [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) to [Kluimont](../../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md), the Westbridge is a massive stone construction spanning the Vael River. Heavily fortified on both ends, it sees constant traffic — merchants, diplomats, and spies all cross here. The Phine side features twin gatehouse towers and a permanent garrison. The only other crossing is far to the south.

**Key Distances:**
- **To Barge:** 90km east (slightly north)
- **To Braewood City:** 160km east-southeast
- **To Kluimont Border:** 10km west (the river)
- **To Western Coast:** 23km west
- **To East Coast:** 190km

**Terrain:**
The land around Phine is flat river delta, prone to seasonal flooding. Rich soil supports farming when not underwater. The city itself is built on slightly raised ground, with extensive drainage systems dating to Tharnic times. Small fishing villages dot the coastline in both directions.

**Climate:**
Maritime, moderated by both ocean and river. Mild winters, cool summers. Fog is common, especially in autumn. The wind rarely stops — Phine is famous for its gusts.

### **Economy & Trade:**

**Primary Industries:**
- **Border Trade:** The legal crossing point for [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)-[Kluimont](../../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md) commerce
- **Toll Collection:** Crossing fees fund both city and Crown coffers
- **Fishing:** Northern ocean and river provide abundant catch
- **Shipbuilding:** Small but skilled yards produce river vessels
- **Warehousing:** Storage for goods awaiting inspection or transport

**The Phine Docks:**
Phine maintains docks on both the river (south) and the ocean (north) side. Ocean-going vessels are rare — the northern waters are treacherous and most maritime trade uses Mortling on the east coast. The river docks see far more traffic, primarily barges moving goods to and from [Kluimont](../../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md).

**Trade Networks:**
- **West (Kluimont):** Arcane goods, exotic materials, occasional black market items (smuggled)
- **East (Barge/Capital):** Manufactured goods, weapons, grain, finished products
- **North/South (Coasts):** Fish, salt, maritime supplies

**Smuggling:**
The border makes smuggling inevitable. Crown officials fight a constant battle against illicit trade. The Masked Lanterns of [Barge](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md) have connections here. Kluimontian agents operate with varying degrees of subtlety.

### **History:**

**Tharnic Era (pre-730 C.C.):**
The Tharnic Empire built the original Westbridge as part of their continental road network. Phine was a garrison town protecting the crossing and taxing traffic. The drainage systems that still function today date from this period.

**Post-Empire Period (730-900 C.C.):**
When the empire fell, Phine became contested territory. For decades, it changed hands between early [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) and proto-Kluimontian factions. Eventually [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) secured it, establishing the border at the river.

**The Border Wars (circa 850-900 C.C.):**
Multiple conflicts over the bridge and surrounding territory. Both kingdoms learned the cost of direct assault on fortified crossings. Peace treaties eventually established the current arrangement — the bridge is shared, the banks are sovereign.

**Modern Era:**
Phine has grown wealthy on legal trade while losing untold revenue to smuggling. The Crown treats it as a security priority but has learned that the locals respond better to autonomy than to heavy-handed rule. The Lord-Governor position was created specifically for Phine — a title that acknowledges Crown authority while granting unusual independence.

### **Notable Locations:**

- **The Westbridge Gatehouse:** Twin fortified towers marking [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)'s side of the bridge. The crossing point is narrow, easily defended, and always watched.
- **The Governor's Compound:** Lord-Governor [Westmarch](../../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Lord-Governor%20Callum%20Westmarch.md)'s official residence and administrative center. Notably modest compared to other Crown buildings — a deliberate choice.
- **The Windmarket:** Open-air trading plaza near the docks, named for Phine's constant breezes. Merchants from both kingdoms meet here.
- **The River Walk:** A promenade along the Vael, offering views of [Kluimont](../../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md)'s southern bank. Popular at sunset.
- **The Watchers' Spire:** Ancient Tharnic lighthouse, still operational. Guides ships approaching from the north.
- **The Phine River Docks:** Busy wharves handling [barge](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md) traffic to and from [Kluimont](../../../Kingdoms/Kluimont/Kluimont.md).

### **Relationships:**

**With Other Settlements:**
- **Barge:** 90km east; Phine goods flow through [Barge](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Barge.md) to the capital; friendly rivalry for northern trade dominance
- **Braewood City:** Crown authority flows from the capital; Phine accepts taxation as necessary but guards its autonomy
- **Kluimont:** Across the bridge; officially foreign, practically neighbors; complex relationship of trade and suspicion
- **Adaham:** Smaller port village to the east along the river; part of Phine's economic sphere

**With the Crown:**
Phine is strategically vital, which means Crown presence is unavoidable. However, centuries of experience have taught the Crown that Phine responds better to autonomy than control. The Lord-Governor position exists nowhere else in [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md).

### **Current Events & Hooks:**

- **Bridge Incident:** A Kluimontian diplomat was murdered on [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)'s side of the bridge last month. Tensions are high. War seems unlikely but not impossible.
- **The Fog Ships:** Vessels have been seen in the northern waters during fog — ships that don't appear in any port records and don't respond to signals.
- **Smuggling Network:** Commander [Coldwell](../../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Bridge-Commander%20Serena%20Coldwell.md) believes a major smuggling operation is using the city docks. She's right but can't prove it without revealing her source.
- **The Locked Warehouse:** A building in the docks district has been sealed for thirty years. The previous Lord-Governor ordered it closed. No one remembers why.
