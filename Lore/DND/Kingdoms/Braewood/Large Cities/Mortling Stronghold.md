>_All star ratings (Wealth, Military, Happiness) are in **perspective to other settlements of the same size category**._

### **Name:**

Mortling Stronghold
### **Kingdom:**

Braewood

### **Population:**

**80,000** (smallest of Braewood’s large cities; only Darewaeton in Lavalto is larger)

### **Wealth:**

★★★☆☆ (3 stars)

- Wealth is secondary to defense.
    
- Most resources are funneled into fortifications, armaments, and military research.
    

### **Military:**

★★★★★ (5 stars — the most militarized large city in the kingdom)

- Serves as the first line of defense against Kluimont, with massive walls, barracks, and siege weapon stockpiles.
    
- Nearly every family has military ties and is expected to make personal sacrifices for the kingdom’s safety.
    

### **Happiness:**

★★★½ (3.5 stars)

- Despite financial limitations, pride in purpose and duty keeps morale steady.
    
- Community spirit is strong, bolstered by shared values of service and resilience.
