>_All star ratings (Wealth, Military, Happiness) are in **perspective to other settlements of the same size category**._

### **Name:**

Braewood City

### **Kingdom:**

Braewood

### **Leader/King:**

King Tarvin Braeden IV

### **Population:**

**220,000** (largest city in the country and kingdom capital)

### **Wealth:**

★★★½ (3.5 stars)

- Immense wealth is concentrated in royal courts, noble estates, and major merchant guilds.
    
- Large swaths of poor districts and crowded worker neighborhoods bring the average down.
    
- Known for opulent trade markets and imposing noble quarters.
    

### **Military:**

★★★★★ (5 stars)

- The most fortified and militarized city on the continent.
    
- Headquarters of Braewood’s grand army, home to elite royal guards and massive garrisons.
    
- Streets often filled with parades, drills, and recruitment campaigns.
    

### **Happiness:**

★★★½ (3.5)

- General stability due to strong governance and protection.
    
- Prosperity for those in the merchant and military classes; hope for social mobility keeps unrest limited.
    
- However, poorer districts show signs of discontent beneath the surface.
    

### **History:**

- Founded by the Braeden warlord line as a river fortress before expanding into the kingdom’s heart.
    
- Survived civil wars, rebellions, and countless border conflicts, becoming a symbol of unwavering power.
    
- Famous for grand festivals celebrating military victories and trade prosperity, but also known for secret political intrigue in its noble courts.
