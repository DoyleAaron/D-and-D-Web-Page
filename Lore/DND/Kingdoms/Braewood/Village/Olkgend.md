>_All star ratings (Wealth, Military, Happiness) are in **perspective to other settlements of the same size category**._

### **Name:**
Olkgend

### **Kingdom:**
[Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)

### **Settlement Type:**
Village

### **Leader:**
**Drill-Master Vance Korrath** — A grizzled human veteran in his early sixties who retired from the Span Wardens to train the next generation. His missing left hand (lost to a Kluimontian raider) does nothing to diminish his authority. Every soldier who guards [the Grand Span](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/POI/The%20Grand%20Span.md) learned their trade under his instruction.

### **Population:**
**~1,200** permanent residents; **~800** trainees at any given time. The population fluctuates significantly as recruit classes cycle through.

### **Racial Demographics:**
- **Human (70%):** Most trainees and permanent staff. The backbone of the military.
- **Dwarf (15%):** Weapons masters and siege engineers. Highly respected.
- **Halfling (8%):** Support staff—cooks, quartermasters, healers. Essential but underappreciated.
- **Other (7%):** Mixed recruits from across [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md); occasionally elven observers from [Islefield](../../../Kingdoms/Islefield/Islefield.md).

---

### **Geography & Climate:**

**Position:**
- Located 10km south of the [Gitstin](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md)-[Kadena](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md) road (branch at the 35km mark from [Gitstin](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md), 15km from [Kadena](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md)).
- Approximately 45km from [Kadena](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md) via road, 45km from [Gitstin](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md) via road.
- Positioned on elevated ground with clear sightlines in all directions—chosen for defensive training.
- 25 km south of the Great [Lavalto](../../../Kingdoms/Lavalto/Lavalto.md) Lake shore.

**Road Network:**
- **To Gitstin-Kadena Road (10km N):** Branch road connecting to the main route.
- **To Kadena (45km NW via road):** Primary connection to the bridge city.
- **To Gitstin (35km NE via road then branch):** Secondary connection to the trade hub.
- **No direct road to Trares (30km S):** The terrain is too rough; travelers must go through [Gitstin](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md) or [Kadena](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md).

**Distances (Straight Line):**
- North: 25 km to the Great [Lavalto](../../../Kingdoms/Lavalto/Lavalto.md) Lake coast
- South: 45 km to the southern coast
- East: 135 km to the eastern coast
- West: 50 km to the bridge ([Kadena](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md))

**Terrain:**
- High ground surrounded by scrubland and sparse woodland.
- Training grounds are intentionally varied—hills, ravines, and open fields.
- Stone quarries nearby provide building material and training obstacles.

**Climate:**
- Warm and dry; hot summers require dawn training to avoid heat exhaustion.
- Winters are mild but include deliberate cold-weather exercises.
- Exposure to the elements is considered part of training.

### **Wealth:**
★★☆☆☆ (2 stars)

- Olkgend runs on Crown military funding, not commerce.
- Trainees live simply; luxury is discouraged.
- The village economy consists of supplying training needs.
- Permanent residents are modestly comfortable but not wealthy.

---

### **Military:**
★★★★★ (5 stars)

- The most militarized village in [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md)—its entire purpose is training soldiers.
- Produces the elite Span Wardens who guard [the Grand Span](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/POI/The%20Grand%20Span.md).
- Training facilities include obstacle courses, siege towers, archery ranges, and sparring yards.
- Every permanent resident has military training; even the cooks can fight.
- The village could hold against an army ten times its size.

---

### **Happiness:**
★★★☆☆ (3 stars)

- Residents take pride in their essential role, but life is hard.
- Trainees endure grueling schedules; complaints are not tolerated.
- Permanent residents accept the military lifestyle as the price of purpose.
- Strong camaraderie among those who survive training.
- Limited entertainment—the tavern is popular, but fighting outside the ring is forbidden.

---

### **Economy & Trade:**

**Primary Industries:**
- **Military Training:** The village's sole purpose and industry.
- **Equipment Maintenance:** Smithies, armorers, and weaponsmiths serve the training program.
- **Quarrying:** Stone from local quarries builds fortifications and training obstacles.
- **Provisioning:** Food and supplies come from [Gitstin](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Town/Gitstin.md) and [Kadena](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Medium%20City/Kadena.md).

**Trade Relationships:**
- **Kadena (45km NW):** Provides funding, receives trained soldiers.
- **Gitstin (35km NE):** Supplies food and goods; soldiers go there for leave.
- **Braewood Crown:** Direct oversight of training standards and recruitment.

---

### **History:**

**Tharnic Era:**
- The site was a Tharnic military outpost guarding the bridge approaches.
- When the Empire fell, the fortifications were abandoned.

**Post-Tharnic Era:**
- As [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) and [Islefield](../../../Kingdoms/Islefield/Islefield.md) established joint bridge control, the need for trained guards became apparent.
- Olkgend was re-established as a training village roughly 300 years ago.
- The Span Warden tradition emerged here, creating an elite corps loyal to the bridge itself.

**Recent History:**
- [Drill-Master Vance Korrath](../../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Drill-Master%20Vance%20Korrath.md) took command fifteen years ago.
- Training standards have become the highest in the kingdom.
- Graduates are sought after by noble households and Crown forces alike.
- A recent proposal to expand the facility was approved; construction begins next spring.

---

### **Notable Locations:**

- **The Proving Grounds:** Vast training fields where recruits drill from dawn to dusk. Obstacle courses, sparring circles, and archery ranges cover the area.

- **The Crucible:** An advanced training facility simulating bridge combat—narrow walkways, limited visibility, and simulated river crossings.

- **Korrath's Hall:** Drill-Master [Vance](../../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Harbormaster%20Pel%20Vance.md)'s headquarters and the village administrative center. Contains the Hall of Names, listing every Span Warden who died in service.

- **The Forge Row:** Smithies where weapons and armor are made and repaired. Dwarf masters train apprentices in military metalwork.

- **The Last Drink:** The only tavern in Olkgend. Fighting is strictly forbidden—violators run the Crucible in full armor.

### **Training Notes:**

**The Span Warden Program:**
- Six months of basic training: physical conditioning, weapons drill, formation fighting.
- Six months of bridge specialization: checkpoint procedures, crowd control, narrow-space combat.
- Final assessment: survive a week on the Crucible with minimal supplies.
- Graduates receive the Warden's Pin and assignment to [the Grand Span](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/POI/The%20Grand%20Span.md).

**Washout Rate:**
- Roughly 40% of recruits fail to complete the program.
- Failed recruits may join regular [Braewood](../../../Kingdoms/Braewood/Braewood.md) forces or return home.
- There is no shame in failing—only in not trying.

**The Korrath Method:**
- "Train until you break, then train some more."
- Drill-Master [Korrath](../../../Character%20Lore/Modern%20Characters/Drill-Master%20Vance%20Korrath.md) believes comfort breeds weakness.
- His graduates are the most disciplined soldiers in the kingdom.
