>_All star ratings (Wealth, Military, Happiness) are in **perspective to other settlements of the same size category**._

### **Name:**

Kluimont City

### **Kingdom:**

Kluimont

### **Leader/High Archon:**

High Archon Lysanthe Kilmora

### **Population:**

**180,000** (the most mysterious and secretive of the capitals, heavily fortified and cloaked in arcane wards)

### **Wealth:**

★★★★ (4 stars)

- Wealth flows through arcane trade, smuggling routes, and magical artifact creation.
    
- The Arcane Synod and elite mage families command immense hidden fortunes.
    
- Common folk and monstrous enclaves fare modestly but benefit from the city's magical defenses and controlled commerce.
    

### **Military:**

★★★½ (3.5 stars)

- Low on raw military numbers, but arcane might more than compensates.
    
- The city’s true defense lies in powerful wards, summoned guardians, and mage legions that can be unleashed in times of crisis.
    
- A small but elite navy and infiltration teams patrol the surrounding seas and isles.
    

### **Happiness:**

★★★★ (4 stars)

- Citizens enjoy safety and magical conveniences, with lower-class folk generally protected under the rule of secrecy and order.
    
- Monstrous races (goblins, orcs, tieflings) find rare acceptance here, fostering loyalty.
    
- Some fear the hidden dragon and the Arcane Synod’s experiments but see them as necessary evils for protection and prosperity.
    

### **History:**

- Established as a pirate haven and mage conclave in the post-imperial chaos, Kluimont evolved into an arcane city-state.
    
- Over centuries, mages consolidated power, and the city became a fortress of secrecy and magical learning.
    
- Known for its soaring crystal spires, floating arcane orbs, and marketplaces where enchanted items change hands in secretive twilight deals.
