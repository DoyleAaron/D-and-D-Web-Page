>_All star ratings (Wealth, Military, Happiness) are in **perspective to other settlements of the same size category**._

### **Name:**

Eimgend

### **Kingdom:**

Kluimont

### **Population:**

**92,000** (3rd largest large city on the continent)

### **Wealth:**

★★★★ (4 stars)

- Wealth flows through arcane exports, shipbuilding, and controlled maritime trade.
    
- Mage academies and arcane guilds dominate the city’s economy.
    

### **Military:**

★★★★½ (4.5 stars)

- The largest naval fleet outside of Kluimont’s capital city is stationed here.
    
- Military presence includes arcane defenses, enchanted warships, and teleportation anchors.
    

### **Happiness:**

★★★ (3 stars)

- Mages and arcane scholars live luxuriously, but common folk are often overlooked.
    
- Social stratification is pronounced; non-magical citizens are content but disconnected from city leadership.
