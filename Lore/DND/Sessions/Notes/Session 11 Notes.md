## **1. Accepting Liora’s Offer**

- The party returns to **Liora** and formally **accepts her proposed job**.
    
- Payment is unconventional: **three questions**, to be answered truthfully by Liora and her network.
    

### **The Agreed Questions**

1. **Information on Balthazar** — his history, works, and known movements.
    
2. **Relations between Dawerton and Kluimont** — political tensions, trade, and hidden conflicts.
    
3. **Relations between Dawerton and Islefield** — alliances, grievances, and trust.
    

- Liora agrees, emphasizing discretion and results.
    

---

## **2. Beginning the Investigation**

- The party begins investigating disturbances within **Dawerton**.
    
- Early information gathering reveals a troubling pattern:
    
    - Multiple citizens across different districts report **shared nightmares**.
        
    - Descriptions are consistent despite no contact between witnesses.
        

---

## **3. The Nightmares**

- Common elements reported:
    
    - A sense of pressure or suffocation.
        
    - Familiar voices calling from the dark.
        
    - Dreams ending abruptly with fear or grief.
        
- The shared nature suggests:
    
    - **External influence**, not coincidence.
        
    - Possible **ritual magic**, curse, or psychic effect radiating through the city.
        

---

## **4. The Apparition**

- A young girl reports seeing:
    
    - **Her deceased grandfather** walking the streets at night.
        
    - The figure did not speak, but watched silently before vanishing.
        
- Other witnesses confirm sightings of:
    
    - Familiar dead figures,
        
    - Seen briefly in alleys or near homes,
        
    - Always at night.
