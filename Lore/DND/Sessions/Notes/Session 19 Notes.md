# **📘 Session 19 Summary - "Backburner and Bloodstone"**

## **1. Strategic Shift at the Docks**

- **Morjor** and **Amdir** discuss their next move and choose to put the **Spehlina mission** on the backburner for now.
- Instead of pushing the main arc immediately, they decide to focus on **smaller Wayfarers' Accord tasks**.
- Their new short-term plan is to **head to the capital** and regroup around lower-risk contracts.

---

## **2. Varin Stays Behind**

- **Varin** separates from the group and remains behind to handle business in town.
- From the **2,000 gp** earned from **Captain Kirk**, he gives **1,000 gp** to **Rekka**.
- Purpose of the payment: support and watch over the **surviving goblins and dwarves**.

---

## **3. The Gemstone Purchases**

- Varin spends the remaining gold on spell-oriented jewelry.
- He buys:
  - **One topaz necklace**
  - **One red-gemmed necklace** from a young merchant
- At the time of purchase, the merchant appears ordinary.

---

## **4. Hidden Threat: The Cursed Necklace**

- The young merchant is secretly a **vampire**.
- The red-gemmed necklace is actually a **suppressed cursed relic**.
- The relic is tied to the **Sealed God** beneath **Lavalto Volcano**, whose return is being pursued by **Father Maltrius Kael**, the vampiric high priest of **Asmodeus**.
- Varin does not realize he has just put on a long-game curse trigger.

---

## **5. Divine Consequences for Varin**

- The cursed necklace begins to interfere with Varin's divine channeling.
- Immediate effect direction:
  - It hinders his **cleric magic** reliability.
  - It disrupts his ability to commune clearly with **Bahamut**.
- This sets up a spiritual pressure arc as his faith and power become unstable.

---

## **6. Unseen Eyes**

- While the party reorients, the **Queen is actively scrying on them**.
- Their movements, choices, and alliances are now under royal magical surveillance.
- Session closes under a tone of political and supernatural tension.
