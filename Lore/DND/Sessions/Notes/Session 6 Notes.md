# **📘 Session 6 Summary – “The Book’s First Secret”**

## **1. Return to Sheyton**

- The party reappears in Sheyton after the vault’s teleportation.
    
- They immediately begin investigating **how to open Balthazar’s Book**.
    
- The day becomes an **information-gathering crawl** through Sheyton’s limited arcane resources.
    

---

## **2. Library Research**

- They visit the local library to search for anything referencing:
    
    - Balthazar,
        
    - Star charts,
        
    - Ancient vaults,
        
    - Magical codices.
        
- **Amdir charms the receptionist**, gaining unrestricted access to the private stacks.
    
- Despite searching, information is fragmented—Balthazar’s work predates most recorded archives.
    

---

## **3. The Wizard’s Tower**

- The group heads to Sheyton’s modest **wizard tower** for expert insight.
    
- After questioning the resident mages, they learn a crucial detail:
    

### **How to Open Balthazar’s Codex**

> **The Book can only be opened when it is submerged in water that has touched a magical artefact gifted by Balthazar.  
> The water must carry the “signature” of one of his relics.**

- This links directly to the **fountain water** from the Quad Vault—the water that touched their sacrifices and the fountain’s rewards.
    
- The wizard further explains that Balthazar created **fail-safes** to ensure only the worthy could use his designs; each relic carries a signature the Book recognises.
    

---

## **4. Decision to Return to Braewood**
- On the return they freed some slaves from a slave trader
- Armed with this knowledge, the party realises:
    
    - They must return to Braewood to report their findings,
        
    - And seek access to resources, authority, and controlled magical environments to safely open the Codex.
        
- The session ends with the party **departing Sheyton**, heading back toward Braewood under the Queen’s protection and instructions.
