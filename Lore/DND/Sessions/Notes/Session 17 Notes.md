# **📘 Session 17 Summary - "Blood on the Water"**

## **1. Ambush at Sea**

- While moving through the Sapphire Strait, the party was caught in a major ambush by **Captain Iona's fleet**.
- Marina was used as leverage and the confrontation became immediate and personal.

---

## **2. Full Naval Engagement**

- The battle expanded into multi-ship chaos with boarding actions, hull damage, and command-level targets.
- The party and allies fought through pirate formations to reach the flagship and break Iona's hold on the fight.

---

## **3. Vendetta Revealed**

- Iona's motive was confirmed as long-buried revenge tied to Kirk's past.
- The conflict stopped being "pirates vs sailors" and became a generational blood debt.

---

## **4. Victory at Heavy Cost**

- The party survived and forced the battle's resolution, but losses were severe.
- By session end, the emotional and logistical cost was already clear and pointed directly into the aftermath session.

---

## **Major Outcomes**

- Iona's fleet engagement became a defining campaign battle.
- Kirk's history was exposed in front of the party.
- Marina's fate was secured, but at a price that destabilized the group.
