# **📘 Session 16 Summary - "The Rimduff Isles"**

## **1. Departure with Kirk**

- The party sailed with **Captain Kirk** toward the Rimduff Isles to recover **Marina**.
- Travel set the tone for a naval-focused arc and raised the stakes away from city politics.

---

## **2. Contact with Iona's Territory**

- On arrival near Iona-controlled waters, the party confronted fortified pirate positions and layered defenses.
- The rescue objective forced tactical choices between stealth, direct assault, and ship engagement.

---

## **3. Marina Rescue Operation**

- The team pushed through pirate resistance and reached the prison objective.
- **Marina** was recovered alive, though the mission left lasting fallout with Iona's network.

---

## **4. Iona Thread Escalates**

- Captain Iona's conflict with Kirk moved from rumor to direct enemy status.
- The session closed with clear signs that the pirate war was not truly over.

---

## **Major Outcomes**

- Marina was rescued.
- Kirk's personal stakes became party stakes.
- Iona's faction was damaged but not politically erased.
- The campaign shifted into open naval conflict consequences.
