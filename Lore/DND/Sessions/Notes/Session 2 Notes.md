# **📘 Session 2 Summary – “The Envoy’s Task”**

## **1. Forced into Service**

- The party is brought before **Marelle** (Envoy) and **Seral** (Paladin Knight).
    
- They are accused of serious crimes and given a choice:
    
    - **Retrieve a magical amulet**, or
        
    - **Be executed**.
        
- They are assigned:
    
    - **Alan** – nervous rookie soldier.
        
    - **Paynr Wulkor** – a temporary PC.
        

---

## **2. Investigating the Red Scarf Cleric**

- The group investigates a suspected **Red Scarf priest**.
    
- They find a church currently holding a **sermon/mass**.
    
- After questioning the priest, they make him suspicious and he retreats to the basement.
    

---

## **3. The Basement Horror**

- In the cellar beneath the church they discover:
    
    - **Children, men, and women** kept malnourished and **mind-controlled**.
        
    - Controlled by the priest using a **corrupt arcane pendant**.
        
- Combat breaks out:
    
    - The party kills the **Red Scarf priest**.
        
    - Only a **few enslaved victims** die in the chaos.
        
    - The remaining captives are freed once the pendant loses power.
        

---

## **4. Aftermath & Party Conflict**

- The group debates what to do next.
    
- **Tearius**, shaken by the events and unwilling to continue under the Envoy, **leaves the party**.
    

---

## **5. Paynr’s Betrayal**

- While Tearius is leaving:
    
    - **Paynr kills Nehinyathu** (Tearius’s best friend).
        
    - This act pushes Tearius fully away from the mission.
        

---

## **6. Return to Marelle (Disaster)**

- The remaining three players prepare to return the amulet.
    
- When they arrive:
    
    - They discover **Alan the rookie murdered**.
        
    - Tearius killed Alan to use his death as leverage to reach Paynr.
        
    - The session ends with Tearius **capturing and castrating Paynr** in a brutal act of revenge.
