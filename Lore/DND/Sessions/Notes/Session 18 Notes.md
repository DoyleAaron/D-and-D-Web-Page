# **📘 Session 18 Summary - "Aftermath"**

## **1. Counting the Dead**

- The session opened on a damaged Wavecutter after the naval battle.
- Crew losses were catastrophic, including multiple recruits tied to Morjor's decisions.
- The emotional weight of victory overshadowed the material rewards.

---

## **2. Kirk and Marina Reunited**

- Kirk got Marina back, but the cost broke his momentum.
- He shifted from action-first captain to guilt-driven survivor, focused on getting home.

---

## **3. Morjor's Guilt Lands**

- Morjor had to face the direct consequences of recruitment choices after seeing who did not return.
- Crew grief and silence became central table tone for the session.

---

## **4. Varin Steps Away**

- Varin left the active party after the mission's moral cost became too high.
- The farewell was quiet and final in tone, but not framed as permanent closure.

---

## **5. Loose Ends and Hard Choices**

- Rewards, loot, and bounty follow-through were handled under heavy tension.
- The group had to decide what to do with captured assets and whether to press onward despite loss.

---

## **Major Outcomes**

- The Rimduff arc closed with major emotional fallout.
- Party cohesion shifted under pressure.
- Varin's departure changed group dynamics immediately.
- The campaign pivoted back toward Dawerton/Lavalto with new scars.
