# **📘 Session 15 Summary - "Guild Work and Smoke"**

## **1. Captain Kirk Enters the Story**

- The party was introduced to **Captain Kirk** and his missing-daughter problem.
- This opened the Rimduff/Iona thread as a live side arc tied to future sea travel.

---

## **2. Wayfarers Mission Taken**

- Instead of jumping straight into larger endgame threads, the party took a guild job through the **Wayfarers' Accord**.
- Mission focus: **retrieve a stolen statue** from Tidewatch.

---

## **3. The Statue Job Went Loud**

- The mission escalated in the slums and the target building was **blown up** during the operation.
- The statue objective and extraction became chaotic under collapsing conditions.

---

## **4. Varin Saves Rekka**

- In the middle of the chaos, **Varin saved Rekka**.
- This became one of the defining beats of the session and shifted how that relationship is remembered.

---

## **5. What Did Not Happen**

- Liora did **not** directly explain the 28th vampire-hunt cycle to the party in this session.

---

## **Major Outcomes**

- Captain Kirk was established as a key ally/contact.
- The party completed a messy but successful guild operation.
- Varin's intervention with Rekka became a major interpersonal consequence.
- Momentum built toward the Rimduff sea arc.
