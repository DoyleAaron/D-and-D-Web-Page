# **📘 Session 4 Summary – “The Den Beneath Plalisle”**

## **1. To Plalisle – Wagon, Wolves & Smitler**

- Party haggles with a stablemaster, trading **garments + coin** for a **horse and carriage rental**.
    
- On the road they’re **attacked by wolves**;  
    **Adolf Smitler nearly dies**, but the party stabilises him.
    
- Reaching the foothills, they leave **injured Smitler with the horse**, then enter the woods on foot.
    

---

## **2. Dungeon Entrance – Bat Room Misread**

- They find the first chamber: **slab tiles + an unlocked door**.
    
- Instead of testing the door, they step on the trapped tiles:
    
    - Triggers a swarm of **bats** including a **larger boss bat**.
        
    - Killing the boss bat automatically **opens the door**.
        
- They proceed, slightly frustrated but still intact.
    

---

## **3. Room 2 – The Counterweight Puzzle Disaster**

- The party completely fails to intuit the weight + chains mechanism.
    
- They:
    
    - Throw random junk around.
        
    - Throw **objects at each other**.
        
    - Pour **oil in the bowl** and **set it on fire**.
        
    - Generally turn the puzzle into a war crime.
        
- **Amdir** eventually passes the puzzle by repeatedly **throwing dead bats into the bowl** until the weight requirement is satisfied.
    
- Chains pulled → **door opens**.
    

---

## **4. Room 3 – The Peacebinding Hall**

- The group becomes suddenly cautious.
    
- They **leave their weapons and foci** as instructed.
    
- Proceed through without triggering the stone guardians.
