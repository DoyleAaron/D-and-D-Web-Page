# **📘 Session 13 Summary – "The Father's Domain"**

## **1. Return to the Tavern**

- After the brutal fight at the priest's house, the party retreats to the tavern.
    
- They take a **long rest** to recover their depleted resources.
    
- **Elira** approaches them with a proposition:
    
    - She offers to accompany them instead of Bram.
        
    - After the close call, she doesn't want to put her men in further danger.
        

---

## **2. The Sealed Door**

- The following day, the party returns to the haunted house.
    
- They find the **door sealed shut** — unable to open it by any means.
    
- With no way forward, they decide to regroup:
    
    - The party goes for drinks to pass the time.
        
    - They wait for nightfall.
        

---

## **3. The Door Opens**

- Upon returning at night, the door **stands open**.
    
- The house feels different — darker, more oppressive.
    
- Inside, they encounter a **Wight Acolyte**:
    
    - A twisted servant of the darkness within.
        
    - The party defeats it after a tense fight.
        

---

## **4. Father Halden's Domain**

- Beyond the acolyte, the party enters **Father Halden's domain**.
    
- The corrupted priest awaits them in his sanctum.
    
- The battle is **gruelling and nearly fatal**:
    
    - The party is pushed to their limits.
        
    - **Varin** is forced to use the **first charge on his arm** to survive.
        
- Despite the odds, they **slay Father Halden**, ending his corruption.
