# **📘 Session 8 Summary – “Road, River, and Reputation”**

## **1. Escort Team Assembled**

The party meets the full Braewood escort detail assigned to the Dawerton mission:

- **Envoy Pasan** – Diplomatic lead, non-combatant.
    
- **Maera of Silverhill** – Linguist and analyst.
    
- **Sergeant Elira Thorn** – Frontline commander.
    
- **Corporal Bram Stone** – Ranger scout.
    
- **Sister Calene Dusk** – War cleric and spiritual support.
    
- **Aric “Ash” Vell** – Rogue skirmisher and intelligence asset.
    

Initial dynamics form quickly:

- **Aric and Amdir** bond early over shared attitudes and humour.
    
- **Maera** shows clear disdain toward Amdir, remaining cold and critical.
    
- **Sister Calene** displays noticeable interest in **Amdir and Varin**, often using **Morjor** as an excuse to remain close to them.
    

---

## **2. Overland Travel: Braewood → Cleamond → Mortling**

- The journey on foot proceeds without incident.
    
- Cleamond serves as a brief rest stop.
    
- Arrival at **Mortling Stronghold** reinforces Braewood’s military culture and tension near the Kluimont border.
    
- No hostile encounters occur; the escort detail remains alert but unchallenged.
    

---

## **3. Ship Passage: Mortling → Dawerton**

- The party boards a small vessel for the final leg to Dawerton.
    
- The voyage is calm, with no environmental or external threats.
    

### Onboard Developments

- **Morjor defeats Aric** in a friendly hand-to-hand spar, earning quiet respect.
    
- Interpersonal tensions intensify:
    
    - Sister Calene crosses personal boundaries during the voyage, creating discomfort and consequences for party dynamics.
        
    - This incident marks a clear fracture in trust toward her character.
        

---

## **4. Arrival in Dawerton**

- The ship docks without delay.
    
- The party arrives in **Dawerton**, a busy port city steeped in trade, secrecy, and intrigue.
    
- Session ends as the group steps onto the docks, poised to begin investigations into:
    
    - Night shipments,
        
    - Volcanic reagents,
        
    - And Lavalto-linked trade routes.
