# **📘 Session 9 Summary – “The Forgotten Diplomat”**

## **1. Unexpected Escort**

- The party is assigned to travel alongside a second group:
    
    - **Tarth** – a temporary PC, **black dragonborn**, acting as a Kluimont diplomat.
        
    - **Four Kluimont knights** from the city of **Eigmend**.
        
- The joint escort is tense from the outset due to:
    
    - Ongoing political friction between **Braewood and Kluimont**.
        
    - Mutual distrust and sharp, antagonistic remarks between both groups.
        

---

## **2. Rising Hostility**

- The journey is marked by escalating provocation:
    
    - **Amdir deliberately trips Tarth**, worsening relations.
        
    - Insults and posturing continue between the two escorts.
        
- By the time the groups reach a tavern for the night, tempers are already frayed.
    

---

## **3. Tavern Confrontation**

- Inside the tavern, conversation between the two groups turns openly hostile.
    
- Arguments nearly escalate into a **full brawl**, only narrowly avoided.
    
- The night ends with tension unresolved and both sides retreating to rest under uneasy terms.
    

---

## **4. The Missing Memory**

- When the party awakens the following morning:
    
    - **Tarth is gone**.
        
    - More disturbingly, **none of the party can remember Tarth at all**.
        
- Only indirect signs remain:
    
    - Extra beds disturbed.
        
    - Knights confused or unsettled.
        
    - Gaps in conversation and memory.
        
- The Kluimont knights depart shortly after, offering little explanation.
