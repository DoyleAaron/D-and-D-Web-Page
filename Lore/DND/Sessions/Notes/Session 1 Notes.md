# **Session Summary – The Night at the Mogged Pigeon**

## **1. Gathering in the Mogged Pigeon**

- The four party members were drinking and resting in the **Mogged Pigeon Pub**.
    
- Suddenly, **a young girl burst in**, panicked.
    
- The group followed her outside to investigate a robbery.
    

---

## **2. Splitting Up to Investigate**

### **Back of the Pub**

- **Tearius** checked the back alley.
    
- He met **Nehinhatu**, the bike repairman (Israel-themed NPC).
    
- **Dylan** approached Tearius suspiciously, shoved him, then left and **headed toward the suspected hideout**.
    

### **East Alley**

- **Amdir, Morjor, and Varin** pursued a **suspicious man** who slipped into an alley.
    
- They tracked him deeper into the alleys.
    

---

## **3. Arrival at the Thieves’ Hideout**

- Dylan arrived first and saw **a kid lock the warehouse door** while fleeing.
    
- The other three (Amdir, Morjor, Varin) continued chasing the **adult thief** running down the alley.
    

### **Attempted Ambush**

- While the party strategised outside the hideout,  
    one thief tried to sneak out and ambush them.
    
- **Morjor and Tearius** dealt with him, and he escaped after being beaten back.
    

---

## **4. Entering the Warehouse**

- The party decided to **break in**.
    

### **Left Side (Tearius)**

- Tearius slipped inside through a side entrance.
    
- He ran into **two thieves trying to flee**.
    
- Tearius **robbed them**, took coin and items, then let them go.
    

### **Front Entrance (Amdir, Morjor, Varin)**

- The remaining thieves burst out the front, leading to a fight:
    
    - **Morjor** took out **two thieves** quickly.
        
    - **Varin kept missing his firebolts** at the leader, **Rook**.
        
    - **Rook knocked Varin unconscious**.
        
    - Morjor, badly injured, tried to retreat but was **chased by Rook and another thief**.
        

### **Inside the Warehouse**

- Amdir searched for Tearius, eventually finding him in the back room **looting crates**.
    
- Tearius secretly pocketed a **stopwatch**, hiding it from Amdir.
    
- The shared loot was split between them.
    

---

## **5. Rooftop Escape Attempt**

- Morjor, cornered by Rook, **jumped out a window** in a desperate escape attempt.
    
- Varin was stabilised and brought out just as the **City Guard arrived**.
    

---

## **6. Arrival of the City Guard**

- The guards arrived in force, led by:
    
    - **Seral**, an Oathbreaker Paladin (terrifying reputation).
        
    - **Alren**, a young cadet.
        

### **Immediate Consequences**

- **Rook immediately surrendered**.
    
- Guards helped Varin, gave him water and care.
    
- Morjor was caught outside the window and **arrested**, but handled gently.
    
- Rook and another thief were **thrown into rough custody**, chained harshly.
    

---

## **7. Tearius' Big Mistake**

- Seral entered the warehouse and shouted for anyone inside to **come out with hands up**.
    
- Tearius and Amdir **panicked**, looking for places to hide.
    
- Tearius attempted to **burn the warehouse down** to escape.
    
- Amdir knocked Tearius out cold to stop him.
    

### **Detainments**

- Seral rushed in, dragged them both out, and **arrested them**.
    
- Amdir tried to talk his way out of it but Seral effortlessly subdued him.
    

---

## **8. Session End**

- The party concluded the night **in prison**, awaiting judgment from the authorities of Sheyton.
    

---

# **Key NPC Moments**

- **Seral**: Dominant, commanding, ruthless but disciplined. Made the arrests personally.
    
- **Alren**: Kind-hearted cadet, clearly uncomfortable with the harsh treatment.
    
- **Rook**: Gave up immediately once surrounded.
    
- **Tearius**: Got himself knocked out by an ally.
    
- **Amdir**: Tried diplomacy, failed.
    
- **Varin**: Knocked out by Rook but aided by guards afterward.
    
- **Morjor**: Dramatic window escape attempt, still arrested but lightly.
    

---

# **Major Outcomes**

- The thieves were defeated or scattered.
    
- Tearius secretly acquired a **mysterious stopwatch**.
    
- The group is **now imprisoned**, likely to meet:
    
    - Officials,
        
    - Interrogations,
        
    - Or a forced job offer.
        

The session ended at the **perfect narrative cliffhanger**.
