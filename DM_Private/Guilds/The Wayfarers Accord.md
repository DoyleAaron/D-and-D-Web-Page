# The Wayfarers' Accord – Adventurer's Guild

## Overview

The **Wayfarers' Accord** is an international guild of mercenaries, investigators, monster hunters, and problem-solvers. Founded over 200 years ago in the neutral city of **Adaham**, the guild operates across all major kingdoms with licensed chapters in most cities and towns.

**Guild Motto:** *"For coin, for cause, for completion."*

---

## Guild Benefits

### Membership Perks
- **Travel Papers:** Guild credentials grant easier passage between kingdoms
- **Guild Lodging:** Discounted rates at affiliated inns
- **Information Network:** Access to guild boards across the continent
- **Legal Protection:** Guild contracts are legally binding; payment is guaranteed
- **Insurance:** Partial death benefits for registered next-of-kin
- **Equipment Access:** Discounts at guild-affiliated smiths and suppliers

### Membership Cost
- **Registration Fee:** 10 gp (one-time)
- **Annual Dues:** 5 gp (waived if 3+ jobs completed per year)

## Tier System

### Group vs Individual Ranking

**Individual Rank:** Your personal standing based on solo mission completion.  
**Group Rank:** The rank your registered party holds, allowing access to higher-tier jobs.

| Rank | Individual Requirement | Group Requirement |
|------|------------------------|-------------------|
| **E** | Starting rank | Starting rank |
| **D** | Complete 10 solo E-tier jobs | Complete 1 E-tier as group |
| **C** | Complete 10 solo D-tier jobs | Complete 1 D-tier as group |
| **B** | Complete 10 solo C-tier jobs | Complete 1 C-tier as group |
| **A** | Complete 10 solo B-tier jobs | Complete 1 B-tier as group |
| **S** | Complete 3 A-tier jobs + Capital Board Acceptance | Complete 3 A-tier jobs + Capital Board Acceptance |

### Mission Access Rules

| Your Rank | Can Accept |
|-----------|-----------|
| E-tier | E-tier only |
| D-tier | E, D, and C-tier |
| C-tier | E, D, C, and B-tier |
| B-tier | E, D, C, B, and A-tier |
| A-tier | All except S-tier |
| S-tier | All tiers |

**Important:** You can always attempt jobs **one tier above** your current rank—this is how you rank up. But the guild won't guarantee your safety or success.

## Job Board System

### How It Works

Job boards exist in every guild chapter. The size and variety depend on the location:
- **Capital Cities:** Full boards with all tiers, including S-tier
- **Major Towns (Dawerton):** E through A-tier, limited selection
- **Villages:** E and D-tier only, occasionally C-tier

### Posting Fees (For Clients)

| Tier | Posting Fee | Visibility |
|------|-------------|------------|
| E | 5 gp | Local chapter only |
| D | 25 gp | Local + nearby towns |
| C | 100 gp | Regional (within kingdom) |
| B | 500 gp | Kingdom-wide |
| A | 2,000 gp | Multi-kingdom (major cities) |
| S | 10,000 gp | All capital boards |

The Spehlina bounty (10,000 gp reward) was posted with A-tier visibility fees, meaning it appears in guilds across Braewood, Dawerton, and into Lavalto territory.

## Tier Descriptions

### E-Tier: "Errands"
**Risk Level:** Minimal  
**Description:** Simple tasks requiring no combat skills  
**Typical Pay:** 1-10 gp  
**Examples:** Deliveries, pet-sitting, message running, basic labor

### D-Tier: "Difficulties"
**Risk Level:** Low  
**Description:** Minor problems requiring some skill or investigation  
**Typical Pay:** 15-75 gp  
**Examples:** Petty theft investigation, pest extermination, escort through safe roads

### C-Tier: "Challenges"
**Risk Level:** Moderate  
**Description:** Jobs requiring professional skills, some danger possible  
**Typical Pay:** 100-500 gp  
**Examples:** Murder investigations, noble protection, dangerous creature tracking

### B-Tier: "Battles"
**Risk Level:** High  
**Description:** Serious danger, combat likely, death possible  
**Typical Pay:** 500-2,000 gp  
**Examples:** Monster hunts (dangerous), criminal organization takedowns, war-adjacent work

### A-Tier: "Annihilations"
**Risk Level:** Extreme  
**Description:** Death is common, requires elite professionals  
**Typical Pay:** 2,000-15,000 gp  
**Examples:** Major monster exterminations, kingdom-threatening problems, dungeon clearing

### S-Tier: "Sagas"
**Risk Level:** Legendary  
**Description:** World-altering tasks that may take years or decades  
**Typical Pay:** Negotiated (often includes land, titles, artifacts)  
**Access:** 3 A-tier completions + Capital Board acceptance  
**Examples:** Recovering legendary artifacts, stopping planar invasions, ending ancient curses

## Guild Procedures

### Accepting a Job
1. Approach the Board Keeper or Registrar
2. Present guild credentials
3. Sign the job contract (magical binding)
4. Receive job details and client contact
5. Complete the job
6. Return to guild with proof of completion
7. Collect reward (guild takes 10% fee)

### Failing a Job
- No payment
- Job returns to board
- If contract broken willfully: reputation penalty
- If death occurs: guild notifies next-of-kin, no penalty

### Disputes
- Guild arbitrates between client and adventurer
- Guildmaster has final say
- Fraud by either party results in blacklisting
