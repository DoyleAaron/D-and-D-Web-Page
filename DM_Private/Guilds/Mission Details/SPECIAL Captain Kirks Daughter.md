# SPECIAL: "Captain Kirk's Daughter" (Non-Guild Mission)

## Overview
**Client:** Captain Kirk (Temp PC)  
**Location:** Quey / Rimduff Isles  
**Duration:** Variable (7-14 days)  
**Reward:** 2,000 gp  
**Combat Expected:** Yes (pirates)

**NOTE:** This is NOT a Wayfarer Accord mission. Completing it does NOT grant guild rank advancement.

---

## The Job
Captain Kirk's adopted daughter **Marina** was traveling to on business and never arrived. She hasn't made contact—extremely unlike her. Kirk is desperate and will pay 2,000 gp for her safe return.

---

## DM ONLY – The Truth

### What Happened
Marina's ship was intercepted by **Captain Iona**, a notorious pirate who operates from the **Rimduff Isles**. Iona's crew captured the ship, took valuables and prisoners, and scuttled the vessel.

### Marina's Current Status
- Alive and held for ransom/sale
- Imprisoned on Iona's ship or at her island hideout
- Has not been harmed (yet)—Iona recognizes her value

### Captain Iona
- Human female, 38, ruthless and cunning
- Commands the ship **The Crimson Tide**
- Known for taking prisoners for slave trade
- Has a bounty of 1,500 gp on her head
- Operates from a hidden cove in the Rimduff Isles

---

## Investigation Path

### Quey (First Stop)
- Marina never arrived—confirmed by her business contacts
- Dock records show her ship **The Saltwind** was expected 5 days ago
- No wreckage spotted, no survivors found
- Rumors: "Pirates have been active near the Rimduff passage"

### Rimduff Isles (Pirate Territory)
- Dangerous waters, multiple small islands
- Information can be bought from shady contacts
- Gather Information DC 16: Learn about Captain Iona's reputation
- Gather Information DC 18: Approximate location of her cove

### Finding The Crimson Tide
- Requires either:
  - Tracking (Survival DC 18 over multiple days)
  - Buying information (150+ gp to the right contact)
  - Capturing and interrogating one of Iona's crew

---

## Captain Iona's Forces

### The Crimson Tide (Ship)
- Caravel-class vessel, fast and maneuverable
- Crew of 20 pirates

### Iona's Cove (Hidden Base)
- Small island with natural harbor
- Makeshift fortifications
- Prison cave for captives
- 30+ pirates when fully gathered

### Key NPCs

**Captain Iona**
- Use Bandit Captain stat block with +2 to all stats
- AC 17 (studded leather + shield)
- HP 85
- Wields a magical cutlass (+1, deals extra 1d6 cold damage)
- Will negotiate if outmatched—Marina as bargaining chip

**First Mate Craw**
- Hobgoblin male, disciplined and brutal
- Use Hobgoblin Captain stat block
- Fiercely loyal to Iona

---

## Possible Approaches

### Direct Assault
- Storm the ship or cove
- Dangerous—numbers favor pirates
- Kirk's water form is essential here

### Stealth Rescue
- Infiltrate at night
- Free Marina and escape before alarm
- Stealth DC 14+ throughout

### Negotiation/Ransom
- Iona will demand 1,000 gp for Marina
- Can be talked down to 600 gp with Persuasion DC 18
- Kirk may refuse on principle

### Deception
- Pose as buyers, traders, or recruits
- Get close, then strike or extract Marina

## 🗺️ Travel Encounters

*Use these during the journey to Adaham, Quey, or the Rimduff Isles. Roll or pick as needed.*

### Road Encounters (Dawerton → Adaham)

#### Encounter 1: Highwaymen Ambush
**Setup:** Bandits block the road with a fallen tree.  
**Enemies:** 4 Bandits + 1 Bandit Captain  
**Difficulty:** Medium  

| Token | Stat Block | HP | AC |
|-------|------------|----|----|
| Bandit (x4) | Bandit | 11 | 12 |
| Bandit Leader | Bandit Captain | 65 | 15 |

**Tactics:** 2 bandits in road, 2 hidden in trees with crossbows. Leader waits to assess threat.  
**Resolution:** Will flee if leader drops or 3+ bandits fall. May surrender for their lives.  
**Loot:** 2d10 gp per bandit, leader has 25 gp + stolen jewelry (50 gp).

**Map Prompt:**
> Top-down fantasy battle map, D&D style, no grid. Forest road blocked by large fallen tree. Dense woodland on both sides. Rocky outcrops providing cover. Wagon tracks visible. 50x40 feet. Afternoon dappled lighting through tree canopy.

---

#### Encounter 2: Merchant in Distress
**Setup:** Overturned cart, merchant waving for help. Could be genuine or trap.  
**Roll 1d6:** 1-4 = Genuine, 5-6 = Trap

**If Genuine:**
- Merchant Dorian, wheel broke, needs help
- No combat, roleplay encounter
- Grateful, may share rumors or give 10 gp

**If Trap:**
- "Merchant" is a Scout, signals hidden bandits
- **Enemies:** 1 Scout + 3 Bandits

| Token | Stat Block | HP | AC |
|-------|------------|----|----|
| Fake Merchant | Scout | 16 | 13 |
| Hidden Bandit (x3) | Bandit | 11 | 12 |

**Map Prompt:**
> Top-down fantasy battle map, D&D style, no grid. Country road with overturned wooden cart. Spilled cargo (crates, barrels). Tall grass on both sides of road. Small copse of trees nearby. 40x30 feet. Midday lighting.

---

#### Encounter 3: Wolves at Dusk
**Setup:** Party hears howling as night approaches. Hungry wolf pack stalks them.  
**Enemies:** 6 Wolves  
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium

| Token | Stat Block | HP | AC |
|-------|------------|----|----|
| Wolf (x6) | Wolf | 11 | 13 |

**Tactics:** Pack tactics, try to surround. Focus on isolated targets. Flee if 3+ killed.  
**Resolution:** Successful Intimidation DC 14 or fire drives them off.  
**Loot:** Wolf pelts (5 gp each).

**Map Prompt:**
> Top-down fantasy battle map, D&D style, no grid. Forest clearing at dusk. Dead campfire ring in center. Fallen logs and rocks for cover. Dense treeline surrounding area. 40x40 feet. Twilight lighting with orange sky.

### Sea Encounters (Sailing to Rimduff Isles)

#### Encounter 4: Pirate Scout Ship
**Setup:** Small pirate sloop approaches to investigate. Could be Iona's scouts.  
**Enemies:** 1 Bandit Captain + 6 Bandits (pirates)  
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard

| Token | Stat Block | HP | AC |
|-------|------------|----|----|
| Pirate Captain | Bandit Captain | 65 | 15 |
| Pirate (x6) | Bandit | 11 | 12 |

**Tactics:** Pull alongside, attempt boarding. Captain hangs back initially.  
**Resolution:** If defeated, can interrogate survivors for Iona's location (Intimidation DC 12).  
**Loot:** Ship has 50 gp, captain has map fragment (advantage on finding cove).

**Map Prompt:**
> Top-down fantasy battle map, D&D style, no grid. Two ships side by side on open water. Main ship (party's) is larger caravel. Smaller pirate sloop alongside with boarding planks. Rope ladders, barrels for cover. 60x40 feet. Bright daylight, choppy waves.
