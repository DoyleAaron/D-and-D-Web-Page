# Adaham Shops

*Reusable port-town market toolkit for multiple sessions*

Adaham is a turnover market. Most days are common gear and consumables. Some days, premium cargo lands and rare items appear for a short window.

---

## How To Use This File Fast

1. Pick a shop.
2. Choose a stock mode for that session.
3. Pull items from the tables.
4. Mark sold items and restock next in-game day.

### Stock Modes

| Mode | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Light | 8-12 total items visible in a shop |
| Normal | 12-18 total items visible |
| Busy Port Day | 18-26 total items visible |

## Reusable Session Stock Builder

Use this at the start of each in-game day.

### Step 1: Market Mood

Roll `1d6`.

| d6 | Mood | Effect |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Thin supply | -30% visible stock |
| 2 | Quiet | -15% visible stock |
| 3 | Normal | Normal stock |
| 4 | Busy docks | +20% visible stock |
| 5 | Convoy day | +35% visible stock |
| 6 | Festival traffic | +50% visible stock, +10% prices |

### Step 2: Premium Cargo Check

Roll `1d8`.

| d8 | Result |
|---:|---|
| 1-3 | No premium cargo |
| 4-5 | 1 uncommon magic item appears in town |
| 6-7 | 2 uncommon magic items appear |
| 8 | 1 rare item appears |

### Step 3: Price Drift

Roll `1d6`.

| d6 | Price Change |
|---:|---|
| 1 | -10% (surplus) |
| 2-5 | No change |
| 6 | +10% (shortage) |

### Step 4: Restock Pace

| Item Type | Restock |
|---|---|
| Mundane gear | Daily |
| Potions | Every 1d2 days |
| Uncommon magic | Every 1d4 days |
| Rare magic | Every 2d6 days |

## DM Notes

- Keep 2-3 premium items visible at once unless party is very wealthy.
- If party buys out a category, apply a 1d4 day restock delay.
- If party sells rare loot publicly, next day has +1 premium cargo check result.
- Use curio shelf items to seed rumors and side quests.
