## Quick combat script

- **Openers:** _Faerie Fire_ (advantage factory) or _Hypnotic Pattern_ (hard control).
    
- **Single target ruin:** _Dissonant Whispers_ → ally opportunity attacks; or _Heat Metal_ vs. armoured target.
    
- **Pick-ups:** _Healing Word_ as bonus action; _Vicious Mockery_ to blunt a hitter.
    
- **Out of combat:** _Suggestion_ to bypass, _Invisibility_ for scouting, _Mage Hand/Minor Illusion_ for tricks.

### **Childhood**

Zaryan was born in **Qrey**, the northern harbor city of Kluimont. Life there was unforgiving — a place of salt-stained docks, long winters, and soldiers constantly on edge against piracy from the Rimdiff Isles. His family was poor, uneducated, and often ignored by the city’s ruling elite.

Though he grew up in hardship, Zaryan learned to endure. He became the boy who gave his younger siblings his share of bread, the one who defused street fights with sharp words instead of sharper blades, and the one who refused to abandon anyone in need.

---

### **Meeting the Brothers**

When Zaryan was conscripted into the Kluimont military, he struggled to keep up with seasoned recruits. Many mocked him for being ignorant of highborn ways and unskilled in the arts of war. But **Ughyntor** noticed the fire in him — not the kind that burned in the throat of a dragonborn, but the fire of someone who _cared_.

Ughyntor extended a hand. Burthetyr, shy and uneasy, was the first to treat him as family. From then on, the three trained, ate, and fought together. The bond was so natural that the other soldiers started calling them “the Trio,” as though the three were born brothers instead of forged by fate.

---

### **The Heart of the Trio**

If Ughyntor is the shining knight and Burthetyr the shaper of flame, Zaryan is the **heart**. He knows when to steady Ughyntor’s anger and when to lift Burthetyr’s doubts. His bardic words heal wounds that swords cannot touch, and his compassion makes him the tether that keeps the brothers grounded.

Where Ughyntor inspires, Zaryan reassures. Where Burthetyr hesitates, Zaryan encourages. Where both are pushed to breaking, Zaryan is the one who holds them together.

---

### **Why He Fights**

Zaryan does not fight for law, rank, or country. He fights for his brothers. He is lawful in that he honors bonds, but he is good in that he would gladly _break the law_ if it meant saving Ughyntor or Burthetyr.

His compass is not the crown of Kluimont, but the lives of those he loves. For him, loyalty is not a soldier’s oath but a sacred bond.

---

### **Role in the Flamebond Trio**

- **Ughyntor the Unsullied**: The shield and leader, burning with conviction.
    
- **Burthetyr the Iron-Scaled**: The flame-shaper, quiet but powerful.
    
- **Zaryan of Qrey**: The heart, binding them together, reminding them _why_ they fight.
    

Together, they are inseparable — brothers not just by blood or fire, but by choice.

---

### **Themes of His Story**

- **The Harbor-Born Survivor:** Grew up in Qrey’s rough streets, toughened by hardship.
    
- **The Third Brother:** Not of blood, but bound tighter than kin.
    
- **The Compassionate Soldier:** His weapon is loyalty, his shield is love.
    
- **The Lawful Good That Bends:** He honors what is right but breaks law and oath if it threatens his brothers.
