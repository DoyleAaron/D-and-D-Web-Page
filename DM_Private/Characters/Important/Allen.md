## Young Soldier, Cadet **Alren** (sympathetic)

**Look & Vibe:** Armour slightly too big; keeps glancing at the captives with worry.  
**Personality:** Earnest, conflicted, wants to do right.  
**Background:** A baker’s son who believes in oaths… and is learning how messy they are.

- **AC** 16 (chain shirt, shield)
    
- **HP** 20 (3d8+6)
    
- **Speed** 30 ft.
    
- **STR 15 (+2), DEX 12 (+1), CON 14 (+2), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 11 (+0)**
    
- **Proficiency** +2
    
- **Skills** Perception +3, Insight +3, Athletics +4
    

**Actions**

- **Spear.** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 piercing (1d8+2 two-handed).
    
- **Light Crossbow.** +3 to hit, 1d8+1 piercing.
    

**Reaction (1/rest)**

- **Protective Interpose.** When a creature Alren can see within 5 ft. would be hit, impose **disadvantage** on the attack (he steps in with his shield). On a hit anyway, Alren takes **half** the damage dealt.
    

**Roleplay Hooks:** If the party showed restraint or compassion, he’ll later whisper a warning or quietly vouch for them.
