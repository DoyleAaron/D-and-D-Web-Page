
## **Lord Adrian Silwynn – Vampiric Patriarch (Level 10)**

**Role:** Ruler of Dawerton; pro-outside-world; primary pureblood diplomat. Oversees political matters and maintains order among vampires.

**Personality/Roleplay:**

- Calm, aristocratic, and subtly intimidating.
    
- Speaks with patience, rarely raising his voice.
    
- Meticulous, preferring careful observation before acting.
    
- Loyal only to those he trusts implicitly; can detect deception easily.
    
- Avoids direct combat unless diplomacy fails or his life/manor is threatened.
    

**Appearance:**

- Tall, elegant, silver hair swept back neatly.
    
- Always in formal dark robes or tailored coats with silver trim.
    
- Eyes a piercing violet, glowing faintly in dim light.
    
- Often carries a cane topped with a small onyx orb (more for prestige than combat).
    

**Stats:**

- **Level/CR:** 10 (Vampire, mostly narrative-focused)
    
- **AC:** 16
    
- **HP:** 144
    
- **Speed:** 30 ft, fly 40 ft (bat form)
    
- **Ability Scores (approximate for CR 10 vampire):**
    
    - STR 16 (+3)
        
    - DEX 16 (+3)
        
    - CON 16 (+3)
        
    - INT 18 (+4)
        
    - WIS 15 (+2)
        
    - CHA 20 (+5)
        

**Saving Throws:** Dex +7, Con +7, Wis +6, Cha +9  
**Skills:** Persuasion +9, Insight +6, Stealth +7  
**Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft, passive Perception 12  
**Languages:** Common, Elvish, Infernal

**Abilities:**

- **Shapechanger:** Can transform into mist or bat form.
    
- **Legendary Resistance (3/day):** Automatically succeed on a failed saving throw.
    
- **Dominate Person (1/day, DC 17):** Can charm one humanoid.
    
- **Regeneration (20 HP/turn):** Regains HP each round; sunlight or running water damages.
    
- **Mist Form:** Can move through tiny openings and avoid opportunity attacks.
    
- **Charm:** Can charm humanoids within 30 ft (DC 17).
    

**Attacks:**

- **Multiattack:** Bite + Unarmed Strike
    
    - **Bite:** 1d10 + 6 piercing, plus 7 necrotic damage, can regain HP equal to damage dealt.
        
    - **Unarmed Strike:** 1d8 + 4 bludgeoning.
        
- **Optional Spells/Abilities:** Can cast _Charm Person_, _Invisibility_ (self only), or minor necrotic magic if narrative requires.
    

**Legendary Actions (3/round):**

1. **Move:** Fly or shift up to 30 ft without provoking opportunity attacks.
    
2. **Bite Attack:** Make one bite attack.
    
3. **Charm Attempt:** Target must succeed on DC 17 Wis save or be charmed for 1 round.
    

**Notes:**

- Usually **avoids conflict in the city**; acts as a mediator.
    
- Will only fight if diplomacy fails or purebloods are threatened.
    
- Skilled at negotiation, intimidation, and manipulation.
    

**Magic Items:**

- **Cane of Command:** Grants +2 to Persuasion and can cast _Suggestion_ 3/day.
    
- **Ring of Shadow Step:** Teleport up to 30 ft in dim light/darkness 3/day.
    
- **Cloak of Resistance:** +1 to all saving throws.
    

**Narrative Hooks:**

- Sees himself as the voice of reason among purebloods.
    
- Often in tense meetings with Seraphine, the L13 pureblood head.
    
- Could act as a guide, mentor, or secret ally for PCs who respect vampire politics.
