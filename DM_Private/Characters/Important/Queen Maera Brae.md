# Queen Maera Braeden

_Medium humanoid (human), lawful neutral — Bard (College of Lore) 10_

**AC** 17 (breastplate, _cloak of protection_)  
**HP** 78 (12d8 + 24)  
**Speed** 30 ft.

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|10 (+0)|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|20 (+5)|

**Saving Throws** Dex +6, Cha +9; (from Bard prof + Cloak)  
**Skills** Insight +8, Persuasion +13, Deception +9, Performance +9, History +6, Perception +4  
**Senses** passive Perception 14  
**Languages** Common, Dwarvish, Elvish  
**Proficiency Bonus** +4  
**Challenge** ~7 (DM-facing; she’s a social/controller, not a bruiser)

### Class Features (Bard 10, College of Lore)

- **Bardic Inspiration (d10, 5/Long Rest).** Bonus action: creature within 60 ft gains 1d10 to one roll (as RAW).
    
- **Jack of All Trades.** Add +2 (PB/2) to any ability check not already including proficiency.
    
- **Song of Rest (d6).** During short rests, allies who spend Hit Dice heal +1d6.
    
- **Cutting Words (Lore 3).** Reaction: subtract 1d10 from a creature’s attack/check/damage (within 60 ft, must hear her).
    
- **Additional Magical Secrets (Lore 6).** Two off-list spells learned (she uses **counterspell** and **shield**).
    

### Spellcasting

**Spellcasting Ability** Charisma (spell save **DC 17**, +9 to hit).  
**Cantrips (at will):** _vicious mockery, mage hand, minor illusion, message_  
**1st (4 slots):** _charm person, dissonant whispers, comprehend languages, disguise self_  
**2nd (3 slots):** _calm emotions, suggestion, mirror image, detect thoughts_  
**3rd (3 slots):** _hypnotic pattern, dispel magic, sending_  
**4th (3 slots):** _greater invisibility, dimension door_  
**5th (2 slots):** _hold monster, modify memory_  
**Magical Secrets:** _counterspell_ (3rd), _shield_ (1st)

> She uses **calm emotions / hypnotic pattern / hold person or monster / suggestion / modify memory** to manage crises; **counterspell** and **shield** keep her safe.

### Actions

- **Sceptre (counts as mace).** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ **4 (1d6+1) bludgeoning**.
    
- **Vicious Mockery (cantrip).** One creature within 60 ft, **DC 17 Wis save** or **2d4 psychic** and **disadvantage** on its next attack roll.
    

### Bonus Actions

- **Bardic Inspiration (d10)** — as above.
    

### Reactions

- **Cutting Words** — as above.
    
- **Shield** — +5 AC until start of next turn (from Magical Secrets).
    
- **Counterspell** — as normal.
    

### Legendary/Paragon?

None. She’s not a solo boss; her power is in spells, items, and guards.

### Attunements & Magic Items (all DMG, RAW)

- **Amulet of Proof against Detection and Location** — hides her from _divination_ (e.g., _scrying, locate object/person_). _(Attunement)_
    
- **Ring of Mind Shielding** — blocks telepathy and mind-reading (_detect thoughts_ can’t read her surface thoughts). _(Attunement)_
    
- **Cloak of Protection** — **+1 AC and saves**. _(Attunement)_
    
- **Breastplate** (worn under formal attire).
    
- **Optional flavour only (no attunement left):** A ceremonial sceptre (counts as mace).
    

> She uses **all three attunement slots** (ring, amulet, cloak). Everything here is straight from the DMG.

### Tactics (RAW tools only)

- Opens with **calm emotions** or **hypnotic pattern** to de-escalate; **counterspell** any obvious blast/control aimed at her.
    
- Uses **shield** if targeted in melee/ranged to spike AC to **22** for the round.
    
- If things truly go south, **dimension door** with an aide.

## Appearance (quick flavour you can read)

High-neck midnight surcoat over white silk, slim braided circlet, breastplate worked into the bodice, a ceremonial sceptre at hand. Calm eyes; each word measured.
