# LADY ENVOY MYRELLE (Druid 5 — covert traitor)

**Look & Vibe:** Velvet-dark silks over travelling leathers, a subtle amethyst ring, and a pleasant, precise smile that never reaches her eyes.  
**Personality:** Poised, surgical, amused by the world as if it were a chessboard.  
**Background:** Crown envoy who “recovers” artefacts before anyone else can; secretly part of a hidden order hoarding magical items and **framing Kluimont**.

- **AC** 16 (from **barkskin**, usually pre-cast; otherwise 12 with leather)
    
- **HP** 38 (5d8+15)
    
- **Speed** 30 ft.
    
- **STR 10, DEX 12 (+1), CON 16 (+3), INT 13 (+1), **WIS 16 (+3)**, CHA 16 (+3)**
    
- **Proficiency** +3
    
- **Saves** Int +4, Wis +6
    
- **Skills** Insight +6, Deception +6, Persuasion +6, Nature +4
    
- **Senses** passive Insight 16, passive Perception 13
    
- **Spell Save DC** **14**, **Spell Attack** **+6**
    

**Spellcasting (Druid 5) — prepared for court and control**

- **Cantrips (at will):** _druidcraft, thorn whip_ (+6 to hit, **2d6** piercing, pull 10 ft. on hit), _guidance_.
    
- **1st-level (4 slots):** _entangle_ (DC 14; restrains), _faerie fire_ (DC 14; grant advantage & negate invisibility), _goodberry_, _cure wounds_ (1d8+3).
    
- **2nd-level (3 slots):** _barkskin_ (AC becomes 16), _spike growth_ (area control; 2d4 per 5 ft. moved), _pass without trace_ (+10 Stealth to chosen allies).
    
- **3rd-level (2 slots):** _moonbeam_ (DC 14; **2d10** radiant, action to move), _call lightning_ (outdoors, DC 14; **3d10** lightning each round).
    

**Actions**

- **Sickle.** +3 to hit, 1d4+1 slashing (only if forced).
    
- **Thorn Whip.** +6 to hit (cantrip), **2d6** piercing and pull 10 ft.
    

**Bonus Action / Utility**

- **Wild Shape (2/day).** CR 1/2 or lower, no flying (typical forms):
    
    - **Black Bear:** AC 11, HP 19, **Bite +5 (1d6+3)**, **Claws +5 (2d4+3)**.
        
    - **Giant Spider:** AC 14, HP 26, **Bite +5 (1d8+3 + DC 11 CON save vs 2d8 poison; half on success)**, climb 30 ft., web (recharge 5–6).
        

**Intrigue Features**

- **Velvet Mask.** Advantage on Deception checks to conceal motives in formal settings.
    
- **Calculated Eye.** As a bonus action, mark one creature she can see; until end of her next turn she has advantage on Insight and Deception against that creature.
    

**Tactics (social):** Opens with **barkskin** already up, keeps **faerie fire** and **entangle** ready to humiliate threats without bloodshed. In a fight, drops **spike growth** to punish movement or **moonbeam** to zone. Will **not** reveal full power unless forced.  
**Perception 20** at the table: a player only feels “something is off.”

**Morale:** Withdraws behind guards or a legal pretext if seriously threatened; prefers leverage to violence.
