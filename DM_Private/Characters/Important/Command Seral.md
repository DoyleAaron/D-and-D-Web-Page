- **Appearance**: Early 40s, scarred face, blackened armour trimmed in silver, greatsword at her side. Her eyes carry cold fury.
    
- **Reputation**: Known for results, feared for her methods. Some whisper she broke with her original oath but was kept in command for sheer effectiveness.

# COMMANDER SEREL VEYNAR (Oathbreaker Paladin, “do not fight at level 1”)

**Look & Vibe:** Blackened plate, scarred lip, eyes like cold iron. She speaks softly and everyone moves.  
**Personality:** Absolute, pitiless, surgical in violence.  
**Background:** Once the pride of Sheyton; broke her sacred oath in a border war, kept on for results.

- **AC** 18 (plate)
    
- **HP** 102 (12d8+48)
    
- **Speed** 30 ft.
    
- **STR 18 (+4), DEX 12 (+1), CON 18 (+4), INT 11 (+0), WIS 14 (+2), CHA 16 (+3)**
    
- **Proficiency** +4
    
- **Saves** Con +8, Wis +6, Cha +7
    
- **Skills** Intimidation +7, Athletics +8, Perception +6
    
- **Senses** passive Perception 16
    
- **Alignment mask:** “Lawful Crown” (truth: herself)
    

**Actions**

- **Greatsword.** +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., _Hit:_ **2d6+4** slashing.
    
- **Profane Smite (3/day).** When Serel hits with a melee attack, add **+2d8 necrotic** damage.
    
- **Dreadful Command (Recharge 5–6).** Creatures of her choice within 30 ft. that can hear her must succeed on a **DC 15 WIS save** or become **frightened** until the end of their next turn. On success, immune for 24 hours.
    

**Bonus Action**

- **Relentless Advance.** Move up to 10 ft. without provoking opportunity attacks.
    

**Spellcasting (Paladin-like, DC 15, +7 to hit)**  
Slots: 1st (4), 2nd (3), 3rd (2)  
Typical spells prepared: **command, compelled duel, wrathful smite, branding smite, hold person** _(via grim rite),_ **silence** _(tactical)_.

> _Use sparingly—she doesn’t need them to terrify level-1 PCs._

**Aura of Dread.** Enemy creatures that start their turn within **10 ft.** of Serel must make a **DC 15 WIS save** or be **frightened** until the start of their next turn. Creatures that succeed are immune to this aura for 24 hours.

**Tactics:** Demonstrations, executions, breaking lines. If the party does something truly reckless, she maims rather than kills—then **twice as many guards** arrive. She is not here to be defeated.

**Morale:** Unshakeable. She withdraws only on political orders.
### Commander Seral, the Oathbreaker

Seral was born in the heart of **Braewood**, where loyalty to crown and steel runs as deep as the mountain veins. From her earliest days, she showed an unmatched talent with the blade, a quick mind for battlefield tactics, and a presence that could command even the most stubborn soldiers. It wasn’t long before she rose through the ranks, earning the rare honour of serving as a **Queen’s Guard**—one of the elite few sworn by sacred oath to defend the sovereign with their life.

For years, Seral lived and breathed that oath. She was known for her unshakable vigilance, her merciless precision, and her absolute devotion to the Queen. But then came the fateful campaign: an envoy dispatched to Sheyton, where whispers of unrest and hidden powers were stirring. It was here Seral’s loyalty cracked.

The exact truth is murky—some say she **defied a direct order** to protect innocents, others whisper she **betrayed her queen for personal ambition**, while darker voices claim she was swayed by forbidden magic. Whatever the cause, Seral **broke her oath**, and for such a crime, death would have been the expected sentence.

Yet Seral’s power was too great to waste. The Queen, bitter but pragmatic, stripped her of the sacred mantle of Queen’s Guard, branding her forever as **Oathbreaker**, and demoted her to the rank of **General**. She would serve not as a symbol of loyalty, but as a weapon to be pointed where the crown demanded.

Now stationed in **Sheyton** as part of Braewood’s envoy, Seral bears her title with grim pride. She is feared more than respected, her name carrying both awe and suspicion. Soldiers whisper that her very presence curses the battlefield, that the gods turned their gaze from her when she shattered her vow.

Seral herself wears the name **Oathbreaker** like armour. She is a commander of unmatched skill, but her motives are clouded. Does she still serve the Queen out of duty? Or is she biding her time, forging her own destiny in the shadows of Sheyton’s streets?
