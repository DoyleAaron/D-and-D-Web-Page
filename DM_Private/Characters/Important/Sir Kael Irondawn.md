## 👑 Royal Guard Captain — _Sir Kael Irondawn_

**Race/Class:** Human (Champion Fighter, Lv 14)  
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral  
**Role:** Personal protector of the Queen; immovable in both stance and loyalty.

### Appearance & Personality

Broad-shouldered, short-cropped grey hair, burnished breastplate etched with the Braewood stag. Calm voice, rarely blinks. Treats every word in court as potential threat assessment. Never smiles except when discussing sword forms.

### Combat Stat Block

|Attribute|Value|
|---|---|
|**AC**|19 (plate + shield)|
|**HP**|162 (14d10 + 70)|
|**Speed**|30 ft|
|**STR 18 (+4)** DEX 14 (+2)** CON 20 (+5)** INT 10 (+0)** WIS 14 (+2)** CHA 12 (+1)**||

**Saving Throws:** Str +9, Con +10, Wis +7  
**Skills:** Athletics +9, Perception +7, Intimidation +6  
**Senses:** passive Perception 17  
**CR:** 10 (≈5,900 XP)

### Traits

- **Indomitable (2/day):** Reroll a failed saving throw.
    
- **Action Surge (1/day):** Take an additional action.
    
- **Second Wind (1/day):** Heal 1d10 + 14 HP.
    
- **Aura of Protection (trained reflex):** Allied creatures within 10 ft gain +1 AC when Kael isn’t incapacitated.
    

### Actions

- **Multiattack.** Three melee attacks with Longsword.
    
- **Longsword.** +9 to hit, 1d8 + 4 slashing (1d10 + 4 versatile).
    
- **Shield Bash (Recharge 5–6).** +9 to hit, 1d6 + 4 bludgeoning; target Str save DC 17 or knocked prone.
    
- **Defensive Stance (Bonus, 1/short rest).** Until next turn, Kael and adjacent creature gain resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing.
    

### Tactics

Keeps one step between any stranger and the Queen. Opens with bash to prone target, follows with precision strikes. If the Queen is threatened, uses Action Surge to interpose and strike twice.

### 🧱 **Tactical Notes**

Kael’s kit makes him a front-line juggernaut: AC 21 (Plate + Shield +1 each), fear-immune leader, and radiant counter to fiendish threats (fits with Braewood’s “holy/defensive” image).  
If he fights, he forms a **protective wedge around the Queen**, forcing duelists into him.
