# **Father Maltrius Kael – Vampric Cleric of Asmodeus (Final Boss)**

**Race:** Vampire (customized)  
**Class:** Cleric (Forge/Death/Trickery flavor), Level 12  
**Alignment:** Lawful Evil  
**Deity:** Asmodeus  
**CR:** 13  
**XP:** 10,000

---

### **Stats**

|Ability|Score|Modifier|
|---|---|---|
|STR|16|+3|
|DEX|18|+4|
|CON|16|+3|
|INT|14|+2|
|WIS|20|+5|
|CHA|18|+4|

**AC:** 18 (natural armor + infernal wards)  
**HP:** 180 (16d8 + 112)  
**Speed:** 30 ft, fly 40 ft (bat form), climb 20 ft

**Initiative:** +4 (DEX)

**Saving Throws:** Wis +10, Cha +9, Dex +9, Con +8  
**Skills:** Arcana +7, Religion +7, Persuasion +9, Intimidation +9  
**Damage Resistances:** necrotic; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks  
**Damage Immunities:** fire (infernal blessing)  
**Condition Immunities:** charmed, frightened  
**Senses:** darkvision 120 ft, passive Perception 15  
**Languages:** Common, Infernal, Abyssal

---

### **Vampiric Traits**

- **Shapechanger:** Can transform into a bat or mist. Mist form can pass through 1-inch cracks, fly 40 ft, cannot attack.
    
- **Regeneration:** Regains 15 HP at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 HP and isn’t in sunlight or running water.
    
- **Vampiric Bite:** Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: 1d10+6 piercing damage plus 10 necrotic damage. Target must succeed on DC 18 Constitution save or have their max HP reduced by the necrotic damage.
    
- **Legendary Resistance (3/day):** If Maltrius fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.
    

---

### **Cleric Abilities**

- **Domain:** Trickery / Death-flavored (reflecting Asmodeus)
    
- **Channel Divinity (2/rest):**
    
    1. **Hellfire Rebuke:** 30 ft radius; creatures that hit him with a melee attack take 4d8 fire damage (DEX save for half, DC 18).
        
    2. **Infernal Command:** Force 1 creature within 30 ft to make a WIS save or be charmed/controlled for 1 minute.
        
- **Divine Strike:** Adds 1d8 fire or necrotic damage to weapon attacks once per turn.
    
- **Spellcasting:** (WIS 20, DC 18, +10 to hit)
    

**Prepared Spells:**

- Cantrips: _Thaumaturgy, Toll the Dead, Sacred Flame, Inflict Wounds_
    
- 1st: _Inflict Wounds, Command, Hellish Rebuke_
    
- 2nd: _Mirror Image, Scorching Ray, Hold Person_
    
- 3rd: _Fireball (flavored as infernal flames), Animate Dead, Counterspell_
    
- 4th: _Wall of Fire, Blight_
    
- 5th: _Flame Strike, Destructive Wave_
    
- 6th: _Harm, Blade Barrier (optional final stage)_
    

---

### **Attacks**

- **Multiattack:** 1 Bite + 2 Claw attacks
    
- **Claw:** Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: 1d8+6 slashing damage.
    
- **Bite:** +9 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: 1d10+6 piercing + 10 necrotic (see vampiric trait).
    
- **Hellfire Blast (Recharge 5–6):** 30-ft line, 8d6 fire damage (DEX save for half).
    

---

### **Legendary Actions (3/round)**

1. **Move:** Fly up to half speed.
    
2. **Bite Attack:** Make a bite attack.
    
3. **Infernal Command:** Use Channel Divinity (1/day limit resets each round).
    

---

### **Lair Actions (Volcano Manor)**

- **Magma Pulse (initiative 20):** Pools of molten lava erupt in a 20 ft radius, dealing 4d10 fire damage (DEX save for half).
    
- **Infernal Mist:** 1 creature of Maltrius’s choice within 30 ft must succeed on WIS save or be frightened until the end of its next turn.
    
- **Blood Echo:** If a creature is reduced to 0 HP, the lava absorbs the blood, and Maltrius regains 15 HP.
    

---

### **Magic Items**

- **Infernal Staff of Asmodeus:** +2 magic quarterstaff; can cast _Fireball_ 3/day, _Counterspell_ 2/day. Can use Channel Divinity without expending a slot once per day.
    
- **Amulet of Vampiric Domination:** Grants +2 AC, +1 to spell attack rolls, and advantage on Charisma (Persuasion/Intimidation) checks vs mortals.
    
- **Ring of Hellfire Resistance:** Immune to fire; can spend 1 reaction to absorb half fire damage and release it in 15 ft radius (4d6 fire).
    

---

### **Description / Tactics**

- Father Maltrius Kael is **tall, gaunt, and imposing**, with **ashen skin, crimson eyes, and faint burns scarred across his body**. His voice carries the weight of authority and threat.
    
- He **prefers controlling the battlefield** with fire and fear.
    
- In combat, he opens with **Mirror Image** and **Wall of Fire**, then targets healers and spellcasters first.
    
- When weakened (<50% HP), he transforms into **mist**, then strikes with **Bite** to regain HP or reposition.
    
- **Lair and Legendary Actions** make the fight cinematic and terrifying — lava and environment constantly threaten the PCs.
