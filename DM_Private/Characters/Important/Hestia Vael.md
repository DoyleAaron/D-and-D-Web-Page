## 🔮 Royal Advisor —Hestia Vael_

**Race/Class:** Half-Elf Warlock 15 (The Great Old One Patron)  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good (fights corruption through insight)  
**Role:** The Queen’s arcane eye and manipulator of minds.

### Appearance & Personality

Tall, willow-thin, silver-white hair bound in iron rings. Speaks softly but every syllable feels weighted. Her left eye glows faint violet when she channels power. Pragmatic and eerily calm — she claims to “hear the echoes of decisions before they are made.”

### Spellcasting

**Warlock Lvl 15**, Spell Save DC 18, Spell Atk +10  
**Slots (5th-level, 3 slots)**  
**Invocations:** Agonizing Blast, Repelling Blast, Misty Visions, Fiendish Vigor, Witch Sight, Eldritch Mind.

**Cantrips:** Eldritch Blast (3 rays, 1d10 + 5 each), Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, Prestidigitation.  
**Spells Known:** Armor of Agathys, Hex, Hold Person, Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Fear, Dimension Door, Synaptic Static, Scrying, Wall of Force, Contact Other Plane, Far Step, Telekinesis.

### Traits

- **Awakened Mind:** Telepathic 30 ft communication.
    
- **Eldritch Master (1/day):** Regain all spell slots after 1-minute meditation.
    
- **Mind Ward (reaction):** Impose Disadvantage on a single Wis save vs a spell once per short rest.
    

### Actions

- **Eldritch Blast.** 3 beams (120 ft) +10 to hit, 1d10 + 5 force each; push 10 ft on hit.
    
- **Armor of Agathys (5th lvl).** 50 temp HP and cold damage retaliation when hit by melee.
    
- **Synaptic Static (once per combat).** 20-ft radius psychic explosion (8d6 psychic, Int save 18 half).
    
- **Wall of Force (once per day).** Used to seal audience hall in emergencies.
    

### Tactics

Opens with **Synaptic Static** to confuse threats, uses **Eldritch Blast + Repelling** to shove enemies from the Queen. Maintains **Armor of Agathys** constantly in court sessions.

### 🧙 **Tactical Notes**

Lysandra’s build supports her as a **battlefield controller and political manipulator**.

- **Defensive loop:** _Armor of Agathys (5th)_ + _Mirror Image_ + _Wall of Force_ = self-contained fortress.
    
- **Offense:** _Eldritch Blast_ (Repelling) and _Synaptic Static_ for large-scale disruption.
    
- **Utility:** _Scrying, Dispel Magic,_ and _Counterspell_ handle subterfuge or betrayal attempts.
    

### 💠 **Personality Use of Gear**

She wears all items as understated jewelry — nothing ostentatious. The cloak is near-black until it catches torchlight, then flickers like liquid stars. Her staff is always beside the throne dais, quietly humming when detecting lies or arcane distortions.
