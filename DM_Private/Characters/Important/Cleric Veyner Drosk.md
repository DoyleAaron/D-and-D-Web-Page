# **Pendant Cleric (Red Scarf Council Member)**

_Medium humanoid (human), lawful evil_

**Armour Class** 12 (robes, shield of faith possible)  
**Hit Points** 18 (4d8)  
**Speed** 30 ft.

**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 11 (+0) | **CON** 12 (+1) | **INT** 13 (+1) | **WIS** 15 (+2) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Saving Throws** Wis +4  
**Skills** Religion +3, Intimidation +4  
**Senses** passive Perception 12  
**Languages** Common, Celestial  
**Challenge** 1 (200 XP)

---

### **Traits**

**Pendant of Subjugation.** The cleric wields the pendant. While attuned, he can control up to **6 commoners** at once (they act on his initiative). If a humanoid with **10 HP or less** starts its turn within 30 ft. of the pendant, it must succeed on a **DC 12 Wisdom save** or fall under his control. Controlled commoners lose free will until the pendant is destroyed or the cleric dies.

**Dark Conviction.** When reduced to half HP, the cleric gains **Advantage on attack rolls** until the start of his next turn (fanatic resolve).

---

### **Actions**

**Mace.** _Melee Weapon Attack_: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) bludgeoning.

**Command Horde (Recharge 5–6).** The cleric orders all controlled commoners to attack in unison. Each controlled commoner can move up to its speed and make one attack immediately.

**Subjugating Pulse (1/day).** The pendant emits a wave of psychic energy. Each creature of the cleric’s choice within 20 ft. must succeed on a **DC 12 Wisdom save** or be frightened until the end of their next turn.

---

### **Spellcasting (Cleric-lite)**

The cleric is a **2nd-level spellcaster** (Wisdom, spell save DC 12, +4 to hit). He has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will):** Thaumaturgy, Sacred Flame (1d8 radiant, DC 12 Dex), Spare the Dying
    
- **1st level (2 slots):** Command, Shield of Faith, Healing Word

# **Controlled Commoner**

**Armour Class** 10  
**Hit Points** 4 (1d8)  
**Speed** 30 ft.

**STR** 10 | **DEX** 10 | **CON** 10 | **INT** 8 | **WIS** 9 | **CHA** 8

**Actions**  
**Club.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +2 to hit, 1d4 bludgeoning damage.

---

### **Encounter Balance**

- Cleric (CR 1) + 6 commoners (CR 0 each) → “Medium–Hard” for a level 1 party of 4.
    
- Commoners are weak (most go down in one hit), but they force the party to make choices: cut down innocents or push through to the cleric.
    
- Pendant mechanics keep the flavour without overwhelming the PCs.
### **Pendant Mechanics**

This is where we make the fight special.

**Pendant of Subjugation** _(working name)_

- **Effect**: Controls weak humanoids (commoners, peasants, malnourished thugs).
    
- **Control Limit**: Up to **12 creatures** at once.
    
- **Control Conditions**:
    
    - Only affects creatures with **<10 HP** or **CR 1/4 or lower**.
        
    - Does not affect PCs unless they are **0 HP but stable/unconscious** (so no mind control on the party mid-fight — avoids railroading).
        
- **Pendant Actions (Cleric can use 1/round)**:
    
    - _Command Horde_: As a bonus action, he can order all controlled commoners to move/attack.
        
    - _Drain Will_: Choose 1 controlled creature; it takes 1 damage but gains Advantage on its next attack.
        
    - _Dominate New Victim_: If a humanoid drops below 10 HP, the pendant can make them roll a **Wisdom save DC 13**. On a fail, they join the horde.
