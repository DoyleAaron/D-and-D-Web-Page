## Tactics

- **Fanaticism:** Fight to the death (Dark Devotion)
- **Protect Leader:** Cultists shield their fanatic/priest
- **Ritual:** May be performing ritual when encountered
- **Numbers:** Attack in groups (4-8 cultists)
- **Spellcasters:** Fanatics use *hold person*, then coup de grace

---

## Variants by Deity/Patron

### Sea Cult (Drowned God)
- Add swimming speed 20 ft.
- Sacrifice victims to the sea
- May have a Merrow or Sea Hag ally

### Death Cult (Undead Lord)
- Fanatics can cast *animate dead*
- May have skeleton/zombie servants
- Wear bone jewelry

### Infernal Cult (Devil Worship)
- Add fire resistance
- Fanatics can cast *hellish rebuke* (reaction)
- May have imp familiar

### Aberrant Cult (Far Realm)
- WIS saves at disadvantage near leader
- Fanatics can cast *dissonant whispers*
- Strange mutations possible
