## ✨ Homebrew Spells (Full Details)

### Tinn's Interlocking Aegis *(5th-level abjuration, concentration 1 min)*
- Summon up to **3 linked barrier panels** (10 ft. each) within 60 ft.
- Each panel: **AC 17, 30 HP**. Impassable.
- **Bonus action:** Slide or rotate one panel up to 15 ft.
- *Round 1 default opener in combat.*

### Laughing Rebuff *(3rd-level abjuration, reaction)*
- Trigger: creature Vaelor can see targets him with an attack.
- **Impose disadvantage** on the roll.
- On a **miss:** attacker takes **2d8 force damage**.
- Vaelor makes a remark. It's usually decent.

### Harbor Dome Protocol *(7th-level abjuration, concentration 10 min)*
- **20-ft radius hemisphere** centered on ground point.
- Allies inside: **half cover** + **advantage on saves** vs. spells from outside.
- Dome HP: **60**. On destruction: all allies inside gain **8 temp HP**.
- *Use when multiple allies need mass protection.*

### Silent Corridor *(4th-level abjuration)*
- **40 ft. × 10 ft. lane** of suppressed hostile magic.
- Enemy **spell attacks** through the lane: **disadvantage**.
- Allied movement through: **no opportunity attacks**.
- No concentration. Duration: 1 minute.

## 🗺️ Combat Patterns

### Round 1
- **Default:** `Tinn's Interlocking Aegis` — establish geometry, protect key allies.
- **Mass threat:** `Wall of Force` to split the field.

### Round 2
- Hold `Counterspell` reaction. Reposition allies with bonus action Aegis slide.
- If safe: `Globe of Invulnerability` for sustained spell-heavy fight.

### Round 3+
- Large-scale threat: `Harbor Dome Protocol` to protect group.
- Single priority target: `Forcecage` (no save, no teleport out).
- Fleeing option: `Dimension Door` (brings one ally, 500 ft.).

### Retreat Priority
1. Civilians
2. Diplomatic personnel
3. Himself
