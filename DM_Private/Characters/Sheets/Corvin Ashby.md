## Background

### History
- Former clerk for a merchant house
- Fired for skimming money
- Fell into debt with the Mudlark gang
- Stole the marble statue to pay off debts
- Now hiding in the slums with his sister

### The Theft
- Stole a valuable marble statue from a sculptor
- Owes the Mudlarks 200 gp
- Statue is worth 500 gp
- Thought he could sell it and keep the difference

### Current Situation
- Hiding in a slum tenement
- Protected by 3 Mudlark thugs (he owes them)
- His sister Vella is with him (she's furious)
- Terrified of both the guild and the Mudlarks

---

## Roleplay Notes

### Voice
- Whiny and pleading
- Makes constant excuses
- Blames everyone but himself

### Mannerisms
- Wrings hands nervously
- Eyes dart to exits
- Flinches at sudden movements

### In Combat
- Will NOT fight
- Surrenders immediately
- Hides behind sister or thugs
- May try to flee if opportunity arises

---

## Interrogation

### What He'll Reveal Easily
- Where the statue is (in the room)
- That he stole it (obvious)
- That he owes the Mudlarks

### What He'll Reveal Under Pressure
- The Mudlarks' local hideout location
- Names of gang members
- His former employer's shady dealings

### What He Won't Reveal
- Nothing—he'll tell everything to save himself

---

## Resolution Options

### Return Him to the Sculptor
- Sculptor wants the statue back
- May or may not press charges
- Corvin faces prison or worse

### Let Him Go
- He'll flee the city
- Mudlarks will hunt him
- Unlikely to survive long

### Help Him
- Party could pay his debt (200 gp)
- Or deal with the Mudlarks for him
- Corvin becomes grateful (useless) ally
