## Background

### History
- Works as a seamstress in the market district
- Always cleaning up Corvin's messes
- Parents died years ago, she raised him
- Honest worker, good reputation (until now)

### Why She's Here
- Corvin came to her for help after the theft
- She couldn't turn him away (family)
- Furious but protective
- Hoping to find a way out of this mess

### Her Perspective
- Knows Corvin is guilty
- Knows the statue should be returned
- Just wants this to end without her brother dying
- Will cooperate with reasonable people

---

## Roleplay Notes

### Voice
- Exasperated, tired
- Sharper than Corvin's whining
- Occasionally shows genuine fear

### Mannerisms
- Glares at Corvin frequently
- Stands protectively despite anger
- Practical, looks for solutions

### In Combat
- Will NOT initiate violence
- May step between party and Corvin
- Will surrender to protect brother

## Interaction with Party

### If Approached Diplomatically
- Relieved to talk to reasonable people
- Will help convince Corvin to cooperate
- May offer information about the Mudlarks

- Will cooperate to prevent violence
- Still protects Corvin verbally

- Tries to get Corvin to surrender
