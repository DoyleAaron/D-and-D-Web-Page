## 👤 Pip Willowbark – Field Medic

*Small humanoid (halfling), neutral good*

**Armor Class:** 13 (leather armor)  
**Hit Points:** 22 (5d6+5)  
**Speed:** 25 ft.

---

### Proficiencies

**Saving Throws:** WIS +5, INT +4  
**Skills:** Medicine +7, Nature +4, Perception +5, Sleight of Hand +4, Survival +5  
**Tools:** Herbalism Kit, Healer's Kit, Alchemist's Supplies, Poisoner's Kit  
**Languages:** Common, Halfling, Elvish  
**CR:** 1 (200 XP)

---

### Traits

**Halfling Luck.** Reroll 1s on attack rolls, ability checks, or saving throws.

**Brave.** Advantage on saves vs. frightened.

**Nimble.** Can move through the space of any Medium or larger creature.

**Combat Medic.** Pip can use a healer's kit as a bonus action instead of an action.

**Battlefield Triage.** When Pip stabilizes a creature with 0 HP, that creature regains 1 HP instead of just becoming stable.

**Apothecary's Eye.** Pip can identify potions, poisons, and herbal remedies without a check. Advantage on checks to identify magical effects on creatures.

---

### Actions

**Sling.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 4 (1d4+2) bludgeoning damage.

**Dagger.** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 4 (1d4+2) piercing damage.

**Administer Potion (Action).** Pip feeds a potion to a willing or unconscious creature within 5 feet.

**Treat Wounds (Action, requires Healer's Kit).** One creature within 5 feet regains 1d6+4 HP. Can only benefit one creature once per short rest.

---

### Bonus Actions

**Quick Patch (Healer's Kit).** Stabilize a creature at 0 HP within 5 feet.

**Stimulant Injection (1/Day).** One creature within 5 feet gains advantage on their next saving throw and can immediately repeat a save against one condition affecting them.

---

### Reactions

**"Get Down!"** When an ally within 5 feet is targeted by an attack Pip can see, Pip can shout a warning, granting the ally +2 AC against that attack.

### Appearance
- **Age:** 42 (middle-aged for a halfling)
- **Height:** 3'2"
- **Build:** Slightly plump, soft hands kept meticulously clean
- **Hair:** Curly chestnut brown, usually tied back
- **Eyes:** Warm brown, with reading spectacles perched on nose
- **Clothing:** Practical vest with many pockets, herb-stained apron

- **Demeanor:** Cheerful, unflappable, dark sense of humor
- **Values:** Every life has value, pain is optional
