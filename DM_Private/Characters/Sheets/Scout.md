## Traits

### Keen Hearing and Sight
The scout has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or sight.

---

## Actions

### Multiattack
The scout makes two melee attacks or two ranged attacks.

### Shortsword
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

### Longbow
*Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.

---

## Reactions

### Skirmisher
When a foe ends its turn within 5 feet of the scout, the scout can move up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

## Tactics

- **Stealth First:** Always tries to observe before engaging
- **Range:** Prefers to fight from 60+ feet away
- **Skirmish:** Uses Skirmisher to avoid melee
- **Retreat:** Will flee if brought below 25% HP
- **Reporting:** Primary goal is often intel, not combat

---

## Variants

### Bandit Scout
- Serves a bandit gang
- Will report party size/strength to captain
- Less loyal, may be bribed

### Military Scout
- Serves army or kingdom
- More disciplined, follows orders
- Carries signal horn or flare

### Urban Scout (Spy)
- Use Spy stat block instead
- Operates in cities
- Disguise kit proficiency
- Insight +5 for reading people
