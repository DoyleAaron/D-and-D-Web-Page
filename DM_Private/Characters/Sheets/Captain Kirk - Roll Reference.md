## 📖 Roleplay & History Checks

### What Kirk Knows (No Roll Needed)
- His own naval career and battles
- The seas around Braewood, Adaham, Rimduff Isles
- Ship operations, tactics, naval warfare
- His pact with Zephyrion
- The Spehlina (was saved by it as a youth)
- Marina (his adopted daughter)

### History Checks (INT +0, or use Persuasion/Charisma to recall stories)
| DC | What He Remembers |
|----|-------------------|
| 10 | General naval history of Braewood |
| 12 | Major battles he participated in |
| 15 | Admiral Varn Frostwind (Iona's father) |
| 15 | The Pirate Queen (his ancestor) |
| 18 | Obscure naval tactics or ship designs |
| 20 | Zephyrion lore, water deity politics |

### Insight Checks (WIS +1 Land / +2 Water)
| DC | What He Notices |
|----|-----------------|
| 10 | Obvious lies or discomfort |
| 13 | Hidden motives in conversation |
| 15 | Deception in experienced liars |
| 18 | Complex emotional states |

### Persuasion Checks (CHA +6 Land / +8 Water)
*Kirk is a natural leader and speaker*
| Situation | Modifier |
|-----------|----------|
| Commanding sailors | Advantage |
| Naval/military topics | Advantage |
| Talking about his past | Normal |
| Emotional topics (Marina) | Disadvantage |

### Intimidation Checks (CHA +6 Land / +8 Water)
| Situation | Modifier |
|-----------|----------|
| Against pirates/sailors | Advantage (reputation) |
| In Water Form | Advantage (appearance) |
| As Hestaria | Auto-success on most mortals |
