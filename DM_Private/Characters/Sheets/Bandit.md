## Tactics

- **Groups:** Bandits fight in groups of 3-6
- **Ambush:** Prefer to attack from hiding (+2 to first attack)
- **Morale:** Flee if leader falls or 50%+ casualties
- **Surrender:** Will surrender if cornered and offered terms

---

## Variants

### Bandit Archer
- Replace scimitar with shortsword (1d6 + 1)
- Primary weapon: Longbow (+3, 1d8 + 1, range 150/600)
- Stays at range, uses cover

### Bandit Brute
- AC 11 (no armor)
- HP 16 (3d8 + 3)
- STR 14 (+2)
- Weapon: Greatclub (+4, 1d8 + 2 bludgeoning)
- Intimidating but slow
