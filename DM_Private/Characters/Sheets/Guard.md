## Tactics

- **Formation:** Guards fight in pairs or groups
- **Defensive:** Use shields, protect each other
- **Alarm:** One guard raises alarm while others engage
- **Lawful:** Follow orders, don't pursue beyond jurisdiction
- **Capture:** Prefer to subdue rather than kill (usually)

---

## Variants

### City Watch
- Standard guard stats
- Carries club (1d4 + 1 bludgeoning) instead of spear
- Manacles on belt
- Will call for backup

### Palace Guard
- Elite Guard stats
- Fancy uniform (intimidating)
- Trained to protect specific person
- Will die before abandoning post

### Dockside Guard
- Veteran Guard stats
- Light armor (AC 14, leather + shield)
- Cutlass instead of spear
- Knows the docks, can call for help from sailors
