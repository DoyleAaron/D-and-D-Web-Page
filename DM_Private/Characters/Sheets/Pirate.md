## Tactics

- **Boarding:** Rush to grapple enemies, prevent escape
- **Numbers:** Pirates swarm targets (3+ on one enemy)
- **Ranged:** Use crossbows before boarding
- **Morale:** Flee if captain falls, or if half crew is down
- **Surrender:** May surrender if offered terms

---

## Variants

### Pirate Archer
- Longbow instead of crossbow
- +3 to hit, 1d8 + 1 piercing, range 150/600
- Stays in rigging or crow's nest

### Pirate Brute
- AC 11 (shirtless)
- HP 16 (3d8 + 3)
- STR 14 (+2)
- Weapon: Greatclub (+4, 1d8 + 2)
- Used for intimidation and heavy lifting

### Pirate Powder Monkey
- Young, quick
- DEX 14 (+2)
- Speed 35 ft.
- Carries powder kegs, lights fuses
- Non-combatant, will flee
