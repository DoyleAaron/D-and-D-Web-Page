*Medium humanoid (dwarf), chaotic good*

**Armor Class:** 14 (leather apron with metal plates)  
**Hit Points:** 32 (5d8+10)  
**Speed:** 25 ft.

### Proficiencies

**Saving Throws:** CON +4, INT +6  
**Skills:** Arcana +6, Investigation +6, Perception +2, Sleight of Hand +4  
**Tools:** Tinker's Tools (+8), Smith's Tools (+6), Thieves' Tools (+4), Alchemist's Supplies (+6)  
**Languages:** Common, Dwarvish, Gnomish  
**CR:** 1 (200 XP)

---

### Traits

**Dwarven Resilience.** Advantage on saves vs. poison, resistance to poison damage.

**Stonecunning.** +2 to History checks related to stonework.

**Mechanical Genius.** Advantage on checks to understand, repair, or disable mechanical devices.

**Jury-Rig (1/Short Rest).** Torgun can spend 1 minute to create a temporary fix for any broken mechanical device. It works for 1 hour before failing again.

**Trap Sense.** Torgun has advantage on saves against traps and +5 to passive Perception to detect them.

**Explosive Expertise.** Torgun has resistance to fire damage from his own devices (he's learned the hard way).

---

### Actions

**Light Hammer.** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 3 (1d4+1) bludgeoning damage.

**Hand Crossbow (Modified).** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage.  
*Special:* Can fire specialty bolts (see Gadgets).

**Throw Gadget.** Torgun throws one of his gadgets (see Inventory). Range 20/60 ft.

**Deploy Trap (1 minute).** Set up a mechanical trap in a 5-foot space.

---

### Bonus Actions

**Quick Toss.** Throw a small gadget (smoke bomb, flash powder) as a bonus action.

**Activate Gadget.** Activate a pre-set mechanical device within 30 feet.

---

### Reactions

**"Hit the Deck!"** When one of Torgun's devices is about to explode unexpectedly, he can shout a warning. All creatures within 10 feet gain advantage on the DEX save.

## 🎭 Personality & Roleplay

### Appearance
- **Age:** 95 (young adult for a dwarf)
- **Height:** 4'4"
- **Build:** Stocky, one arm slightly more muscular (hammering arm)
- **Hair:** Singed brown beard with brass beads, wild hair usually on fire
- **Eyes:** Bright blue, magnified by thick goggles
- **Face:** Soot-stained, missing half an eyebrow (explosion), manic grin
- **Clothing:** Leather apron covered in burn marks, tool belt with 47 pockets, goggles on forehead
- **Notable:** Always smells like oil and gunpowder, fingers stained with grease

### Personality
- **Demeanor:** Enthusiastic, easily distracted, talks very fast about projects
- **Values:** Innovation over tradition, "if it works, it works"
- **Flaw:** Gets so excited about projects he forgets safety
- **Voice:** Rapid-fire, technical jargon, frequent tangents

### Quotes
> *"Hold this. Don't touch that. Actually, don't hold that either. Just... stand back."*

> *"It's not a flaw, it's a FEATURE. The explosion is part of the design!"*

> *"I could make ye a crossbow that fires three bolts at once! Well, two. One of 'em goes backwards. Still working on that."*

> *"Sleep? I'll sleep when I'm dead! Or when this prototype stops ticking. EVERYONE OUT!"*

> *"Traditional dwarven smithing is fine, FINE, but have ye considered... GEARS?"*

> *[After explosion]* "That was actually better than expected."

> *The workshop is organized chaos. Gears cover every surface. Things whir, click, and occasionally spark. A partially-assembled clockwork dog sits on the main workbench, one leg twitching.
