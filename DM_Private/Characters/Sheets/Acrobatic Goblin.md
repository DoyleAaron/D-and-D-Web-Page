# Acrobatic Goblin – Enemy Sheet

*Nimble, chaotic, and infuriatingly hard to pin down*

---

## 🎪 Overview

These aren't your standard cave-dwelling goblins. Acrobatic goblins are trained performers, circus escapees, or members of a tumbling war-band. They flip, cartwheel, and bounce around the battlefield, striking from unexpected angles.

**Use When:** You want combat to feel chaotic and mobile, or need enemies that frustrate melee-heavy parties.

---

## 🤸 Acrobatic Goblin

*Small humanoid (goblinoid), neutral evil*

**Armor Class:** 15 (leather armor + DEX)  
**Hit Points:** 10 (3d6)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., climb 20 ft.

### Proficiencies

**Saving Throws:** DEX +6  
**Skills:** Acrobatics +6, Stealth +6, Performance +2  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 9  
**Languages:** Common, Goblin  
**CR:** 1/2 (100 XP)

---

### Traits

**Nimble Escape.** The goblin can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.

**Tumbling Defense.** When the goblin moves at least 10 feet before making an attack, it doesn't provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of its turn.

**Evasion.** If the goblin is subjected to an effect that allows a DEX save for half damage, it takes no damage on a success and half on a failure.

**Spring Off.** The goblin can use allies, walls, or enemies as springboards. It can move through a creature's space (friend or foe) without penalty and gains +5 ft. to jump distance.

---

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The goblin makes two attacks: one with its dagger and one with its kick, OR two dagger throws.

**Acrobatic Dagger.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 6 (1d4+4) piercing damage.  
*Special:* If the goblin moved at least 15 feet this turn, add +2 damage (8 total).

**Flying Kick.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 6 (1d4+4) bludgeoning damage. Target must succeed on a DC 12 STR save or be pushed 5 feet.

**Thrown Dagger.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 6 (1d4+4) piercing damage.

---

### Bonus Actions

**Nimble Escape.** Disengage or Hide.

**Tumble Through.** The goblin can attempt to move through a hostile creature's space. The creature must succeed on a DC 14 DEX save or the goblin moves through and gains advantage on its next attack against that creature.

---

### Reactions

**Uncanny Dodge (1/Turn).** When an attacker the goblin can see hits it with an attack, it can halve the damage.

## ⚔️ Tactics

### General Strategy
1. **Never stand still** – always move at least 15 feet to trigger bonuses
2. **Hit and run** – attack, then Nimble Escape to Disengage
3. **Use the environment** – climb walls, swing from ropes, bounce off allies
4. **Swarm isolated targets** – 3-4 goblins focus one PC, then scatter

### Opening Round
- Tumblers and Acrobatics rush in from multiple directions
- Trapeze Artists drop from above
- Jesters throw pocket sand at dangerous-looking PCs

### Mid-Combat
- Keep mobile – never let PCs corner them
- Blade Dancers target wounded enemies for finishing blows
- If a goblin is hurt, it tumbles away and lets others take over

### Retreat
- At 50% casualties, goblins flee in all directions
- They scatter – chasing one means losing the others
- May regroup for ambush later

- 2 Trapeze Artists
- Environment: Ship rigging, warehouse with rafters, or tall trees

### The Blade Dance (Hard)
- 2 Goblin Blade Dancers
- 4 Acrobatic Goblins
- Environment: Open arena, lots of room to move

### Goblin Flash Mob (Chaotic)
- 8 Tumblers (CR 1/4)
- 2 Jesters
- Goal: Steal something and escape, not kill the party

## 🎨 Appearance & Flavor

### Visual Description
> *The goblin lands in a crouch, yellow eyes gleaming with manic glee. It wears tight leather wrappings, fingers and toes exposed for grip. Bells jingle from its ears. Before you can react, it backflips over your sword swing, cackling.*

### Sound Effects
- Constant giggling and cackling
- Bells or chimes on their clothing
- "Wheee!" during jumps
- Raspberry sounds when they dodge

### Combat Narration
> *"The goblin runs up the wall, kicks off, and spirals through the air—its dagger catching the torchlight as it descends toward your neck."*

> *"Three goblins bounce off each other like a living pinball machine, each one striking a different party member before landing in a cackling pile."*

## 📚 Scaling Guide

### For Level 1-2 Parties
- Use Tumblers (CR 1/4) primarily
- 1 Acrobatic Goblin as "leader"
- Lower HP by 2-3 per goblin

### For Level 3-4 Parties
- Mix of Acrobatics and Blade Dancers
- Add environmental hazards
- Use standard stats

### For Level 5+ Parties
- Use Blade Dancers primarily
- Add a Goblin Boss (standard) commanding them
- Increase numbers or add other goblin types
- Give some goblins poison on their blades (+1d4 poison)
