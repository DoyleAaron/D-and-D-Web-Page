## Traits

### Hold Breath
While out of water, the octopus can hold its breath for 1 hour.

### Underwater Camouflage
The octopus has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made while underwater.

### Water Breathing
The octopus can breathe only underwater.

---

## Actions

### Tentacles
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 10 (2d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it is grappled (escape DC 16). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the octopus can't use its tentacles on another target.

### Ink Cloud (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest)
A 20-foot-radius cloud of ink extends all around the octopus if it is underwater. The area is heavily obscured for 1 minute, although a significant current can disperse the ink. After releasing the ink, the octopus can use the Dash action as a bonus action.

## Tactics

- **Ambush:** Uses camouflage to surprise prey
- **Grapple:** Grabs target, drags underwater to drown
- **Ink Escape:** If reduced below 25% HP, uses ink cloud and flees
- **Ship Attack:** Wraps tentacles around hull, reaches for crew
- **Drowning:** Pulled underwater, target suffocates

### Drowning Rules Reminder
- Can hold breath for 1 + CON modifier minutes (minimum 30 seconds)
- When out of breath, survives CON modifier rounds
- Then drops to 0 HP and is dying

---

## Ship Combat

### Attacking the Ship
- **Hull:** AC 15, HP 100 (or more for larger ships)
- Octopus deals half damage to structures
- If hull reaches 0, ship begins sinking

### Crew Targets
- Can reach 15-20 ft. onto deck
- Grapples and drags overboard
- Crew must make DC 13 STR save or be pulled off

---

## Encounter Ideas

### Sea Voyage (CR 1)
- Single Giant Octopus attacks during calm waters
- Tentacles reach over railings
- Kirk's water form can fight it in the water

### Reef Ambush (CR 3)
- Monstrous Octopus lurks near reef
- Party in rowboat or swimming
- Attacks from hidden position

### Kraken's Warning (CR 5)
- Kraken Spawn attacks as a sign of worse things below
- Desperate fight, may attract other creatures
- Foreshadowing of true kraken in deep waters
