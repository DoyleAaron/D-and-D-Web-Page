# Rekka "Redtooth"

*"You can hand over the cargo, or I can take it from your corpse. Your choice."*

---

## Overview

**Name:** Rekka "Redtooth"  
**Race:** Half-Orc  
**Class Equivalent:** Fighter  
**CR:** 2 (Bandit Captain)  
**Role:** Bandit Leader, Mini-Boss (D-03)

---

## Appearance

- **Age:** Early 30s
- **Build:** Tall, muscular, imposing
- **Skin:** Grey-green, weathered
- **Hair:** Black, shaved on sides, topknot
- **Eyes:** Yellow-green, predatory
- **Notable Features:**
  - **Filed teeth stained red** (hence the name)
  - Tribal scarification on arms
  - Wears trophies from victims (rings, teeth)
  - Perpetual sneer showing those red teeth

---

## Personality

- **Demeanor:** Cruel, calculating, patient
- **Values:** Strength, profit, reputation
- **Weakness:** Pride—won't back down from a challenge
- **Voice:** Low growl, deliberate, enjoys intimidation

### Quotes
> *"I file my teeth with the bones of merchants who thought they could fight back."*

> *"The road belongs to me. You're just passing through... if I let you."*

> *"Surrender and I'll only take your goods. Fight and I'll take everything."*

## Traits

### Relentless Endurance (1/Long Rest)
When reduced to 0 HP but not killed outright, Rekka drops to 1 HP instead.

### Savage Attacks
When Rekka scores a critical hit with a melee weapon, roll one additional damage die.

---

## Actions

### Multiattack
Rekka makes three melee attacks: two with her greataxe and one with her bite. Or she makes two ranged attacks with javelins.

### Greataxe
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 9 (1d12 + 3) slashing damage.

### Bite
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage.

### Javelin
*Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.

---

## Reactions

### Parry
Rekka adds 2 to her AC against one melee attack that would hit her. She must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.

---

## Tactics

### Before Combat
- Scouts targets from ambush
- Blocks road with fallen tree
- Positions archers in trees
- Sends 2 bandits to demand surrender first

### Opening
- Lets bandits engage first to assess threat
- Enters combat on round 2 unless challenged directly
- Targets anyone who looks like a leader

### Mid-Combat
- Uses multiattack aggressively
- Bite attack is intimidation as much as damage
- Calls orders to reposition bandits
- Focuses on one target until they drop

### Desperate (Below 20 HP)
- Relentless Endurance keeps her in the fight
- May attempt to flee if clearly outmatched
- Will NOT surrender—too proud
- Would rather die fighting

---

## Background

### History
- Born to an orc raiding party, human mother
- Raised in the wilds, joined bandit gang young
- Rose to leadership through violence
- Has led this band for 3 years
- Known throughout the region

### The Redtooth Name
- Files her teeth to sharp points
- Stains them with blood mixed with red ochre
- Ritual from her orc heritage
- Psychological warfare—terrifies victims

### Her Band
- 6 bandits in her core group
- Attacks merchant caravans on the southern road
- Hides in forest camp between raids
- Recently hit the wine shipment

---

## The Bandit Camp

### Location
- Forest clearing, 2 miles off the road
- 3 canvas tents (Rekka's is largest)
- Central fire pit
- Stolen goods stacked near Rekka's tent

### Defenses
- 2 bandits on watch at any time
- Horses for quick escape
- Trail is trapped (DC 14 to notice, 1d6 piercing)

## Roleplay Notes

### Voice
- Low, rumbling growl
- Speaks slowly, deliberately
- Punctuates threats with a smile (showing teeth)

### Mannerisms
- Runs tongue over filed teeth
- Fingers trophy jewelry when thinking
- Stands with arms crossed, dominating space

### Negotiation
- Respects strength
- Might parley if party seems dangerous
- Demands all goods + 50 gp "passage fee"
- Will accept a challenge to single combat (honor demands it)
