## Roleplay Notes

### As NPCs
- Information sources (gossip, local knowledge)
- Quest givers (simple problems)
- Witnesses to events
- Hostages or rescue targets

### As Obstacles
- Panicking crowds
- Angry mobs (use group rules)
- Bystanders in combat

### As Allies
- Can perform simple tasks
- Provide local knowledge
- Distraction or numbers in emergencies

---

## Angry Mob Rules

When commoners group up:

| Mob Size | Effective CR | Attack Modifier | Damage |
|----------|--------------|-----------------|--------|
| 3-5 | 1/4 | +3 | 6 (2d4 + 1) |
| 6-10 | 1/2 | +4 | 9 (2d6 + 2) |
| 11-20 | 1 | +5 | 14 (4d6) |
| 20+ | 2 | +6 | 21 (6d6) |

**Mob Traits:**
- Disadvantage on DEX saves (too crowded)
- Advantage on STR checks (many hands)
- Disperses if 25%+ casualties or leader killed
