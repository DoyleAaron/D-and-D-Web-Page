## 👴 Aldric Thornwell – The Patriarch

*"We've little to offer, but what we have, we share."*

**Role:** Family head, former laborer  
**Age:** 67  
**Race:** Human  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good

### Appearance
- **Build:** Thin, stooped, joints swollen from years of hard work
- **Face:** Deeply lined, kind eyes clouded with early cataracts
- **Hair:** Wispy white, balding
- **Hands:** Calloused, arthritic, trembling slightly
- **Clothing:** Patched wool tunic, frayed rope belt, worn boots with holes

### Personality
- **Demeanor:** Proud but weary, refuses charity but accepts "fair trade"
- **Values:** Family above all, honest work, dignity in poverty
- **Flaw:** Too proud to ask for help even when desperate
- **Voice:** Gravelly, speaks slowly, occasional coughing fits

### Stats (Commoner, Aged)

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **AC** | 10 |
| **HP** | 4 (reduced from age) |
| **Speed** | 20 ft. (slow, uses walking stick) |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 8 (-1) | 8 (-1) | 8 (-1) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) |

**Skills:** Insight +4, History +2 (local), Animal Handling +4  
**Tools:** Farmer's tools, fishing tackle

**Actions:**
- **Walking Stick:** +1 to hit, 2 (1d4-1) bludgeoning
- **Thrown Stone:** +1 to hit, range 20 ft., 1 bludgeoning

### Background
- Former farmhand, worked the same lord's fields for 40 years
- Wife died 8 years ago (fever)
- Lost the farm to debt 3 years ago
- Now lives in a rundown cottage with his daughter's family
- Knows every local road, landmark, and hiding spot

**Age:** 34

## 🎭 Roleplay Hooks

### Quest Hooks
| Hook | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Missing Child** | Lily wandered into the forest/ruins following her "friend" |
| **Desperate Plea** | Maren asks party to deliver something dangerous for coin |
| **Tam's Trouble** | Boy got mixed up with thieves, needs rescue |
| **Aldric's Secret** | The old man knows where something is buried from his youth |
| **Eviction** | Landlord is kicking them out – party can intervene |
| **Illness** | Pip or Aldric is sick, needs rare medicine |

### Moral Dilemmas
- Party finds Maren has stolen bread – turn her in or help?
- Tam offers to guide party into danger for coin – let him risk himself?
- A villain offers to "help" the family in exchange for information about the party
- The family witnesses something they shouldn't have

### Rewards for Helping
The family has almost nothing, but:
- Aldric knows local history, hidden paths, old stories
- Maren can mend equipment, cook meals, provide a safe place to rest
- Tam can guide, spy, deliver messages
- Lily's "friend" might be a fey who grants a minor boon
- The family becomes loyal allies – worth more than gold
