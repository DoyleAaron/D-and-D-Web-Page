## City Guard (Rank-and-File)

**Look & Vibe:** Ordered lines, polished helms, tired eyes.  
**Personality:** Professional, curt.  
**Background:** Sheyton locals doing a dangerous job under a terrifying commander.

- **AC** 16 (chain shirt, shield)
    
- **HP** 11 (2d8+2)
    
- **Speed** 30 ft.
    
- **STR 13 (+1), DEX 12 (+1), CON 12 (+1), WIS 12 (+1)**
    
- **Proficiency** +2
    
- **Skills** Perception +3
    
- **Senses** passive Perception 13
