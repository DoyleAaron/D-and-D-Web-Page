## Combat Tactics

### When Alarm Is Raised
- Round 1: Nearest pirates engage
- Round 2: Reinforcements arrive (2-4 more)
- Round 3+: Continue trickling in until all engaged

### Group Tactics
- **Swarm:** Pirates try to gang up 2-3 on one target
- **Protect the Captain:** If Iona is present, they prioritize her safety
- **Flee Threshold:** If half are down and leadership falls, morale breaks

### Morale
- **With Iona/Craw:** Fight until ordered to retreat
- **Without Leadership:** Check morale if half are defeated (DC 10 WIS or flee)

## Surrender & Negotiation

### When They'll Surrender
- Iona defeated AND Craw defeated/surrendered
- More than half the crew down
- Offered terms (their lives for departure)

### What They'll Trade
- Location of loot
- Safe passage for the party
- Information about Iona's contacts

### What They Won't Do
- Turn on each other (pirates' code)
- Fight for free (need payment or threats)
- Die for nothing
