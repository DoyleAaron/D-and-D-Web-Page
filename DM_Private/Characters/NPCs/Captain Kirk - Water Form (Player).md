# Captain Kirk – Water Form Stats

*These stats apply when you are on a boat sailing on a natural body of water (lake, ocean, river). You don't need to be submerged—being on deck is enough.*

**Your patron's connection is now direct. You feel the power of the tide flowing through you.**

---

## 🌊 Basic Information

**Captain Kirk, Tide-Touched**  
**Armor Class:** 18 (divine blessing)  
**Hit Points:** 104 (doubled from land form)  
**Speed:** 30 ft., swim 60 ft.

## Enhanced Features

**Everything from your land form, PLUS:**

**Blessing of the Tide.** While on a boat at sea:
- Your HP is doubled (already calculated)
- You can **breathe underwater** if you enter the water
- You have **resistance to cold damage**
- You have **advantage on all attack rolls** while on the water
- You gain **swim speed 60 ft.** if you enter the water

**Improved Critical.** Critical hits on **18-20** (enhanced from 19-20).

**Tidal Surge (3/Day).** As a **bonus action**, move up to 30 feet through water without provoking opportunity attacks. You have advantage on your next attack this turn.

**Pressure Adaptation.** You are immune to the effects of deep water pressure.

---

## Actions (Water Form)

**Multiattack.** You make **three attacks** with Tidebreaker.

**Tidebreaker (Empowered).**  
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 2d8 + 8 slashing damage plus 2d6 cold damage.  
*How to Roll:* Roll 1d20 + 10 to hit. On hit, roll 2d8 + 8 + 2d6 for damage.

**Riptide Strike (Recharge 5-6).**  
Channel the ocean's wrath. All creatures within 20 feet must make a **DC 16 Strength saving throw**.  
*On Failure:* 4d8 bludgeoning damage and pulled 15 feet toward you.  
*On Success:* Half damage, not pulled.  
*Recharge:* At the start of your turn, roll 1d6. On 5-6, this ability recharges.

**Tidal Wave (1/Day).**  
Create a powerful wave in a 30-foot cone.  
All creatures must make a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**.  
*On Failure:* 4d10 bludgeoning damage and knocked prone.  
*On Success:* Half damage, not prone.

- You have **advantage** on attacks against creatures in the water
