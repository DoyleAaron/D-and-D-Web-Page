## Background

### History
- **Origin:** Was a slave child, captured by raiders
- **Rescue:** Kirk rescued her during a naval engagement
- **Adoption:** Kirk raised her as his own daughter
- **Training:** Learned seamanship from the best
- **Life:** Works as a sailor, travels trade routes

### The Kidnapping
- Marina was traveling to **Quey** on a merchant vessel
- The Crimson Tide attacked the ship
- Iona recognized her name—connection to Captain Kirk
- Taken as a hostage for ransom/leverage

### Current State
- Held in the prison cave for over a week
- Defiant but weakening (poor food, cold, damp)
- Not tortured—Iona wants her alive and presentable for ransom
- Other prisoners may be in the caves too

---

## Relationship with Kirk

### Father-Daughter Bond
- Kirk is the only family she's ever known
- She learned everything about the sea from him
- Fierce loyalty—would die for him
- Knows about his pact but not the full details
