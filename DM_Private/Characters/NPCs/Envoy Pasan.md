

**Race:** Human  
**Class:** Bard (College of Eloquence) / Rogue (Diplomat style) – optional multiclass if you want negotiation + finesse  
**Background:** Noble or Courtier (fits envoy role)  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good

---

### **Ability Scores** _(Standard Array Example)_

- **Strength:** 10 – Average, not meant for combat
    
- **Dexterity:** 12 – Enough for nimble gestures
    
- **Constitution:** 12 – Can handle travel, fatigue
    
- **Intelligence:** 14 – Knowledgeable in trade, law, and culture
    
- **Wisdom:** 13 – Perceptive, reads people well
    
- **Charisma:** 16 – Highly persuasive and charming
    

---

### **Skills**

- **Persuasion (Expert)** – Master negotiator
    
- **Insight** – Reads emotions and intentions
    
- **Deception** – Diplomatic tricks if needed
    
- **History / Arcana** – Knowledge of cultures and trade goods
    
- **Perception** – Notices subtleties in negotiation
    

---

### **Equipment**

- Fine clothing with queen’s insignia
    
- Ledger and quill for trade deals
    
- Light dagger (for ceremonial purposes, rarely used in combat)
    
- A signet ring from the queen
    

---

### **Personality Traits**

- Polite, diplomatic, and highly patient
    
- Prefers words over conflict but can act decisively if insulted
    
- Curious about foreign cultures and their practices
    

**Ideal:** Stability – Ensuring the kingdom prospers through trade.  
**Bond:** Loyal to the queen and her court.  
**Flaw:** Can be overly trusting of flattery, risking deception.

---

### **Backstory / Roleplay Notes**

Envoy Pasan is the queen’s trusted representative, tasked with securing trade agreements and maintaining peaceful relations with distant islands. Though not a warrior, his charm, eloquence, and tact make him a valuable asset to the crown. He often travels with gifts, letters of introduction, and knowledge of economic trends to impress potential trade partners.
