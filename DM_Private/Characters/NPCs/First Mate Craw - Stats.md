## Traits

### Martial Advantage
Once per turn, Craw can deal an extra 10 (3d6) damage to a creature he hits with a weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of Craw that isn't incapacitated.

---

## Actions

### Multiattack
Craw makes two greatsword attacks.

### Greatsword
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 9 (2d6+2) slashing damage.

### Javelin
*Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage.

### Leadership (Recharges after Short or Long Rest)
For 1 minute, Craw can utter a special command or warning whenever a nonhostile creature that he can see within 30 feet of him makes an attack roll or a saving throw. The creature can add a d4 to its roll provided it can hear and understand Craw. A creature can benefit from only one Leadership die at a time. This effect ends if Craw is incapacitated.

---

## Tactics

### Opening
- Takes position with clear sightlines
- Uses Leadership immediately to buff nearby pirates
- Stays near at least 2 allies for Martial Advantage

### Mid-Combat
- Focuses on the strongest-looking opponent
- Calls tactical orders to reposition pirates
- Exploits flanking opportunities ruthlessly

### Desperate
- Will fight to the death if ordered to hold position
- May surrender if Iona is defeated and offers are made
- Values survival over pride (unlike Iona)

## Background

### History
- Former soldier in a hobgoblin legion
- Discharged after his unit was disbanded following a failed campaign
- Wandered as a mercenary before Iona recruited him
- Has served as First Mate for three years
- The most disciplined member of the crew by far

### Role on The Crimson Tide
- Maintains crew discipline
- Handles tactical operations during raids
- Manages the crew roster and shares
- Commands when Iona is occupied

### Motivation
- **Payment** – Iona pays well and on time
- **Structure** – The ship gives him the order he craves
- **Respect** – The crew fears and respects him
- **No loyalty to Iona personally** – Would consider offers if she falls

---

## Connections

### Allies
- **Captain Iona** – Employer, mutual respect
- **The crew** – Obey him out of fear and efficiency

### Potential Leverage
