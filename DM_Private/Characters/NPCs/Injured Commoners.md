# **Starved Adult (Commoner Pawn)**

**Look & Vibe:** Hollow cheeks, sunken eyes, scarf-tied wrists. Moves in jerks, like a puppet half-asleep.  
**Personality:** Vacant, submissive, twitching with suppressed fear.  
**Background:** Farmers, cobblers, mothers, brothers — taken, broken, and bound by the pendant’s will.

- **AC** 11 (ragged clothes, no armour)
    
- **HP** 6 (2d8–2)
    
- **Speed** 30 ft.
    
- **STR 10 (+0), DEX 11 (+0), CON 8 (–1), INT 9 (–1), WIS 8 (–1), CHA 6 (–2)**
    
- **Proficiency** +2
    
- **Saves** None
    
- **Skills** None
    
- **Senses** passive Perception 9
    
- **Alignment mask:** “Faithful flock” (truth: enslaved victim)
    

---

**Actions**

- **Dagger.** +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., _Hit:_ **1d4** piercing.
    
- **Desperate Lunge (1/Day).** The adult makes a dagger attack with Advantage. On a hit, it also takes **1 damage** itself (straining its body past breaking).
    

---

**Morale:** Do not retreat. They fight until ordered down or dropped to 0 HP. On pendant’s release, collapse in exhaustion and confusion.

# **Starved Child (Broken Youth)**

**Look & Vibe:** Thin arms, wide terrified eyes. Holds a dagger with both hands like it weighs too much.  
**Personality:** Silent, glassy, only moves when commanded.  
**Background:** Orphans, snatched sons and daughters, stripped of hope and left to rot under the pendant.

- **AC** 10 (rags)
    
- **HP** 3 (1d6)
    
- **Speed** 25 ft.
    
- **STR 6 (–2), DEX 10 (+0), CON 8 (–1), INT 8 (–1), WIS 7 (–2), CHA 5 (–3)**
    
- **Proficiency** +2
    
- **Saves** None
    
- **Skills** None
    
- **Senses** passive Perception 8
    
- **Alignment mask:** “Faithful flock” (truth: enslaved victim)
    

---

**Actions**

- **Dagger.** +0 to hit, reach 5 ft., _Hit:_ **1d4–2** piercing (minimum 1).
    
- **Collapse.** If reduced to half HP, the child automatically falls **Prone** until commanded again (represents frailty).
