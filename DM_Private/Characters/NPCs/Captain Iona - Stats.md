**Name:** Captain Iona Frostwind  
**Race:** Human  
**Class Equivalent:** Fighter/Rogue  
**CR:** 3 (enhanced Bandit Captain)  
**Role:** Pirate Captain, Antagonist

---

- **Age:** Late 30s

- **Hair:** Black, braided with silver beads
- **Eyes:** Pale grey, unsettlingly cold

## Actions

### Multiattack
Iona makes **three melee attacks**: two with her Frostbite Cutlass and one with her dagger. Or she makes two ranged attacks with her hand crossbow.

### Frostbite Cutlass (+1)
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 9 (1d8+4) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) cold damage.

*This enchanted cutlass is perpetually cold to the touch. Frost forms on surfaces it strikes.*

### Dagger
*Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 6 (1d4+3) piercing damage.

### Hand Crossbow
*Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage.

---

## Reactions

### Parry
Iona adds 3 to her AC against one melee attack that would hit her. To do so, she must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.

---

## Tactics

### Opening
- Assesses the threat before committing
- Orders crew to engage while she observes
- Targets obvious spellcasters or healers first

### Mid-Combat
- Uses terrain (ship rigging, crates) for cover
- Keeps mobile, doesn't let herself get surrounded
- Calls orders to crew to flank or reposition

### Desperate
- Will retreat if reduced below 25 HP
- Uses crew as cover to escape
- May attempt to negotiate if cornered
- **Will not surrender** – would rather die fighting

## Background

### History
- Born in the coastal towns near Braewood
- Daughter of **Admiral Varn Frostwind**, a notorious pirate admiral
- At age 12, watched Captain Kirk kill her father after a naval battle
- Her father had surrendered, but Kirk executed him anyway (from Iona's perspective)
- **The Truth:** Varn drew a hidden blade after surrendering – Kirk killed him in self-defense. Iona only saw the killing, not the betrayal.
- Spent 15 years building her fleet and reputation for this revenge
- Rose through the ranks through competence, not cruelty
- Took command of The Crimson Tide five years ago
- Has avoided capture through cunning and bribery

### The Crimson Tide
- Caravel-class vessel, fast and maneuverable
- Crew of ~30 pirates
- Known for red-dyed sails (crimson = blood of her father)
- Operates primarily in the Rimduff Isles
- Commands 4 additional ships in her fleet (5 total)

### Why She Took Marina
- **NOT for ransom** – This is personal vengeance
- **Leverage** – To force Kirk to surrender himself
- **Revenge** – A daughter for a father: Kirk took hers, she'll take his
- **Ultimate Goal:** Make Kirk suffer, then kill him

### The Vendetta
> *"Do you remember my father? Admiral Varn Frostwind? You murdered him after he surrendered. I watched you do it."*

- Iona has dreamed of this moment for 15 years
- She will NOT accept gold or negotiation
- She wants Kirk to surrender – to feel powerless as her father did
- If Kirk refuses and she's losing, she will kill Marina out of spite
- **She cannot be reasoned with** – her hatred runs too deep

## Frostbite Cutlass – Item Details

*Weapon (scimitar), uncommon (requires attunement)*

This curved blade is perpetually cold. Frost creeps across any surface it touches, and your breath mists when you hold it.

**Bonus:** +1 to attack and damage rolls.

**Frost Strike:** On a hit, the target takes an additional 1d6 cold damage.

**Flavor:** The blade was recovered from a shipwreck in northern waters. Its origin is unknown, but it has served Iona well.
