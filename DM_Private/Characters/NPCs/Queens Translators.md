## 🗣 Translator 1 — _Rylin Embercrest_

**Race/Class:** Bronze Dragonborn Scholar (Expert Archetype)  
**Alignment:** Lawful Good  
**Languages:** Common, Draconic, Dwarvish, Elvish, Infernal, Celestial.

### Description

Tall bronze scales, scholarly robes lined with translation runes. Carries a wax tablet and stylus that shimmer faintly when he listens. Rylin speaks in precise, musical cadence; often finishes others’ sentences politely.

### Personality

Loves linguistic puzzles and believes diplomacy is the highest art. Loyal to the Queen, but secretly fascinated by Kluimont texts and forbidden scripts.

### Quick Stats (non-combat)

- **AC 12**, **HP 28**, **Speed 30 ft**, **INT 16**, **WIS 12**, **CHA 14**.
    
- **Skills:** Arcana +5, History +5, Insight +3, Persuasion +4, Linguistics +8 (custom).
    
- **Ability:** _Tongue of Fire_ (1/day) — expels a brief draconic flame to draw attention or light seals (1d6 fire, 5-ft cone).

## 🗣 Translator 2 — _Maera of Silverhill_

**Race/Class:** Human Savant (Expert Linguist)  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good  
**Languages:** Common, Orcish, Gnomish, Halfling, Sylvan, Primordial.

### Description

Short woman in layered scholar’s garb with dozens of feathered quills tucked into her sash. Wears half-moon spectacles and keeps a charm for each language she’s mastered.

### Personality

Brisk, witty, always correcting idioms. Teases Rylin for over-formal translations. Maintains meticulous transcripts of every royal meeting.

### Quick Stats (non-combat)

- **AC 11**, **HP 24**, **Speed 30 ft**, **INT 17**, **WIS 13**, **CHA 12**.
    
- **Skills:** History +6, Insight +4, Performance +3, Investigation +6.
    
- **Ability:** _Polyglot’s Recall_ — may comprehend or translate any heard language for 1 minute (1/short rest).
