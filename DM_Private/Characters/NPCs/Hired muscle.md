#### **Multiattack.**

The hired muscle makes **two melee attacks** with their cudgel or shortsword.

#### **Cudgel (Melee Weapon Attack):**

+4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
**Hit:** 6 (1d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage.

#### **Shortsword (Melee Weapon Attack):**

+3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
**Hit:** 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

#### **Crossbow, Light (Ranged Weapon Attack):**

+3 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target.  
**Hit:** 5 (1d8 + 1) piercing damage.  
_(Each carries 10 bolts.)_

### **Tactics**

- **Round 1:** Two attack with melee weapons to block the road; two take cover behind the wagons and fire crossbows.
    
- **Round 2+:** They fight defensively, focusing on weaker-looking foes.
    
- If **two fall**, the rest may **flee** or **surrender** (Intimidation DC 12 can end the fight early).

# 👉 **3 bludgeoning damage**

Here’s why:

- Base unarmed damage = **1**
    
- STR modifier = **+2**
    

**1 + 2 = 3 damage**

So every successful punch/kick/headbutt they land deals:

🎲 **3 bludgeoning damage (no roll, flat damage)**
