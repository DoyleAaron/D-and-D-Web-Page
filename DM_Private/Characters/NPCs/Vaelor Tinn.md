# Vaelor Tinn – DM Reference & Full Statistics

## Character Overview

**Vaelor Tinn** is the Kluimont delegate to the Kadena-Rye Summit. He is the 8th Seat of the Arcane Synod and a specialist in abjuration and defensive ward architecture.

### Key Story Points (DM Only)
- He has **never been permitted to leave Kluimont** until this assignment. This trip is genuine wonder for him.
- He admires adventurers because they go places he was never allowed to go.
- If the party treats him with respect, he may quietly become a back-channel ally inside Kluimont.
- **Not evil, not naive.** He serves Kluimont loyally but is not ideologically rigid. He will not actively undermine the Synod, but he also has a conscience.
- He is aware of the Trinity items only in the abstract—Synod classified—but he is smart enough to read a room.

### Background Summary
- Born in Kluimont City to a ward-keeper mother
- Earned his Council seat in his early thirties — unusually fast
- Spent years requesting permission to travel; denied every time
- Named delegate to the Kadena-Rye Summit in 1225 C.C. — first time off the island as an adult
- Specialty: barrier networks, counterspell architecture, anti-assassination wards

### Signature Class Features

**Arcane Ward (Abjurer):** Ward HP 37 when fully charged; absorbs damage before Vaelor takes any.

**Projected Ward (Abjurer 6):** As a reaction, redirect the Arcane Ward's damage absorption to an ally within 30 ft for one hit.

**Spell Resistance (Abjurer 14):** Advantage on saving throws vs. spells, and resistance to spell damage.

**Shield Savant (Vaelor variant):** When he casts `Shield`, it grants +7 AC instead of +5.

**Layered Counter:** When `Counterspell` succeeds, Arcane Ward regains 6 HP.

### Homebrew Spells

**Tinn's Interlocking Aegis** *(5th-level abjuration, concentration up to 1 minute)*
- Create up to 3 linked 10-ft barrier panels at points within 60 ft.
- Each panel has AC 17 and 30 HP. Creatures can move around them but not through them.
- As a bonus action each turn, rotate or slide one panel up to 15 ft.

**Laughing Rebuff** *(3rd-level abjuration, reaction)*
- Trigger: a creature you can see targets you with an attack roll.
- Effect: impose disadvantage on that roll. On a miss, the attacker takes 2d8 force damage.
- Vaelor typically makes a light remark as part of the casting.

**Harbor Dome Protocol** *(7th-level abjuration, concentration up to 10 minutes)*
- 20-ft radius hemisphere centered on a point on the ground or deck.
- Allies inside gain half cover and advantage on saves against spells cast from outside.
- The dome has 60 HP. If destroyed, all allies inside gain 8 temporary HP.

**Silent Corridor** *(4th-level abjuration)*
- 40-ft long, 10-ft wide lane of suppressed hostile magic.
- Hostile spell attacks made through the lane are made at disadvantage.
- Allied movement through the lane does not provoke opportunity attacks.

### Combat Pattern

- **Round 1:** Open with `Tinn's Interlocking Aegis` or `Wall of Force` to establish battlefield geometry.
- **Round 2:** Hold `Counterspell` posture; reposition allies behind cover.
- **Round 3+:** `Globe of Invulnerability` or `Harbor Dome Protocol` if mass threat appears.
- **Retreat Logic:** Civilians first, then diplomatic assets, then himself. He won't leave someone behind unless the mission forces it.

---

### Non-Combat Utility

- Excellent for escort missions, negotiation stability, crowd control.
- Can seal off bridge approaches, corridors, or open decks without escalating to lethal force.
- Narrative foil to Chris Meudec: same institution, completely different character.

---

### Roleplay Notes

- Speaks casually and directly. No speeches.
- If insulted or mocked, gives a mild laugh and moves on. Does not retaliate.
- Will ask questions about places the party has been—genuine curiosity, not intelligence gathering.
- If asked about Kluimont, he answers honestly within limits, without leaking anything classified.
- He is not a coward, but he is not a glory-seeker either. He does what needs doing and checks on the people nearby.

---

### DM Secret Hook (Do Not Share)

Vaelor was not the first choice for this summit assignment. He was selected after the original delegate (5th Seat) declined for undisclosed reasons. He does not know this, and it would bother him if he did.

More importantly: he has genuinely never been outside Kluimont's immediate waters before. If the party notices his quiet fascination with things as ordinary as open road travel or market smells, and says nothing about it — just lets him have the moment — he will remember. That memory may matter later if the party ever needs access inside Kluimont.
