# Alpha wolf
### **Wolf Alpha**

_Medium beast, unaligned_

**AC** 14 (natural armour) • **HP** 45 (6d8 + 18) • **Speed** 40 ft.  
**STR** 16 (+3) **DEX** 15 (+2) **CON** 16 (+3) **INT** 4 (−3) **WIS** 14 (+2) **CHA** 8 (−1)

**Saving Throws** Str +5, Dex +4  
**Skills** Perception +4, Stealth +6  
**Senses** passive Perception 14  
**Languages** — (understands simple commands from allied druids or rangers)  
**Challenge** 1 (200 XP) **Proficiency Bonus** +2

---

### **Traits**

**Keen Hearing and Smell.**  
The wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

**Pack Tactics.**  
The wolf has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the wolf’s allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and isn’t incapacitated.

**Alpha Howl (Recharge 5–6).**  
As a bonus action, the Alpha lets out a commanding howl.  
All allied beasts or wolves within 60 ft. that can hear it gain advantage on their next attack roll before the end of their next turn.  
Enemies within 30 ft. must succeed on a **DC 12 Wisdom saving throw** or become **frightened** until the start of the Alpha’s next turn.

---

### **Actions**

**Bite.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ **10 (2d6 + 3)** piercing damage.  
Target must succeed on a **DC 13 Strength saving throw** or be **knocked prone**.

**Rend (Recharge 4–6).**  
The Alpha tears into a prone or grappled creature.  
_Hit:_ **15 (3d6 + 5)** piercing damage.

---

### **Optional Flavour Additions**

- Grey-white scars, thicker mane, glowing yellow eyes.
    
- Commands nearby wolves telepathically via low growls and posture.
    
- When reduced below half HP, the Alpha automatically uses **Alpha Howl** once (if available).
