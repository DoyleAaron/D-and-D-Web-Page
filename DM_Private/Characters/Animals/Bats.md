## 🦇 Swarm of Bats

- **Medium swarm of Tiny beasts, unaligned**
    
- **AC:** 12
    
- **HP:** 22 (5d8)
    
- **Speed:** 0 ft., fly 30 ft.
    
- **STR 5, DEX 15, CON 10, INT 2, WIS 12, CHA 4**
    
- **Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened, Grappled, Paralyzed, Petrified, Prone, Restrained, Stunned
    
- **Senses:** Blindsight 60 ft. (can’t use if deafened), Passive Perception 11
    
- **Challenge:** 1/4 (50 XP)
    

**Traits**

- **Echolocation:** Blinded while deafened.
    
- **Keen Hearing:** Advantage on Perception checks that rely on hearing.
    
- **Swarm:** Can occupy another creature’s space; can’t regain HP or gain temp HP.
    

**Actions**

- **Bites (Swarm Attack).** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 0 ft., one creature in the swarm’s space.
    
    - Hit: **2d4 piercing damage**, or **1d4 if the swarm has half HP or fewer**.
