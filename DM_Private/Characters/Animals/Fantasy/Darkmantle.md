

**AC** 11 • **HP** 22 (5d6+5) • **Speed** 10 ft, **fly** 30 ft

- **Crush.** _Melee_ +5, reach 5 ft, **1d6+3** bludgeoning, and the target is **grappled (escape DC 13)**. Until the grapple ends, the target is **restrained** and **blinded**, and the darkmantle can’t Crush another target.
