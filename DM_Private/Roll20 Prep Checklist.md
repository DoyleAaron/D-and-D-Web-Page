# Roll20 Prep Checklist – All Missions

## Overview

This document contains all the maps and tokens you need to create for the Wayfarer Accord missions and special encounters. Each section includes:
- **AI Map Prompts** (for generating top-down battle maps)
- **Token List** (all NPCs/enemies with stats)
- **Notes** for setup

## Captain Kirk's Daughter (Rimduff Isles)

### Maps Needed: 3

#### Map 1: The Crimson Tide (Pirate Ship Deck)
**AI Prompt:**
> Top-down fantasy battle map, D&D style, no grid. Pirate ship deck from above (caravel-class). Main deck with masts, rigging, cargo hatches. Captain's cabin at stern. Forecastle with ballistae. Rope ladders, barrels, crates for cover. Crimson red sails visible. 60x25 feet deck area. Ocean water visible around ship. Dramatic sunset lighting.

#### Map 2: Iona's Cove (Beach Approach)
**AI Prompt:**
> Top-down fantasy battle map, D&D style, no grid. Hidden pirate cove beach. Sandy beach with rocky outcrops. Makeshift wooden fortifications (palisade walls, watchtower). Several rowboats on beach. Path leading up to cave entrance. Ship anchored in cove waters. Tropical island vegetation. 70x50 feet. Daytime coastal lighting.

#### Map 3: Prison Cave
**AI Prompt:**
> Top-down fantasy battle map, D&D style, no grid. Natural sea cave converted to prison. 40x30 feet interior. Iron cage cells built into rock walls (3 cells). Guard post with table and chairs. Torches on walls. Crates of supplies. Single entrance from cove. Dripping water, barnacles on lower walls. Dim, oppressive atmosphere.

### Tokens Needed:

| Token | Stat Block | HP | AC | Notes |
|-------|------------|----|----|-------|
| Captain Iona | Bandit Captain (enhanced) | 85 | 17 | +2 stats, +1 cutlass with cold damage |
| First Mate Craw | Hobgoblin Captain | 39 | 17 | Hobgoblin, disciplined |
| Pirate (x20) | Bandit | 11 | 12 | Crew, not all fight at once |
| Marina | Commoner | 4 | 10 | Kirk's daughter, prisoner |

**Captain Iona Custom Stats:**
- Use Bandit Captain with: +2 to all ability scores
- HP 85, AC 17 (studded leather + shield)
- Frostbite Cutlass: +7 to hit, 1d8+4 slashing + 1d6 cold damage
- Multiattack: 3 attacks
