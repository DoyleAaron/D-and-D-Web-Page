# **📘 Session 12 Summary – “Where the Dead Linger”**

## **1. Adrian’s Notes**

- **Amdir discovers written notes** exchanged between **Adrian** and **Liora**.
    
- The notes reference:
    
    - **One of Balthazar’s gifted items**,
        
    - Its historical movement,
        
    - And its possible connection to Dawerton.
        
- This confirms that:
    
    - Dawerton’s leadership knows more about Balthazar than they admit.
        
    - Information is being deliberately compartmentalised.
        

---

## **2. Reinforcing the Group**

- The party recruits **Corporal Bram Stone** to assist with the investigation.
    
- Bram provides:
    
    - Local scouting,
        
    - Street-level credibility,
        
    - Additional protection given the escalating threat.
        

---

## **3. Testing the Nightmares**

- The party devises a test:
    
    - **Two members deliberately sleep** to see if they experience the shared nightmares.
        
- Result:
    
    - **No nightmares occur**.
        
- Conclusion:
    
    - The phenomenon is **selective**, not citywide.
        
    - Targets may be chosen based on proximity, bloodlines, guilt, or exposure.
        

---

## **4. The Missing Priest**

- Through questioning a **terrified vendor**, the party learns of:
    
    - A **priest who recently vanished**,
        
    - Last seen acting strangely before disappearing.
        
- The priest’s home is identified as a possible focal point.
    

---

## **5. The Haunted House**

- The party locates the priest’s residence.
    
- Inside, they are attacked by **multiple hostile spirits**.
    
- The fight is brutal:
    
    - Several party members nearly die.
        
    - Resources are heavily depleted.
        
- Despite this, the party **survives and prevails**, cleansing the house.
