## **VARIN — Chosen of Bahamut**

### What Varin Knows

- Varin’s remaining arm has been **blessed by Bahamut**.
    
- The arm can **heal even the most severe wounds**, including:
    
    - Mortal injuries
        
    - Curses
        
    - Lost limbs
        
- The arm holds **10 divine charges**.
    

### Hidden Mechanics / Arc

- Each use makes the arm **more draconic**:
    
    - Scales harden
        
    - Heat radiates
        
    - Subtle changes to posture and presence
        
- As charges are spent:
    
    - NPCs may react with awe or fear
        
    - Dragons, dragonborn, and ancient beings may _sense_ him
        
- If all charges are used:
    
    - The arm becomes fully draconic
        
    - Varin’s role as a **living symbol of Bahamut** becomes unavoidable
        

### Narrative Pressure

- Bahamut did not bless Varin lightly.
    
- Varin will eventually face a choice:
    
    - Be a **healer and shield**, or
        
    - Become a **weapon of divine judgement**
        
- Chaos-aligned entities (including the Chaos cleric) will see Varin as a **threat**.

## **MORJOR — Balthazar’s Bearer**

### What Morjor Knows

- **Balthazar personally recruited Morjor**.
    
- He was given a **magical ring** once belonging to the **Queen of Islefield**.
    
- His task:
    
    - Absorb the **magical components of the Trinity of Balthazar** into the ring.
        
    - Deliver it to the **Queen of Islefield**.
        
    - Serve as her **advisor**, replacing her corrupt current one.
        

### Hidden Truths

- The ring is not just a container:
    
    - It is a **failsafe**
        
    - It prevents the Trinity from being united by anyone else
        
- Morjor is meant to be:
    
    - A **political stabiliser**
        
    - A living key
        
    - A final safeguard against Chaos
        

### Dawerton Connection

- The **gifted item currently influencing Dawerton** is **Morjor’s Trinity component**.
    
- This makes Morjor:
    
    - A target for vampires
        
    - A target for Marelle
        
    - A liability in Dawerton if discovered
        

### Narrative Pressure

- Morjor’s loyalty will be tested:
    
    - Islefield’s Queen
        
    - The party
        
    - Balthazar’s long-dead will
        
- If Morjor dies before delivering the ring:
    
    - The ring may shatter
        
    - Or fall into Chaos-aligned hands

## **AMDIR — The Willing Pawn**

### What Amdir Knows

- Amdir is **actively helping Marelle**.
    
- He believes:
    
    - Marelle’s goal is to infiltrate **Kluimont**
        
    - Helping her will secure **entry, status, or power** there
        
- He wants into Kluimont badly enough to **ignore red flags**.
    

### What Amdir Has Learned in Dawerton

- **Bram is also working for Marelle**.
    
- The **Queen of Braewood suspects the Clerk of treason** and has begun an investigation.
    
- A **gifted item of Balthazar** is currently in Dawerton.
    
- Marelle wants:
    
    - The **Trinity items recovered**
        
    - Then quietly handed to **pureblood vampires in Lavalto**
        

### What Amdir Does _Not_ Know

- Marelle’s **true motive**:
    
    - She wants to be **turned into a vampire**
        
    - She does **not care** about Chaos, kingdoms, or fallout
        
- The vampires intend to:
    
    - Use the Trinity to revive the **God of Chaos**
        
- Amdir is expendable once the items are secured.
    

### Narrative Pressure

- Amdir is on a **collision course**:
    
    - Loyalty to party vs ambition
        
    - Truth vs denial
        
- Marelle will:
    
    - Reward him just enough to keep him useful
        
    - Sacrifice him without hesitation if needed

- Bram is secretly **working for Marelle**.
    
- He believes:

### Narrative Use

- Bram is a future:

    - Potential redemption
        
    - Or scapegoat when Marelle needs distance
