# **📘 Session 7 Summary – “Paper, Steel, and a New Shore”**

## **1. Back in Braewood – Shopping & Tension**

- The party returns to **Braewood** and spends time shopping and resupplying.
    
- **Amdir** gets into a heated argument with a vendor, causing tension within the group and briefly turning the others against him.
    
- **Morjor** is verbally harassed by a shady local during the outing, highlighting the city’s undercurrent of hostility despite its royal polish.
    
- The shopping trip reinforces internal party strain and the less-friendly side of Braewood’s streets.
    

---

## **2. Clerk Hand-off & Rewards**

- The party meets with the **clerk** to receive their promised compensation and paperwork.
    
- Each member:
    
    - Redeems **Quartermaster chits**,
        
    - Selects gear upgrades, utility packs, consumables, and **one Minor Curio** from the Crown Tray.
        
- Subtle irregularities in the records hint at **clerical manipulation**, though nothing is yet openly exposed.
    

---

## **3. Audience with the Queen**

- The party is granted a short audience with the **Queen of Braewood**.
    
- She formally acknowledges their success and shifts focus away from Kluimont.
    
- She presents a **new proposal**:
    

### **The Lavalto Assignment**

- **Objective:** Escort and protect a diplomatic group to **Lavalto**, while quietly gathering information.
    
- **Concerns:**
    
    - Nighttime port activity,
        
    - Volcanic reagents,
        
    - “Nocturnal shipments” of unclear purpose.
        
- The Queen stresses:
    
    - Dawerton’s instability,
        
    - The need to secure trade and diplomacy before tensions escalate.
        

---

## **4. Escort Detail Assigned**

The party will guard:

- **Envoy Pasan**
    
- **Translator Maera of Silverhill**
    
- **Four Braewood Guards**
    

This mission is framed as **relationship-building**, not enforcement.

---

## **5. Rewards for the Mission**

- **Base Pay:** 100 gp per character
    
- **Expenses:** 25 gp per character
    
- **Total:** **125 gp per character**
    
- Additional benefits:
    
    - Quartermaster support,
        
    - Minor royal favors,
        
    - Conditional bonuses for uncovering valuable intelligence in Lavalto.
        

---

## **6. Decision**

- After discussion, the party **accepts the Queen’s offer**.
    
- Session ends with preparations underway for travel to **Lavalto**, unknowingly stepping closer to the vampire-controlled heart of the larger plot.
