# **📘 Session 10 Summary – “Courtesies and Cracks”**

## **1. The Dawerton Council**

- The party attends a **formal diplomatic meeting** at Dawerton’s council chamber.
    
- Introductions are polite and proceedings are smooth:
    
    - Trade, ports, and shipping routes discussed openly.
        
    - No overt hostility or accusations.
        
- **Liora**, a prominent council figure, quietly studies the party’s presence and extra muscle.
    
- The meeting ends positively, with **no conflict**.
    
- Liora extends an invitation for a **private dinner**, framed as a gesture of openness.
    

---

## **2. Library & the Mayor**

- Before dinner, the party visits the **Dawerton library** to research:
    
    - Local history,
        
    - Volcanic trade,
        
    - Ancient magic and artefacts.
        
- They encounter **Adrian**, the **vampiric mayor of Dawerton**.
    
- The exchange is cordial and civil:
    
    - Polite conversation,
        
    - Mutual respect maintained.
        
- However, the party senses that **Adrian is concealing something** beneath his calm demeanor.
    

---

## **3. Dinner with Liora**

- The party is formally escorted to **Liora’s residence** for dinner.
    
- The evening is elegant and controlled:
    
    - Liora asks careful questions about the party’s travels and role.
        
    - She subtly probes their intentions without accusation.
        
    - Mentions of old families, missing shipments, and volcanic interests surface indirectly.
        
- By the end of the dinner:
    
    - Liora proposes a **potential job**, hinting at deeper involvement.
        
    - The party chooses not to answer immediately, requesting time to discuss privately.
        
- Liora accepts this without protest.
