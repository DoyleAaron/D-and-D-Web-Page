# **📘 Session 5 Summary – “The Four Fountains”**

## **1. Audience With Balthazar**

- The four PCs stand before the Quad Fountain.
    
- Each asks **one personal question**; several confirm the **Book is indeed present**.
    
- They enter their individual sacrificial chambers.
    

---

## **2. The Sacrifices**

### **Morjor**

- Asks again where the book is.
    
- **Offers nothing.**
    
- His “sacrifice” receives no weight.
    

### **Amdir**

- Sacrifices his **pendant** – moderate personal value.
    

### **Varin**

- Sacrifices his **entire arm** (highest cost offering of the session).
    

### **Tearius**

- Sacrifices his beloved **bike**, formerly tied to Nehinyathu and now to Smitler.
    
- Strong sentimental weight.
    

Collectively the offerings rise high enough to unlock the highest-tier reward.

---

## **3. The Rewards**

- The fountains deliver:
    
    - **The Chart of the Threefold Way** (Balthazar’s Book).
        
    - **Compass of Safe Passage** (Compass of Lines Unseen).
        
- Overflow from the tier climb generates **one additional reward** beneath the apex tier.
    

---

## **4. Teleportation & Return**

- After receiving the book, the vault’s exit rune triggers.
    
- The entire group is **teleported back to Sheyton**, bypassing the return journey.
    
- **Session 5 ends** as they reappear in the town where everything began.
