# Upper Floor

## Purpose Of This Floor

This is where the vampires are. The party has not cleared this floor yet.

The brood knows the lava golem is dead. They know the party went downstairs. They are preparing for the party to come back up.

---

## Current Defenders

- **Isolde Valdris** at full health
- **Theron Valdris** at full health
- **Senna Valdris** at full health
- **3–4 Human Servants** as interference and alarms
- **1 House Golem** anchoring the floor

---

## Suggested Positioning

### Isolde

- Holds a central room or hall intersection
- Lets the others commit first unless a weak target appears
- Uses charm pressure, grapples, and finishing bites once the party is split

### Senna

- Stays near Isolde
- Acts as the bodyguard and interruption piece
- Punishes melee PCs who tunnel on Isolde

### Theron

- Uses darkness, flanking, and vertical routes
- Goes after whoever looks isolated, wounded, or caster-heavy
- The party cast a light spell last session — Theron will target the caster or light source first
- Withdraw rather than stand still in the middle of the group

### House Golem

- Prevents an easy rush into the master rooms
- Pins frontliners while the vampires work angles

### Servants

- Close doors
- Trigger alarms
- Stand in the way of clean lines, dashes, and retreat lanes

---

## Mood Notes

The upper floor should feel desperate and furious.

- The brood knows their basement defense is gone
- They are cornered, not cautious
- Isolde should speak like the party has already taken too much from her

---

## Suggested Isolde Lines

- "You killed my guardian. That was the last mercy I had left."
- "The priest will come for what's his. You've only borrowed time."
- "You think freeing the traitor changes anything? Maltrius doesn't forget."
