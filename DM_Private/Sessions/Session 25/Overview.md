# Session 25 — Blackthorn House Continued / Spehlina Setup

## What Happened Last Session (Session 24)

- The party returned to Blackthorn House for a second assault.
- They cast a light spell.
- They pushed downstairs and killed the **Lava Golem**.
- **No vampires were killed** — all three (Isolde, Senna, Theron) remain alive upstairs.
- Liora may or may not have been freed from her chains (confirm at start of session).

---

## Session 25 Start State

The party is inside Blackthorn House. The basement is cleared. The upper floor still holds three vampires, remaining golems, and servants.

### Already Dead

- **Human servants killed:** 3 of 9
- **House golems destroyed:** 3 of 5 (2 wrought-iron from Session 23, 1 lava golem from Session 24)
- **Lava Golem:** Destroyed

### Still Alive

- **Human servants remaining:** 6
- **Wrought-Iron Golems remaining:** 2
- **Vampires remaining:** 3
  - **Isolde** (full HP)
  - **Senna** (full HP)
  - **Theron** (full HP)

---

## Session Objective

1. Free Liora if not already done.
2. Clear the upper floor — kill or neutralize the three vampires.
3. Loot the house.
4. Return to Adrian for reward.
5. Possible pivot toward the **Spehlina** threat.

---

## Tone

The house fight should feel like a crescendo. The party has momentum from killing the golem. The vampires are cornered, furious, and fighting for survival — not ambush tactics anymore.

After the house clears, the tone shifts. Dawerton owes the party, but the 28th is approaching and the Spehlina is still in play.

---

## Suggested Session Flow

1. Free Liora / secure the basement (if not done).
2. Push upstairs for the vampire fight.
3. Vampire kill-box on the upper floor — Isolde, Senna, Theron, golems, servants.
4. Loot the house room by room.
5. Return to Dawerton — Adrian reward scene.
6. Liora debrief — Maltrius intel, 28th warning.
7. **Spehlina hook** — either through Maltrius's orders (if found) or through Dawerton contacts reporting lake activity.

---

## If The Party Wins The House

- Liora survives and returns to Dawerton badly shaken but useful.
- Adrian delivers the reward (Scroll of Purity + Wand of Banishment).
- The party gains hard confirmation that Maltrius is escalating toward the 28th.
- The Spehlina becomes the next clear threat.

---

## Spehlina Transition

Once the house is cleared, the Spehlina should begin surfacing as a threat:

- Maltrius's orders (if found) describe using corpses and bait ships to guide the creature toward the Grand Span.
- Fishermen report worsening lake conditions — dead fish, oily water, strange sounds at night.
- Adrian or Liora can confirm the creature exists and that the 28th is the target date.
- The guild has this rated as an A-Tier threat for good reason.

The party needs to decide: hunt the Spehlina before the 28th, or prepare to defend the Grand Span.
