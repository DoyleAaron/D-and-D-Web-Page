# The Spehlina — Potential Next Encounter

*Gargantuan aberration (corrupted elemental), chaotic evil*

**AC:** 16 | **HP:** 312 (25d20 + 50) | **Speed:** 10 ft., swim 60 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 22 (+6) | 14 (+2) | 20 (+5) | 6 (-2) | 14 (+2) | 8 (-1) |

**Saves:** CON +10, WIS +7
**Resistances:** Fire, Poison; nonmagical B/P
**Immunities:** Necrotic
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened, Poisoned
**Senses:** Blindsight 120 ft., Darkvision 60 ft.
**CR:** 15

### Key Traits
- **Corruption Aura (30 ft, water only):** DC 17 CON or 4d6 necrotic + 1 exhaustion level. Success = immune 24 hrs.
- **Legendary Resistance (3/day)**
- **Death Throes:** On death, 60 ft radius DC 18 CON or 10d10 necrotic + poisoned 1 hr. Water within 1 mile corrupted.
- **Siege Monster:** Double damage to objects/structures.

### Actions
- **Multiattack:** 1 slam + 2 tendrils (can swap 1 tendril for Corrupted Spray)
- **Slam:** +11, reach 15 ft., 4d10+6 bludgeoning (double vs structures)
- **Tendril:** +11, reach 30 ft., 3d8+6 bludgeoning + 3d6 necrotic. Target grappled (DC 17 escape). Up to 6 grappled.
- **Corrupted Spray (Recharge 5-6):** 60 ft cone, DC 17 DEX, 8d8 necrotic. Fail = blinded until end of next turn.
- **Swallow:** +11, reach 5 ft, 1 grappled Large or smaller creature. Swallowed = blinded, restrained, 6d6 acid + 4d6 necrotic per turn. 40+ damage in one turn from inside → DC 20 CON or regurgitate.

### Legendary Actions (3/turn)
| Action | Cost | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| Tendril Strike | 1 | One tendril attack |
| Corrupting Presence | 2 | Grappled creatures take 4d6 necrotic |
| Submerge | 2 | Sink 60 ft, pull grappled creatures |
| Agonized Thrash | 3 | DC 17 STR or 4d10 bludgeoning + pushed 15 ft. Boats take 44 dmg. |

### Lair Actions (Initiative 20)
- **Corrupted Undercurrent:** 30 ft sphere, difficult terrain, 3d6 necrotic/turn
- **Call the Tainted:** Summon 1d4 corrupted fish swarms
- **Volcanic Vent:** 20 ft radius, DC 16 DEX or 5d8 fire

### The Dilemma
- **If killed:** All absorbed corruption releases. Lake becomes toxic within months. Shores suffer blight.
- **True fix:** Destroy the source (Maltrius / seal infernal vents), THEN purify or mercy-kill it.
- **The party likely doesn't know this yet.**

### Weaknesses
- Not immune to radiant — full damage
- DEX +2, so AoE DEX saves are reasonable
- INT 6, CHA -1 — vulnerable to those saves
- On land it has only 10 ft speed — luring it out of water cripples it
- Out of water, Corruption Aura doesn't trigger
