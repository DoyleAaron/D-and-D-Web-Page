# Liora — Session 25 Notes

## State When Found

- Alive
- Half HP or worse depending on how hard you want the scene to land
- Exhaustion level 1
- No spell slots
- Silver burns on wrists
- Furious first, relieved second

---

## How She Acts Immediately After Rescue

Liora is not sentimental in the moment.

- First concern: whether the house is actually clear
- Second concern: whether the party learned anything useful
- Third concern: getting out before Maltrius's people can respond

She does not break down in front of the party.

---

## What Changes In Her After This

This should harden Liora.

- She becomes less willing to work alone on field investigations
- She treats the Maltrius situation as fully open war, even if it still must stay politically hidden
- She now owes the party personally, not just professionally

---

## What She Shares After A Night To Recover

- The brood was preparing to rewrite her loyalty, not just torture her
- Maltrius is moving with more confidence than before
- The 28th is not another routine feeding night; it is a planned escalation
- Adrian is in more danger than he admits

---

## Longer-Term Relationship Shift

If rescued alive, Liora becomes:

- Sharper
- More direct
- Less tolerant of hesitation
- More personally protective of the party's access in Dawerton

---

## If You Want A Quiet Scene After

Give her one controlled moment where the damage shows.

Example:

> She thanks no one in public. Later, privately, she asks one practical question: "When you ran, did you think I was already dead?" She does not ask it to accuse. She asks it because she needs the answer.
