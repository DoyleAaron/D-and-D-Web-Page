*Medium undead, neutral evil*

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| AC | 15 |
| HP | 58 |
| Speed | 35 ft., climb 20 ft. |
| STR | 16 (+3) |
| DEX | 16 (+3) |
| CON | 14 (+2) |
| INT | 10 (+0) |
| WIS | 12 (+1) |
| CHA | 14 (+2) |

**Saves:** DEX +5, WIS +3
**Skills:** Perception +3, Stealth +5, Athletics +5
**Resistances:** Necrotic; bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Vulnerability:** Radiant
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13

## Traits

- **Regeneration 5**
- **Spider Climb**
- **Sunlight Hypersensitivity**

## Actions

- **Multiattack:** Two claw attacks
- **Claw:** +6 to hit, 2d4+3 slashing
- **Bite:** +6 to hit, grappled/incapacitated/restrained target only, 1d6+3 piercing + 2d6 necrotic

## Reaction

- **Guardian Lunge:** When Isolde is hit by a melee attack and Senna is within 10 ft., Senna swaps places with Isolde and becomes the target instead

## Session 25 Tactics

- Stay within protection range of Isolde as often as possible
- Intercept melee tunnel vision
- Force the party to spend extra actions getting clean access to Isolde
