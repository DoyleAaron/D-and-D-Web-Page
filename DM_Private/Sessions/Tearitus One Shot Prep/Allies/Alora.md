## Personality

- Quieter than Rami, but sharper
- Found faith after her parents died — clings to the Light domain as armour against the dark
- The wooden sun pendant was her mother's; she clutches it when frightened
- Pragmatic healer — patches people up while scolding them for getting hit

---

## Death Scene

Heals Rami once, then channels Radiance of the Dawn — it burns two vampires. Lady Seraphina grabs her mid-channel and drains her. The light fades from her pendant.
