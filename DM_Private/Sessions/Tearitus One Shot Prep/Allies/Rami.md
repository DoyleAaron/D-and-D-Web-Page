## Rogue Features

**Subclass:** Swashbuckler

- Sneak Attack: 3d6
- Cunning Action
- Uncanny Dodge
- **Rakish Audacity:** Add CHA to initiative. Sneak Attack on 1v1 without needing advantage
- **Fancy Footwork:** No opportunity attacks from creatures you attacked in melee this turn

---

## Warlock Features

**Patron:** The Undying *(reflavored as an ancestral vengeance pact — his rage at his parents' killers "answered back")*

- Pact Magic: 2 × 3rd-level slots (recover on short rest)
- **Pact of the Blade:** Summons a shadow-wreathed rapier
- Eldritch Invocations:
  - **Thirsting Blade** — Extra Attack with pact weapon
  - **Agonizing Blast** — Add CHA mod to Eldritch Blast damage
  - **Devil's Sight** — Darkvision 120 ft, see through magical darkness

**Spells Known (6):**
- Cantrips: Eldritch Blast, Minor Illusion
- 1st–3rd: Hex, Darkness, Misty Step, Vampiric Touch, Counterspell, Spirit Shroud

## Personality

- Quiet, intense, protective of Alora
- Carries deep rage behind a calm exterior
- His warlock pact manifests as dark shadowy energy — he doesn't fully understand it, only that his hatred of vampires "answered back"
- Distrusts everyone until they've proven themselves under pressure
