## Gas Gadgets (Unique)

Smitler specialises in improvised gas-based devices. He crafts these during short/long rests using alchemist's supplies (proficient). He can carry **3 prepared gadgets** at a time.

| Gadget | Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Choking Bomb** | Action, throw 20 ft | 10-ft radius cloud. Creatures starting turn in cloud: DC 14 CON save or **poisoned** and take **2d6 poison damage**. Half on save. Cloud lasts 1 minute or until dispersed by wind |
| **Smoke Screen** | Bonus Action (Fast Hands), throw 20 ft | 15-ft radius **heavily obscured** area. Lasts 3 rounds |
| **Sleeping Gas Vial** | Action, throw 15 ft | 5-ft radius. Creatures: DC 14 CON save or fall **unconscious** for 1 minute. Wakes if damaged or shaken awake |
| **Stink Bomb** | Bonus Action (Fast Hands), throw 20 ft | 10-ft radius. Creatures: DC 13 CON save or **disadvantage on attack rolls** for 1 round. Beasts and creatures with keen smell auto-fail |
| **Tear Gas** | Action, throw 20 ft | 10-ft radius cloud. Creatures: DC 14 CON save or **blinded** for 1 round and take **1d6 poison damage**. Cloud lingers 2 rounds |

**Crafting:** During a short rest, Smitler can craft 1 gas gadget (alchemist's supplies + 5 gp materials). During a long rest, up to 3.
