# TEARITUS — PARTIAL VAMPIRE
### Player Reference Card (Give to Dylan)

---

## What Happened

Father Maltrius bit you in the lair beneath Lavalto Volcano. He was interrupted before the turning was complete. You are not fully a vampire — but you are not fully human anymore either.

A guard working for Lord Adrian and Liora let you escape. Before you left, you were given a ring.

---

## The Daylight Ring

- While wearing it: **you can walk in sunlight safely**
- Cost: **your maximum HP is reduced by 25%**
- If you take it off during the day: you take 20 radiant damage at the start of every turn while in sunlight

---

## Day vs Night

### During the Day (ring on)
- You're mostly human
- HP reduced by 25%
- No vampire powers
- No vampire weaknesses
- You look pale. Your eyes occasionally flash red when you're emotional

### At Night
- **Your full HP returns** (ring penalty goes away)
- Vampire powers switch on:
  - Regeneration: 5 HP at the start of your turn
  - Spider Climb
  - Darkvision 120 ft
  - Advantage on Perception checks
  - **Vampiric Bite** available (see below)
- **All vampire weaknesses apply at night:**
  - Running water: 10 acid damage per round
  - You cannot enter a home without an invitation
  - A stake through the heart paralyses you
  - Garlic: disadvantage on attacks and ability checks nearby
  - Holy symbols presented at you: WIS save DC 15 or frightened
  - Silvered weapons ignore your regeneration

---

## Vampiric Bite (Night Only)

Melee attack. +DEX to hit. Deals 1d6 + DEX piercing + 2d6 necrotic. You heal equal to the necrotic dealt.

**Every time you use it, you lose control a little more.**

---

## The Sanity Meter

The DM is tracking something privately. You won't know the exact number. You'll feel it instead — the itch in your gums, the way you clock a friend's pulse before their face, the moment blood stops smelling like copper and starts smelling like something else.

**The basic rule:**
- Feeding heals you and makes you stronger in combat
- Every bite pulls you closer to losing yourself
- If you hit zero, you're gone — fully vampire, no longer you

**What helps:**
- Long rests (without feeding)
- Healing magic
- Holy ground
- Resisting the urge when it hits

**What hurts:**
- Biting someone
- Feeding willingly
- Watching an ally get badly hurt

---

## The Choice

Stay weak and human — or get strong and lose yourself.

Every bite is a step toward the monster.
