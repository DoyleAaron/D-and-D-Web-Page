## Asmodeus Abilities

Maltrius draws power directly from his pact with Asmodeus — the Lord of the Nine Hells. These go beyond standard vampirism and reflect infernal contract magic.

---

### Devil's Bargain (2/day)
*"I can make the pain stop. All you have to do is... agree."*

As a reaction to taking damage, or as an action on his turn, Maltrius offers a creature within 30 ft a binding contract. The creature chooses to accept or refuse:

| Contract | Benefit to Creature | Cost |
|----------|--------------------|----|
| **Contract of Mercy** | The triggering damage is reduced to 0 | Cannot make attacks against Maltrius until the end of their next turn |
| **Contract of Clarity** | Remove one condition affecting them | Must use their next action to attack the nearest ally |
| **Contract of Survival** | Stabilize and regain 20 HP | Soul is **marked** — if they die before a long rest, they cannot be resurrected without a *Wish* spell |

Accepting requires the creature to say one word aloud: *"Agreed."* Maltrius always knows if a contract is broken.

---

### Hellfire Brand (Recharge 5–6)
*The mark of Asmodeus burns into flesh like a brand iron.*

One creature within 60 ft makes a DC 19 CON save. On a failure, they are **branded** for 1 minute.

**While branded:**
- Takes **2d6 fire damage** at the start of each of their turns
- Must succeed on a DC 15 CON save when casting a spell or using a class feature, or the ability is **suppressed** that turn
- Maltrius has **advantage on all attacks** against them
- The brand can be suppressed (not removed) for 1 round by dousing with holy water or a *Dispel Magic* (DC 19)

---

### Soul Tithe (Passive)
*Asmodeus is always collecting.*

When Maltrius reduces a creature to 0 HP with a Bite attack, their soul is **claimed**:
- *Revivify* automatically fails on them
- *Raise Dead* and *Resurrection* require the caster to succeed on a DC 20 CHA check, or they fail
- The claim is broken by *Greater Restoration* cast within 1 hour of death, or a *Wish*

---

### Asmodeus's Edict (1/day)
*He speaks with the Nine Hells behind every word.*

All creatures within 60 ft that can hear Maltrius make a DC 20 WIS save.
- **Failure:** The creature must obey the next single-word command Maltrius speaks on his next turn (*Kneel. Drop. Run. Fight.*), as per *Command* with no additional save
- **Failure by 5+:** Also **frightened** of Maltrius for 1 minute
- **Success:** Immune to this ability for 24 hours

---

### Chains of Perdition (Recharge 5–6)
*Iron links forged in Nessus tear up from the floor.*

Maltrius slams the ground. Chains erupt in a 20-ft radius around a point within 60 ft. Each creature in the area makes a DC 19 DEX save.
- **Failure:** **Restrained** and takes 3d8 piercing damage. Chains have AC 15 and 20 HP. A creature can break free with a DC 20 STR check (action)
- **Success:** Half damage, not restrained
- The chains count as **silvered**. They persist until the end of Maltrius's next turn or until destroyed

## Actions

- **Multiattack:** 2 attacks (Infernal Claw + Bite, or 2 × Infernal Claw)
- **Infernal Claw:** +9 to hit, 2d6+4 slashing + 2d6 fire damage
- **Bite:** +9 to hit, 1d6+4 piercing + 3d6 necrotic. Target's max HP reduced by necrotic dealt. Maltrius regains HP equal to necrotic dealt. Triggers **Soul Tithe** on a killing blow
- **Infernal Word — Kneel (Recharge 5–6):** 30-ft cone. DC 19 WIS save or **stunned** until end of their next turn. Fail by 5+: also **frightened** for 1 minute

---

## Legendary Actions (3/round)

- **Move:** Move up to half speed without provoking opportunity attacks
- **Cantrip (1 action):** Cast Toll the Dead
- **Brand Flare (1 action):** All currently branded creatures take 1d8 fire damage (no save)
- **Infernal Grasp (2 actions):** One creature within 30 ft: DC 19 STR save or pulled 15 ft toward Maltrius and **restrained** by shadowy chains until end of their next turn
- **Vampiric Bite (3 actions):** Make a Bite attack against a restrained or incapacitated creature. If the creature is already branded, this triggers Soul Tithe on a killing blow

---

## Lair Actions (on initiative count 20)

- **Volcanic Vents:** A 10-ft square of Maltrius's choice — DC 16 DEX save or 3d6 fire damage
- **Infernal Whispers:** One creature — DC 16 WIS save or frightened until initiative 20 next round. On a fail of 5+, Maltrius hears their deepest fear and narrates it aloud to the room
- **Shadows Deepen:** All light sources dim by one step (bright → dim → darkness) until initiative 20 next round
- **Asmodeus Watches:** Maltrius's eyes flash gold. All branded creatures take an automatic 1d6 fire damage. No save
