# The Six Purebloods

All purebloods present during the ambush in Maltrius's lair. All share standard vampire traits unless noted otherwise.

**Standard Vampire Traits (all purebloods):**
- Regeneration: 10 HP/start of turn (disabled by radiant damage, sunlight, or running water)
- Spider Climb
- Shapechanger (bat, mist, humanoid)
- Vampire Weaknesses (sunlight, running water, stakes, invitation, garlic, holy symbols)
