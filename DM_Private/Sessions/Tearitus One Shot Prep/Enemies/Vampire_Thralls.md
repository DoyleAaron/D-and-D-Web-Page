# Vampire Thralls — Level 6

Weaker spawn and guards throughout the lair. Not true purebloods — turned soldiers and servants.

---

## Vampire Thrall

**CR:** 3 (scaled to Level 6 encounter budget) | **Proficiency Bonus:** +3

| Stat | Score | Mod |
|------|-------|-----|
| STR | 16 | +3 |
| DEX | 14 | +2 |
| CON | 14 | +2 |
| INT | 10 | +0 |
| WIS | 10 | +0 |
| CHA | 10 | +0 |

**HP:** 52 (6d8 + 12) | **AC:** 14 (leather + natural) | **Speed:** 30 ft, climb 20 ft

**Saving Throws:** DEX +5, CON +5
**Skills:** Stealth +5, Perception +3
**Damage Resistances:** Necrotic; bludgeoning/piercing/slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft

---

## Vampire Traits

- **Regeneration:** 5 HP at start of turn (disabled in sunlight/running water)
- **Spider Climb**
- **Sunlight Hypersensitivity:** 10 radiant damage/round in sunlight; disadvantage on attacks and checks
- **Stake Through the Heart:** Destroys thrall if staked while incapacitated
- **Running Water:** 10 acid damage/round if fully immersed
- **No Invitation:** Cannot enter a residence without invitation

---

## Actions

- **Multiattack:** 1 Claw + 1 Bite
- **Claw:** +6 to hit, 1d6+3 slashing
- **Bite:** +6 to hit, 1d6+3 piercing + 2d6 necrotic. Target's max HP reduced by necrotic dealt. Thrall regains HP equal to necrotic dealt

---

## Tactics

Fight as a pack. One grapples, others bite. Not tactically brilliant but dangerous in numbers.

---

## Suggested Encounters

| Encounter | Composition |
|-----------|-------------|
| Tunnel patrol | 3 thralls |
| Guard room | 4 thralls + 1 thrall sergeant |

**Thrall Sergeant** (upgrade one thrall): +20 HP, Multiattack becomes 2 Claws + 1 Bite.
