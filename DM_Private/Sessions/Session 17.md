**In-Game Date:** TBD (continues from Session 15/16)

**Theme:** Naval ambush, personal vendetta, high-stakes rescue

## 📍 Session Overview

The party travels to **Adaham** to retrieve Captain Kirk's ship, **The Wavecutter**. Upon sailing toward the Rimduff Isles, they are ambushed by **Captain Iona's fleet** – five ships lying in wait. Iona reveals her true motivation: Kirk killed her father, **Admiral Varn Frostwind**, during his naval service. She has Marina tied to the mast of her flagship as leverage, demanding Kirk surrender himself in exchange for his daughter's life.

---

## 🎭 The Truth – Iona's Vendetta

### What Kirk Knows
- Admiral Varn Frostwind was a notorious pirate admiral
- Kirk killed him in battle 15 years ago during a naval engagement
- It was war – Kirk was following orders
- He didn't know Varn had a daughter
- Varn DID surrender, but then drew a hidden blade
- Kirk killed him in self-defense

### What Iona Knows
- Her father was executed by Kirk personally after surrender
- She watched from hiding as a young girl (age 12)
- She's spent 15 years building her fleet for this moment
- Marina isn't just ransom – she's *revenge*

### The Truth (DM Only)
- Varn DID surrender, but then drew a hidden blade
- Kirk killed him in self-defense
- Iona only saw the killing, not the betrayal
- This can be revealed if Kirk is pressed or chooses to explain

## 🎲 Ship Combat Rules

### Initiative
- Roll initiative for each ship (use captain's DEX modifier)
- The Wavecutter uses the highest party member's initiative

### Ship Actions (Per Round)

Each ship can take **ONE ship action** plus crew actions:

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Sail** | Double speed this round, -2 AC |
| **Hard Turn** | 90° turn, half speed |
| **Ram** | Move into enemy ship, both take 3d10 bludgeoning |
| **Boarding** | If adjacent, crew can board enemy ship |
| **Evasive** | +2 AC, half speed |

### Weapon Attacks

**Ballista** (requires 2 crew)
- +6 to hit, range 120/480 ft.
- 3d10 piercing damage to hull OR
- 3d10 piercing to a creature (if targeting crew)
- Reload: 1 action

**Fire Pot** (requires 1 crew)
- +4 to hit, range 60 ft.
- 2d6 fire damage + ship catches fire
- Fire deals 1d10 damage per round until extinguished (DC 12 crew check)

### Crew Actions
- Crew can make ranged attacks (use Bandit stats, +3 to hit, 1d8+1)
- Spellcasters can target ships or crew
- Kirk can dive overboard and attack from the water

### Ship Damage Effects

| Hull HP | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| 75% | Minor damage – no effect |
| 50% | Taking Water – Speed halved |
| 25% | Sinking – 10 rounds until underwater |
| 0 | Destroyed – Immediate sinking |

## 🎯 Decision Points

### Kirk Surrenders
If Kirk surrenders:
- Iona takes him prisoner
- Marina is released (thrown overboard, sailors rescue her)
- Party must decide: Leave Kirk or mount a rescue?
- **New mission:** Infiltrate Iona's cove to save Kirk

### Party Negotiates
Iona's terms:
- Kirk surrenders, everyone else goes free
- She will NOT accept gold
- She will NOT release Kirk
- Persuasion checks are at **disadvantage** – this is personal

### Party Fights
- Full naval battle
- Iona will kill Marina if she's clearly losing (DC 18 Perception to notice her moving toward Marina)
- Kirk may dive overboard to swim to the flagship directly

### Kirk Dies
If Kirk dies:
- Iona's vendetta is satisfied
- She may release Marina as a "gift" – the daughter to mourn as she mourned
- Or she keeps Marina as a trophy
- Party inherits Kirk's mission and ship
