## Major RP Scene: Royal Briefing in Braewood

## Queen's Core Agenda
The Queen wants speed, clarity, and controllable assets before the bridge summit.

She covers:
- Current summit framework at the Kadena-Rye bridge.
- Heightened concern over internal traitors.
- The report of a Balthazar-linked item found near Dawerton.
- Direct check on party progress and reliability.

## Queen's Questions to the Party
Use these in order, or pick as needed:

1. "What do you know right now that I do not?"
2. "How exposed are the roads and ports between Dawerton and the bridge route?"
3. "What is your read on the Dawerton item report - real lead, bait, or planted panic?"
4. "If I grant you resources now, what is your exact next move?"

## What She Reveals
- The bridge meeting is still on schedule, but security assumptions are changing daily.
- Suspected traitors exist inside military and courier channels.
- **Bram Stone has been outed as a traitor.**
- Bram is connected to Mirelle's network (same larger covert stream Amdir has interacted with).
- She does not need to reveal who exposed Bram unless forced by pointed questions.

DM note: if pressed hard, she can confirm she acted on "multi-source intelligence," without naming Mirelle.

Because of the party's proven field work, the Queen authorizes one magic item per character through her court wizard.

## Item Grants
- **Bag of Holding** (party utility; shared custody recommended)
  - Wondrous item, uncommon
  - Holds up to 500 lb, not exceeding 64 cubic feet
  - Retrieving an item from the bag takes an action
- **Cloak of Protection** (suggested recipient: Amdir)
  - Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)
  - While wearing it, gain +1 bonus to AC and saving throws
- **Boots of Speed** (suggested recipient: Morjor)
  - Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)
  - Bonus action to activate (click heels)
  - Doubles walking speed, and creatures have disadvantage on opportunity attacks against you
  - Lasts up to 10 minutes per dawn cycle (can be split in chunks of at least 1 minute)
- **Ring of Protection** (suggested recipient: Varin)
  - Ring, rare (requires attunement)
  - Gain +1 bonus to AC and saving throws while wearing it

DM note: if Boots of Speed feels too strong for current balance, swap Morjor to **Winged Boots** (rare, attunement; fly speed equal to walking speed up to 4 hours per dawn cycle).

## Post-Audience Prep (What to Have Ready)

You are now in a 30-day clock to summit. Prep by branch so you can pivot instantly based on player choice.

## Branch A: They Take Funding and Return to Dawerton (Liora + Scroll)
Likely player logic: secure the scroll first, then return with leverage.

### Time Risk (DM)
- Full round trip plus investigation can consume most or all of the month.
- If they spend too long in Dawerton, the summit may begin without them.

### Prep This Ahead
- 3-tier clue ladder for finding Liora fast:
  - Tier 1: rumor and dock-worker witness leads
  - Tier 2: one false lead and one compromised contact
  - Tier 3: Liora location, but extraction pressure
- A travel clock tracker (Day 1 to Day 30) with visible milestone beats:
  - Day 10: first summit lockdown notices
  - Day 20: bridge military hardening
  - Day 27: bridge sealed
  - Day 30: summit
- Fail-forward outcome if they arrive late: they still gain critical intel from scroll even if summit is already in motion.

## Branch B: They Pivot to Spehlina First
Likely player logic: stop catastrophe route before political meeting.

### Time Risk (DM)
- Solving Spehlina properly (not just killing) may take multiple operations.
- If they only wound or delay it, sabotage risk remains active.

### Prep This Ahead
- One recon scene (fisher survivors, dead water signs, route maps).
- One source-of-corruption lead scene (infernal vent, cult courier, tainted ritual site).
- Three outcomes to deploy quickly:
  - Success: sabotage risk reduced before summit
  - Partial: creature still active but less controllable
  - Failure/delay: bridge threat escalates

## Branch C: They Try to Do Both
Likely player logic: split priorities and race the clock.

### Prep This Ahead
- A hard choice moment around mid-month: continue Dawerton chase or turn back for summit security.
- Consequence matrix:
  - Choose Dawerton: stronger relic intel, weaker summit control
  - Choose Spehlina: stronger summit safety, weaker relic intel
  - Try both fully: exhaustion, attrition, and higher ambush risk
