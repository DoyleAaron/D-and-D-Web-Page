# Mayor Adrian — Reward and Fallout

## Adrian's Position After Success

Adrian is relieved, but not calm.

- Liora being taken proved Maltrius can reach deeper into Dawerton than he feared.
- A successful rescue does not relax him. It convinces him things are accelerating.
- He becomes more useful once he knows the party can actually finish ugly work.

---

## Reward He Gives The Party

### Immediate Payment

- **10,000 gp total** as promised

### Additional Support

- **4 vials of holy water** from concealed stores
- **One Dawerton seal of authority** for discreet civic cooperation before the 28th

Use the seal for one of the following later:

- access to restricted records
- quiet guard support
- fast stable, transport, or lodging arrangements

---

## How Adrian Delivers It

He does not present it like gratitude. He presents it like grim business settled correctly.

Example tone:

> "You did what I could not. The city remembers results better than sentiment. Take the gold. Take the seal. If we survive the month, perhaps then we can discuss thanks."

---

## What Adrian Wants Next

- A full account of what Liora learned
- Confirmation of how directly Maltrius is now acting
- A realistic answer from the party on whether they intend to strike early or prepare for the 28th

---

## If Liora Dies

Adrian changes immediately.

- He still pays the promised gold if the brood is destroyed
- He becomes colder and less trusting
- He stops speaking about "containing" Maltrius and starts speaking about surviving him
