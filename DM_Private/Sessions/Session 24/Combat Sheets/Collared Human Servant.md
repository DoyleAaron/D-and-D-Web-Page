# Collared Human Servant — Combat Sheet

*Medium humanoid, neutral (dominated)*

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| AC | 12 |
| HP | 11 |
| Speed | 30 ft. |

## Actions

- **Butcher Knife:** +3 to hit, 1d4+1 slashing
- **Collar Pulse (1/day):** One creature within 5 ft. makes DC 11 CON save or takes 1d6 lightning

## Special Rule

- **Break the Collar:** If a servant takes 10+ radiant damage in one hit, or someone disables the collar with DC 14 Arcana or Thieves' Tools, the servant falls unconscious and is freed from control

## Session 24 Tactics

- Use as blockers, alarms, and flank enablers
- They should not feel brave; they should feel compelled
- If freed, they collapse rather than join the fight
