# Isolde Valdris — Combat Sheet

*Medium undead, lawful evil*

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| AC | 16 |
| HP | 110 |
| Speed | 30 ft., climb 30 ft. |
| STR | 18 (+4) |
| DEX | 16 (+3) |
| CON | 18 (+4) |
| INT | 14 (+2) |
| WIS | 14 (+2) |
| CHA | 17 (+3) |

**Saves:** DEX +6, CON +7, WIS +5, CHA +6
**Skills:** Deception +6, Insight +5, Perception +5, Stealth +6
**Resistances:** Necrotic; bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Vulnerability:** Radiant
**Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 15

## Traits

- **Regeneration 10:** Regains 10 HP at start of turn if not hit by radiant or holy water since last turn
- **Spider Climb**
- **Sunlight Hypersensitivity**

## Actions

- **Multiattack:** Two attacks, usually Claw + Bite if target is controlled
- **Claw:** +7 to hit, 1d8+4 slashing; target grappled, escape DC 14
- **Bite:** +7 to hit, grappled/incapacitated/restrained target only, 1d6+4 piercing + 3d6 necrotic; Isolde heals necrotic dealt
- **Blood Command (Recharge 5-6):** One creature within 30 ft. that took necrotic damage this encounter makes DC 14 WIS save or is charmed until end of its next turn

## Session 24 Tactics

- Do not expose Isolde first
- Let servants and golems pin targets before she commits
- Focus bites on already isolated or restrained characters
- Use Senna as a bodyguard, not disposable support

## On Death

- Drops **Ring of Regeneration**
- Drops **Blood Matriarch Signet**
- Drops **Basement Key**
