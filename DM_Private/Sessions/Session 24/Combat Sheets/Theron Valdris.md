## Traits

- **Regeneration 5**
- **Spider Climb**
- **Sunlight Hypersensitivity**

## Actions

- **Multiattack:** Two claw attacks
- **Claw:** +6 to hit, 2d4+3 slashing
- **Bite:** +6 to hit, grappled/incapacitated/restrained target only, 1d6+3 piercing + 2d6 necrotic
- **Pounce Through Shadows (Recharge 5-6):** Teleport 20 ft. between dim-light spaces and make one claw attack

## Session 24 Tactics

- Hunt backliners and wounded PCs
- Attack from angles the party thought were safe
- Punish attempts to reopen or reuse the escape route
- Withdraw rather than stand still in the middle of the group

## On Death

- Drops **Cloak of Elvenkind**
- Drops **Dagger +1**
