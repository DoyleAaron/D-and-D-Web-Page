# Lava Golem — Combat Sheet

*Large construct, unaligned*

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| AC | 17 |
| HP | 133 |
| Speed | 25 ft. |
| STR | 20 (+5) |
| DEX | 8 (-1) |
| CON | 18 (+4) |
| INT | 3 (-4) |
| WIS | 10 (+0) |
| CHA | 1 (-5) |

**Saves:** CON +7, WIS +3
**Damage Immunities:** Fire, Poison, Psychic
**Damage Resistances:** Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Exhaustion, Frightened, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., Tremorsense 30 ft., passive Perception 10

## Traits

- **Heated Body:** A creature touching the golem or striking it in melee within 5 ft. takes 2d6 fire damage
- **Molten Core:** Below 66 HP, Heated Body becomes 3d6 fire and speed increases by 5 ft.
- **Immutable Form**
- **Magic Resistance**
- **Siege Monster**

## Actions

- **Multiattack:** Two slam attacks
- **Slam:** +8 to hit, 1d10+5 bludgeoning + 2d6 fire
- **Magma Spray (Recharge 5-6):** 15-ft. cone, DC 15 DEX, 8d6 fire or half on success; area becomes difficult terrain until the start of the golem's next turn

## Session 24 Tactics

- Never leaves the basement
- Prioritizes whoever is nearest Liora or the pillar
- Punishes melee recklessness hard
- Reward clever cold-based play if the party thinks tactically

## On Destruction

- Drops **Infernal Control Shard**
