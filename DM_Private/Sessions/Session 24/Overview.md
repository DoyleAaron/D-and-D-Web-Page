# Session 24 — Return to Blackthorn House

## In-Game Date

**Primary assumption:** March 9th–10th, 1225 (Thursday–Friday)

Use this as a direct continuation of the failed first assault. If the party returns the same night, keep the enemy placements but reduce time for rest. If they return the next day, keep the same placements and run Varin's curse at the next day threshold.

---

## What Happened Last Session

- The party went to Dawerton looking for **Liora**.
- They checked her house, then city hall, and met **Mayor Adrian**.
- Adrian pointed them toward **Blackthorn House**.
- The party entered the manor, tried to withdraw, and the fight escalated into a full retreat.
- **Varin** dropped into death saves during the escape.
- The party got out through a window. No party member died.
- They did **not** reach the basement.

---

## Session 24 Start State

Blackthorn House is now on alert.

- The broken escape window is now **boarded from the inside**.
- The brood has had time to **reset the house**, drag bodies, and redistribute defenders.
- All surviving major enemies return at **full HP**.
- The defenders are no longer reacting like surprised nobles. They are treating the manor like a siege site.
- Liora is still below, alive unless you decide the party delayed too long.

---

## Enemy Status Carry-Over

### Already Dead

- **Human servants killed:** 3 of 9
- **House golems destroyed:** 2 of 5 total golems

### Still Alive

- **Human servants remaining:** 6
- **Golems remaining:** 3
- **Vampires remaining:** 3
  - **Isolde**
  - **Senna**
  - **Theron**

### Current Breakdown for Play

Use the remaining 3 golems as:

- **2 Wrought-Iron House Golems** active in the upper manor defenses
- **1 Lava Golem** guarding the basement and Liora

That preserves the user's recap while keeping the basement guardian from Session 23 in play.

---

## Session Objective

The party is returning to Blackthorn House to finish what they started:

1. Breach the manor again.
2. Defeat the remaining brood and defenders.
3. Reach the basement.
4. Free Liora.
5. Escape with proof, survivors, or loot.

---

## Tone for This Session

This should feel different from the first assault.

- The house is no longer mysterious.
- The defenders are angry, disciplined, and ready.
- The party should feel that their escape bought them survival, but cost them the advantage.

Use the manor like a hunted-house stronghold, not a static dungeon.

---

## Opening Read Aloud

> "Blackthorn House waits in the dark exactly where you left it, but it no longer looks wounded. The shattered window you escaped through is boarded shut from within, fresh timber nailed across the frame. No lantern burns openly, yet faint movement passes behind curtained glass. The place feels awake now. Not lively. Aware. Like the whole house has been listening for your return."

---

## Core Threats This Session

### 1. A Prepared Defense

- Servants act as alarms, blockers, and sacrificial flankers.
- Golems hold choke points and protect stairs.
- The vampires do not rush alone. They strike once the party is pinned.

### 2. Liora's Clock

- Liora remains chained in the basement ritual chamber.
- The longer the party delays once inside, the more drained and furious she becomes.
- If you want pressure, add signs the binding rite is being prepared again.

### 3. Varin's Curse

- Run the next day of the curse depending on when they return.
- The infernal imagery in the basement should hit harder now that the party failed once already.

---

## Suggested Session Flow

1. Regroup and decide approach.
2. Re-entry at Blackthorn House.
3. Ground floor pressure and alarm response.
4. Upper floor vampire kill-box.
5. Basement breach.
6. Lava golem fight.
7. Free Liora.
8. Return to Dawerton for Adrian's payment and fallout.

---

## If The Party Wins

- Liora survives and returns to Dawerton badly shaken but still useful.
- Adrian releases the promised reward.
- The party gains hard confirmation that **Maltrius is escalating toward the 28th**.
- Blackthorn House becomes a major victory, but not the end of the vampire problem.

---

## If The Party Retreats Again

- The brood either evacuates Liora or accelerates the ritual.
- Adrian loses confidence in the party's reliability.
- Liora's survival becomes uncertain.
- Maltrius gains more time and confidence.

If they fail twice, treat Blackthorn House as an enemy operation, not just an encounter site.
