# Upper Floor

## Purpose Of This Floor

This is the real ambush floor.

The vampires know the party survived the first clash. They will not repeat the same mistake. Run this level as a coordinated counterattack built around chokepoints, dim light, and forced overextension.

---

## Current Defenders

- **Isolde Valdris** at full health
- **Theron Valdris** at full health
- **Senna Valdris** at full health
- **3 Human Servants** as interference and alarms
- **1 House Golem** anchoring the floor

---

## Suggested Positioning

### Isolde

- Holds a central room or hall intersection
- Lets the others commit first unless a weak target appears
- Uses charm pressure, grapples, and finishing bites once the party is split

### Senna

- Stays near Isolde
- Acts as the bodyguard and interruption piece
- Punishes melee PCs who tunnel on Isolde

### Theron

- Uses darkness, flanking, and vertical routes
- Goes after whoever looks isolated, wounded, or caster-heavy
- If the party uses the same escape route mentality again, he pressures their backline hard

### House Golem

- Prevents an easy rush into the master rooms or attic
- Pins frontliners while the vampires work angles

### Servants

- Close doors
- Trigger alarms
- Stand in the way of clean lines, dashes, and retreat lanes

---

## Mood Notes

The upper floor should feel personal.

- The brood knows the party came back.
- They are angry, embarrassed, and hungry.
- Isolde should speak like the party already missed their best chance.

---

## Suggested Isolde Lines

- "You should have kept running."
- "You were spared by panic once. Not twice."
- "If you came back for the traitor, then die knowing she heard you fail the first time."
