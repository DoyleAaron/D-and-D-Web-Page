# Ground Floor

## Current State

The ground floor is no longer the main kill zone. It is the pressure chamber.

- Signs of the first fight remain: gouged floorboards, smashed furniture, dried blood.
- The defenders have dragged corpses and debris to improve movement and lines of sight.
- The point is to slow the party, reveal their position, and push them toward the stairs under pressure.

---

## Current Defenders

- **2 Human Servants** in the entry side of the floor
- **1 House Golem** positioned to block the cleanest route upward
- The remaining defenders use sound and timing from upstairs, not immediate overcommitment

---

## Tactics

### Human Servants

- Do not fight bravely
- Use themselves to block doors, grapples, and movement lanes
- Try to keep one PC in place long enough for the golem to close

### House Golem

- Holds the central route or main staircase
- Uses slam attacks to knock people back from forward momentum
- Focuses on whichever character looks most likely to break formation

---

## The Hidden Basement Door

The basement door is still behind the heavy tapestry.

- The party can discover it the same way as before
- If they find it early, it remains **Arcane Locked**
- Reaching it under pressure should feel valuable but dangerous
