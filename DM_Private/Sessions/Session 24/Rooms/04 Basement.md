# Basement

## Entry Condition

The party did not reach the basement last session, so everything here remains live.

- Basement door is still **Arcane Locked**
- Liora is still imprisoned
- The **Lava Golem** is still active
- Ritual evidence remains in place

---

## The Door

- Key route: loot **Isolde's Basement Key**
- Magic route: *Knock* or *Dispel Magic*
- Force route: possible, but loud and costly

If the vampires are still alive upstairs, opening the basement before finishing them can trap the party between floors.

---

## Chamber State

Everything from Session 23 still applies, but it hits harder now because the party knows what it cost them to get here.

- Liora is chained to the pillar
- Her wrists are burned raw from the silver
- The ritual chamber still shows Maltrius's reach
- The lava golem exists only to stop rescue

---

## Liora's Current Condition

- Alive
- Exhausted
- Furious that the party had to retreat at all
- Still valuable once freed

If you want to sharpen the moment, have her greet them with anger before relief.

Example line:

> "You came back. Good. Kill it first. Then untie me."

---

## Lava Golem Role

The lava golem is the final hard gate of the house.

- It does not chase beyond the basement
- It protects the pillar area and punishes melee greed
- It is the last thing standing between the party and the truth they came for

---

## Key Information Liora Gives After Rescue

- The Valdris brood serves **Father Maltrius Kael** directly
- The ritual was meant to overwrite her loyalty
- Maltrius is preparing something larger for the **28th**
- This cell was only one forward arm of a wider move

See **Loot.md** for full item descriptions and rolls. Key items:

- **Mace of Disruption** — extra 2d6 radiant vs fiends/undead, destroys them at 25 HP or below (DC 15 Wis save)
- **+1 Shield** — etched with Infernal wards
- **Potion of Superior Healing** — restores 8d4 + 8 HP
- **Infernal Ritual Dagger** — nonmagical, used in blood rites, worth 50 gp
- **Asmodean Prayer Beads** — obsidian beads carved with Infernal contract clauses, worth 30 gp
- **Silver ritual bowl** — worth 75 gp, Infernal summoning script
- **Infernal Control Shard** from the lava golem after destruction — worth 200 gp to the right buyer
