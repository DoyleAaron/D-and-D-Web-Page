## Features

- **Disciple of Life:** Healing spells restore extra 2 + spell level HP
- **Blessed Healer:** When she heals another, she regains 2 + spell level HP
- **Channel Divinity (2/short rest):**
  - *Turn Undead:* DC 15 WIS or turned 1 minute
  - *Preserve Life:* Restore up to 30 HP split among creatures within 30 ft. (can't raise above half max)

## Roleplay

Calm, steady, deeply faithful. Navigates by the stars and moon — sees them as Selûne's guidance. Soft-spoken but unshakeable in battle. Will strongly favor saving the Spehlina. Comes with Harlan as part of the Ironwake crew.
