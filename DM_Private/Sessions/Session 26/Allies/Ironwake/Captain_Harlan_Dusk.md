## Roleplay

Gruff, pragmatic, fiercely loyal. Swears constantly. Calls Kirk "old barnacle." Will grumble about being dragged into things but fights hard. Semi-retired, running cargo routes on the Ironwake.

**Recruitment:** DC 8. Wants 500 gp deposit for ship repairs (returned if Ironwake survives).
