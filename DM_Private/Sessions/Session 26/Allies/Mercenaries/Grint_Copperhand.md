## Roleplay

All business. Counts coins openly. Doesn't care about the Spehlina's tragic backstory. Fights hard because his reputation is his livelihood.

**Morale:** Below 15 HP, he starts looking for an exit. If he goes down or flees, his sellswords roll DC 12 WIS each round or flee too.

**Recruitment:** DC 14. Costs 200 gp/head (1,200 gp total). DC 18 Persuasion to talk down to 150 each (900 gp). Offering a cut of the 10k bounty drops DC to 12.
