### Control Water — Tactical Uses
This is potentially game-changing in a lake fight:
- **Part Water:** Create a trench — expose the Spehlina, force it onto partial land
- **Whirlpool:** Trap the Spehlina, restrain ships
- **Redirect Flow:** Push the creature away from damaged ships
- **Lower Water:** Reduce depth to limit the Spehlina's mobility

### Destructive Wrath + Call Lightning
Maximize 3d10 = **30 lightning damage** in one bolt. Save for a critical moment.

**Radiant per Round:** Very high. Spirit Guardians + Guiding Bolt is devastating. Plus utility, healing, and Revivify.

## Roleplay

Warm but commanding. Carries herself like someone used to being listened to. Seasoned traveler and healer. Will take charge of divine strategy if nobody else does. Pushes hard for purification over killing.

**Recruitment:** DC 15 (DC 13 if she meets Morjor/Varin). No gold — condition is save, not kill.
