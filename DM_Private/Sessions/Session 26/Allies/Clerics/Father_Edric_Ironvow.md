### Key Combo for Purification
**Crusader's Mantle** adds **1d4 radiant to every melee hit** from every ally within 30 ft. With a full crew swinging, this stacks fast.

**Radiant per Round:** High. Spirit Guardians + Sacred Flame or Crusader's Mantle + party attacks.

## Roleplay

Stern, honorable, direct. Speaks in short sentences. Judges by actions, not words. Carries a worn copy of Tyr's Laws. "Tyr's work is not for sale."

**Recruitment:** DC 13. Frame it as justice — "protecting the summit" or "stopping Asmodeus's corruption." Mercenary arguments raise DC to 18. Won't accept payment.
