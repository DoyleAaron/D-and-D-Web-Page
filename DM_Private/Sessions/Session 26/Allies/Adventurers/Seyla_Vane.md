## Features

- **Sneak Attack:** 3d6 (need ADV or ally within 5 ft of target)
- **Cunning Action:** Bonus action Dash, Disengage, or Hide
- **Uncanny Dodge:** Reaction to halve one attack's damage
- **Rakish Audacity:** Add CHA (+2) to initiative. Sneak attack if no other creature within 5 ft of target
- **Fancy Footwork:** After melee attack, target can't opportunity attack her

## Roleplay

Quick-witted, always calculating risk vs. reward. Respects competence. If the party impresses her, she becomes more cooperative. Will not throw her crew's lives away.

**Recruitment:** DC 17. Wants 2,000 gp + salvage (DC 20 for 1,500 gp). Queen's authority: -3 DC. Brings *The Luckpenny*.
