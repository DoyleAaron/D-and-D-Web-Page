## Racial Traits (Half-Orc)

- **Relentless Endurance (1/long rest):** Drop to 1 HP instead of 0
- **Savage Attacks:** Extra damage die on melee crits

---

## Features

- **Rage (4/long rest):** +2 damage, resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing, ADV on STR checks/saves. 1 minute.
- **Reckless Attack:** ADV on STR melee attacks; attacks against her have ADV
- **Danger Sense:** ADV on DEX saves she can see
- **Feral Instinct:** ADV on initiative. Act normally on surprise if she rages
- **Divine Fury:** First hit each turn while raging: **+1d6+3 RADIANT damage**
- **Warrior of the Gods:** Revive spells on her need no material components
- **Fanatical Focus (1/rage):** Reroll a failed saving throw

## Roleplay

Loud, devout in a wild unstructured way. Prays to Bahamut by punching things. Calls Varin "Holy Man" with genuine reverence. Roars Bahamut's name mid-combat. Will be upset if the plan is to just kill the Spehlina — *"Bahamut says protect the innocent, and that thing is a prisoner!"*

May ask Varin for a blessing before battle (awkward given his curse).

**Recruitment:** DC 5 if Varin asks. DC 10 if someone else asks — wants to know Varin approves. No payment needed.
