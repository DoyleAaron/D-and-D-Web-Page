# The Luckpenny

*Light Salvage/Escort Vessel — Seyla Vane's Ship*

---

## Map Size

| Dimension | Feet | Squares (5 ft grid) |
|-----------|------|---------------------|
| **Length** | 60 ft | **12 squares** |
| **Width** | 15 ft | **3 squares** |

> Place as a 12×3 rectangle. Noticeably smaller than the other two ships. Sits lowest in the water. Position it at the back of the formation — it's fast enough to dart in and out but can't take punishment.

## Spehlina Damage Reference

| Attack | Hull Damage | Condition After (from full HP) |
|--------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| 1× Slam | 56 | Sinking (24 HP remaining) |
| 2× Slam | 112 | Destroyed |

**One slam sinks this ship.** Seyla knows this — she will not bring the Luckpenny close unless she has to.

---

## Tactical Notes

- Seyla runs hit-and-run. Close to 100 ft range, fire the ballista, pull back fast.
- **Dorek's Longbow** has 150 ft range — he doesn't need the ship to move close. He can fire from safety the whole fight.
- Fiora will push to move the ship closer. Seyla will refuse. This is a good small RP moment.
- If the Spehlina turns on the Luckpenny directly, Seyla immediately disengages — she's not dying for anyone's mission.
