## Conditions

| Hull HP | Condition | Effect |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| 150–100 | **Seaworthy** | No penalties |
| 99–75 | **Damaged** | Speed reduced to 30 ft/round |
| 74–40 | **Badly Damaged** | Speed 20 ft/round. Crew DC 12 CON each round or be too panicked to fire weapons |
| 39–1 | **Sinking** | Speed 10 ft/round. Taking on water — sinks in 1d4 hours unless repaired (DC 15 carpenter's tools) |
| 0 | **Destroyed** | Sinks immediately over 1d4 rounds. Everyone on board must swim or drown |

## Spehlina Damage Reference

The Spehlina's **Slam** deals **56 damage** to ships (Siege Monster, double damage to objects).  
Its **Agonized Thrash** (legendary action) deals **44 damage** to boats within 20 ft.

| Attack | Hull Damage | Condition After (from full HP) |
|--------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| 1× Slam | 56 | Damaged (94 HP remaining) |
| 2× Slam | 112 | Badly Damaged (38 HP remaining) |
| 3× Slam | 168 | Destroyed |

## Notes

- Flagship of this operation. Biggest and toughest vessel.
- Kirk is most effective in Water Form while aboard.
- If the Wavecutter sinks, Kirk's Water Form advantage is lost — he'd be in the open lake, which is worse.
