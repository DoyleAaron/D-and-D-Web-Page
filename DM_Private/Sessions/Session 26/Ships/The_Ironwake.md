## Tactical Notes

- **Fastest ship in the fleet.** Use it to circle the Spehlina, firing harpoons to tether and slow it.
- Harlan will instinctively manoeuvre to protect the Wavecutter. He knows how to read a naval fight.
- If badly damaged, Harlan will pull the Ironwake back to create distance — he's not suicidal.
- The harpoon tether is best used to prevent the Spehlina from Submerging with grappled crew members.
