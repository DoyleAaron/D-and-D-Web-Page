## Session Overview

The party (Morjor, Amdir, Varin, + Captain Kirk) must confront the **Spehlina**, a corrupted lake guardian, before it can be weaponized against the upcoming summit. They are currently in Dawerton (or nearby lakeside settlement) and need to recruit allies before taking to the water.

**Key Tensions:**
- Everyone is scared of the Spehlina — persuasion won't be easy
- Varin's cursed necklace is degrading his divine power — the party thinks *he* is the problem
- The party has a Scroll of Curse/Disease Removal — they might waste it on Varin instead of saving it for the Spehlina's purification
- Captain Kirk has a personal connection to the Spehlina (it saved him as a youth) — he wants it alive

**Victory Conditions:**
1. **Kill it** — 312 HP, CR 15. Triggers Death Throes (everyone in 60 ft takes 10d10 necrotic, DC 18 CON). Water within 1 mile is corrupted.
2. **Purify it** — Deal 100 radiant/holy damage total to cleanse Asmodeus's corruption. See mechanics below.
3. **Use the Scroll** — The Scroll of Curse/Disease Removal can purify the Spehlina instantly if applied, but the party might use it on Varin first.

## The Spehlina — Purification Mechanics

Track total **radiant damage** dealt to the Spehlina across the fight.

| Radiant Damage Dealt | Effect |
|---|---|
| **25 (25%)** | The Spehlina shudders. Its eyes flicker blue for a moment. Narrate a crack in the corruption. |
| **50 (50%)** | **Breakout Attempt.** The Spehlina rolls 1d20 — needs **15+** to break free for one round. On success: it stops attacking for that round. Next round it must pass the same check or resume attacking. Higher odds at this threshold. |
| **75 (75%)** | **Stronger Breakout.** Same 1d20 check but the DC drops — needs **12+**. On success: stops attacking, gets another check next round at 12+. The creature visibly fights itself, wailing. |
| **100 (100%)** | **Fully Purified.** Corruption drains away. The Spehlina returns to its original translucent blue-white form. It is no longer hostile. It remembers Kirk. |

> **Note:** The Scroll of Curse/Disease Removal bypasses all of this and purifies the Spehlina immediately if used on it. But the party may burn it on Varin's necklace curse instead.

**Sources of Radiant Damage Available:**
- Varin's cleric spells (unreliable due to curse — consider having him roll a d20 each time; on a 1-5, the spell fizzles or does half damage)
- Sacred Flame, Guiding Bolt, Spirit Guardians, etc.
- Any recruited clerics
- Rekka may have some divine flavor attacks (Bahamut blessing)
- Holy Water (2d6 radiant, can be thrown)
