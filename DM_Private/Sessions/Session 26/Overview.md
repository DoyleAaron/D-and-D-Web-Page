## Session Overview

The party (Morjor, Amdir, Varin, + Captain Kirk) must confront the **Spehlina**, a corrupted lake guardian, before it can be weaponized against the upcoming summit. They are currently in Dawerton (or nearby lakeside settlement) and need to recruit allies before taking to the water.

**Key Tensions:**
- Everyone is scared of the Spehlina — persuasion won't be easy
- Varin's cursed necklace is degrading his divine power — the party thinks *he* is the problem
- The party has a Scroll of Curse/Disease Removal — they might waste it on Varin instead of saving it for the Spehlina's purification
- Captain Kirk has a personal connection to the Spehlina (it saved him as a youth) — he wants it alive

**Victory Conditions:**
1. **Kill it** — 312 HP, CR 15. Triggers Death Throes (everyone in 60 ft takes 10d10 necrotic, DC 18 CON). Water within 1 mile is corrupted.
2. **Purify it** — Deal 100 radiant/holy damage total to cleanse Asmodeus's corruption. See purification table below.
3. **Use the Scroll** — Scroll of Curse/Disease Removal purifies the Spehlina instantly, but the party might use it on Varin first.

## Purification Tracker

Track total **radiant damage** dealt to the Spehlina across the fight.

| Radiant Dealt | Effect |
|---|---|
| **25 (25%)** | The Spehlina shudders. Its eyes flicker blue for a moment. A crack in the corruption. |
| **50 (50%)** | **Breakout Attempt.** Spehlina rolls 1d20 — needs **15+**. On success: stops attacking for 1 round, then must pass the same check next round or resumes. |
| **75 (75%)** | **Stronger Breakout.** Same check but needs **12+**. The creature visibly fights itself, wailing. |
| **100 (100%)** | **Fully Purified.** Corruption drains away. Returns to translucent blue-white form. No longer hostile. Remembers Kirk. |

**Radiant Damage Sources Available:**
- Varin's cleric spells (unreliable due to curse — consider d20 roll, 1-5 fizzle/half damage)
- Recruited clerics (Sacred Flame, Guiding Bolt, Spirit Guardians, Radiance of the Dawn)
- **Rekka's Divine Fury** — 1d6+3 radiant per round while raging
- **Father Edric's Crusader's Mantle** — adds 1d4 radiant to ALL allies' attacks in 30 ft.
- Holy Water — 2d6 radiant, thrown

## DM Notes

### Encounter Balance
- **Spehlina is CR 15** vs a level 8 party of 3 + Kirk (water form ~level 12). Deadly by itself.
- With full recruitment, the party has massive action economy but allies are fragile. AoE (Agonized Thrash, Corrupted Spray, Lair Actions) will chew through low-HP allies.
- **Corruption Aura** (DC 17 CON, 4d6 necrotic + exhaustion) is the real killer. Consider: only applies within 30 ft in water — ship distance mitigates.
- Kirk's Water Form is the biggest combat asset (+10 ADV, 2d8+8+2d6 cold).

### Key Decision Points
1. **Scroll on Varin or the Spehlina?** Mother Thessaly can detect the curse if recruited.
2. **Purify or kill?** Kirk and Rekka push hard for purification. Clerics agree. Mercs/adventurers don't care.
3. **How much gold on recruitment?** Budget matters.

### Questions
1. **Is this Lavalto Lake?** That's where lore places the Spehlina, but Lavalto is vampire territory.
2. **How much gold does the party have** after giving 10k to Liora?
3. **Varin's curse mechanic** — specific roll (d20, 1-5 fizzle) or narrative?
4. **Is the Wavecutter fully crewed** after Session 18 losses?
5. **Is Rekka still near Dawerton?**
6. **Pre-summit mission or at the summit?**
