## 💰 Payment and Rewards

### Gold Payment
| Type | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Base Pay | 100 gp per member |
| Travel Expenses | 25 gp per member |
| Quartermaster Chit | Up to 50 gp equipment/repairs |

### Magical Items Received

**Varin (Cleric)** – *Amulet of Sacred Waters*
- Holy symbol infused with divine water energy
- **Ability:** Channel holy water through the amulet (5 uses/day)
- On attack: +1d6 radiant damage (vs undead/fiends) or 1d6+Wis healing (ally touch)
- Recharges at dawn

**Amdir (Wizard)** – *Grimoire of Amplification*
- Leather-bound spellbook crackling with arcane energy
- **Ability:** 1/day, cast a spell at double damage/healing
- Recharges at dawn
- Can also be used as a spellcasting focus

**Morjor (Monk)** – *Insignia of the Claws*
- Bronze insignia depicting crossed beast claws
- **Ability:** Unarmed strikes count as magical (+1 to attack and damage rolls)

---

## 🩸 The Blood Pact

Liora offered the party a **blood pact**:
- Ensures they **die if they break it** (reveal her secrets)
- **The party accepted the pact**

### What Liora Revealed
After the pact was sealed:
- **Liora is a vampire**
- She fears **Father Maltrius Kael** (pureblood vampire in Lavalto)
- She does NOT want Maltrius to find Balthazar's resources before she does
- The **28th is Vampire Hunt Night** – vampires from Lavalto hunt on this night every month

---

## 📜 The Deal – Finding the Dungeon

### The Scroll
- Liora knows a **wizard in Lavalto** who can create a **Chosen-locating scroll**
- The spell locates a **person** (a Balthazar Chosen), not an item
- A Chosen can open the hidden Balthazar dungeon in Lavalto

### Cost Split
| Source | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| **Total Cost** | 15,000 gp |
| Liora's Contribution | 5,000 gp |
| Party Must Find | 10,000 gp |

### The Party's Agreement
- They agreed to help find a way to cover the remaining 10,000 gp
- Liora mentioned the **Spehlina bounty** (10,000 gp total) as an option

---

## 🗡️ Maerelle's Information

After the meeting with Liora, **Maerelle** spoke privately with the party:

### What She Revealed
- The Queen suspects a **traitor** in her ranks
- A **Balthazar item** has been traced to Dawerton
- The Queen's wizard advisor tracked it using magic pattern matching
- Maerelle did NOT share this with Liora (she withheld it from her)

### What the Party Now Knows
- They need a **Chosen** to open the Balthazar dungeon
- Someone in Dawerton has a Balthazar item (unknown: it's Morjor's ring)
- The Queen wants the item found

---

## 📖 Information About Balthazar

### The Hidden Lair
> *"Deep in the Lavalto volcano, there is said to be a hidden lair belonging to Balthazar. But it can only be opened by a Chosen."*

### The Chosen Explanation
- A **Balthazar Chosen** is similar to how gods have chosen champions
- Chosen emanate **faint magic** from their patron/master
- Items have unique signatures, making them harder to track than people

---

## 🏛️ The Wayfarers' Accord

Liora told the party about the **Wayfarers' Accord** adventurer's guild:
- Location: Wavecrest Hall, Dawerton Docks
- They can take the **Spehlina bounty** (10,000 gp) to fund the scroll
- But they need to rank up first (starts at D-tier, bounty requires B-tier)
