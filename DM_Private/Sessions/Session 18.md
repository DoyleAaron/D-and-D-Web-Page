**In-Game Date:** February 28th, 1225 (Tuesday) - day after Session 17
**Location:** The Wavecutter, sailing back / Adaham
**Theme:** Consequences, guilt, farewells — heavy RP session

## 📋 Key Beats

### 1. The Cost of Victory
- The Wavecutter is battered (84/150 hull)
- **Half the crew is dead** — Kirk's AOE abilities killed many of them
- The halflings are all dead — they were parents. Morjor recruited them personally.
- Deck is wrecked, blood everywhere, bodies need to be dealt with
- Surviving crew (dwarves, old humans, goblins) are shaken, angry, or grieving

### 2. Captain Kirk – Wants to Go Home
- Kirk got Marina back. That's all he wanted.
- He's horrified by the crew deaths — many caused by his own power
- He wants to sail the Wavecutter back to Adaham and be done
- **He will NOT eagerly sign up for the Spehlina mission** — needs convincing
- Kirk still cares about saving the Spehlina (not killing it), but the cost of this fight weighs on him
- Marina will pressure him to just go home

**Kirk's Mood:** Quiet, guilt-ridden, avoids eye contact with Morjor

### 3. Morjor's Guilt
- He recruited the halfling crew. They were parents.
- Now they're dead — killed in a fight that wasn't theirs
- This should hit hard in RP
- Other surviving crew may look at Morjor with resentment or sadness
- **Possible moment:** A surviving crewmate asks "Who's going to tell their children?"

### 4. Varin Leaves the Party
- Varin (Aiden) is leaving due to the moral weight of the deaths
- He can't reconcile what happened — too many innocents died
- **How to play it:**
  - Varin announces he's staying behind in Adaham (or wherever they dock)
  - Quiet farewell, not dramatic — he's a cleric, he's processing
  - Leave the door open for return ("If you need me, send word")
  - Give Aiden a proper send-off moment
- **Mechanically:** Varin is removed from the party roster

### 5. Payment & Loot
- **Kirk's promised reward:** 2,000 gp for rescuing Marina
- Kirk may try to reduce it or hesitate given the deaths — or he honours it because a deal is a deal
- **Iona's loot** (if collected):
  - Frostbite Cutlass (+1) — 500 gp or keep
  - Captain's Spyglass — 100 gp
  - Rimduff Charts — 50 gp
  - Iona's personal gold — 150 gp
- **Captured ships** — what does the party do with them?
  - Sell in Adaham? Keep one? Scuttle them?
  - Each ship worth ~500-1,000 gp
- **Bounties** (if turning in pirates):
  - Captain Iona: 1,500 gp (Braewood)
  - Each Bosun: 200 gp
  - Captured pirates: 10 gp each

### 6. Recruiting Kirk for the Spehlina
- The party still needs Kirk for the Spehlina mission (he's a massive asset at sea)
- Kirk's secret: he wants to SAVE the Spehlina, not kill it
- **Persuasion needed** — Kirk won't go willingly after what just happened
- Possible angles:
  - "The Spehlina is suffering, like those crew members suffered. You can end that."
  - "You're the only one who can do this. No one else knows the waters."
  - Marina could be the key — if she tells Kirk to go, he might
  - The 10,000 gp bounty could fund the Balthazar scroll (party's real goal)
- **DC suggestion:** Persuasion DC 15, with advantage if Marina supports it
