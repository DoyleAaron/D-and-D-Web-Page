# Encounter 1 — Volcanic Screechers

**Type:** Combat (Medium)  
**Location:** Coastal waters or road along the coast, within sight of Lavalto's volcanic peaks  
**Trigger:** During travel from Braewood to Dawerton, as the party nears the volcanic coastline  
**Theme:** The volcano's corruption is spreading — even the wildlife is affected

---

## Setup

As the party travels along the coast (by ship or coastal road), they spot dark shapes circling above the water ahead. Three large winged creatures — bat-like with charred, cracked skin glowing faintly orange at the joints — are hunting. They've been driven from their volcanic roosts by increasing seismic activity and are aggressively territorial.

> **Read Aloud:**  
> "The salt wind shifts, carrying a faint sulfur stench. Ahead, three dark shapes wheel above the waves — too large for gulls, too fast for pelicans. Their wings trail thin lines of heat-shimmer, and as one banks toward you, you see cracked, ember-veined skin stretched over a frame like a monstrous bat. It shrieks — a sound like steam escaping a kettle — and the other two turn to follow."

## Tactics

- Screechers use **Flyby** to swoop in, attack, and fly back out of melee range.
- They target isolated creatures or anyone at the edge of a ship/road.
- One opens with **Ember Screech** on a cluster; the other two dive on whoever is deafened.
- If two are killed, the third flees toward the volcano.

## Terrain Notes (If at Sea)

- Ship deck: 30 ft. × 10 ft. usable space.
- Rigging can be climbed (Athletics DC 12) to get within melee of circling screechers.
- Falling from rigging: 10 ft. = 1d6 bludgeoning, into water = swimming required.

## Terrain Notes (If on Road)

- Coastal cliff road, 15 ft. wide. Drop-off on one side (30 ft. to rocks/water).
- Scattered boulders provide half cover.
- Horses may panic: Animal Handling DC 12 to control, or horse bolts 60 ft.
