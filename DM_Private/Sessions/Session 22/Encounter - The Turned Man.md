# Encounter 2 — The Turned Man

**Type:** Combat / RP (can resolve either way)  
**Location:** Dawerton — a residential street near the eastern market  
**Trigger:** Liora or a guard report directs the party; or they hear screaming  
**Theme:** The human cost of Maltrius's expansion — a man destroyed by something he didn't choose

---

## Setup

**Edric Voss** was a carpenter. Three nights ago, one of Maltrius's brood vampires entered Dawerton and fed on him, leaving enough of its blood to turn him. Edric woke the next night confused, starving, and unable to control the hunger. He killed his wife (Maren) and young son (Tobin, age 8). He's been hiding in his family home since, horrified by what he's done, occasionally lurching into the street when the hunger overwhelms him.

When the party arrives, Edric is crouched in the doorway of his home. The door is smashed. Blood smears the frame. Neighbors are watching from shuttered windows. A town guard lies unconscious (not dead) in the street — Edric knocked him out but couldn't bring himself to feed again.

> **Read Aloud:**  
> "The street is too quiet for midday. Shutters are closed. A town guard lies face-down near a water trough, breathing but still. At the end of the row, a man crouches in a broken doorframe. His clothes are stiff with dried blood. His hands shake. When he looks up, his eyes are red-rimmed and wet — not with rage, but with something worse. He whispers: 'I can smell you from here. Please. Please don't come closer.'"

**Saves:** DEX +4, WIS +2  
**Resistances:** Necrotic; Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing from nonmagical attacks  
**Vulnerabilities:** Radiant  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12  
**CR:** 3 (700 XP)

**Regeneration.** Edric regains 5 HP at the start of his turn if he has at least 1 HP and isn't in sunlight or running water. If he takes radiant or holy water damage, this trait doesn't function until the start of his next turn.

**Sunlight Hypersensitivity.** While in sunlight, Edric has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.

**Spider Climb.** Edric can climb difficult surfaces, including ceilings, without needing an ability check.

**Unstable Mind.** At the start of each of Edric's turns, roll 1d6:
- **1–2:** Edric is paralyzed by guilt. He does nothing (crouches, weeps, begs for death).
- **3–4:** Edric acts normally (attacks nearest creature driven by hunger).
- **5–6:** Edric tries to flee, dashing away from all creatures.

**Actions:**

- **Multiattack.** Edric makes two attacks: one with claws, one with bite (or two claws if no grappled target).
- **Claws.** *Melee:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 8 (2d4 + 3) slashing damage. Target is grappled (escape DC 13) if Medium or smaller.
- **Bite.** *Melee:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one willing/grappled/incapacitated/restrained creature. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. Edric regains HP equal to necrotic dealt. Target's max HP reduced by same until long rest.

## RP Resolution Options

The party **does not have to fight Edric.** This encounter can end several ways:

| Approach | DC | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| **Persuasion** (talk him down) | DC 14 | Edric stops fighting. Weeps. Surrenders. Asks the party to kill him or find a cure. |
| **Religion** (invoke Bahamut/divine presence) | DC 13 | Edric recoils from divine energy. Becomes docile for 1 minute. |
| **Intimidation** (overpower his hunger) | DC 15 | Edric cowers. Stops attacking but remains dangerous if approached. |
| **Restrain** (grapple + bind) | Opposed STR | Can be subdued physically. Rope + manacles hold him. |
| **Radiant damage** (any source) | — | Suppresses regeneration and hunger for 1 round. He becomes lucid. |

**If killed:** Edric crumbles. The guilt on his face fades to relief. The neighbors emerge. A woman asks about Maren and Tobin. Silence.

**If subdued/saved:** Liora can arrange containment. She knows the condition can be reversed if the **master vampire who turned him is killed within 30 days** (or via Greater Restoration). This creates a clock for the Brood encounter.

## Scene Details

- **Maren and Tobin's bodies** are inside the house (covered with a bedsheet, Edric did this himself).
- **Neighbors:** Frightened. An old woman, **Hilde**, watched from her window. She saw "a tall figure in a dark cloak" enter Edric's home three nights ago and leave before dawn.
- **Guard (Corporal Brennan):** Unconscious, stable. Will confirm Edric was "a good man" when he wakes.
