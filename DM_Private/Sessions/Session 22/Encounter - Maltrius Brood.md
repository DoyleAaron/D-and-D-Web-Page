# Encounter 3 — Maltrius's Brood

**Type:** Combat (Hard/Deadly)  
**Location:** Ruined farmstead on the road between Dawerton and Lavalto volcano, ~2 hours outside town  
**Trigger:** Liora gives the party the location; or the party tracks them from Hilde's description  
**Theme:** Maltrius's reach extends beyond Lavalto — this family serves him out of fear, loyalty, and hunger

---

## Setup

The Valdris brood has moved from roadside ambushes into a stolen **noble manor house** outside Dawerton. They now operate as a "respectable household" by day and a hunting cell by night.

- **House name:** Blackthorn House (2 stories, attic, and full basement)
- **Public facade:** quiet wealthy family with staff
- **Reality:** vampire family, iron golem wardens, and collared human servants with blank expressions

The collared servants are alive, but mentally suppressed by blood-and-metal control collars. They move mechanically, speak little, and obey simple commands.

> **Read Aloud (arrival at dusk):**  
> "A grand country manor rises behind wrought-iron fencing and dead rose hedges. Warm candlelight glows in tall windows, but no music, no laughter, no life drifts out. At the gate stand two servants in black livery, each with a steel collar etched with tiny runes. Their eyes are open, but empty. Somewhere inside, something heavy drags across polished wood."

---

## Difficulty Target

This version is tuned to feel like a **CR 7-8 boss encounter** for a level 6 party by splitting action into phases instead of dumping all enemies at once.

- **Phase 1 (Ground Floor):** House Golem + 4 Collared Servants
- **Phase 2 (Upper Floor):** Isolde + Theron
- **Optional Escalation:** Senna joins only if party is dominating the fight

If you run all named enemies simultaneously, it becomes a high-deadly encounter.

**CR 6 (2,300 XP)**

| Stat | Value |

| **CON** | 18 (+4) |

| **WIS** | 14 (+2) |

**Saves:** DEX +6, CON +7, WIS +5, CHA +6  
**Skills:** Deception +6, Insight +5, Perception +5, Stealth +6  
**Resistances:** Necrotic; bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical attacks  
**Vulnerabilities:** Radiant  
**Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 15

**Regeneration.** Isolde regains 10 HP at start of turn if she has at least 1 HP and isn't in sunlight and didn't take radiant or holy-water damage since her last turn.  
**Spider Climb.** Can climb difficult surfaces, including ceilings.  
**Sunlight Hypersensitivity.** Disadvantage on attacks/checks in sunlight.

**Actions**
- **Multiattack.** Two attacks: one Claw and one Bite (or two Claws).
- **Claw.** +7 to hit, 1d8+4 slashing; target grappled (escape DC 14).
- **Bite.** +7 to hit, one grappled/incapacitated/restrained target; 1d6+4 piercing + 3d6 necrotic. Isolde heals necrotic dealt.
- **Blood Command (Recharge 5-6).** One creature within 30 ft. that took necrotic damage this encounter makes DC 14 WIS save or is charmed until end of its next turn.

## Tactical Flow (Recommended)

1. **Foyer breach:** servants stall, golem engages frontline.
2. **Stair choke point:** house layout forces split pressure.
3. **Upper floor reveal:** Isolde + Theron engage after golem falls or after 3 rounds.
4. **Optional Senna entry:** only if party is winning hard.

---

## Special Story Rule: Edric's Cure Clock

Killing **Theron** still satisfies the "kill the sire-line source" condition for Edric's reversal window.
