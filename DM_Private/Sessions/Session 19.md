## Opening Intelligence Beat - Royal Scry Check (Mandatory)

The first hard beat is a **scrying pass** from Braewood's court.

- Purpose: verify party location and behavior after prolonged turbulence at sea.
- Queen's stance: suspicious of traitors in general, but does **not** currently place the party as primary suspects.
- Why now: the summit was called because of missing/active Balthazar assets and growing distrust between powers.

### Run It Quickly
- Have each character roll a hidden **Wisdom save** (or use passive for fast play).
- On fail: they feel watched, cold pressure at the temples, brief silver-water reflection effect.
- On strong fail: give one symbolic image (bridge chains, three crowns, volcanic ash).
- On success: they sense an attempted divination but can mask surface intent for a moment.

### Optional Counterplay
- `Nondetection`, `Mind Blank`, lead-lined warding, holy sanctum, or deliberate magical noise can obscure details.
- Arcana/Religion/Insight checks can identify this as high court divination rather than vampire surveillance.

## Saving the Spehlina (Expanded Cleansing Options)

Killing the Spehlina is still the wrong first answer while the infernal source remains active.

### Why It Was Corrupted
- The creature was infected by long-term infernal-magical runoff tied to Father Maltrius Kael's volcano domain.
- The same broader corruption web supports his resurrection agenda in the mountain.

### Baseline Canon Method
1. **Seal/stop source first** (Maltrius or vents).
2. **Greater Restoration + Remove Curse + Ceremony** performed in synchronized ritual windows.
3. Kirk anchors the creature on open water while casters work.

### Additional DnD-Compatible Cure Approaches
1. **Hallow Basin Rite (shoreline method)**
- `Hallow` cast on a ritual shoreline circle.
- Lure Spehlina into sanctified boundary using `Control Water`.
- Pair with `Dispel Evil and Good` pulses to peel infernal imprint.

2. **Three-Faith Purge (cleric conclave method)**
- Three divine casters each hold one function: cleansing body, cleansing spirit, sealing corruption.
- Suggested spell stack: `Greater Restoration`, `Heal`, `Ceremony` over consecutive rounds.

3. **Mercy-Containment Contingency (if cleanse fails)**
- Only valid after source is cut.
- Use `Hallow` + consecrated barriers + mass purification support to contain death-throe release.

4. **Nature-Spirit Rebinding (druidic support)**
- Combine druidic magic (`Greater Restoration` equivalent effects via circle rituals) with divine curse removal.
- Framed as restoring original guardian function, not only removing affliction.

### Discovery Paths For Party
- Temple scholars, nature sages, and old Islefield records on sacred guardians.
- Corruption sample testing in port shrines.
- Kirk testimony about Spehlina behavior before full decline.
