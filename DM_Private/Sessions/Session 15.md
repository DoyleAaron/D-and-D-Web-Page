## 📍 Opening Scene – Liora's Reward

After defeating Father Halden, **Liora** grants the party **3 questions** as their reward.

### Question 1: Information about Balthazar

**What Liora Knows:**
- There is a **dungeon hidden somewhere in Lavalto** said to be one of Balthazar's.
- **Warning:** Lavalto is extremely dangerous.
  - A **dragon** is rumored to live in the volcanic mountain range.
  - More critically: **Pureblood vampires** rule there.
- To find the dungeon, you would need a **Balthazar Chosen** – the dungeon only opens for them.

**Additional Lead:**
- Liora knows **someone in Kluimont** (she doesn't reveal it's a Council of Ten mage).
- For a **hefty sum**, he could cast a **modified deity-chosen location spell** to find a Balthazar Chosen.
- **Why this works:** The spell is a tweak of one that locates a deity's chosen. Items are harder – the Queen's wizard advisor could only track the item by copying Balthazar's magic pattern from the book (Session 1 dungeon) and searching for that signature. But a Chosen is a living vessel, not an object.
- **Cost hint:** "It would not be cheap. Thousands of gold."

- Kluimont was supposed to send an **envoy** to Dawerton.

- Father Maltrius performed a **mass memory-wiping spell** – the party has no memory of meeting Tarth.

**Who They Are:**

- **Commander Seral** – Military escort, appears legitimate

**Official Story:**
- They're investigating on behalf of the Queen.
- The Queen's **wizard advisor** has been tracking one of Balthazar's gifted items.
- The trail leads to **Dawerton**.

**Hidden Truth:**
- Merelle is really here for her own gain (same agenda as Amdir unknowingly shares).
- The item they're tracking is **Morjor's ring** – and no one knows he has it.
- The ring was given to Morjor by Balthazar after the dungeon in Session 4.

**What They Bring:**
- **Mission rewards** from the Queen for the completed mission:
  - **100 gp per party member** (base pay)
  - **25 gp per party member** (travel expenses)
  - **Quartermaster chit** (up to 50gp in equipment/repairs)
  
**Queen's Offer:**
- If the party can **find the Balthazar item**, the Queen will grant each member **any request within her power**.

## 🗺️ Lavalto Geography – The Volcanic Mountain Range

**Scale:**
- The Lavalto volcano range is **massive** – nearly **60 km in diameter**.
- A labyrinth of peaks, calderas, lava tubes, and volcanic terrain.

**Key Locations:**

### 1. Father Maltrius Kael's Domain
- The **pureblood vampire cleric of Asmodeus** rules from somewhere within.
- He has **subdued a red dragon** that serves him.
- Even he has **not found** the Balthazar dungeon.

### 2. The Hidden Balthazar Dungeon
- Location unknown – even to Father Maltrius.
- **Entry requirement:** Only opens for a **Balthazar Chosen**.
- The party doesn't know Morjor qualifies.

### 3. Lavalto Lake
- Large volcanic lake at the base of the range.
- Home to a **massive lake monster** (see quest below).

## 🐉 Quest Hook: The Terror of Lavalto Lake (A-Tier)

### The Bounty
| Source | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Dawerton | 5,000 gp |
| Crown of Braewood | 5,000 gp |
| **Total** | **10,000 gp** |

### The Creature: The Spehlina
- Once a **peaceful guardian** that purified the lake by absorbing pollution
- **Corrupted by Asmodeus's influence** seeping from Father Maltrius Kael's domain
- Now a 60-foot horror in constant agony, attacking anything in the water

**The Dilemma (Party Won't Know):**
- If killed, the corruption it has absorbed will be **released into the lake**
- Without the Spehlina to absorb it, the ongoing infernal seepage will **corrupt the waters**
- Shores of Dawerton and nearby settlements will suffer blighted water, dead fish, and worse

*See: [The Spehlina](../Creatures/The%20Spehlina.md) for full stat block and lore*

### Potential Allies: The Saltheart Crew
Monster hunters from Adaham interested in the bounty:
- **Captain Nessa Drenn** – Grizzled sea captain seeking redemption
- **First Mate Korvax** – Massive half-orc, afraid of deep water
- **Harpooner Ysh** – Triton scout who can breathe underwater

**Their Offer:** 50/50 split. They provide ship and expertise, party provides combat power.

## ⚓ NEW ALLY – Captain Kirk (Temp PC)

### Who He Is
- **Captain Kirk** – Retired naval captain from **Adaham**
- Former captain of the **King's personal ship and fleet** for Braewood
- Descendant of the **Pirate Queen, Ruler of the Reef Confederacy**
- Has a **warlock pact** with a neutral water deity (**Zephyrion, the Indifferent Tide**)
- Sacrificed: one eye (wears eyepatch), his manhood, his soul after death

### His Secret Connection to the Spehlina
- When Kirk was younger, the Spehlina **saved his life** (before its corruption)
- He knows about the bounty and fears someone will kill the creature
- **His hidden goal:** Save the Spehlina, not destroy it
- This creates interesting tension with the party's likely objective

### His Daughter – Marina
- Adopted—Kirk rescued her from raiders where she was a slave
- She's a sailor, was traveling to **Quey** on business
- She **never arrived** and hasn't made contact—extremely unlike her
- Kirk is desperate to find her

**DM Secret:** Marina was kidnapped by **Captain Iona**, a menacing pirate captain who roams the **Rimduff Isles**. Kirk does not know this.

---

### How He Enters the Story

**Location:** Wayfarers' Accord guild hall

**Scene:** When the party arrives at the guild, Kirk is at the counter arguing with **Yenna Cole** (the registrar). He's trying to post a mission to find his daughter but is frustrated by the evaluation process.

> **Read Aloud:**
> *An older man in a weathered naval coat argues heatedly with the half-elf behind the counter. An eyepatch covers his left eye, and a curved sword hangs at his hip. Despite his age, he carries himself with commanding authority.*
>
> **Kirk:** "We don't have time for your evaluation process! My daughter's been missing for over a week!"
>
> **Yenna:** "Sir, I understand, but the Accord has procedures—"
>
> **Kirk:** "Damn your procedures! I'll pay **two thousand gold** to anyone who helps me find her. Right now."

**If the party interjects:** Kirk will allow them to help immediately—he's desperate and they look capable.

**If the party doesn't interject:** Kirk will notice them and approach directly, offering the job.

### Important Notes
- This is **NOT a Wayfarer task**—no guild rank promotion
- Reward: **2,000 gp** for finding Marina
- The mission leads toward the Rimduff Isles and Captain Iona

### Kirk's Power Levels
- **On Land:** Level 6 (Fighter 4/Warlock 2)—competent but not exceptional
- **In Water:** Massively empowered—effectively level 12, can help the party take on the Spehlina
- **Trump Card:** Can transform into **Hestaria** (sea monster)—10 minutes of immense power, then he dies
