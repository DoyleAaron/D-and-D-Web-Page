## Varin Curse Track (Run Daily)

The red-gem necklace is a suppressed cursed relic tied to the Sealed God thread.

## Daily Roll Rule
- **Once per in-game day, roll 1d20 for Varin's curse check.**
- Start at **DC 5** on Day 1 of active symptoms.
- **Increase the DC by +1 each day** (Day 2 = DC 6, Day 3 = DC 7, etc.).

## On Failed Check
- Varin suffers spell interference for that day:
  - First leveled cleric spell cast each day has a disruption effect (pick one): delayed trigger, reduced range, or visual corruption (ash-red light, infernal crackle).
  - Communication/prayer to **Bahamut** returns fragmented omens instead of clear guidance.
- Add one nightmare intensity step (see below).

## On Successful Check
- No mechanical penalty that day, but keep flavor signs (ringing in ears, faint heat at the throat, uneasy dreams).

## Nightmare Progression (Nightly)
1. **Stage 1:** Smoke-filled temple, distant dragon voice muffled by stone.
2. **Stage 2:** Volcanic cavern, chained altar, red gemstone heartbeat.
3. **Stage 3:** A faceless priest in infernal vestments (Maltrius silhouette) offering "clarity".
4. **Stage 4+:** Varin wakes with prayer words replaced by unknown phrases; disadvantage on first Religion check next day.

DM note: remove or suppress stages only after meaningful curse investigation and cleansing steps.
