# Session 16 – "The Rimduff Isles"

**In-Game Date:** TBD (after Session 15)  
**Location:** Rimduff Isles – Captain Iona's Territory

---

## 📍 Session Overview

This session covers the party's voyage to the Rimduff Isles to rescue **Marina** (Captain Kirk's daughter) from **Captain Iona**.

---

## 🚢 The Voyage

### Kirk's Ship
- Captain Kirk retrieves his ship from **Adaham**
- Ocean voyage to the Rimduff Isles
- Kirk's **water form** activates while sailing

### Potential Sea Encounters
- Pirates spotting the ship
- Storm/navigation challenges
- Sea creatures

---

## 🏝️ Rimduff Isles – Iona's Cove

### The Location
- Hidden pirate cove on one of the Rimduff Isles
- **The Crimson Tide** (Iona's ship) anchored in the cove
- Beach fortifications with watchtower
- Prison cave built into the cliff face

### Approach Options
1. **Frontal Assault** – Attack the beach fortifications
2. **Stealth Infiltration** – Sneak in at night 
3. **Naval Battle** – Ship-to-ship combat
4. **Diplomacy** – Attempt to negotiate/barter

---

## ⚔️ Key Encounters

### Encounter 1: Beach Approach
- **Location:** Iona's Cove
- **Enemies:** 
  - Pirate lookouts (2-4 Bandits)
  - Watchtower archer (Scout)
- **Alarm:** If raised, reinforcements arrive in 2 rounds

### Encounter 2: The Crimson Tide
- **Location:** Pirate ship deck
- **Enemies:**
  - First Mate Craw (Hobgoblin Captain)
  - Pirates (8-10 Bandits)
- **Note:** Captain Iona may or may not be aboard

### Encounter 3: Prison Cave
- **Location:** Sea cave prison
- **Enemies:**
  - Prison guards (3 Bandits)
  - Captain Iona (if cornered here)
- **Objective:** Rescue Marina

### Boss Fight: Captain Iona
- **HP:** 85, **AC:** 17
- **Frostbite Cutlass:** +7 to hit, 1d8+4 slashing + 1d6 cold
- **Multiattack:** 3 attacks per round
- **Tactics:** Uses terrain, orders crew strategically
