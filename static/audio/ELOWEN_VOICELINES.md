## 🎙️ Recording Tips

1. **Voice:** Friendly, confident female elf with a hint of playful wit
2. **Accent:** Whatever feels natural - slight Irish/Celtic works well for elves
3. **Energy:** Welcoming like a favorite bartender, not overly formal
4. **Pace:** Moderate, let words breathe - these play while reading text
5. **Character greetings:** Each should feel like she genuinely knows them
6. **Format:** MP3, 128kbps, mono is fine
